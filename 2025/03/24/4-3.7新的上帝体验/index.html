第七章：新的上帝体验

发表于2025-03-24|更新于2025-03-29|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷新的上帝体验
|总字数:8 k|阅读时长:33 分钟

The New God Experience

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on January 3, 2017 in Boulder, Colorado
2017 年 1 月 3 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

There must be a new experience of God in the world, an experience of God that is not bound by strict belief or admonition, an experience that is not defined by religious authorities or traditions. For no tradition can define an experience of God, as no religion can define God’s Will, purpose and activity in the world. There must be a new experience of God, a New God experience: pure, refreshing, uplifting, empowering.

世界上必须有一种新的上帝体验，一种不受严格信仰或劝诫约束的上帝体验，一种不受宗教权威或传统定义的体验。因为没有传统可以定义上帝的体验，就像没有宗教可以定义上帝在世界上的意志、目的和活动一样。必须有一种新的上帝体验，一种新的上帝体验：纯洁、清新、振奋、赋予力量。

This is the aim of all true religions that have been initiated by God, but in the hands of the separated, in the hands of people here on Earth living apart from Creation, these things are turned into scripture, admonition, control, definition and resistance to others whose definitions are different, whose prescriptions are different, whose admonitions are different.

这是所有由上帝发起的真正宗教的目标，但是在分离者的手中，在地球上远离造物主生活的人们的手中，这些东西被变成了经文、警示、控制、定义和抵抗，而其他人的定义不同，他们的处方不同，他们的警示也不同。

What is pure and holy becomes separated and diffracted in this world. And so God must speak again at this great turning point where humanity faces a world in decline and the reality of life in the universe, the reality of contact that is occurring in the world today and has been occurring for some time.

纯粹和神圣的东西在这个世界上变得分裂和分散。因此上帝必须在这个伟大转折点再次讲话，人类正面对一个衰落的世界，面对宇宙生命的现实，面对当今世界正在发生并且已经发生一段时间的接触的现实。

Humanity is at a great threshold and needs a new experience of God, an experience that comes from God directly through a power of Knowledge that God has put within each person, a greater Intelligence deep beneath the surface of your worldly mind, waiting to be discovered, the great endowment from God, a gift of unparalleled value to guide you, to protect you and to lead you to a greater life of service in the world.

人类正处在一个伟大的门槛，需要一种对上帝的新体验，这种体验直接来自上帝，通过上帝放置在每个人内在的一种内识力量，它深藏在你世俗心灵的表面之下、等待被发现的一种更伟大智能，这是来自上帝的伟大馈赠，这是无可比拟的价值礼物，来指引你、保护你并引领你走向一种服务世界的更伟大生命。

You experience God through the power and presence of this Knowledge, through the guidance of this Knowledge. By taking the Steps to Knowledge, you begin to experience its power and its presence because this is your God connection, you see. This is the immortal part of you that no religion can fully describe. It is beyond the intellectual grasp of humanity and any race in this Greater Community of life in the universe.

通过这种内识的力量和存在，通过这种内识的指导，你体验到了上帝。通过开展内识的进阶，你开始体验到它的力量和存在，因为这是你与上帝的连接，你看。这是你不朽的部分，没有宗教可以完全描述。它超出了人类和宇宙生命大社区中任何种族的智力理解的范围。

Define God and you will lose God. Admonish others’ definition of God and you will lose God. Hold on to God as an exclusive right or privilege and you will lose God.

定义上帝，你就会失去上帝。劝告他人对上帝的定义，你就会失去上帝。坚持认为上帝是独有的权利或特权，你就会失去上帝。

For God is not about contraction and being defensive, about condemning others. This is the old definition of God that humanity must now outgrow. The experience of God is here to unite humanity, to end its ceaseless conflicts, to end competition and contention between the religions of the world, which have all been initiated by God and changed by man. How little humanity knows of God—God’s Will, Presence and Power in this world.

因为上帝不是关于收缩和防御，也不是关于谴责他人。这是人类必须超越的旧有上帝定义。上帝的体验是为了团结人类，结束人类之间无休止的冲突，结束世界各大宗教之间的竞争和争斗，这些宗教都由上帝发起，却被人类改变。人类对上帝——上帝在这个世界上的意志、存在和力量——的知道是多么的少啊。

What is this experience We speak of here today? It is a deep experience of purpose and meaning and direction in life. It is not about having fantastic high experiences or reveling in a Divine Presence. You may have moments where you experience such things, but the New God experience is about emerging out of confusion, the fog of confusion, emerging out of living a haphazard life without a true direction. It is coming back to your primary purpose for being here, which God has put within you, within Knowledge within you. It is quiet. It is deep. It is clear, taking you step by step into a new life, leading you out of the fog of the world.

我们今天在此谈论的这种体验是什么？它是对生命的目的、意义和方向的深度体验。它不是关于拥有奇妙的崇高体验或陶醉于神圣的存在。你可能有过这样的经历，但新的上帝体验是关于走出困惑、走出困惑的迷雾，走出没有真正方向的杂乱无章的生命。它是回到你在这里的首要目的，上帝把它放在你内在，放在你内在的内识里。它是安静的。它是深邃的。它是清晰的，带你一步步走向新的生命，带领你走出世界的迷雾。

It is not something that happens all at once. It is not an idea. It is not a concept. It is not an insight. It is a growing revolution and revelation within you that God has set in motion when you are ready.

它不是一下子发生的事情。它不是一个想法。它不是一个概念。它不是一种洞察力。它是你内心不断增长的革命和启示，当你准备好时，上帝就会启动它。

God does not need worship and prostration. God needs for you to do what you came here to do, to carry out your greater purpose for being here, which is as yet unknown to you, for it does not reside within your intellect but within a far deeper place within you, a place that few have explored.

上帝不需要崇拜和俯首称臣。上帝需要你去做你来此要做的事，去履行你在此的更伟大目的，这对你来说仍是未知的，因为它不在你的智力之中，而是在你内在的一个更深层次的地方，一个很少有人探索过的地方。

When you come into this experience, you begin to understand other religions—their core strengths and truth. And you can tell what has been added on to them, for human convenience or adoption or by those who seek to use religion for power and personal gain. You begin to see the world in a very different way. You see the suffering of people with compassion, for you know now, finally, that without this power of Knowledge to guide them, they will be lost in this world—governed by others, living a haphazard life of struggle, difficulty and deprivation. Be they rich or poor, they are lost within this world—unknown to themselves, unknown to others, seeking constant stimulation or escape from their own internal crisis.

当你进入这种体验时，你开始理解其他宗教——它们的核心力量和真理。你可以分辨出它们被添加了什么，是为了人类的便利或采纳，还是那些试图利用宗教获得权力和个人利益的人。你开始以一种非常不同的方式看待世界。你以同情心看待人们的苦难，因为你现在终于知道，如果没有内识的力量来指导他们，他们将迷失在这个世界上——被他人统治，过着充满挣扎、困难和匮乏的随意生命。无论他们富有还是贫穷，他们都迷失在这个世界上——不知道他们自己，不知道他人，不断寻求刺激或逃避他们自身的内在危机。

God has the answer. But you must experience God clearly, openly, without the confines of belief, without demanding intellectual understanding, without assuming you know things you do not know, without believing things that are not based upon the deeper Knowledge within you.

上帝有答案。但你必须清晰、公开地体验上帝，不受限于信仰，不要求智力上的理解，不假设你知道你不知道的事情，不相信那些不基于你内在更深层次内识的事情。

There must be a clearing away first, an opening, gradually, so that you can come to understand the greater truth of your life and transition your life into this greater truth, which is a process that requires many steps and certain thresholds.

首先必须有一个清理，一个开放，逐渐地，这样你才能理解你生命的更伟大真理，并将你的生命转变为这个更伟大的真理，这是一个需要许多步骤和一定门槛的过程。

The journey begins with dissatisfaction. The journey begins with recognizing that you are not living the life you know you are meant to live. The journey begins with recognizing that all of the human commentary in the world, alone, cannot give you the answer that you seek.

旅途始于不满。旅途始于认识到你没有过上你想要的生命。旅途始于认识到世界上所有人类的评论都无法给你你想要的答案。

For your greater purpose in life is not a human creation. It is a Divine creation, and Knowledge has been put within you to bring you to it, to enable you to follow it step by step. You will not fully understand it until much later, but understanding comes later if it is true and efficacious.

因为你生命中更伟大的目的不是人类创造的。它是神圣的创造，内识被放置在你的内在，带你走向它，让你能够一步一步地追随它。你要等到很久以后才能完全理解它，但如果它是真实而有效的，理解就会随之而来。

People around the world are seeking a New God experience. Their religions have become dry and brittle, empty, formulaic, repetitive, but lacking the essence and the substance and the true relationship that must be emphasized, and that is emphasized in this new experience of God.

世界各地的人们都在寻求一种新的上帝体验。他们的宗教变得枯燥、脆弱、空洞、公式化、重复，却缺乏必须强调的本质、实质和真正的关系，而这些正是这种新的上帝体验所强调的。

In a way, it is not a new experience. It is an ancient experience. But it is an experience that so few have had that it must be brought anew, where all that impedes it and holds it back and covers it up must be cleared away.

从某种意义上说，它不是一种新的体验。它是一种古老的体验。但很少有人有过这种体验，因此必须重新开始，必须清除所有阻碍它、束缚它、掩盖它的事物。

You have been sent into the world for a greater purpose. Those who sent you, your Spiritual Family, are watching over you to see how you navigate this difficult world, this world of confusion and corruption, this world of fear and anger.

你被派到这个世界是为了一个更伟大的目的。那些派你来的存在，你的精神家庭，正在注视着你，看你如何驾驭这个艰难的世界，这个混乱和腐败的世界，这个恐惧和愤怒的世界。

It is not an easy place to be. But you have been sent here for a purpose, and that purpose lives within you and holds for you your New God experience. For once you begin to relate to the power and presence of Knowledge within you, once you begin to become aware of it and allow it to reshape and guide your life, you will know that you are not alone. You will know that Greater Powers are watching over you. You will know that you have a core responsibility and will not want to give yourself to things of no meaning or value, purpose or direction.

它不是一个容易的地方。但你被派到这里是为了一个目的，这个目的存在于你的内在，为你保留着你的新上帝体验。因为一旦你开始与你内在内识的力量和存在相关，一旦你开始察觉到它并允许它重塑和指导你的生命，你就会知道你并不孤单。你会知道更伟大的力量正在注视着你。你会知道你有一个核心责任，并且不想把你自己交给没有意义或价值、目的或方向的事情。

This is the New God experience. It is not about having a big insight or a moment of prescience alone. It is a reality emerging in your life, simply at first, in very subtle ways, as you struggle with self-doubt and uncertainty. Perhaps it is contrary to your notions of religion and what you have been taught. Perhaps it is contrary to the views and beliefs of those around you who influence you. Perhaps it does not make any sense in a rational way. But what is human reason but a coping mechanism to deal with the uncertain and difficult world?

这就是新的上帝体验。它不只是关于拥有一个伟大的洞察力或一瞬间的预知。它是你生命中出现的现实，一开始是简单的，以非常微妙的方式，当你与自我怀疑和不确定性作斗争时。也许它与你的宗教观念和你所学的事物相反。也许它与你周围影响你的人的观点和信仰相反。也许它没有任何理性的意义。但人类理性除了是一种应对不确定和困难世界的应对机制之外，还有什么呢？

The greater truth is beyond these things. The intellect is a great servant, but it cannot be the master. Rationality is a useful tool in many ways, but it is not the ultimate truth of your life. Religious belief can help or hinder you, but it must lead you to this greater experience that We speak of here today if it is to be true and meaningful.

更伟大的真理超越这些。智力是一个伟大的仆人，但它不能成为主人。理性在很多方面是一个有用的工具，但它不是你生命的终极真理。宗教信仰可以帮助你或阻碍你，但它必须引导你走向我们今天在此讲述的更伟大的体验，如果它是真实和有意义的。

The world needs a new experience of God, beyond religious edicts and beliefs, beyond the controlling forces of religion or public opinion that guide, sway and control so many people.

世界需要一种新的对上帝的体验，超越宗教法令和信仰，超越引导、影响和控制如此多人的宗教或舆论的控制力量。

This is part of God’s great gift to you, which is waiting to be discovered, which is so close at hand. But you are looking out away from it. It is standing with you and behind you, but you are looking elsewhere—captivated by the world, afraid of the world, trying to be happy in this world.

这是上帝赐予你的伟大礼物的一部分，它正等待着你去发现，它就在你身边。但你却在回避它。它就在你身边，在你身后，但你却在看别处——被世界迷住，害怕世界，试图在这个世界上快乐。

Yet God is with you, but you are not with God. The New God experience is within you, but you are not searching for it yet. It is not an intellectual enterprise. It is a deep reckoning within yourself, into the greater part of you that you know very little about, the great well of wisdom of Knowledge within you that you experience perhaps only momentarily here and there or [when] you feel its power to restrain you in certain things or to compel you in other things.

然而上帝与你同在，但你却不与上帝同在。新的上帝体验就在你内在，但你尚未去寻找它。它不是一项智力事业。它是你自己内在的深度清算，清算你知之甚少的你内在更伟大的部分，清算你内在内识的伟大智慧之井，你或许只是在这里或那里短暂地体验到它，或者[当]你感到它的力量在某些事情上约束你或在其他事情上强迫你时。

This is the beginning of the New God experience. But you must come to terms with your life. You cannot be traveling in many directions at once. You cannot be pulled and swayed by others or by the dictates of culture and religion if you are to have this greater freedom We speak of, if you are finally to be able to experience God’s Presence and Power in your life, moving you through the Knowledge that God has put within you, and seeing this Knowledge in others and responding to it within them, and forgiving people everywhere who have not yet found this greater power to guide them.

这是新上帝体验的开始。但你必须接受你的生命。你不能同时朝多个方向前进。如果你想拥有我们所说的这种更伟大的自由，如果你想最终能够在你的生命中体验上帝的存在和力量，通过上帝赋予你内在的内识来推动你，在他人身上看到这种内识并在他们身上做出回应，原谅世界各地那些还没有找到这种更伟大力量来指导他们的人，你就不能被他人或文化和宗教的命令所牵引和左右。

For in this world and in life, there is only Knowledge and the need for Knowledge. There is the experience of God and the direction of God, and then there is everything else.

因为在这个世界和生命中，只有内识和对内识的需求。有上帝的体验和上帝的指引，然后才是其他一切。

Here your mind becomes free. It breaks the chains of obligation. It breaks the chains of submission to worldly powers and forces. Here your mind begins to expand, now able to consider greater things, now able to feel compassion for yourself and others as you learn to see life clearly as it truly is and not merely as what it seems to be.

在这里，你的心灵变得自由。它打破了义务的枷锁。它打破了屈从于世俗权力和势力的枷锁。在这里，你的心灵开始扩展，现在能够考虑更伟大的事情，现在能够对你自己和他人产生同情心，因为你学会了清楚地看到生命的真实面目，而不仅仅是它的表面。

God has put this power within you. And in the world today God is calling for this power through a greater Revelation for the world. For men and women of good will everywhere must rise to serve humanity at this great turning point, facing a world of declining resources and growing environmental disruption, facing a world of greater contention between nations and groups [over] who will have access to the remaining resources of the world—a time of great danger, but also a time of great opportunity.

上帝把这种力量置于你内在。在当今世界，上帝正通过对世界的更伟大启示召唤这种力量。因为在这个伟大的转折点上，世界各地善良的男女必须挺身而出服务人类，面对一个资源不断减少、环境破坏日益加剧的世界，面对一个国家和群体之间为谁将获得世界剩余资源而发生更大争执的世界——这是一个巨大危险的时代，但也是一个伟大机遇的时代。

For God has spoken again now to bring the pure Revelation to people everywhere, to provide the Steps to Knowledge and the means for having the New God experience, this new experience of God.

因为上帝现在再次讲话，把纯粹的启示带给世界各地的人们，提供内识进阶和获得新上帝体验的方法，这种上帝的新体验。

This is going forwards and backwards all at once. You go forward because you reach further than others have ever gone. But you go back because you go back to what is essential within you—what is permanent, what is true, what is unchanging, what is beyond the control and manipulation of culture, politics and religion.

这既是前进，又是后退。你前进是因为你比别人走得更远。但你后退是因为你回到了你内在的本质——永恒的、真实的、不变的、超越文化、政治和宗教控制和操纵的。

Here religion becomes a living reality within you. Here you are able to embrace your tradition, if you have one. It becomes alive with the reality of the new experience of God.

在这里，宗教成为你内心的一个活生生的现实。在这里，如果你有传统的话，你可以接受它。它随着对上帝的新体验而变得鲜活起来。

This is how God revitalizes and restores and renews the great Messages that have been sent into the world, the great building blocks of human civilization, the heart and the soul of human reality, meaning and purpose in this world. This revitalizes what is true. This sets aside what is unnecessary or untrue. This is outgrowing strict and vehement belief, for you have a greater security now, a greater experience to guide your life.

这就是上帝如何振兴、重建和更新那些被发送到这个世界的伟大讯息、人类文明的伟大基石、人类现实的核心和灵魂、这个世界的意义和目的。这振兴了真实。这抛弃了不必要或不真实。这超越了严格和激烈的信仰，因为你现在拥有了一种更伟大的安全感、一种指导你生命的更伟大的体验。

This is what We speak of here today. It has everything to do with who you are and why you are in the world. It has everything to do with what can finally unite humanity so that it can face the great hazard and the great challenge from within the world and from beyond.

这就是我们今天在此讲述的内容。它关系到你是谁以及你为何身处这个世界。它关系到什么能够最终团结人类，以便它能够面对来自世界内部和外部的巨大危险和伟大挑战。

This is why God has spoken again and sent a New Messenger into the world—not to create another separate religion but to revitalize everything that has been given before to bring the New God experience here, which everyone is searching for in their pursuits, in their romances, in their quests for power or wealth. In essence, they are searching for their reconnection with God and their greater purpose for being here. But they are searching where it cannot be found. They are traveling down empty pathways with no resolution. They are looking elsewhere while God is standing behind them and alongside them.

正因为如此，上帝再次讲话，并派遣一位新信使来到这个世界——不是为了创造另一个独立的宗教，而是为了复兴过去被赋予的一切，以将新的上帝体验带到这里，这是每个人在他们的追求、在他们的爱情、在他们的权力或财富追求中寻找的。本质上，他们在寻找他们与上帝的重新联接以及他们身在此地的更伟大目的。但他们在寻找无法找到的东西。他们在没有决心的空虚道路上前行。他们在向别处看，而上帝就站在他们身后、与他们并肩而立。

Come back. Be still. Stop your vain pursuits. Hold yourself back from where everyone else seems to be going and you will have a chance to experience this power, this presence, this inner resonance. Do not define it, for you cannot define it. Do not make a belief out of it, for it is greater than belief. Do not use it to judge others, for it is here to bless others. Do not use it to condemn yourself and your errors in the past, for it is here to restore you, to strengthen you and to give you what you have sought in all other things.

回来吧。保持安静。停止你徒劳的追求。远离其他人似乎都在去的地方，你将有机会体验这种力量、这种存在、这种内在的共鸣。不要定义它，因为你无法定义它。不要把它当成一种信仰，因为它比信仰更伟大。不要用它来评判他人，因为它是为了祝福他人而存在的。不要用它来谴责你自己和你过去的错误，因为它是为了恢复你、增强你并给你你在所有其他事情中寻求的东西。

We bring the Revelation, We who watch over this world. You do not know Our names, for We are the many speaking as One and the One speaking as many—a reality you cannot consider living in Separation in this world. We are the One to translate the Will of God into words, into practices, into actions, into clarification. With the Power of Creation, We bring this to you.

我们带来启示，我们守护着这个世界。你不知道我们的名字，因为我们是合众为一进行讲话的，而一以众进行讲话——这是你无法想象的在这个世界里生活在分离中的现实。我们是将上帝意志转化为语言、实践、行动和澄清的存在。凭借创造的力量，我们将其带给你。

Do not be afraid to step beyond your ideas and beliefs, for there is freedom there. Do not be afraid to go beyond what you have been taught, for there is freedom and reprieve there. Do not be afraid to explore what lives within you, for beyond the shadows of fear, doubt and regret there is the presence of Knowledge.

不要害怕超越你的想法和信仰，因为那里有自由。不要害怕超越你所学的东西，因为那里有自由和解脱。不要害怕探索你内心的东西，因为在恐惧、怀疑和遗憾的阴影之外有内识的存在。

God calls to you for this. God always calls for this. This is the return to relationship with your Source through the power and presence of Knowledge that God has placed within you. For only Knowledge can hold this relationship. Ideologically, it is too great. It is too expansive.

上帝呼唤你这样做。上帝总是呼唤你这样做。这是通过上帝放置在你内心的内识的力量和存在，​​回归与你本源的关系。因为只有内识才能维持这种关系。从意识形态上讲，它太伟大了。它太膨胀了。

For your God is also the God of countless races in the universe, so different from you, in galaxies upon galaxies, in dimensions beyond them. What religion can contain this? What religion can explain this? What religion can define such a thing? It is not possible. It is arrogant. It is a hopeless quest. And it leads to repression for people, and subjugation.

因为你们的上帝也是宇宙中无数种族的上帝，这些种族与你们如此不同，他们存在于一个又一个星系，存在于比它们更远的维度。什么宗教可以包含这一点？什么宗教可以解释这一点？什么宗教可以定义这样的事情？它是不可能的。它是傲慢。它是无望的追求。它会导致对人民的压迫和征服。

The world cannot afford this now. It cannot afford ceaseless conflict, or humanity will waste away the remaining resources of the world, leaving you in a situation far more desperate and tragic than anything that has ever occurred here before.

世界现在无法承受这样的结果。它无法承受无休止的冲突，否则人类将浪费掉世界剩余的资源，让你们陷入一种比这里过去发生过的任何事都更加绝望和悲惨的境地。

This great need, this great danger, this great threat must be understood, or you will not see why God has spoken again. And you will not see the great need for you to have this new experience of the Divine Power and Presence. For that is the pure religion, you see, practiced by people within religion and beyond religion, practiced throughout the universe by those very different from you, practiced universally because God has one Plan for the whole universe, not for this one little world alone.

你必须理解这个伟大的需求、这个巨大的危险、这个巨大的威胁，否则你就不会看到上帝为何再次讲话。你也不会看到你对神圣力量和存在的这种新体验的迫切需求。因为这是纯粹的宗教，你看，它被宗教内部和宗教之外的人们所实践，被整个宇宙中与你截然不同的生命所实践，被普遍地实践，因为上帝对整个宇宙有一个计划，而不仅仅是对这个小世界。

You can see here in contrast how different this is from what preoccupies people and what religions use to admonish others and to corral their own followers. This is religion made by man, not by God. This is religion used for human purposes and ambitions. It is not the Will of God.

对比之下，你可以看出这与人们所关注的以及宗教用来劝诫他人和约束信徒的宗教有多么不同。这是人为的宗教，不是上帝创造的宗教。这是为人类的目的和野心而使用的宗教。它不是上帝的意志。

God’s Will is that humanity rise above its divisions to help to protect and restore the world and to protect it from intervention from the universe around you, which threatens you at this moment, a reality that few people in the world are aware of at all.

上帝的意志是人类超越分歧，帮助保护和重建世界，并保护它免受周围宇宙的干预，这种干预此刻正威胁着你们，但世界上很少有人察觉到这个现实。

It is like a crew fighting on a ship, fighting with itself, while the ship is taking on water and beginning to list to one side. But the crew keeps fighting over who will have control, who will be captain, who will be crew, who will be the leader, who will be the follower.

它就像船员在船上打架，船在进水，开始向一侧倾斜，船员们却在与自己斗争。但船员们仍在争斗，争夺谁来掌舵，谁来当船长，谁来当船员，谁来当领导者，谁来当追随者。

This ship is taking on water. It is in danger. Human civilization is taking on water. It is in danger. It is fragmenting already. Nations are breaking down. Whole populations of people will be moving in the future as their lands dry out or become uninhabitable, a reality never seen in this world before at this scale.

这艘船正在进水。它处于危险之中。人类文明正在进水。它处于危险之中。它已经四分五裂。国家正在瓦解。未来，随着土地干涸或变得无法居住，整个人口将被迫迁移，这是世界上从未见过的如此大规模的现实。

Where will the power come from? Where will the inspiration come from? Where will the inner strength and integrity come from to face such a world and to serve it in such a way that human unity can grow and human freedom can be preserved and expanded?

力量从何而来？灵感从何而来？面对这样的世界，并为之服务，使人类团结得以发展，人类自由得以维护和扩展，内在的力量和完整性从何而来？

It must come from this well that We speak of. It must come from Knowledge within the individual. It must come from God, who wills this for humanity. For this must happen now, for the alternative is far too grave to even speak of. It is driven by need. It is also driven by courage and honesty.

它必须来自我们所说的这口井。它必须来自个人内在的内识。它必须来自上帝，上帝愿意为人类做到这一点。因为这必须现在发生，因为另一种选择太过严重，甚至不值得一提。它是由需求驱动的。它也是由勇气和诚实驱动的。

In your heart, you will know this to be true, but you must be free enough of the confines of your thinking to see this and to know this, to feel this and to begin to take the Steps to Knowledge that will lead you back to what is true and timeless within yourself.

在你的内心深处，你会知道这是真的，但你必须足够自由地摆脱你思维的局限，才能看到这一点，知道这一点，感受这一点，并开始开展内识进阶，这将引领你回归你内心的真实与永恒。

At that level, there is no confusion. At that level, there is no condemnation. There is only the service to be rendered. There is compassion for the world, but also strength and determination to carry out what must be done.

在内识的层面上，没有困惑。在内识的层面上，没有谴责。只有需要提供的服务。对世界有同情心，也有力量和决心去完成必须做的事情。

It [Knowledge] is the core of your strength. It is the core of your power. It is the core of your inspiration. It is leading you back to the life you were meant to live. For only this can lead you there. Belief alone in a great saint or prophet cannot lead you there. You must return to what God has put within you to follow. This is the New God experience.

它（内识）是你能力的核心。它是你力量的核心。它是你灵感的核心。它正在引导你回到你本该过的生命。因为只有它才能带你到那里。单靠信仰一位伟大的圣人或先知无法带你到那里。你必须回到上帝赋予你内在的内识。这就是新的上帝体验。
