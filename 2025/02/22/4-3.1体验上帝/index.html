第一章：体验上帝

发表于2025-02-22|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷新的上帝体验
|总字数:10 k|阅读时长:42 分钟

Experiencing God

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on February 5, 2009 in Boulder, Colorado
2009 年 2 月 5 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

People want many things from God. People want inspiration and protection. They want advantages. They want miracles. They want to be saved from the outcome of their own errors or the consequences of other people’s errors.

人们想从上帝这里得到许多东西。人们想要灵感和保护。他们想要优势。他们想要奇迹。他们想要从他们自身犯错的结果或他人犯错的后果中得到拯救。

They want God to provide so many things for them without asking anything in return. They do not want their lives to be directed or impacted or restrained. But they want God to do so many things for them, as if God were a kind of servant, an errand boy for the shifting desires of the individual.

他们希望上帝为他们提供许多事物而不要求任何回报。他们不希望他们的生命受到指导、影响或约束。但他们希望上帝为他们做许多事情，好像上帝是一位仆人，一位为个人不断变化的欲望服务的差役。

People want God to stop suffering and war when in fact the way that they live and how they regard others is promoting war. They want God to intervene, but only at their request and only according to their desires and dictates. And when God seems to disappoint, well, their belief is shattered, or their disbelief is reinforced.

人们希望上帝停止苦难和战争，但事实上他们的生活方式和对待他人的方式却在助长战争。他们希望上帝干预，但只能应他们的要求，按照他们的愿望和指示。当上帝似乎令人失望时，他们的信仰就会破灭，或者他们的不信就会得到加强。

They blame God for all the mistakes that people make regarding religion and religious teaching. They blame God for leading people astray. Or they claim that God does not exist at all, and it is all made up by people.

他们把人们在宗教和宗教教导方面犯下的所有错误都归咎于上帝。他们指责上帝将人们引入歧途。或者他们声称上帝根本不存在，一切都是人类编造出来的。

So it is not an environment where the Divine can really communicate effectively and freely to the individual. That is why God has put Knowledge within you—a deeper Intelligence, an Intelligence that is not guided or corrupted by the world, an Intelligence that you cannot control or manipulate or use as a kind of resource for yourself.

因此这不是一个神圣能够真正有效和自由地与个体沟通的环境。正因为如此上帝把内识放置在你的内在——一种更深层次的智能，一种不受世界引导或腐蚀的智能，一种你无法控制或操控或用作你自己的一种资源的智能。

People have a very dysfunctional relationship with God. They fear God, if they believe in God at all. But they want many things, and they have great expectations. They want God to validate their religious beliefs and religious ideology. They want God to fulfill their prophecies: the return of their messenger or their saint. They want God to condemn and crush their opponents and those whom they condemn. They want God to create Hell so this punishment can be exacted for an eternity. They want God to be like the descriptions of God in the ancient books.

人们与上帝的关系非常不正常。他们敬畏上帝，如果他们真的相信上帝的话。但他们想要许多事物，并且抱有很大的期望。他们希望上帝证实他们的宗教信仰和宗教意识形态。他们希望上帝实现他们的预言：他们的信使或圣徒的回归。他们希望上帝谴责和粉碎他们的对手和他们谴责的人。他们希望上帝创造地狱，这样这种惩罚就可以永远实施。他们希望上帝就像古书中对上帝的描述一样。

Or they want God to be a kind of modern person. Or they want God to be an old man or a saintly woman. Or they want God to be beyond all these things. But still, they claim they know God’s Will and can define God’s intentions.

或者他们希望上帝是一种现代人。或者他们希望上帝是一位老人或一位圣洁的女人。或者他们希望上帝超越所有这些。但他们仍然声称他们知道上帝的意志，并能定义上帝的意图。

Here God becomes everything you want and everything you do not want. You project your fears and your desires, your judgments and your admonitions upon God. This is not relationship here. It is fantasy and projection.

在这里，上帝成为你想要的一切和你不想要的一切。你把你的恐惧和欲望、你的判断和你的劝告投射到上帝身上。这不是关系。它是幻想和投射。

What is your relationship with God? And how can God be known in your life? These are valuable questions, but they can only be approached if you come forward without such expectations and demands. This can only be approached if you understand the confusion and the mistakes that people make and continue to make regarding their understanding of the Divine.

你与上帝的关系如何？你如何在生命中知道上帝？这些都是有价值的问题，但只有当你不抱有这些期望和要求时，你才能接近这些问题。只有当你理解人们对上帝的理解所犯的和继续犯的困惑和错误时，你才能接近这些问题。

You have to come forward understanding your own intentions. Why are you seeking this relationship? What do you expect that it will provide for you? What are your secret wishes? What do you want to hide from God? What is it you do not want God to know about you or your past behaviors? Are you coming forward with the desire to be in relationship alone, or are there other incentives that are operating here?

你必须理解你自身的意图。你为什么要寻求这种关系？你期望它能为你提供什么？你的秘密愿望是什么？你想对上帝隐瞒什么？你不想让上帝知道你或你过去的行为的什么？你是带着只想建立关系的愿望走上前来，还是有其他动机在起作用？

Your approach is important, and it is important that you know your intentions. For if you do not know your intentions, you will not understand the nature of your engagement and the disappointment of your expectations; and why God will not give you what you think you want or deserve; how your desires and real needs can be fulfilled; and how God will assist you in this matter.

你的方法是重要的，知道你的意图也是重要的。因为如果你不知道你的意图，你就不会理解你参与的自然和你期望的失望；以及为什么上帝不给你你认为你想要或应得的东西；你的渴望和真正的需求如何得到满足；以及上帝将如何在这件事上帮助你。

People seek power in God because they do not feel they have any power. Perhaps they have no social or economic power, and so they seek for the supernatural. They want an intervention of supernatural powers to give them social power and to provide them protection against the world that they cannot affect.

人们寻求上帝的力量，因为他们感觉到他们没有任何力量。也许他们没有社会或经济力量，所以他们寻求超自然的力量。他们希望超自然力量的干预能赋予他们社会力量，并保护他们免受他们无法影响的世界的影响。

So your approach is important. And being clear and honest about your intentions is very important. Without this, you will not understand your engagement. God will seem to be unavailable or non-existent or unresponsive to your requests. If you come only as a petitioner or as a manipulator, then God may not become apparent to you in your experience.

因此，你的方法是重要的。明确而诚实地表达你的意图也是重要的。没有这一点，你就不会理解你的参与。上帝的意志似乎无法满足你的要求，或者根本不存在，或者对你的请求没有反应。如果你只是以请求者或操纵者的身份前来，那么上帝可能不会在你的经历中显现出来。

This is a problem for the intellect, for it is filled with needs and fears. And if it has become clever and manipulating, perhaps it will not even understand its own deceptions.

这对智力来说是个问题，因为它充满了需求和恐惧。如果它变得聪明而善于操纵，也许它甚至无法理解它自身的欺骗行为。

It is really a matter for the heart, you see. For it is a deeper urgency that brings you to this relationship beyond what you think it will do for you. It is like a natural attraction, a gravitational pull, you could say, bringing you to this relationship.

你看，这真的是一件关乎内心的事情。因为正是这种更深层次的紧迫感，让你进入这种关系，超出了你想象它能为你带来的好处。它就像一种自然的吸引力，可以说是一种引力，把你带入这种关系。

Where you meet God is in the deeper Intelligence that God has placed within you, an Intelligence called Knowledge. Aside from visions or supernatural events or other kinds of things that are often not authentic, this is really where you meet God.

你与上帝相遇的地方是上帝赋予你的更深层次的智能，即内识。除了幻象、超自然事件或其他通常不真实的事情之外，这才是你真正与上帝相遇的地方。

God does not control or command your intellect. That is dominated mostly by your culture, your family and your friends. That is your worldly mind that is so controlled by the world itself.

上帝不会控制或指挥你的智力。你的智力主要受你的文化、你的家庭和你的朋友支配。你的世俗心灵受到世界本身的控制。

But your deeper Mind is not controlled by the world. It is connected to God. It is the arena where you communicate with God and discern the influences that God has put within you to guide you, to protect you and to lead you to your greater accomplishments in life.

但是你更深层次的心灵不受世界的控制。它与上帝联接。它是你与上帝沟通并辨识上帝赋予你内在的影响力的舞台，这些影响力是为了指引你、保护你并引领你实现生命中的更伟大成就。

So, clearly, taking the Steps to Knowledge and learning how to connect your thinking mind with this deeper Mind are really the way that you will begin to discern a sense of real direction for you. For God is not going to give you a lot of answers to your endless questions. God is not going to have a conversation with you. God is not going to be your friend that you chatter with all day long. God is not going to help you figure out your daily routines and all of the particulars of your life, for that is your responsibility.

因此，很明显，开展内识的进阶，学习如何将你的思维心灵与这个更深层次的心灵连接起来，才是你开始辨别出真正方向感的真正方式。因为上帝不会给你无数问题的答案。上帝不会和你交谈。上帝不会成为你整天喋喋不休的朋友。上帝不会帮你弄清楚你的日常生活和你生命中的所有细节，因为这是你的责任。

God sets a direction and restrains you and encourages you so that you can find this direction and follow it. Knowledge within you holds you back and then sets you forward. Knowledge within you is not drawn to the fantasies that you might have for yourself, and so it shows no response here. And that is a clue and an indicator for you.

上帝设定了一个方向，约束你，鼓励你，这样你就能找到这个方向并遵循它。你内在的内识会阻止你，然后让你前进。你内在的内识不会被你可能对你自己的幻想所吸引，所以它在这里没有回应。这对你来说是一个线索和一个指标。

Here you read the signs and the clues of a deeper movement of your life because your relationship with God is not a conversation. It is not a dialogue. It is sharing a deeper movement, a greater direction.

在这里，你读到了你生命中更深层次运动的迹象和线索，因为你与上帝的关系不是交谈。它不是对话。它是在分享一种更深层次的运动，一种更伟大的方向。

Here you learn to allow Knowledge to redirect your life, to re-establish your true priorities, to counsel you what to give yourself to and what not to give yourself to, who to be with and who not to be with, and what is the appropriate form of a relationship with those with whom you share a greater purpose.

在这里，你学习让内识重新引导你的生命，重新确立你真正的优先事项，建议你把你自己交给什么和不交给什么，该和谁在一起和不该和谁在一起，以及与那些与你有着共同的更伟大目的的人建立什么样的关系才是合适的。

Here you let Knowledge shape your understanding through experience, through constant engagement and experience with Knowledge. Here God is not a bunch of ideas or teachings from the past or even predictions by ancient prophets. It is a living experience within yourself.

在这里，你让内识通过体验、通过对内识的不断参与和体验来塑造你的理解。在这里，上帝不是一堆来自过去的想法或教导，甚至不是古代先知的预言。它是一种生活在你自己内心的体验。

So the question is: “How to be with God?” If God is not a conversation, if God is not an ideology, if God is not simply one who dispenses gifts, how to be with God?

因此，问题是：“如何与上帝同在？”如果上帝不是一种对话，如果上帝不是一种意识形态，如果上帝不仅仅是一个施予礼物的人，那么如何与上帝同在呢？

To be with God, you must follow Knowledge because this is the Intelligence that God has placed within you. Only it knows where you really need to go and how you will find your way. This is beyond the realm and the reach of the intellect.

要与上帝同在，你必须遵循内识，因为这是上帝放置在你内在的智能。只有它知道你真正需要去哪里，以及你将如何找到你的道路。这超出了智力的领域和范围。

Here the intellect must follow. It cannot lead. But the intellect is very important in helping you to discern the particulars of your life. It has a very significant function in helping you to discern particulars of your life, to manage your affairs, to deal with complex issues, to deal with technology, to deal with time, to deal with engagements with others. But it still must have the guidance of Knowledge to really be productive and constructive for you.

在这里，智力必须跟随。它无法领导。但是智力在帮助你辨别你生命的细节方面非常重要。它在帮助你辨别你生命的细节、管理你的事务、处理复杂问题、处理技术、处理时间、处理与他人的交往方面发挥着非常重要的作用。但是它仍然必须有内识的指导才能真正对你富有成效和建设性。

This is so very important to understand because here you do not even have to believe in God, you do not have to have a religion. You can connect with Knowledge whether you are religious or not, whether you believe in a particular religious pathway or not. This is a door that is open to everyone.

理解这一点非常重要，因为在这里你甚至不必相信上帝，你不必有宗教信仰。无论你是否信教，无论你是否相信某种特定的宗教道路，你都可以与内识连接。这是一扇向所有人敞开的大门。

But for everyone it requires sincerity, honesty and true intention. For God will not respond to your false requests, and your hidden agendas, and your dishonest pleas. If you want to be clever, you will receive nothing but silence. If you want to be shrewd, you will receive nothing but silence.

但对每个人来说，它都需要真诚、诚实和真正的意图。因为上帝不会回应你的虚假请求、隐藏的议程和不诚实的恳求。如果你想变得聪明，你得到的只有沉默。如果你想变得精明，你得到的只有沉默。

You cannot fool the Creator of the universe, and you cannot deceive the Knowledge that God has placed within you with clever designs or trick questions, or trying to set a trap to prove God’s existence. Clearly, you would not try to enter a real relationship with a person with these kinds of tricks and manipulations, not if you were really sincere and honest.

你无法欺骗宇宙的创造者，你也无法用巧妙的设计或诡计的问题，或试图设下陷阱来证明上帝的存在来欺骗上帝赋予你的内识。显然，如果你真的真诚和诚实，你不会试图用这些诡计和操纵来与一个人建立真正的关系。

There is always an element of trust, of course. But in this case it is really about trusting something deeper within yourself rather than in a foreign power, or an incomprehensible deity, or in some kind of personification of God that was constructed in antiquity. It is about adhering to a greater power within you.

当然，信任总是有因素的。但在这种情况下，它实际上是信任你自己内心更深层次的内识，而不是信任外来的力量、不可理解的神灵或古代塑造的某种神化形象。它意味着信奉你内心一种更伟大的力量。

Even if you are not sure that God exists, you will be connecting with God and following God’s Pathway if you can find and follow Knowledge within yourself, and build the patience and the discernment and the discretion that this will require of you.

即使你不确定上帝是否存在，如果你能找到并遵循你内在的内识，并构建这要求你的耐心、辨识力和判断力，那么你将能够与上帝联接，并遵循上帝的道路。

You can search the world over for God, but God is right inside. The Intelligence that God has placed within you is right inside. What is going to reunite you with God in reality and in a way that is really honest and truthful is right inside of you. And it is inside of others as well, for Knowledge will speak to you through yourself and through others.

你可以走遍全世界寻找上帝，但上帝就在你的内心。上帝放置在你内心的智能就在你的内心。让你在现实中、以一种真正诚实和真实的方式与上帝重新团聚的内识就在你的内心。它也在其他人的内心，因为内识将通过你自己和他人与你对话。

For Knowledge is here to end your Separation, to end the nightmare of your Separation, the hopelessness of your Separation, the unreality of your Separation. And Knowledge is going to do this by reconnecting you with Knowledge itself, reconnecting you with others, reconnecting you with the world—connecting you in such a way that your real gifts and contribution can come forth, given the real needs of life around you.

因为内识在这里是为了结束你的分离，结束你分离的噩梦、你分离的绝望、你分离的不现实。内识将通过重新连接你与内识本身、重新连接你和他人、重新连接你和世界来做到这一点——以一种让你的真正礼物和贡献能够出现的方式连接你，考虑到你周围生命的真正需求。

This is not about praise and worship; it is about participation and contribution. This is not about building temples and prostrating yourself, for if you cannot follow what God has placed within you to follow, then none of this will mean anything at all. It is only an expression of your ideas and your arrogant views. And whatever you create from this will have no inspiration in it. Your temples will be empty. The Spirit will not be there.

这不是赞美和崇拜，而是参与和贡献。它不是建造寺庙和俯首称臣，因为如果你不能遵循上帝赋予你的内识，那么这一切都没有任何意义。这只是你的想法和傲慢观点的表达。无论你从中创造出什么，都不会有灵感。你的寺庙将是空的。精神将不复存在。

But those who build temples because that is what they are driven to do, they will have Spirit and Presence there. Those who give in the simplest way to others, take care of the person in need, contribute to the community in the simplest and most mundane fashion, there will be inspiration and Presence.

但那些因为有动力而建造寺庙的人，他们将在那里拥有精神和存在感。那些以最简单的方式给予他人、照顾有需要的人、以最简单、最平凡的方式为社区做出贡献的人，将会有灵感和存在感。

You come to know God by doing what God has given you to do and by doing this to the best of your ability; and learning along the way what this really means, what it requires of you, and what it reveals to you.

通过做上帝赋予你的事情并尽你所能地去做，你就会知道上帝；并且在此过程中学习这件事的真正含义、它对你的要求以及它向你揭示的内识。

People can argue incessantly, competing with their different religious ideologies and beliefs. But if they are following Knowledge, they will not have any real issues of contention with one another. That is why Knowledge is the great peacemaker in the world. You may fight wars over ideas, but Knowledge will never engender conflict.

人们可以无休止地争论，用不同的宗教意识形态和信仰相互竞争。但是，如果他们遵循内识，他们就不会有任何真正的争执。这就是为什么内识是世界上伟大的和平缔造者。你可能会为想法而战，但内识永远不会引发冲突。

This is going to require a lot of work and focus on your part. It is not a casual pursuit. It is not something you do once in a while. You do not have a casual relationship with God, for you were sent into the world to contribute certain things in certain situations with certain people. A mission that has brought you into the world is not a casual relationship. It is not a simple friendship. It is not simply you go to God once in a while when you need guidance or relief. That is not a relationship.

这需要你付出大量的努力和专注。它不是一种随意的追求。它不是偶尔为之的事情。你与上帝之间的关系不是随意的，因为你被派到这个世界上是为了在特定情况下向特定的人贡献特定的礼物。将你带到这个世界上的使命并不是一种随意的关系。它不是简单的友谊。它不是简单地在你需要指引或解脱时偶尔去找上帝。这不是一种关系。

So when you decide to get serious about your life, begin to recognize you are really here to do something important, and that your current affairs do not reflect this, then you will come to this relationship with greater and more sincere intentions—not to have simply one contact but to have an ongoing engagement.

因此，当你决定认真对待你的生命，开始认识到你真正来这里是为了做一些重要的事情，而你当前的事情并没有反映这一点，那么你就会以更伟大、更真诚的意图来对待这段关系——不是简单的一次联系，而是持续不断地参与。

This engagement will purify your intentions and refine your understanding because God is not going to give you everything you think you want. Here you are yielding and following something, and using your skills and your intelligence to learn how to discern what is real from what is unreal, what is good from what only looks good.

这种参与将净化你的意图，完善你的理解，因为上帝不会给你你想要的一切。在这里，你屈服并追随某些事物，并运用你的技能和智慧来学习如何辨别什么是真实的，什么是不真实的，什么是好的，什么是仅仅看起来好的。

This will lead you to reassess your own intentions and values and goals. And you must be willing to go where Knowledge wants to take you, or you will fight and struggle and will not understand the real nature of this engagement.

这将引领你重新评估你自身的意图、价值观和目标。你必须愿意去往内识想要带你去的地方，否则你将斗争和挣扎，无法理解这个参与的真正自然。

This relationship will grow over time because it is so immense. At first, it will seem an adjunct to your other relationships, as if God were some kind of distant counselor. But over time this relationship will become more fundamental and more central to your life and your efforts.

这种关系会随着时间的推移而增长，因为它是如此巨大。起初，它似乎只是你其他关系的附属品，好像上帝是某种遥远的顾问。但随着时间的推移，这种关系将变得更加根本，成为你生命和你努力的中心。

Here instead of just giving you what you want, God gives you things to do—constructive activities, things that will renew your life, things that will get you out of trouble and break your attachment to dangerous or unproductive relationships.

在这里，上帝不仅仅给你你想要的东西，还给你一些事情去做——一些建设性的活动，一些能让你焕然一新的事情，一些能让你摆脱困境并打破你对危险或无益关系的依恋的事情。

Because Knowledge is moving you in a direction, you will have to follow that direction. To do that will begin to clarify your life and give you the freedom to follow a greater power that dwells within you.

因为内识引导你朝着一个方向前进，所以你必须遵循那个方向。这样做将开始澄清你的生命，并赋予你自由去追随你内在的更伟大力量。

Beyond the freedom to eat and to sleep and to live with basic security, this is the great freedom: to be inner directed rather than outer directed—not by your ambitions, your addictions, or your desires, but by the power and presence of Knowledge within you.

除了吃饭、睡觉和享受基本安全生活的自由之外，这是伟大的自由：由内指导而非外部的指导——不是由你的野心、你的上瘾或你的欲望引导，而是由你内在内识的力量和存在引导。

You see, God guides you without dominating you, without conquering you, without taking you over because this is a natural attraction. This is an attraction of love and affinity.

你看，上帝引导你，但不会控制你、征服你、控制你，因为这是一种自然的吸引力。这是一种爱和亲和力的吸引力。

You know God because God can only be known. This is not a product of belief. This is greater than belief. This is affinity. It is profound experience.

你知道上帝，因为上帝只能被知道。这不是信仰的产物。这比信仰更伟大。这是亲和力。它是深刻的体验。

And you will not have this experience very often, you see, because God wants to keep you in the world, contributing here. If you experience the ecstasy of your connection with God too frequently, well, you would not want to be in the world dealing with its difficulties and its challenges.

你不会经常有这种体验，因为上帝想让你留在这个世界上，在这里做出贡献。如果你太频繁地体验到与上帝联接的狂喜，那么，你就不会想在这个世界上应对它的困难和它的挑战了。

So God limits the engagement to keep you in the world, focused on the world and on what you must do today and tomorrow and the days to come. Seeking to lose yourself in God, well, that would defeat your very purpose for coming into the world, you see.

因此，上帝限制你的参与，是为了让你留在这个世界上，专注于这个世界以及你今天、明天和未来的日子里必须做的事情。如果你想在上帝那里迷失你自己，好吧，你看，你来到这个世界的目的就会落空。

If you could understand how much work it took to bring you here, how much had to be arranged, how much effort has been placed in your path to bring you here and to attempt to guide you, despite your grievances and your ambitions and everything else that is pulling on you in the world, you would really begin to appreciate your importance here and the value of your existence.

如果你能够理解，为了把你带到这里，需要付出多少努力，需要做多少安排，需要付出多少努力才能把你带到这里并试图引导你，尽管你心中有不满，有野心，有世界上其他一切在拖累你的事物，那么你将真正开始欣赏你在此的重要性和你存在的价值。

So God does not come and conquer you and enslave you and dominate you harshly. God draws you naturally, inexplicably, drawing you back, reconnecting you with Knowledge within yourself, reconnecting you with other people who will play a part in your life—giving you eyes of compassion and understanding of the world, giving you the strength to overcome anger and retribution, giving you tolerance but also strength and the power to endure.

因此上帝不会来征服你、奴役你和严厉地主宰你。上帝自然而然地、不可解释地吸引着你，把你拉回来，重新联接你与你内在的内识，重新联接你和将在你的生命中扮演角色的其他人——赋予你同情和理解世界的眼睛，赋予你力量去克服愤怒和报复，赋予你宽容，也赋予你力量和坚忍的力量。

Here you see beyond ideology alone to look for the power and the presence of Knowledge in others. Here you look to see if a religious leader is really religious or is only a believer in a set of ideas.

在这里，你超越意识形态，去寻找他人身上内识的力量和存在。在这里，你去观察一位宗教领袖是否真的有宗教信仰，还是只是一套想法的信徒。

Here you look with a different kind of vision, responding to Knowledge within yourself and Knowledge in others. Here you represent God in the world without even having to mention God.

在这里，你带着一种不同的视野去看，回应你自己内在的内识和他人内在的内识。在这里，你代表着世界上的上帝，甚至无需提及上帝。

Here you demonstrate a Greater Reality by following a greater reality within yourself. Here you meet the real needs of the world around you rather than trying to fulfill yourself by having everything you want.

在这里，你通过追随你自己内在的更伟大现实来展示一种更伟大现实。在这里，你满足周围世界的真正需求，而不是试图通过拥有你想要的一切来满足你自己。

Here you find relief from the constant aggravations of the mind—relief from fear; relief from anxiety; relief from the dreadful anticipation of loss; fear from need and attachment; freedom from the need for approval from others—so many things that people wear as a crown of thorns.

在这里，你可以摆脱心灵的持续烦恼，摆脱恐惧，摆脱焦虑，摆脱可怕的失落感，摆脱需求和依恋的恐惧，摆脱对他人认可的需求——人们把如此多的事情戴在荆棘的冠冕上。

This is a natural engagement. It is not artificial. It is not ideological. It is not political. It is not militant or military.

这是一次自然的接触。它不是人为的。它不是意识形态的。它不是政治的。它不是好战的或军事的。

You come to know God by what God leads you to do and to give and to understand. Then you know God in such a way that you can be in the world and be focused here.

通过上帝引导你去做、去给予和去理解的事情，你逐渐知道上帝。然后你以这样一种方式知道上帝，你就可以活在这个世界，并专注于此。

Here you become a vehicle, the tip of the pen that writes on the world. Here you become the person who moves others and assists others and helps to preserve and renew the natural world that has fallen under destruction and depravation by human greed and ignorance. Here you are not governed by the consensus of people or by political persuasions or by religious extremism or by economic principles.

在这里，你成为一个载体，成为书写世界的笔尖。在这里，你成为一个推动他人、帮助他人、帮助保护和更新因人类贪婪和无知而遭到破坏和堕落的自然世界的人。在这里，你不受人们的共识、政治信仰、宗教极端主义或经济原则的支配。

You have a greater strength now, a greater adherence within yourself. And you know God slowly here because your focus is to realign your life with Knowledge to provide a new direction for you—a direction that was always there waiting for you, which you now finally have the freedom to discover and to follow.

你现在拥有一种更伟大的力量，你自己的内心拥有一种更伟大的坚持。你在此慢慢地知道上帝，因为你的焦点是重新调整你的生命与内识，为你提供一个新的方向——一个一直在那里等待着你的方向，现在你终于有自由去发现和遵循它。

Here you find freedom from the thousand seductions and deceptions that exist all around you and that circumscribe the lives of so many people. Here you are not caught up in the dreadful struggle within yourself over what you want and what you must do to have what you want. This is such a great freedom, you see. And what replaces this is the power and the presence of Knowledge.

在这里，你会摆脱周围存在的、限制许多人生命的成千上万的诱惑和欺骗。在这里，你不会陷入内心关于你想要什么以及你必须做什么才能得到你想要的东西的可怕斗争中。你看，这是一种多么伟大的自由。取而代之的是内识的力量和存在。

Here you begin to understand the great challenges and the great contributions of God’s Messengers. Here you see that life is really about something else that is different from what most people believe or adhere to.

在这里，你开始理解上帝信使的伟大挑战和伟大贡献。在这里，你看到生命其实是与大多数人所相信或坚持的生命不同的生命。

Here you are prepared for Heaven because you are re-establishing your life within the context of real relationships with others, with yourself and with the world.

在这里，你为天国进行了准备，因为你正在与他人、与你自己、与世界的真正关系背景里重建你的生命。

For Heaven is a state of profound relationship. But you cannot enter this state if you cannot be in relationship with anyone or with yourself successfully. For how could you return to a state of total relationship when you cannot even be in relationship with one person successfully here?

因为天国是一种深厚关系的状态。但是如果你不能成功地与任何人或与你自己建立关系，你就无法进入这种状态。因为如果你在这里甚至不能成功地与一个人建立关系，你怎么能回到完全关系的状态呢？

So you see here how the world is a training ground, a preparation, not only to contribute to this time and this place, not only to be inspired and to inspire the lives of others, but also to prepare you for a greater existence beyond the world.

因此，你在这里看到，世界是一个训练场、一个准备场，不仅是为了给这个时间和这个地方做出贡献，不仅是为了受到启发和激励他人的生命，也是为了让你为超越世界的更伟大的存在做好准备。

Here there is no Judgment Day. You are either ready or you are not to move into a greater existence. If you are not, you will have to continue to work at this level of existence until you can gain the ability to overcome your own prejudices and your own bondage to find the freedom to experience and to follow Knowledge.

这里没有审判日。你要么已经准备好，要么还没有准备好进入更伟大的存在。如果你还没有准备好，你将不得不继续在这个存在层面上工作，直到你能够获得克服你自身偏见和束缚的能力，找到体验和遵循内识的自由。

God is not interested in your ideology. God is not interested in self-righteousness. God is not interested in those who claim to have the only way of redemption. God is not interested in those who think they know how to harness humanity and to govern it according to a set of principles alone. God is not interested in assertions of personal power and wealth. God is not moved by beauty and charm or cleverness.

上帝对你的意识形态不感兴趣。上帝对自以为是的人不感兴趣。上帝对那些声称拥有唯一救赎之道的人不感兴趣。上帝对那些自以为知道如何驾驭人类并仅根据一套原则来治理人类的人不感兴趣。上帝对个人权力和财富的宣称不感兴趣。上帝不会被美貌、魅力或聪明所感动。

But God does respond to honesty and sincerity and the expression of true needs. And Knowledge becomes activated within the individual when this great relationship is really sought and the person is prepared to follow, instead of trying to control and dominate.

但上帝确实回应诚实和真诚以及真正需求的表达。当人们真正寻求这种伟大的关系，并准备好追随它，而不是试图控制和支配时，内识就会在个体的内心被激活。

This has nothing to do with asceticism or monasticism, for those roles are only assigned to very rare and unique individuals. For the Plan of God has to do with people being in the world serving one another and taking care of the world itself.

这与禁欲主义或修道主义无关，因为这些角色只分配给非常罕见和独特的个人。因为上帝的计划与人们在世界上互相服务并照顾世界本身有关。

There is so much to unlearn that it could be said that your progress is really a [process] of shedding, unlearning and setting aside things that before seemed to dominate you and your perception of the world.

有太多的事情需要忘却，可以说，你的进步实际上是一种摆脱、忘却和搁置那些之前似乎主宰你和你对世界的看法的事物的过程。

God frees you so that you can follow a greater direction and gain a greater wisdom and discernment in life. This is how you know God because here you are doing what God has sent you to do. You are following your deepest and most natural inclinations. You are fulfilling the need of the soul.

上帝解放你，是为了让你能够遵循更伟大的方向，在生命中获得一种更伟大的智慧和辨别力。这就是你知道上帝的方式，因为你在这里做上帝派你去做的事情。你在追随你最深沉、最自然的倾向。你在满足灵魂的需求。

You will not know God through fervent spiritual practice or through trying to become a perfect person according to your parameters and criteria. It instead is the more natural progression that re-engages you with life and people in the world.

你不会通过热切的精神练习或试图按照你的参数和标准成为一个完美的人来知道上帝。相反，它是让你重新融入生命并与世人交往的更自然的过程。

This is the natural way. This is the real way. This is the way that all the great saints have emphasized. This is the way of the heart. This is the Path of Knowledge.

这是自然之道。这是真正的道路。这是所有伟大圣人强调的道路。这是心之道。这是内识之路。

Take away ritual and miracles, the pageantry of religion, and what do you have? You have the individual’s choice to follow Knowledge or not, to communicate what Knowledge has to communicate or not, the natural attraction of God or all the other attractions in the world.

除去宗教仪式、奇迹和盛典，你还剩下什么？个人可以选择追随内识或不追随内识，选择传达内识要传达的内容或不传达内识要传达的内容，选择上帝的自然吸引力或世界上所有其他的吸引力。

Knowledge will teach you to what degree you must give up these other attractions. But it will not rob you of the simple pleasures of being in the world: the joy of fellowship, wonderful food, the experience of nature, the appreciation of art. Knowledge delights in giving you these things, but it will take you away from that which is destructive or disorienting, or is deceiving to you.

内识会教导你，你必须在多大程度上放弃这些其他的吸引力。但它不会剥夺你存在这个世界上的简单乐趣：友谊的喜悦、美味的食物、大自然的体验、对艺术的欣赏。内识乐于给你这些事物，但它会带你远离那些对你有破坏性、迷惑性或欺骗性的事物。

You want to know God because you want to know who you are. And who you are is a contributor sent to the world from God. But to know this, you must follow the Intelligence that God has placed within you, for only it knows the way, and only it can help you to discern what is real from what is unreal, what is correct for you and what is not correct.

你想知道上帝，因为你想知道你是谁。而你是谁，是上帝派给这个世界的一位贡献者。但要知道这一点，你必须遵循上帝放置在你内在的智能，因为只有它知道道路，只有它能帮助你辨别什么是真实的，什么是不真实的，什么对你来说是正确的，什么是不正确的。

It is only Knowledge that will free you from condemnation of others, to give you eyes to see, ears to hear and the desire to know the true direction that you were sent here to follow and to contribute.

唯有内识才能让你摆脱对他人的谴责，让你有眼睛去看、有耳朵去听，让你渴望知道你被派到这里去遵循和贡献的真正方向。
