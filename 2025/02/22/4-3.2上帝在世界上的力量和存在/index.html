第二章：上帝在世界上的力量和存在

发表于2025-02-22|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷新的上帝体验
|总字数:6.6 k|阅读时长:27 分钟

God’s Power and Presence in the World

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on April 23, 2011 in Boulder, Colorado
2011 年 4 月 23 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

Recognition must be based upon a deeper experience, recognition of a greater truth, recognition of a New Revelation. This recognition occurs at a deeper level of the mind, beyond the realm of the intellect, beyond the realm of ideas and beliefs, the things that people attach themselves to regarding the Divine Presence and Power in the world.

认知必须基于一种更深层次的体验，对一种更伟大真理的认知，对新启示的认知。这种认知发生在心灵更深层次的层面上，超越智力的领域，超越想法和信仰的领域，超越人们对世界上神圣存在和力量的执着。

This Power is so formidable, but it functions through the individual. It works from the inside out. This is how God works in the world.

这种力量是如此强大，但它通过个人运作。它从内而外发挥作用。这就是上帝在世界上的工作方式。

People want grandiose demonstrations, but the Presence operates differently. It does not make a great show of itself. It speaks to the inner world of the person and reaches from person to person in this way.

人们想要宏伟的示威，但存在却以不同的方式运作。它不会大张旗鼓地炫耀它自己。它向人的内心世界说话，并以这种方式在人与人之间传递信息。

Here the experience of recognition is not based upon the fervency of one’s belief, for people are forever arguing over beliefs and can never really unite at this level.

在这里，认知的体验并不是基于一个人信仰的热烈程度，因为人们永远在争论信仰，永远无法在这个层面上真正团结起来。

The resonance is happening beyond ideas and formulations of ideas. This is where true commitment emerges. This is where real courage emerges. This is where compassion and the experience of Grace emerges.

共鸣超越了想法和想法的表述。真正的承诺由此产生。真正的勇气由此产生。同情心和恩典的体验由此产生。

God’s Power and Presence in the world is demonstrated through selfless actions, through recognition of one person to another, through selfless giving and the commitment to give, the commitment to serve, the commitment to contribute—beyond one’s own edification, and certainly beyond one’s own enrichment.

上帝在世界上的力量和存在是通过无私的行为、通过一个人对另一个人的认知、通过无私的奉献和奉献的承诺、服务的承诺、贡献的承诺来体现的——超越了一个人自身的教化，当然也超越了一个人自身的丰富。

Where does this incentive come from? What is the nature of true goodness? It all comes from the Mystery, the Power and Presence of God in the world.

这种激励来自哪里？真正的善的本质是什么？这一切都来自于上帝在世间的神秘、力量和存在。

People want demonstrations. They want miracles because they are not having the experience. This is all the striving of the mind—the surface mind, the worldly mind. “Prove to me that God is real! Give me a miracle! Give me what I want! Solve my problems!”

人们想要演示。他们想要奇迹，因为他们没有这种体验。这都是心灵的挣扎——表面的心灵，世俗的心灵。“向我证明上帝是真实的！给我一个奇迹！给我我想要的！解决我的问题！”

People think like this because they are not having the true experience, and if they have had the true experience, they are not being patient. They are not recognizing that the encounter with God is intermittent and not continuous. For it is not allowed for you to have too much experience of the Divine, or you will never be willing to stay in the world and provide your service here.

人们这样想是因为他们没有真正的体验，即使他们有真正的体验，他们也没有耐心。他们没有认识到与上帝的相遇是间歇性的，而不是连续的。因为你不允许有太多的神圣体验，否则你永远不会愿意留在这个世界上并在这里提供服务。

You cannot run away to God, for you were sent into the world to serve a world in need. That is why the Presence is not a form of intoxication. It is not a grand escape. It is not ascending far beyond, never to return. Why would God call you back to Heaven when great effort was made to bring you into the world?

你无法逃避上帝，因为你被派到这个世界来服务一个需要帮助的世界。这就是为什么存在不是一种陶醉。它不是一场盛大的逃亡。它不是远走高飞，一去不复返。既然上帝费尽千辛万苦把你带到这个世界，为什么还要把你召回天堂呢？

People want many things, but if they are not having the deeper experience, they are only dealing with their ideas. They are debating their ideas. They are arguing over their ideas. They are trying to understand the ineffable with their ideas. They are trying to comprehend with their ideas. That is like asking your hand to understand your mind. That is like asking a child to understand the world.

人们想要许多事物，但如果他们没有更深层次的体验，他们就只能处理他们的想法。他们在辩论他们的想法。他们在争论他们的想法。他们试图用他们的想法去理解不可言喻的内识。他们试图用他们的想法去领悟。这就像要求你的手去理解你的心灵。这就像要求一个孩子去理解这个世界。

The Power and Presence of God in the world is far beyond human estimation and human evaluation.

上帝在世界上的力量和存在远远超出了人类的估计和人类的评价。

True faith and true adherence comes from a deeper place, from the very soul, the very heart of you, the center of your Being. Here you are not separated from the Creator, but you are separated from your deeper nature. And it is to bridge this gap, to take the Steps to Knowledge, that represents the focus of all true spiritual practice and religious endeavor.

真正的信仰和真正的坚持来自更深的地方，来自灵魂深处，来自你的内心深处，来自你存在的中心。在这里，你并没有与造物主分离，而是与你更深层次的自然分离。而弥合这个差距，开展内识的进阶，代表了所有真正的精神实践和宗教事业的重点。

People want many things, but most of all they need the deeper experience, the true connection. For this meets the need of the soul and begins to re-establish true integrity and balance within yourself.

人们想要许多事物，但最重要的是他们需要更深层次的体验，真正的联接。因为这满足了灵魂的需求，并开始重建你自己内心真正的完整性和平衡。

The Work of God is everywhere, but who can see it? People are obsessing over human error and human tragedy, constantly complaining about the world and venting their anger and frustration upon others. God is with them, but they are looking in another direction. The Presence is here, but people are too busy doing other things to recognize it and respond to it.

上帝的工作无处不在，但谁能看到上帝？人们沉迷于人类的错误和人类的悲剧，不断抱怨世界，将他们的愤怒和沮丧发泄在他人身上。上帝与他们同在，但他们却看向另一个方向。存在就在这里，但人们太忙于做其他事情而无法识别它并做出回应。

God is giving you what you need, not what you want. It is not enough simply to try to satisfy your basic desires, however essential they may be. The connection must happen at a deeper level.

上帝给予的是你需要的，而不是你想要的。仅仅满足你的基本欲望是不够的，无论这些欲望多么重要。这种联接必须在更深层次的层面上发生。

If you are hungry and starving, then you must meet that need, and that will predominate. But even here, the Presence is available. In fact, under these more dire circumstances, you may seek it with greater fervency and adhere to it with a greater dedication. Here the poor are often more rich in Spirit than the rich themselves, who have a great competing agenda differing from their primary purpose and function of being in the world.

如果你感到饥饿，那么你必须满足这种需求，而这将占主导地位。但即使在这里，存在也是可用的。事实上，在这些更可怕的情况下，你可以更热切地寻求它，并以更伟大的奉献精神坚持它。在这里，穷人往往比富人更富有精神，富人有一个与他们存在于世界上的主要目的和功能不同的重大竞争议程。

God’s Power and Presence in the world is so strong that it can arouse a dedication, a commitment and an integrity far greater than anything else could possibly evoke. This can transform a person’s life, elevating them to a greater level of service and clarity, making them a true light shining in a darkening world. The power of force, the power of wealth, the attractions of beauty and charm ultimately cannot compete or compare with this.

上帝的力量和存在在世界上是如此强大，它能够激发一种奉献、一种承诺和一种完整性，远胜于任何其他事物所能唤起的。这可以改变一个人的生命，将他们提升到更高的服务和清晰度水平，使他们成为黑暗世界中真正的光芒。武力的力量、财富的力量、美貌和魅力的吸引力最终都无法与之竞争或相比。

People think they understand the Will of the Creator, but unless they are connected to the deeper Knowledge and Intelligence that God has placed within them, then they are just following their ideas.

人们认为他们理解造物主的意志，但是除非他们与上帝放置在他们内在的更深层次的内识和智能相联接，否则他们只是在遵循他们的想法。

People are killing each other over their ideas. People are demonstrating cruelty over their ideas. This is not where you engage with the Divine. Your ideas will fall into place once you are having a deeper experience. But you must allow the definitions to be open, for once you begin to make contact with the power of Knowledge within yourself that God has placed there to protect you and to direct you, then your life will begin a process of reformation.

人们为了他们的想法而互相残杀。人们为了他们的想法而表现出残忍。这不是你与神圣接触的地方。一旦你有了更深层次的体验，你的想法就会落到实处。但你必须让定义保持开放，因为一旦你开始接触你自己内在的内识力量——上帝把内识放置在你的内心来保护你、指引你——你的生命就会开始一个改革的过程。

How can you possibly make conclusions and sweeping ideas when your life is undergoing a fundamental reformation? If you are true, you will stand aside and allow this to occur instead of trying to control it for your own benefit or to fortify and substantiate your beliefs or preferences.

当你的生命正在经历一场根本性的变革时，你怎么可能做出结论和全面的想法呢？如果你是真诚的，你会站在一边，让这一切发生，而不是为了你自身的利益或为了巩固和证实你的信仰或偏好而试图控制它。

God has spoken again. The Messenger is in the world. It is a great test for humanity as to who can see and who can respond.

上帝已经再次讲话。信使来到这个世界。对于人类来说，谁能看见、谁能回应，它是一次伟大的考验。

Here you will see what is really important. It will not be intellectual brilliance. It will not be eloquence or beauty or charm or the force of personality, for people who possess these things may be the last to see and the last to know. They are dressed up in the obsessions of the world, and these things will not enable them to see. It happens at a deeper level.

在这里，你将看到什么才是真正重要的。它不会是智力上的才华。它不会是口才或美貌或魅力或人格力量，因为拥有这些东西的人可能是最后看到和最后知道的人。他们被世界的痴迷所笼罩，这些东西无法让他们看到。这发生在一个更深层次的层面上。

This is the great test. This is where you see at the time of Revelation who can respond, and the nature of their response, and the purity and clarity of their response. In the Light of the Creator, all deception is revealed and demonstrated. People’s true intentions become evident. It is like the lightning flash on the darkened landscape. For a moment, everything is revealed.

这是一次伟大的考验。在启示之时，你会看到谁能做出回应，以及他们回应的自然，以及他们回应的纯洁性和清晰度。在造物主之光中，所有的欺骗都会被揭露和展示。人们的真正意图变得显而易见。就像黑暗大地上的一道闪电。一瞬间，一切都被显露出来。

You will see in the face of the Revelation who is available to the Power and the Presence of God. Who can respond? Who has the humility, who has the courage to reassess their former ideas and beliefs? Who is willing to undergo the process of reformation that is necessary to take them to a greater and more honest and complete experience of life and expression of purpose?

你将面对启示，看到谁能面对上帝的力量和存在。谁能回应？谁拥有谦逊，谁有勇气重新评估他们先前的想法和信仰？谁愿意经历必要的改革过程，以带领他们走向一种更伟大、更诚实、更完整的生命体验和目的表达？

People will judge the New Message. But the New Message is revealing their intentions. It is saying everything about them. Their success or failure in responding says everything about them, and the degree to which they have come to terms with their own suffering, and the limits of their ideas, and their inability to discern their true direction in life, and the weakness of their beliefs, and the degree to which they have compromised themselves to gain advantage and approval with others.

人们将评判新讯息。但新讯息揭示了他们的意图。它道出了他们的一切。他们回应的成功或失败道出了他们的一切，道出了他们面对他们自身苦难的程度，他们想法的局限，他们无法辨别他们生命真正方向的能力，他们信仰的软弱，以及他们为获得他人利益和认可而自我妥协的程度。

If God speaks again, who can respond? If God shows things that have never been shown before, who is willing to see this? If God defies religious belief and expectations, who has the courage and the humility to be prepared to receive this? If God calls you to a higher service and position in life, who is willing to undergo this process of change, a process they cannot control and cannot really even understand? Who is willing and able to do this? This is the true test of readiness and integrity, honesty and openness.

如果上帝再次讲话，谁能回应？如果上帝展现了从未展现过的事情，谁愿意看到它？如果上帝藐视宗教信仰和期望，谁有勇气和谦逊去准备接受它？如果上帝召唤你去承担生命中更高的服务和位置，谁愿意经历这个改变的过程，一个他们无法控制甚至无法真正理解的过程？谁愿意并能够做到这一点？这是对准备和完整性、诚实和开放的真正考验。

The New Message is entirely honest, and it asks you to be entirely honest, a level of honesty that most people do not even consider: Being true to oneself, being responsive to one’s deeper nature, to look beyond one’s fears and beliefs and preferences, to listen for a greater voice, for the power of Knowledge that God has placed within you. Knowledge will respond, of course, because it is responding to its Source.

新讯息是完全诚实的，它要求你完全诚实，诚实到大多数人甚至都没有考虑过的程度：忠于自己，回应自己更深层次的自然，超越自己的恐惧、信仰和偏好，倾听更伟大的声音，倾听上帝放置在你内在的内识力量。内识当然会回应，因为它回应的是它的本源。

Not everyone is ready to receive the New Revelation. God knows this, and there is no condemnation here. But it is the falsity of people’s position and assumptions about themselves that will be revealed in this process. Those who have intellectual pride, those who think they are intelligent and clever, those who think they can discern everything, those who think they know the truth, those who think they know what honesty is—this is the hypocrisy that will be revealed in the Light of God’s Power and Presence in the world.

并非每个人都准备好接收新启示。上帝知道这一点，这里没有谴责。但正是人们的立场和对他们自己的假设的虚假性将在此过程中显露出来。那些拥有智力傲慢的人，那些认为他们聪明机灵的人，那些认为他们可以辨别一切的人，那些认为他们知道真理的人，那些认为他们知道什么是诚实的人——这些都是伪善，将在上帝在世界上的力量和存在之光中显露出来。

At every great time of Revelation, spread out intermittently over the course of human history, how many have failed to recognize the significance of the event that was taking place in their lives, at that time? And if they had the great opportunity to witness or to recognize the Messenger of that time and era, did they just see another person or did they recognize the power of the Messenger and the Grace that accompanies the Messenger?

在人类历史进程中断断续续出现的每一个伟大的启示时代，有多少人未能认识到当时发生在他们生命中的事件的意义？如果他们有伟大的机会见证或认出那个时期和时代的信使，他们只是看到了另一个人，还是他们认识到了信使的力量和伴随信使的恩典？

People want the Messenger to be a super person—powerful, magnificent, immaculate, utterly pure, well behaved, in accordance with human expectation. But the Messenger is a human being and is always fallible. But it is the Message, and the Presence that accompanies the Messenger, that makes all the difference.

人们希望信使是一位超人——强大、伟大、完美无瑕、绝对纯洁、行为端正，符合人类的期望。但信使是人，总是会犯错。但正是讯息和伴随信使的存在，才产生了所有的不同。

Jesus made mistakes. Muhammad made mistakes. The Buddha made mistakes. All the great leaders that followed them, and all the great teachers in other traditions, they all made mistakes. To live in Separation is to make mistakes. To enter a world of Separation means you will make mistakes. But if the Messengers deliver the Message and do not elevate themselves into a godlike position, then they have achieved their goal.

耶稣犯过错误。穆罕默德犯过错误。佛陀也犯过错误。所有追随他们的伟大领袖，以及所有其它传统的伟大导师，都犯过错误。生活在分离中就是会犯错误。进入分离的世界意味着你会犯错误。但如果信使们传递讯息，而不把他们自己提升到神一般的地位，那么他们就实现了他们的目标。

The test is before you now. The New Revelation is before you. It has crossed your path. What are you going to do with it? How are you going to respond? What are you going to do? The greatest event of your life—are you going to be present for this? Or is this just another event amongst other events to which you cannot really respond?

考验现在就在你的面前。新启示就在你的面前。它出现在你的面前。你打算如何处理它？你打算如何回应？你打算做什么？你生命中最伟大的事件——你会出席吗？或者这只是你无法真正回应的众多事件中的一个而已？

It is a challenge. It is a fair challenge. It is an honest challenge. And it is more than a challenge. It is a gift and a calling and an opportunity for you. The challenge is just part of the engagement. It is not as if God is taking pleasure in challenging you. It is just part of the opportunity to engage with the Source of your life, as it presents itself in a pure and uncorrupted form.

它是一个挑战。它是一个公平的挑战。它是一个诚实的挑战。而且它不仅仅是一个挑战。它是你的一份礼物、一个召唤和一个机会。挑战只是参与的一部分。上帝并不是以挑战你为乐。它只是与你生命源泉参与的机会的一部分，因为它以一种纯粹和未受腐化的形式呈现着它自己。

How will you see the Messenger? Can you feel this? Can you be still?

你将如何看待信使？你能感受到这一点吗？你能保持静止吗？

Can you set aside reactions and judgments and take the time, however long that requires, to consider this individual and the great burden he carries and the great challenge before him? That is a fair question. It is a great opportunity for you and for others. And if you are truly honest with yourself, you would at least say, “Well, I do not know, and I am not sure” if you are not sure.

你能抛开反应和评判，花时间——无论需要多长时间——去思考这个人，思考他肩负的伟大负担和他面临的伟大挑战吗？这是一个合理的问题。这对你和其他人来说都是一个伟大的机会。如果你真正对你自己诚实，如果你不确定，你至少会说：“好吧，我不知道，我不确定。”

At this time of Revelation, God’s Power and Presence reasserts itself in the world—a New Revelation revealing the great change that is coming to the world, and humanity’s destiny in a Greater Community of life in the universe, and the great challenges that will require and place upon the entire human family.

在这个启示的时间，上帝的力量和存在在世界上重申——一个新的启示，揭示了即将降临世界的伟大变革，人类在宇宙生命大社区中的天命，以及这将要求并施加于整个人类大家庭的伟大挑战。

People will debate this. They will contend with this. They will argue against this. They will deny this. But it is all a failure to respond. They are trying to respond, but they are not using the proper facilities and faculties of mind to do this. They are being impacted, but they are still lost in their ideas and in the machinations of the mind when the engagement is happening at a whole other level.

人们将对此进行辩论。他们将对此进行争论。他们将对此进行反驳。他们将否认这一点。但这一切都是回应的失败。他们在努力回应，但他们没有使用适当的设施和思维能力来做到这一点。他们受到了影响，但当参与发生在一个完全不同的层面时，他们依然迷失在他们的想法和心灵的阴谋诡计中。

If you cannot engage with God, how are you going to really engage with anyone else? If you cannot experience affinity with the Power and the Presence of God, how are you going to experience affinity with anyone else?

如果你不能与上帝交往，你怎么能真正与他人交往？如果你不能体验与上帝的力量和存在之间的亲和力，你怎么能体验与他人之间的亲和力？

You may become intoxicated, falling in love temporarily with someone. You may be delighted with another’s presence, but will you be able to experience true affinity with them? If you do not trust God, how will you trust anyone else? If you are not humbled before God, how can you be humbled before anyone else?

你可能会陶醉，暂时爱上某人。你可能会为另一个人的存在而高兴，但你能体验到与他们真正的亲和力吗？如果你不信任上帝，你怎么能信任他人？如果你在上帝面前都不谦逊，你怎么能在别人面前谦逊呢？

That is why the New Message has been brought to the people and not to the leaders, who have elevated themselves and concealed themselves and fortified themselves and isolated themselves. They are circumscribed by how they have created their position in life. It will be more difficult for them even though the New Message is for them as well.

正因为如此，新讯息被带给了民众，而不是领导者，他们抬高了他们自己，隐藏了他们自己，强化了他们自己，孤立了他们自己。领导者被他们在生命中创造的位置所限制。尽管新讯息也是为他们准备的，但对他们来说，这将更加困难。

Stand in judgment of the New Message and you will not see. You will not hear. And you will not know. You will be deaf and blind and arrogant all at once. The Heavenly Host will shake their heads: “It is so unfortunate. That person could not respond. They could do so much for the world. They are lost in their idea of themselves. They cannot see beyond their previous investment and their position, and their accomplishments and acquisitions.”

站在新讯息的审判者的角度，你将看不见。你将听不到。你将不会知道。你将同时变得又聋又瞎又傲慢。天使之众将摇头：“它是如此的不幸。这个人无法回应。他们可以为世界做许多事情。他们迷失在他们自己的想法中。他们看不到他们之前的投资和他们的位置，以及他们的成就和收获。”

God is seeking to empower the human family through the New Revelation— to empower the individual, who is the source of freedom in the world; to give people greater vision, clarity and responsibility. This is the source of human progress in the world.

上帝正寻求通过新启示赋予人类大家庭力量——赋予个人力量，因为个人是世界自由的源泉；赋予人们更伟大的视野、更清晰的认识和责任感。这是人类在世界上进步的源泉。

It is not [that] you will be given a grand new theology to argue about and to debate and to comment on forever and ever. You are being prepared for a new world, and for the rigors and the dangers of entering into the Greater Community itself. You are being prepared for the challenges of great change coming to the world, greater than you can yet realize or anticipate. You are being prepared for a greater life, with greater strength and integrity. But you must submit and receive and learn to become truly honest with yourself about your deeper intentions and your greater responsibilities in being here.

它并不是说你将得到一个宏大的新神学，去永远争论、辩论和评论。你正在为一个新的世界做准备，为进入大社区本身的严酷和危险做准备。你正在为即将降临世界的伟大变革的挑战做准备，这些挑战比你能够意识到或预期的还要大。你正在为一种更伟大的生命做准备，带着更伟大的力量和完整性。但你必须屈服和接受，并学会真正坦诚地面对你自己，面对你在这里的更深层次的意图和更伟大责任。

The Power and the Presence of God will remind you of these things and will call you out of your former understanding, your former predicaments, your unsolvable problems, your compromised position, your weakness regarding your relationship with others. It will call you forth, for it is the Power and the Presence of God in the world.

上帝的力量和存在将提醒你这些事情，并将召唤你走出你以前的理解、你以前的困境、你无法解决的问题、你妥协的立场、你与他人关系中的软弱。它将召唤你，因为它是上帝在世界上的力量和存在。
