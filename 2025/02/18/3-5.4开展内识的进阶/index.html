第四章：开展内识的进阶

发表于2025-02-18|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第三卷深化你的精神实践
|总字数:19.6 k|阅读时长:82 分钟

Taking the Steps to Knowledge

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on April 21, 2000 in Boulder, Colorado
2000 年 4 月 21 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

We would like to speak about learning and living The Way of Knowledge as a fundamental spiritual focus and practice and application in life. To begin with, let Us speak about what Knowledge is and why it is so important for you.

我们想谈谈学习和践行内识之路，将其作为生命中的一种基本精神焦点、精神实践和精神应用。首先，让我们谈谈内识是什么，以及它对你如此重要的原因。

Knowledge represents the deeper spiritual Mind within you, the Mind that knows. This deeper Mind does not think like your personal mind, or your surface mind, thinks. This deeper Mind does not deliberate. It does not compare. It does not speculate, does not fantasize, does not engage in most of the activities that your intellect is involved in.

内识代表着你内在更深层次的精神心灵，即知道的心灵。这种更深层次心灵的思考方式与你的个人心灵或表面的心灵不同。这种更深层次的心灵不会深思熟虑。它不会比较。它不会推测、不会幻想、不会参与你的智力所涉及的大多数活动。

Instead, this deeper Mind is really here on a mission. It is here because it is here to do something important for the world. And it will emerge, possibly, later in your life when you have developed yourself as a person and have gained some degree of independence and independent thinking. When this occurs, there is a chance that this deeper Mind, this Mind We call Knowledge, may begin to assert itself and to emerge within your experience.

相反，这种更深层次的心灵其实是肩负着使命而存在的。它之所以存在，是因为它要为世界做一些重要的事情。它可能会在你成长为一个人并获得一定程度的独立性和独立思考能力之后的生命中出现。当这种情况发生时，这个更深层次的心灵，即我们称之为内识的心灵，就有可能开始发挥它自己的作用，并出现在你的体验中。

The Mind of Knowledge is like no other mind. You could think about it endlessly, but your intellect can only imagine another intellect. You could speculate about what Knowledge is like, but really your speculations would still be based upon your intellect, which can only project images that it can conceive of, images of itself.

内识的心灵与其他的心灵不同。你可以无休止地思考它，但你的智力只能想象另一个智力。你可以推测内识是什么样的，但实际上你的推测仍然基于你的智力，它只能投射它能想到的图像，即它自己的图像。

This is why the deeper Mind of Knowledge seems so mysterious. It is beyond definition. You can describe its manifestations. You can describe any direct experience you may have with it, but really it is so mysterious.

这就是为什么更深层次的内识心灵看起来如此神秘。它无法定义。你可以描述它的表现。你可以描述你对它的任何直接体验，但它真的非常神秘。

Some people say: “Well, this is all the Christ Consciousness” or “This is Buddhahood” or “This is Universal Mind.” And there is a great deal of speculation on what this Mind is really like, how it functions, where it comes from and so forth. But these are all speculations from the intellect.

有些人会说：“好吧，这都是基督意识”或“这是佛性”或“这是宇宙之心”。人们对这种心灵到底是什么样的、它如何运作、它从何而来等等有很多猜测。但这些都是来自智力的猜测。

They cannot encompass the mysterious nature of Knowledge within you. Nor can they describe really how Knowledge works, how it functions, how it manifests itself, why it is here, and what it is here to serve.

它们无法涵盖你内在内识的神秘自然。它们也无法真正描述内识如何工作、如何运作、如何显现、为何存在以及它在此服务于什么。

Therefore, for you, you must allow these things to remain somewhat mysterious. It is only through direct experience with Knowledge itself that you will be able to learn what Knowledge is, how Knowledge functions, and why it is within you.

因此，对你来说，你必须允许这些事情保持某种神秘感。只有通过直接体验内识本身，你才能了解内识是什么、内识如何运作以及它为何存在于你的内在。

However, there are some very general things that We can say about Knowledge that kind of gives it a context and helps you to understand really what it is within you, and perhaps can enable you to prevent making certain mistakes in the way that you think of Knowledge or in your practice and study of The Way of Knowledge.

然而，我们可以就内识说一些非常普遍的事情，这些事情为内识提供了一个背景，并帮助你真正理解内识在你内心是什么，也许可以帮助你避免在你思考内识的方式上、在你实践和学习内识之路的方式上犯某些错误。

Knowledge, in essence, is really God within you. God has given you part of God’s consciousness. It is within you. This consciousness is not like your intellect, however. It is not constantly thinking, deliberating, planning, remembering, imagining, and so forth.

从本质上讲，内识其实就是你心中的上帝。上帝赋予了你上帝意识的一部分。它就在你的内心。然而，这种意识与你的智力不同。它不会不断地思考、斟酌、计划、记忆、想象等等。

It is really more an awareness, a state of mind. It is connected to everything in the universe. Your little piece of God contains your connection with God. It is to assure that while you are living in the world in a state of seeming Separation, you cannot really be apart from God.

它实际上更像是一种觉知，一种心灵状态。它与宇宙中的万事万物息息相关。你那一小块上帝之物包含着你与上帝的联接。它确保当你生活在看似分离的世界中时，你无法真正与上帝分离。

So Knowledge really is your God connection. This deeper part of your mind is your God connection. You cannot really connect with God through your intellect because your intellect was not created by God. It was created by you and by your environment. What was created by God is Knowledge within you, this deeper Mind that We are describing.

因此，内识实际上是你与上帝的联接。你心灵的这个更深层次的部分就是你与上帝的联接。你无法通过你的智力真正与上帝连接，因为你的智力不是上帝创造的。它是由你和你的环境创造的。上帝创造的是你内在的内识，也就是我们所描述的这个更深层次的心灵。

So for now, be content to realize Knowledge is your connection to God, it is how you can experience God most directly, and it is the part of you that really has come into the world for a mission.

因此现在，要满足于认识到内识是你与上帝的联接，它是你最直接体验上帝的方式，它是你真正为了使命而来到这个世界的部分。

In other words, Knowledge contains the memory of your Creator, the memory of your purpose for being here, and the blueprint for your mission in life. There really is a greater activity that you are here to accomplish.

换句话说，内识包含着你的造物主的记忆、你来到这里的目的的记忆，以及你生命使命的蓝图。你来到这里确实是为了完成一项更伟大的活动。

Now there are some people who say: “Oh, I know what my mission is. It is to do this or to do that.” But this is not yet Knowledge. Perhaps they are moving in the right direction. Perhaps they have learned step one or step two of the many steps that they will have to take, but your mission is not simply a definition. And only if you are very advanced in taking these steps can you say with any real certainty and honesty: “This is my mission.”

现在有些人会说：“哦，我知道我的使命是什么。就是做这个或做那个。”但这还不是内识。也许他们正朝着正确的方向前进。也许他们已经学会了他们必须采取的许多步骤中的第一步或第二步，但你的使命不仅仅是一个定义。只有当你在采取这些步骤方面非常先进时，你才能真正确定和诚实地说：“这是我的使命。”

So at the outset, do not claim what your mission is because you really do not have it yet. Even if you are moving in the right direction, you do not have it yet. You will not have your mission until you are firmly established within it; until you can experience what it is on a regular basis; until it directs your life, your activity, your relationships, your understanding, and so forth.

所以一开始，不要声称你的使命是什么，因为你实际上还没有。即使你正朝着正确的方向前进，你也还没有得到它。除非你坚定地立足于你的使命；除非你能定期体验它是什么；除非它指导你的生命、你的活动、你的关系、你的理解等等，否则你不会拥有你的使命。

Knowledge knows. Knowledge within you really knows why you are here and what you must do. It really knows who you must meet and where you must go. And yet Knowledge is dynamic. It is not simply having a pre-recognition of things. It is moving in dynamic relationship with the movement of the world.

内识知道。你内在的内识确实知道你为何在此以及你必须做什么。它确实知道你必须遇见谁以及你必须去哪里。然而内识是动态的。它不仅仅是对事物有预先认知。它与世界的运动在动态的关系中移动。

So if things change, which they are, if circumstances require a different set of actions, well, Knowledge responds immediately. So it is not like you have a plan that is already pre-described. It must constantly be adapting itself to the changing circumstances of life around you.

因此，如果事情发生变化（事实确实如此），如果情况需要采取不同的行动，内识就会立即做出回应。因此，这并不是说你有一个预先描述好的计划。它必须不断适应你周围不断变化的生命环境。

This is why static definitions are inherently incorrect because they cannot adapt. People try to adapt their experiences to their definitions of themselves, their definitions of what they are doing in life, but this is backwards.

这就是为什么静态定义本质上是不正确的，因为静态定义无法适应。人们试图让他们的体验适应他们对他们自己的定义，适应他们对他们在生命中所做的事情的定义，但这是本末倒置的。

Knowledge is able to respond in the moment. It is able to change the plan. It is able to redirect you if circumstances require it. That is why the man or woman of Knowledge must learn to live without a lot of personal definitions. “Well, this is who I am, and this is what I do, and this is how I am, and this is what I believe, and this is my belief system, and this is what I think the world means and life means and purpose means and love means, and so forth.”

内识能够即时作出回应。它能够改变计划。如果情况需要，它能够引导你。这就是为什么内识的男人或女人必须学会在没有太多个人定义的情况下生活。“好吧，这就是我，这就是我所做的，这就是我的样子，这就是我的信仰，这是我的信仰体系，这就是我认为的世界意义、生命意义、目的意义和爱的意义，等等。”

People who are bound by all these definitions cannot move with life. They try to make life move according to their prescriptions, but they cannot move and flow with life. They are bound now by their own ideas and ideologies.

被所有这些定义束缚的人无法与生命同行。他们试图让生命按照他们的规定前进，但他们无法与生命一起前进和流动。他们现在被他们自身的想法和意识形态所束缚。

Therefore, in learning The Way of Knowledge, you learn how to be present in the moment, how to be responsive to your environment, how to follow Knowledge, and to learn to think like Knowledge thinks, and to gain the great awareness of Knowledge, the great power of Knowledge, and the grace of Knowledge because remember, Knowledge is your relationship to God. It is the God within you.

因此，在学习内识之路时，你将学会如何活在当下，如何对周围环境做出反应，如何追随内识，学会像内识那样思考，获得对内识的伟大觉知、内识的伟大力量和内识的恩典，因为记住，内识是你与上帝的关系。它是你内在的上帝。

So each person has this mysterious deeper Intelligence within them. Now obviously most people are not aware of this or connected to it sufficiently that it can really affect their thinking and their behavior. So it is really more like a potential even though Knowledge is really there.

因此，每个人内心都有这种神秘的更深层次的智能。显然，大多数人没有察觉到这一点，也没有充分地与它联接，以至于它无法真正影响他们的思维和行为。所以，尽管内识确实存在，但它实际上更像是一种潜力。

You see, no matter how isolated anyone may feel they are in life, which is really the source of all human suffering, no matter how separate or cast out anyone may think they are, or feel that they are, they are still connected to God. They are connected through Knowledge. So it could be said, then, that taking the Steps to Knowledge is like taking the Steps to God—the God within you and the God within everyone else.

你看，无论人们在生命中感到多么孤立，这其实是所有人类苦难的根源，无论人们认为他们多么孤立或被排斥，或者感觉他们多么孤立或被排斥，他们仍然与上帝相连。他们通过内识相连。因此可以说，开展内识的进阶就像迈向上帝的阶梯——你内在的上帝和每个人内在的上帝。

Some people try to make every aspect of a person Godlike or connected to God, but this is not accurate. The only thing that God created in you is Knowledge. Everything else is a product of your self-creation and co-creation with other people living in Separation.

有些人试图让一个人的每一个方面都像神一样或与上帝相连，但这并不准确。上帝在你身上创造的唯一创造是内识。其他一切都是你的自我创造和与其他生活在分离中的人共同创造的产物。

Now some people may say: “Oh, I don’t believe that! I think God created everything!” But if you think like that, then you must justify everything, and that becomes increasingly difficult to do if you are really honest and objective in your observations of life around you.

现在有些人可能会说：“哦，我不相信！我认为上帝创造了一切！”但如果你这样想，那么你就必须证明一切，而如果你真的诚实客观地观察周围的生命，这一点就会变得越来越困难。

So learning The Way of Knowledge is like learning The Way of God. Taking the Steps to Knowledge is like taking the Steps to God. Living the life of Knowledge is like living the life of God in the world.

因此，学习内识之路就如同学习上帝之路。开展内识的进阶就如同迈向上帝的阶梯。过着内识的生命就如同在世间过着上帝的生命。

So each person has this deeper, more powerful Intelligence. But people do not listen. They do not respond. It is as if it does not exist. People’s minds dominate everything. Their beliefs, their thinking, their social conditioning, their attitudes, their convictions, even their beliefs about God completely dominate a person’s life and perspective, so much so that they cannot really know anything, and the greater Mind of Knowledge within them really is beyond their reach.

因此，每个人都有这种更深层次、更强大的智能。但人们不听。他们不回应。就好像它不存在一样。人们的心灵主宰一切。他们的信仰、他们的思维、他们的社会条件、他们的态度、他们的信念，甚至他们对上帝的信仰完全主宰着一个人的生命和观点，以至于他们无法真正知道任何事情，他们内在更伟大的内识心灵确实超出了他们的能力范围。

They are like slaves to the forces that move people about in the world. They are slaves to fear. They are slaves to desire. They are slaves to their own convictions that are born of fear and desire.

他们就像是世界上那些驱使人们势力的奴隶。他们是恐惧的奴隶。他们是欲望的奴隶。他们是他们自身因恐惧和欲望而产生的信念的奴隶。

Indeed, everyone who lives in the world has had this experience, and most people still live within it very fully. To break out of the personal hell of isolation, to break out of the domination of the mind and the domination of your social conditioning represents freedom in the most fundamental way. For you can be free in the world, but if you are not free inside, you cannot really take advantage of your external freedom.

事实上，世界上的每个人都有过这样的经历，而且大多数人仍然充分地生活在其中。摆脱孤立的个人地狱，摆脱心灵的支配和社会条件的支配，从最根本上代表着自由。因为你可以在世界上自由，但如果你内心不自由，你就无法真正践行你的外在自由。

Look about you in the world. People seem to be free, relatively speaking, compared to say other people in the world. And look what they do with their freedom. They become lost in addictions, they make horrendous mistakes in their relationships and they abuse themselves physically and emotionally.

看看这个世界。与世界上的其他人相比，人们似乎相对自由。看看他们如何使用他们的自由。他们沉迷于毒瘾，在人际关系中犯下可怕的错误，在他们的身体和他们的情感上虐待他们自己。

Certainly, you have seen this. Even within yourself, you know you are not free. Freedom is not simply having no obligations in life or not being accountable to anyone. Freedom really in its greatest and most real sense is the ability to find and to follow Knowledge because here you are finally free to do what you came here to do, which is the source of all real integrity and satisfaction.

当然，你已经看到了这一点。即使在你自己的内心，你也知道你并不自由。自由并不只是在生命中没有义务或不对任何人负责。自由在其最伟大和最真实的意义上，是找到并遵循内识的能力，因为在这里你终于可以自由地做你来这里要做的事情，这是所有真正的完整性和满足的源泉。

So trying to gain more external freedom without this internal freedom,it will not lead to a good result. For your soul will only be satisfied if you can fulfill what you came here to do. This is the deeper happiness that is the real happiness.

因此，试图获得更多的外部自由而没有这种内部自由，不会带来好的结果。因为只有当你能完成你来此要做的事情时，你的灵魂才会感到满足。这才是更深层次的幸福，是真正的幸福。

Yes, there are lighthearted moments. Yes, there are fun things to do in the world. But when We talk about real happiness, We are talking about the satisfaction and the sense of integrity that come from really engaging yourself in your greater purpose and mission here. And to do that, you must find and follow Knowledge because only Knowledge knows what it really is.

是的，有轻松的时刻。是的，世界上有许多有趣的事情可以做。但是当我们谈论真正的幸福时，我们谈论的是真正投入到你在此的更伟大目的和使命中所带来的满足感和完整性的感觉。而要做到这一点，你必须找到并遵循内识，因为只有内识知道它真正是什么。

There are endless beliefs and speculations about spirituality and spiritual purpose and the meaning of your connection to God and spiritual practice and all this. But only Knowledge knows. If you are not connected to Knowledge, no matter how fervently you may believe, no matter how much you may build your idea of yourself upon your spiritual attitudes or beliefs, you do not yet know. And without this freedom to know, you are not really free.

关于精神性和精神目的，以及你与上帝的联接和精神实践的意义，有无数的信仰和猜测。但只有内识知道。如果你没有与内识相连，无论你多么热切地相信，无论你多么将你的自我观念建立在你的精神态度或信仰之上，你都还无法知晓。而没有这种知晓的自由，你就不是真正自由的。

So this brings Us then to learning The Way of Knowledge. Is The Way of Knowledge one Teaching? No. There are many teachings that teach The Way of Knowledge. Yet they are still the minority of spiritual teachings in the world, for most spiritual teachings only teach belief and attitude, and give prescriptions for positive living or constructive living, and so forth.

因此，这就引出了我们学习内识之路。内识之路是一种教导吗？不是。有许多教导内识之路的教导。但它们仍然是世界上少数的精神教导，因为大多数精神教导只教导信仰和态度，并为积极的生活或建设性生活开出处方，等等。

Most spiritual teachings in the world do not reflect The Way of Knowledge. Perhaps it could be said that these teachings really are for people who are not yet ready to learn The Way of Knowledge, but this is only an excuse. Do not try to justify it. It cannot really be justified.

世界上大多数精神教导都没有体现内识之路。也许可以说这些教导确实是为那些尚未准备好学习内识之路的人准备的，但这只是借口。不要试图为其辩解。它真的无法得到辩解。

So there is more than one Teaching in The Way of Knowledge, but these Teachings are rare, and the real teachers of The Way of Knowledge are hard to find. They are not the teachers who are presenting the teachings that will make you feel glamorous or beautiful or radiant or give you high experiences or moving into other dimensions and so forth.

因此，内识之路中存在不止一种教导，但这些教导非常稀少，真正的内识之路老师也很难找到。他们并不是那些传授教导让你感觉魅力四射、美丽动人、光芒四射或给你带来极致体验或让你进入其他维度等等的老师。

They are not the teachings that teach physical or emotional healing. These teachings may be very helpful to prepare someone for The Way of Knowledge, but they are rarely The Way of Knowledge itself because most spiritual teachings emphasize belief, attitude and behavior as the main focal point. They have not really hit the mark. They have not really gone to the source of [where] real happiness, wholeness and integrity exists and where it can emerge throughout your entire Being.

它们不是教导身体治愈或情感治愈的教导。这些教导可能对为内识之路做准备非常有帮助，但它们很少是内识之路本身，因为大多数精神教导都强调信仰、态度和行为是主要焦点。它们并没有真正击中要害。它们并没有真正找到真正的幸福、整体性和完整性的源头，也没有找到它们可以贯穿你的整个存在。

It is okay. There are different levels of teaching for different people. But everyone needs to learn The Way of Knowledge at some point, and the sooner the better. Remember, no justification, no excuses.

没有关系。不同的人有不同的教学层面。但每个人都需要在某个时候学习内识之路，而且越早越好。记住，没有理由，没有借口。

However, The Way of Knowledge must be presented in certain ways to correspond with the greater events that are happening in the world. It is not like a static teaching. As We have said, Knowledge responds to the conditions of the world and the circumstances of the world in order to fulfill its mission here. That is why Knowledge is wise. That is why it is effective.

然而，内识之路必须以某些方式呈现，以对应世界上正在发生的更伟大事件。它不像是一种静态的教导。正如我们所说，内识回应世界的状况和世界的情况，以完成它在此的使命。这就是为什么内识是明智的。这就是为什么它是有效的。

Thus, the teaching in The Way of Knowledge must also be responsive to the conditions of the world, and that is why the Creator keeps giving The Way of Knowledge in new manifestations in order to have the greatest impact given the important events that are going to shape and to affect your life.

因此，内识之路的教导也必须对世界的状况作出回应，这就是为什么造物主不断以新的表现形式给予内识之路，以便对那些将要塑造和影响你的生命的重要事件产生最大的影响。

Such a new Revelation in The Way of Knowledge has recently been given in the Teaching called The Greater Community Way of Knowledge. This is a Teaching that prepares people to engage with the reality that you are no longer alone in the universe or even within your own world. It is the reality that humanity is emerging into a Greater Community and must learn about the realities of life within the universe and the meaning of spirituality beyond the limits of human understanding.

这是一次旅程，你必须做好充分准备。内识和内识进阶会告诉你，你需要什么，你需要和谁在一起，你需要去哪里，才能完成这次旅程，完成你来此要做的事情。

So you become eventually a Greater Community person. Now you are functioning in a bigger arena of life. Now Knowledge can begin to really manifest itself and to really express itself because finally it has the context where it can do this. It is not trying to funnel itself through the narrow awareness of a small-minded person. It finally has the freedom to emerge and to radiate and to cast its grace around you and to express itself through you so that you can become a vehicle for God, a vehicle for Spirit, a vehicle for the Spirit that is truly you. This is the meaning of real creativity. It is the source of happiness and integrity.

因此，你最终会成为一个大社区的人。现在你在一个更大的生命舞台上运作。现在内识可以开始真正地显现它自己，真正地表达它自己，因为它终于有了可以做到这一点的背景。它不是试图通过一个心胸狭窄的人的狭隘觉知来引导它自己。它终于有自由出现、散发光芒、将它的恩典投射到你周围并通过你表达它自己，这样你就可以成为上帝的载体、精神的载体、真正属于你的精神的载体。这就是真正创造力的意义。它是幸福和完整性的源泉。

It is a gradual journey. You are not going to overcome decades of social conditioning and perhaps personal error overnight. You have to build a new foundation, a new basis of relationship, a new attitude towards work and health, what We call the Four Pillars of your life—Relationships, Work, Health and Spiritual Advancement—all built together to give you a greater foundation so that when your breakthroughs come, you will be able to embrace them. You will be able to pass through the great thresholds that are waiting for you.

它是一段循序渐进的旅程。你不可能一夜之间克服几十年的社会制约和个人错误。你必须建立一个新的基础，一个新的关系基础，一种对工作和健康的新态度，我们称之为你生命的四大支柱——关系、工作、健康和精神进步——所有这些共同建立起来是为了给你一个更伟大的基础，这样当你的突破到来时，你将能够拥抱它们。你将能够跨越等待你的伟大门槛。

This is the promise of the preparation in The Greater Community Way of Knowledge. This is the result of following and applying the Steps to Knowledge. This is the result of having a spiritual practice that comes from God rather than a personal invention of someone.

这是大社区内识之路的准备承诺。这是遵循和运用内识进阶的结果。这是拥有来自上帝而非某人个人发明的精神练习的结果。

This is the great promise that is awaiting you. And all the powers that are true in Heaven and Earth are here to encourage you and to support you so that you can fulfill what you came here to do—to become a man or woman of Knowledge, or Spirit, to engage in the Greater Community of your life, and to learn to gain your freedom and to encourage others to do the same.

这是正在等待你的伟大承诺。天国和地球上所有的真正力量都在此鼓励你、支持你，这样你就能实现你来此要做的事情——成为一个有内识或精神的男人或女人，参与你生命的大社区，学习获得你的自由并鼓励他人也这么做。

All the promise for humanity in every field of endeavor, the promise for humanity to become a free and unified race within the Greater Community of life, all depends upon this fundamental association, this reunion that We are speaking of—this reunion, this marriage, between the mind that you think with and the greater Mind of Knowledge that lives within you.

人类在各个事业领域中的所有承诺，人类在生命大社区中成为一个自由统一种族的承诺，全都取决于这种根本的联系，取决于我们所说的这种团聚——取决于你用来思考的心灵和你内在的更伟大内识心灵之间的这种团聚、这种婚姻。

We send our blessings. The Presence of the Teachers is with you.

我们送上我们的祝福。老师们的存在与你同在。
