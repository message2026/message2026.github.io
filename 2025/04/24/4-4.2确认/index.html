第二章：确认

发表于2025-04-24|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷灵魂在地球上的旅程
|总字数:7.6 k|阅读时长:32 分钟

The Confirmation

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on April 2, 2011 in Boulder, Colorado
2011 年 4 月 2 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

This is the Confirmation—the Confirmation of why you have come into the world. The Calling that will call out of you that which you cannot call out of yourself. The Resonance that must come from the Source of life, a resonance that you are naturally attached to and in relationship with. The Confirmation that will bring your life into perspective, finally, giving it the right context, the right meaning and its true application in a changing and difficult world.

这就是确认——确认你为何来到这个世界。召唤将召唤出你自己无法召唤出的内识。这共鸣必然源自生命的本源，这共鸣是你与生俱来依附并与之建立关系的。这确认将带你审视生命，最终赋予它正确的背景、正确的意义，以及它在这个不断变化和艰难的世界中真正的应用。

The Confirmation is what your heart is seeking in all of your pursuits and relationships and happiness and fulfillment of whatever kind—the yearning of the soul for its true engagement in the world. For you have come here for a greater purpose, a purpose that you cannot ascertain from your intellect, your beliefs or your ideas alone. It is a personal revelation, and it does not come all at once.

确认是你的内心在你所有的追求、关系、无论何种幸福和成就中寻求的事情——灵魂对它真正参与世界的渴望。因为你来此是为了一个更伟大目的，一个你无法仅凭你的智力、你的信仰或你的想法来确定的目的。它是一个个人启示，它并非一下子到来。

The Confirmation will be when you resonate with the Source of your life and are called into a greater engagement—a true purpose. So different it is from your plans and goals and schemes for happiness or redemption.

确认将发生在你与你生命之源产生的共鸣，并被召唤进入更伟大的参与——一个真正的目的。它与你追求幸福或救赎的计划、目标和方案截然不同。

If you are not struggling to survive, as so many people tragically are, then you have an opportunity to respond to a greater relationship and a greater engagement. And your engagement will play a great part in the restitution of the world and the great relief of poverty and degradation that afflicts so many.

如果你不像许多人那样悲惨地挣扎于生存，那么你就有机会回应一种更伟大的关系和更伟大的参与。你的参与将在重建世界、以及极大地缓解困扰着如此众多人的贫困和退化方面发挥重要作用。

You know not of your greater calling or what it will mean, what it will look like and whom it will serve. That must be revealed to you over time as you demonstrate a readiness, an honesty and true commitment.

你不知道你的更伟大召唤，也不知道它意味着什么，它是什么样子，它将服务于谁。随着时间的流逝，当你展现出一种准备、一种诚实和真正的承诺时，这些都会向你揭示。

But before this occurs, there is the Calling, there is the Sound, there is the Voice, there is the memory, there is the Word that snaps you out of your obsessive life, recalling an ancient memory, a deep understanding for a moment. Like lightning illuminating the landscape at night, it reveals something at the very heart of you. That is the Confirmation.

但在此之前，有召唤，有声音，亦有声音的呼唤，有记忆，有话语，它们将你从执着的生命中唤醒，唤起一段古老的记忆，让你在片刻间获得深刻的领悟。如同闪电照亮夜空，它揭示了你内心深处的内识。这就是确认。

The Confirmation is not that the Revelation will meet your expectations and refer to your beliefs or ideas, or the beliefs or ideas of your religion or your culture, your history or your nation. These are things for the intellect to dwell upon. But at a deeper level, there is another form of communication that must speak to you and that you in time must speak to others—a greater engagement, a deeper recollection.

确认并非意味着启示将满足你的期望，并提及你的信仰或想法，或你的宗教或你的文化、你的历史或你的国家的信仰或想法。这些是智力去沉思的事情。但是在一个更深层次的层面上，存在着另一种沟通形式必须对你讲话，而你迟早必须对他人讲话——一种更伟大的参与，一种更深层次的回忆。

Indeed, the Confirmation will be different from what you thought it would be. No glory, no grandeur, no accolades. You will not become a great prince, a great avatar, a great savior. No. No. No.

的确，确认与你想象的截然不同。没有荣耀，没有庄严，没有赞誉。你不会成为一位伟大的王子，一位伟大的化身，一位伟大的救世主。不，不，不。

Your role will be on a very humble scale, but its gifts will be profound. You will function without recognition to a large extent, for recognition brings misfortune.

你的角色将会非常谦逊，但它的馈赠却意义深远。很大程度上，你的工作将不被认可，因为认可会带来不幸。

The Confirmation will sound again and again as you take the Steps to Knowledge and journey up this greater mountain—a mountain that you know not of, a mountain that presents itself before you suddenly, as if out of nowhere, from the mystery of your life.

当你开展内识的进阶，攀登这座更伟大的山峰时，确认之声会一次又一次地响起——这座山峰是你不知道的，这座山峰突然出现在你面前，仿佛凭空而来，来自你生命的神秘。

The Confirmation is seeing the world overtaken by the Great Waves of change and the recognition that humanity is not alone in the universe or even within its own world.

确认是看到世界被变革的巨浪所席卷，并认识到人类在宇宙中甚至在它自身的世界中都不是孤单的。

Slowly, you will see as your eyes are opened, you will hear as your ears are opened, as your mind becomes still and clear and not overwhelmed with its fears, its anger and its obsessions.

慢慢地，当你睁开眼睛时，你会看见；当你打开耳朵时，你会听见；当你的心灵变得平静和清晰时，你不会被恐惧、愤怒和困扰所淹没。

This is the Confirmation. You cannot control it. You cannot determine the outcome. You cannot make it be what you want it to be, for this is an engagement with the Source of your life, and the Source sets the terms of engagement. For you do not come to God on your terms or according to your definitions or requirements.

这就是确认。你无法掌控它。你无法决定结果。你无法让它成为你想要的样子，因为这是与你生命本源的一次约定，而本源设定了约定的条件。因为你并非按照你的条件，或根据你的定义或要求来到上帝面前。

God does not have to prove the Divine Presence to you. You have to prove your allegiance to the Presence.

上帝无需向你证明神圣的存在。你必须证明你对神圣存在的忠诚。

The Confirmation will sound within you, far beneath the surface of your mind, down through the Ancient Corridors of your mind, down through layers of memory, down in a part of you that you know not of, being a stranger to yourself. Unless you have ventured deeply beneath the surface of your mind, it is unknown what is there.

确认之音将在你的内心深处响起，深入你心灵的表面之下，穿过你心灵的古老回廊，穿过层层记忆，深入你内心深处某个你不知道、对你来说陌生的部分。除非你深入探索你心灵的表面之下，否则你无法知晓那里究竟有何物。

People are afraid to go there. People are afraid of themselves—their thoughts, their feelings, their emotions, their fears, their regrets. But the Confirmation is at a deeper level, you see. Even the most miserable person can hear it and feel it.

人们害怕去那里。人们害怕他们自己——他们的想法、他们的感受、他们的情绪、他们的恐惧、他们的悔恨。但你看，确认处于更深的层面。即使是最悲惨的人也能听到它，感受到它。

The Confirmation sounds because you have reached a point of readiness, and the world around you is calling for you—calling for you to prepare, for you are not ready for your greater role.

确认之声响起，因为你已到达一个准备点，你周围的世界正在召唤你——召唤你进行准备，因为你还没有为你的更伟大角色做好准备。

It is a signal from within yourself. It is a signal from those who watch over your life from above. It is a signal from the world. Ready or not, there is the signal—sounding on all these frequencies and all these dimensions, sounding together.

它来自你自己内心的信号。它是来自那些在天上守护着你生命的存在的信号。它是来自世界的信号。无论你准备好与否，信号都已存在——在所有这些频率、所有这些维度上，共同发出。

It is the dynamic convergence of what you want, what you know and what the world requires of you. That is the Confirmation.

它是你的渴望、你的知道以及世界对你的要求的动态融合。这就是确认。

But you cannot see it. You cannot touch it. You cannot formulate it in your mind. You cannot fit it in with your ideas. You cannot associate it with your beliefs, for it is beyond these things—beyond the realm and the reach of your intellect.

但你无法看到它。你无法触摸它。你无法在你的心灵中构建它。你无法将它与你的想法相适应。你无法将它与你的信仰联系起来，因为它超越了这些——超越了你的智力的领域和范围。

Many pretend to be religious, to espouse their beliefs and ideas, to prostrate themselves at the temple or the mosque or the church. They speak the words. They give the appearance. But the Confirmation has not yet occurred within them.

许多人假装虔诚，宣扬他们的信仰和理念，在寺庙、清真寺或教堂里匍匐前进。他们说着那些话，装出那些样子。但他们内心深处尚未得到确认。

They are not yet in a deeper engagement. They are fixed on their beliefs and the social advantages it may give them, and the sense of identity it may give them, but the Confirmation has not occurred. This is happening at a whole other dimension.

他们尚未深入参与。他们固执于他们的信仰，以及它可能带来的社会优势和身份认同感，但确认尚未发生。这一切发生在完全不同的维度。

Only in the future will your mind finally catch up and say: “Yes, indeed, that was the truth. Yes, indeed, I did need to follow that impulse. Yes, indeed, I did need to respond to the New Revelation. Yes, indeed, I did need to leave that situation.” It will be obvious to you, for you have reached a better place in life, a place of greater strength, clarity and self-trust.

只有在未来，你的心灵才会最终赶上并说：“是的，的确，那是真理。是的，的确，我确实需要遵循那种冲动。是的，的确，我确实需要回应新的启示。是的，的确，我确实需要离开那种处境。”这对你将显而易见，因为你已到达生命中一个更美好的境界，一个拥有更伟大力量、更明晰和更自信的境界。

But before you reach this point of recognition, you are in grave doubt, perhaps having moments of severe doubt and uncertainty—going against your social conditioning, going against your desires and your preferences, following something mysterious that you cannot quantify or justify.

可是在你达到这个认知点之前，你处于严重的怀疑之中，或许有着严重怀疑和不确定的时刻——违背你的社会条件，违背你的渴望和你的偏好，追随某种你无法量化或证明的神秘事情。

But the Confirmation is pulling you. Once you have heard it, once you have felt it, your life will never be the same.

但确认正在吸引你。一旦你听到它，一旦你感受到它，你的生命将焕然一新。

It is a great Calling — a calling to prepare, a calling to respond, a calling to begin a process of reclamation and redemption that you cannot control and that you cannot direct.

它是一个伟大的召唤——一个进行准备的召唤，一个进行回应的召唤，一个开始一个你无法控制、你无法指挥的唤回和救赎过程的召唤。

Your challenge will be to direct your mind and your affairs and to follow the power and the gift of the Confirmation, which will liberate you from that which can only hold you back and compromise your life. It will liberate you from your false beliefs, your unhealthy associations with others, your dangerous obligations, your anger, your resentment and your unforgiveness. In time, it will draw you out of all of this, this hopeless jungle that has kept you lost and a stranger to yourself.

你的挑战在于掌控你的心灵和事务，并追随确认的力量和礼物，它将把你从那些只会阻碍你、危及你生命的事物中解放出来。它将把你从错误的信念、与他人的不健康交往、危险的义务、愤怒、怨恨和不宽恕中解放出来。假以时日，它将把你从这一切中解救出来，从这片让你迷失自我、让你感到陌生的绝望丛林中解救出来。

Only if you follow the Presence will you know the Presence. Only if you take the Steps to Knowledge and follow the directives of Knowledge will you understand the power of the deeper Intelligence that God has given you. For it is responding to the Confirmation, as it must.

只有当你跟随存在，你才能知道存在。只有当你开展内识进阶并遵循内识的指引，你才能理解上帝赋予你的更深层次智能的力量。因为它正在回应确认，这是必然的。

It has no choice because it is free. It is free of deception. It is free of corruption. It is free of divided purposes. It is free of confusion. It is free of seduction. It is free of fantasy. It is waiting for the Calling, and once it happens, you will begin to respond, initiating a process deep beneath the surface of the mind.

它没有选择，因为它是自由的。它没有欺骗。它没有腐败。它没有分裂的目的。它没有混乱。它不受诱惑。它没有幻想。它正在等待召唤，一旦召唤出现，你就会开始回应，开启一个深藏于心灵表面之下的过程。

If you are truly honest, you will follow the deeper movement of your heart and soul. Even as it draws you away from your beliefs and former investments. It is a question and matter of honesty.

如果你真正诚实，你就会追随内心和灵魂深处的动向。即使它使你远离你的信念和以往的投资。它是一个关乎诚实的问题和事项。

You cannot know Us, but you must know yourself, and the others in your life, and those who will be sent to you to help you to regain your true identity and deeper nature.

你无法知道我们，但你必须知道你自己，知道你生命中的其他人，以及那些将被派到你身边来帮助你重新获得你的真实身份和更深层次自然的人。

You cannot abide with Us, for We are beyond the world, and you have been sent to be in the world—to serve the world, to redeem yourself in the world, to reclaim the memory of your Ancient Home while you live here, to experience the Confirmation, to follow the preparation, to allow your life to be reshaped by Knowledge and the mysterious Presence that will visit you from time to time.

你无法与我们同在，因为我们超越了世界，而你被派来身处世界——为这个世界服务，在世界上救赎你自己，在你生活在这里的时候，重拾对你古老家园的记忆，体验确认，遵循准备，让你的生命被内识以及将不时拜访你的神秘存在重塑。

You cannot return to God, for God wants you to be in the world. How can you be in relationship with the Source of all relationships if you cannot even be in relationship with yourself or with another or with a group of people? It is far beyond your capacity, obviously, to desire such a greater engagement.

你无法回归上帝，因为上帝想要你身处世界。如果你甚至无法与你自己、与他人、与一群人建立关系，你又如何能与所有关系的源泉建立关系呢？显然，渴望如此更伟大的参与远远超出了你的能力。

You cannot bypass your responsibilities of being in the world and your service to the world. You cannot leap out of the world into Heaven! To try to do so means that you do not understand your real responsibilities here, and you cannot face the challenges of following Knowledge and the Presence in the world.

你无法回避你身处世界以及你服务世界的责任。你无法跳出世界进入天堂！试图这样做意味着你不理解你在此的真正责任，你无法面对遵循内识和在世界上存在的挑战。

You can succeed because of the Confirmation. You can know the truth because of the Confirmation. You can demonstrate the truth because of the Confirmation.

你可以成功，因为确认。你可以知道真理，因为确认。您可以证明真理，因为确认。

But along the way, on the long journey itself, you must abide with a deeper presence, a deeper awareness that is only a little light in you at this point. For it to become a burning fire, you must abide with it, attend to it and keep it in your mind and your heart.

但一路走来，在这漫长的旅程中，你必须安住于一种更深层次的存在，一种更深层次的觉知，而这在此刻只是你心中的一缕微光。为了让它变成熊熊烈火，你必须与它共存，关注它，并把它铭记于你的心灵和你的内心。

The Revelation is too great to understand. You can only understand how it can be experienced and expressed in the tangible world. You cannot even imagine what is beyond the tangible world, for your imagination is only equipped with what your senses have provided you thus far.

启示太过伟大，难以理解。你只能理解它如何在物质世界中被体验和表达。你甚至无法想象物质世界之外的世界，因为你的想象力仅仅搭载着你感官迄今为止为你提供的事物。

It is not imagination that bridges the gap. It is following a deeper authority that is beyond your control. You cannot use it to get what you want.

弥合差距的并非想象力，而是追随一种你无法掌控的更深层次的权威。你无法利用它来得到你想要的东西。

Do not depend upon miracles, for that means you are not utilizing your own strength and power effectively. It is a miracle that the Confirmation has occurred and will occur again as you proceed, as you become honest, finally, accountable to your deeper nature and your deeper conscience that God has put there.

不要依赖奇迹，因为那意味着你没有有效地运用你自己的力量和能量。确认的发生本身就是一个奇迹，而且随着你不断前行，随着你变得诚实，最终对上帝赋予你的更深层次的自然和更深层次良知的负责，确认还会再次发生。

This is beyond religious belief. This is beyond the customs and traditions of your faith or your nation or your culture. You must always distinguish that which is permanent and ineffable from that which you think and believe.

这超越宗教信仰。这超越你的信仰、你的国家或你的文化的习俗和传统。你必须始终区分那永恒而不可言喻的内识与你所思考和相信的事物。

The Confirmation and the power of the Presence will liberate you finally from the reins of belief. You will finally be let out of that prison even though the door has been open for you all along.

确认和存在的力量最终会将你从信仰的枷锁中解放出来。即使牢笼之门一直为你敞开，你最终也会被释放。

The greater truth can only be known. The New Revelation can only be known. Its teachings and commentaries can only be known through demonstration and application.

更伟大的真理唯有被知道。新启示唯有被知道。它的教导和评论唯有通过示范和应用才能被知道。

You do not have proof and then you become engaged. You become engaged and that provides the proof.

你没有证据，然后你就参与了。你参与了，这就提供了证据。

You cannot control it, for you are not God. And it is a good thing, for you would only be a cruel and oppressive ruler, as you have been within yourself to such a great extent.

你无法控制它，因为你不是上帝。它是一件好事，因为你只会成为一个残酷暴虐的统治者，就像你内心深处已经如此深沉地存在着一样。

God wills that your true power and abilities be restored to you and that this restoration happen within your engagement with Knowledge deep within yourself and your engagement with others in the world.

上帝希望你恢复真正的力量和能力，并且这种恢复发生在你与你内心深处内识的参与以及你与世界上其他人的参与之中。

If you value your relationship with the Creator, then this is what you must respond to. Prostrating yourself, worshipping, declaring your submission mean nothing if you will not follow that which you are meant to see, know and do.

如果你珍视你与造物主的关系，那么这就是你必须回应的。如果你不遵循你本该看见、知道和做到的事情，那么匍匐、崇拜、宣告你的顺服都毫无意义。

The Confirmation will take you to a deeper engagement, a mysterious engagement, an engagement that you must follow even through long periods where you do not experience the Presence and are unsure of the evidence of Knowledge within yourself.

确认将带你进入一种更深层次的参与，一种神秘的参与，一种你必须遵循的参与，即使在你没有体验存在并且不确定你内在内识的证据的很长一段时间里。

This is where you begin to change your allegiance within yourself. No longer governed by ideas and beliefs and the consensus of others, you are now following a deeper Presence and Power, and its demonstration will always produce good. Even if you have to leave people and disappoint them, it is a movement towards greater freedom for everyone.

从这里开始，你开始改变你内在的忠诚。你不再受制于想法、信仰以及他人的共识，而是追随一种更深层次的存在和力量，而它的展现总会带来善果。即使你不得不离开他人，让他们失望，这也是一场迈向每个人更伟大自由的运动。

The Calling is sounding in the world, for humanity is at a great turning point. It will determine the outcome and the future, not only for your children, but for your life as well. It is a time of great activation. It is a time when the Confirmation must be very strong and many people must respond.

召唤正在世界响起，因为人类正处于一个伟大的转折点。它将决定结果和未来，不仅关乎你的孩子，也关乎你的生命。它是一个伟大激活的时代。它是一个确认必须非常强大、许多人必须做出回应的时代。

It is not the time for one individual to become the demonstration, for that will never meet the needs of the world. It is not the time for one great leader or emancipator to come to the world, for that would only further divide humanity, setting it into contention with itself. For not everyone would accept that leader, no matter how pure and righteous they might be.

现在并非由个人来充当示威游行的时机，因为那永远无法满足世界的需要。现在也并非由一位伟大的领袖或解放者降临世界的时候，因为那只会进一步分裂人类，使其陷入自我冲突。因为并非所有人都会接受这位领袖，无论他多么纯洁正直。

No, it is now a different era, a more mature period where humanity has to grow up and become responsible and powerful and cooperative with itself, recognizing the dangers, responding to the needs of the world, recognizing that you are no longer alone and unknown in the universe and that others are here to take advantage of your difficulties and your expectations.

不，现在已经是一个不同的时代，一个更加成熟的时期，人类必须成长起来，变得有责任感、强大起来，并与它自身合作，认识到危险，回应世界的需求，认识到你们在宇宙中不再孤独和未知，其他种族来到这里是为了利用你们的困难和你们的期望。

It is a time to become engaged in serving others. It is a time for the Confirmation to occur. If you are responding to the New Message and to these words, then the Confirmation is underway, no matter how you respond in the moment.

它是一个投入服务他人的时间。它是一个确认发生的时间。如果你正在回应新讯息和这些话语，那么确认就在进行中，无论你此刻如何回应。

You can reject and deny Us and the power of grace We bring to you, but We are still here. And the Calling is still here. And the Confirmation is still here, waiting for you to respond, waiting for you to become honest and humble, to recognize there are things more important than your ideas, your beliefs or your position in life. These mean nothing in the face of the Divine.

你可以拒绝、否定我们，以及我们带给你的恩典力量，但我们依然在这里。召唤依然在这里。确认依然在这里，正等待着你的回应，等待着你变得诚实谦逊，去认识到有些事情比你的想法、你的信仰或你的人生地位更重要。在神圣面前，这些都毫无意义。

There is only the Calling, the Response, the Engagement and the Return.

只有召唤、回应、参与和回归。

That is all that matters in reality and in the end—the quality of these things, your allegiance to these things and your ability to follow what God has provided now, in the New Revelation, without the weight of human history and the distortion of human commentary and interpretation and the usage by political forces that have obscured God’s previous Revelations. They too generated the Confirmation. But you must have a wise teacher to follow them, or you cannot complete your task.

归根结底，真正重要的，是这些事物的品质，你对这些事物的忠诚，以及你能否遵循上帝如今在新启示中所提供的一切，不受人类历史的重负，不受人类评论和诠释的扭曲，不受政治势力的利用——这些政治势力曾遮蔽上帝先前的启示。这些政治势力也产生了确认。但你必须有一位睿智的导师来遵循它们，否则你无法完成你的任务。

That is why God has sent a New Revelation into the world, for humanity is failing. Its strength is being lost. Its opportunities are being diminished. Its world is in decline. You are facing intervention from beyond.

正因如此，上帝向世界发送了一个新的启示，因为人类正在衰败。它的力量正在丧失。它的机会正在减少。它的世界正在衰落。你们正在面对来自外在的干预。

How will humanity deal with all of this? How will it prepare itself? How will it follow the wise course of action concerning the many decisions that have to be made, not only for nations but for individuals as well?

人类将如何应对这一切？它将如何做好准备？它将如何遵循明智的行动方针，应对那些不仅对国家，也对个人必须做出的诸多决定？

You cannot answer this. Your experts cannot answer this. There is too much confusion. There is too much deception. There is too much assumption.

你们无法回答这个问题。你们的专家也无法回答这个问题。这里面有太多的困惑，太多的欺骗，太多的假设。

The river is polluted. The skies are dark. The environment is contaminated.

河流被污染了。天空阴沉。环境被污染了。

How will you see clearly? How will you navigate the difficult times ahead? You do not have an answer. You are afraid to even ask the question.

你怎么才能看得清楚？你怎么才能渡过未来的艰难时期？你没有答案。你甚至不敢问这个问题。

Look around you. Everyone is pretending that it is not happening, losing themselves in their hobbies, distractions, their romances, their quest for wealth, their personal problems and so forth, as if nothing is happening in the larger world around them that will affect them directly.

看看你的周围。每个人都假装这一切没有发生，沉浸在他们自己的爱好、消遣、爱情、对财富的追求、个人问题等等之中，仿佛周围更广阔的世界没有发生任何会直接影响到他们的事情。

You must listen for the Confirmation. Our words are not just words. The writings are not merely ideas. They are a direct communication to the deepest part of you. If you cannot hear this, then how will your prayers ever be answered, if you cannot hear the answer?

你必须聆听确认。我们的话语不仅仅是文字。这些文字不仅仅是想法。它们是与你内心最深处的直接沟通。如果你听不到这一点，那么你的祈祷又如何得到回应呢？如果你听不到答案。

This is the Confirmation. It is the Power and the Grace that will make all the difference—the unseen, the ineffable, that which you cannot quantify, that which you cannot theorize, that which you cannot predict, that which is beyond the reach of science, that which can only confound philosophy, leaving you beyond words and ideas. That will make all the difference.

这就是确认。它是力量与恩典，它将带来一切不同——那些看不见的、不可言喻的、你无法量化的、你无法理论化的、你无法预测的、科学无法触及的、只能使哲学困惑的、让你无法用言语和观念理解的。它将带来一切不同。

What will enable people to choose that which is right and correct and beneficial when there are so many other attractions leading in different directions? What will enable you to overcome the consensus of others, the wishes of your family, the beliefs of your nation, to follow a Greater Power and a greater purpose?

当有如此多其他的吸引力指向不同的方向时，什么能让人们选择对的、正确和有益的事物？什么能让你克服他人的共识，你家庭的愿望，你国家的信仰，去追随一个更伟大力量和一个更伟大目的？

Our words are not only words. You must listen beyond the words. Let your mind be still. Listen deeply. Do not come to conclusions. Do not judge or analyze. Listen deeply. Contemplate. Live with the question. Do not provide the answers. Be still. Be open. Be discerning.

我们的话语不仅仅是文字。你必须超越言语去倾听。让你的心灵静下来。深入聆听。不要妄下结论。不要评判或分析。深入聆听。沉思。带着问题生活。不要提供答案。保持宁静。保持开放。保持辨识力。

The Confirmation is waiting for you and for others. You cannot control their response but only your own. What voice will you follow? What voice will you listen to?

确认正在等待你和他人。你无法掌控他们的回应，只能掌控你自身的反应。你会追随什么声音？你会听从什么声音？

With so many attractions and seductions and persuasions within you and around you and throughout the world, how can you follow the true Voice?

你的内心、你的周围以及全世界有如此多的吸引力、诱惑和说服，你怎样才能遵循真正的声音呢？

This is a question that only God can answer, and has answered, and is answering through these words and through the words and the sounds and the presence of the Revelation.

这是一个只有上帝才能回答的问题，并且已经回答了，并且正在通过这些话语、通过文字、声音和启示的存在来回答。
