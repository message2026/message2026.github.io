第一章：恩典

发表于2025-04-24|更新于2025-04-24|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷灵魂在地球上的旅程
|总字数:5.8 k|阅读时长:24 分钟

Grace

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on September 8, 2014 in Boulder, Colorado
2014 年 9 月 8 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

Grace, the great Presence becomes directed at you when you are following Knowledge, the deeper Mind that God has given you.

当你追随内识，追随上帝赋予你的更深层次的心灵时，恩典，伟大的存在就会指向你。

Grace, which is an experience of immediate connection with those who have sent you into the world.

恩典，是与那些将你送到这个世界的存在直接联接在一起的体验。

Grace, which comes to you in an act of selfless giving through forgiveness, through contribution.

恩典是通过宽恕、通过贡献而无私奉献而来到你身边的。

It is the most precious thing, you see, because it is the most precious relationship that you are experiencing.

你看，这是最珍贵的东西，因为这是你所经历的最珍贵的关系。

It comes upon you like a power, enveloping you, surrounding you, assuring you.

它就像一种力量，笼罩着你，包围着你，向你保证。

It is completely incomprehensible to the intellect, which cannot fathom intrinsic relationship at this level. You can only yield to it and allow yourself to experience it when it is occurring.

它对于智力而言是完全无法理解的，因为智力无法理解这种层面的固有关系。你只能顺从它，允许你自己在它发生时体验它。

It changes the chemistry of your brain and your experience of your body. It puts you into a different state and alters your perception. It suspends your mind so that you can be present, so that you can experience Grace.

它改变你大脑的化学反应和你对身体的体验。它让你进入一种不同的状态，改变你的感知。它让你的心灵暂停，让你能够在场，体验恩典。

Heaven shines upon those who act in a noble way without condemnation, without hatred, without violence—those who show true compassion, those who are able to forgive in a significant way, those who pray for deliverance with true intention.

天堂眷顾那些行为高尚、没有谴责、没有仇恨、没有暴力的人——那些表现出真正同情心的人、那些能够以有意义的方式宽恕的人、那些真心祈求解脱的人。

It is upon these that Heaven shines its Great Rays. Only for moments, for you must return to the world. Only for moments, for you cannot linger forever in this Presence, not yet, not until your mission is complete here on Earth, a mission that lives within you, which you cannot yet fathom.

天堂的伟大光芒正照耀着这些。只是片刻，因为你必须回归世界。只是片刻，因为你无法永远停留在这个存在，现在还不行，直到你在地球上的使命完成为止，那存在于你心中、你尚无法理解的使命。

You experience this, be you of any faith tradition or of no faith tradition. It is your mind and your behavior and the nature of your engagement with the world that brings Grace to you and allows you to bring Grace to others through you, with you and around you.

无论你信仰什么，或没有信仰，你都会体验到这一点。正是你的心灵、你的行为以及你与世界互动的自然，将恩典带给你，并让你能够通过你、与你一起、在你周围将恩典带给他人。

People try to experience Grace in their romantic pursuits, thinking that falling in love is like Grace. But it is not, you see. True recognition in relationship can bring the experience of Grace, but true recognition in relationship comes in time, through engagement and activity and participation together—two minds uniting, moving in the same direction.

人们试图在浪漫的追求中体验恩典，认为坠入爱河也是一种恩典。但你看，并非如此。关系中的真正认同能够带来恩典的体验，但关系中的真正认同需要时间，需要通过共同的投入、活动和参与——两个心灵合一，朝着同一个方向前进。

The intoxication of romance is not what We speak of here today. This is something far greater, more permanent, for it speaks of your permanence in the world. It reminds you that you have been sent for a purpose, and that those who sent you are watching you at that [this] moment.

浪漫的陶醉并非我们今天在此所谈论的。这是一种更为伟大、更永恒的内识，因为它诉说着你在世间的永恒。它提醒你，你被派来是为了一个目的，而那些派你来的存在此刻正在注视着你。

Grace. It is the experience of Divine relationship. It is the experience of that which is ineffable and pervasive. It is your experience of the invisible. But who you are is invisible—that which is permanent within you, that which is passing through this life, that which is still connected to God, that part of you called Knowledge that God has put there to guide you, to direct you in this life towards greater service and fulfillment.

恩典。它是神圣关系的体验。它是不可言喻且无所不在的体验。它是你对无形之物的体验。但你是谁却是无形的——你内在永恒的内识，正在经历此生的内识，依然与上帝联接的内识，你被称为内识的那个部分，上帝将其放置在那里来指引你，指引你在此生走向更伟大的服务和成就。

When that part of you resonates with those who sent you, you can have an experience of Grace. It is recognition of relationship at a level far beyond the realm and the reach of the intellect, but oh so powerful can it be, and so restorative to you.

当你的内识部分与那些派遣你的存在产生共鸣时，你就能体验到恩典。它是对关系的认知，其层面远远超越了智力的范畴和范围，但它却如此强大，如此能治愈你。

We live in this state, you see. It is like the air We breathe. It is Our environment. It is compassionate and gracious, but so powerful in what it can do.

你看，我们生活在这种状态中。它就像我们呼吸的空气。它是我们的环境。它充满同情心和仁慈，却又蕴藏着无比强大的力量。

Any individual who can be guided by this can overcome any obstacle in the world, any force in the world, or even in the universe—the Greater Community in which you live.

任何能够受到这个指引的个体，都能够克服世界上的任何障碍，世界上的任何力量，甚至宇宙中——你所生活的大社区中——的任何障碍。

That is not to say you can prevent things from happening or that you can overcome all the forces of nature or even the activities of others. What it means is that they cannot overtake who you are, and your relationship with those who sent you and your mission here on Earth.

这并不是说你能阻止事情发生，也不是说你能战胜一切自然力量，甚至战胜他人的活动。我的意思是，它们无法超越你，也无法超越你与派遣你来到地球的存在之间的关系，也无法超越你的使命。

It is that part of you that is indestructible, the part of you that will live longer and beyond everything you touch and see, that part of you that is connected to your Source that gives such power and permanence, courage, destiny and determination. This too is Grace, continual Grace.

它是你身上坚不可摧的一部分，它是你身上将永存的部分，超越你触及和看见的一切，它是你身上与源头相连的部分，赋予你力量与永恒、勇气、天命与决心。这也是恩典，持续不断的恩典。

It is not that you have the luxury of some beautiful moment. This kind of Grace is what propels you forward.

它并非你拥有了某些美好时刻的奢侈，而是这种恩典驱使着你前进。

It is not an experience of ecstasy but of inner determination, moving you even when you do not want to move, directing you when you want to direct yourself, holding you back from those things you want to do, which are not good for you, which will weaken you and create delay.

它不是一种狂喜的体验，而是一种内心决心的体验，即使在你不想动的时候，它也会推动你，在你想要引导你自己的时候指导你，阻止你去做那些你想做的事情，那些对你没有好处的事情，那些会削弱你并造成拖延的事情。

This too is Grace, you see. People do not think of this as Grace, but that which directs and restores your life is most certainly Grace. That which gives you purpose and meaning and true direction is most certainly Grace.

你看，这也是恩典。人们不认为这是恩典，但指引和重塑你人生的，无疑是恩典。赋予你目的、意义和真正方向的，无疑是恩典。

That which disappoints your fantasies, your dreams, your desires and holds you back, and teaches you how to hold yourself back, that is Grace.

那些使你的幻想、你的梦想、你的愿望落空并阻碍你前进的事物，以及那些教你如何阻止你自己前进的事情，无疑是恩典。

That which does not follow the passions and views and prejudices of your culture, your nation or your religion, that which is uncompromising regarding what is true, that is Grace.

不追随你的文化、你的国家或你宗教的激情、观点和偏见，对真理的毫不妥协，无疑是恩典。

That which looks on beauty, wealth and charm with objectivity, with clarity and without preference, that too is Grace. For it saves you. It breaks the chains of addiction, passion, obsession, seduction, manipulation. It breaks these chains because they have no effect on Grace. And when you become strong with Grace, they have no effect on you.

客观、清晰、不带任何偏好地看待美貌、财富和魅力，这也是恩典。因为它拯救你。它打破成瘾、激情、痴迷、诱惑和操纵的枷锁。它打破这些枷锁，因为它们对恩典毫无影响。当你在恩典中变得强大时，它们对你也毫无影响。

You look around, and people are like in chains, enslaved to other forces—external forces, but internal forces as well.

环顾四周，人们就像被锁链束缚着，被其他驱动力所奴役——外部的驱动力，以及固有的驱动力。

In a moment of Grace, when the Great Rays are shone upon you, you have a moment of freedom from all these things, even your dire circumstances. You will have a moment of reprieve from all that holds you back and oppresses you.

在恩典的时刻，当伟大的光芒照耀着你，你将获得片刻的自由，摆脱所有这些事物，甚至摆脱你悲惨的处境。你将获得片刻的喘息，摆脱所有阻碍你、压迫你的一切。

In life you must earn this freedom. You must return and learn what Grace is teaching you and see the difference now that you can see such a dramatic contrast between your normal state of mind and this moment of Grace you have experienced.

在生命中，你必须赢得这份自由。你必须回归，学习恩典的教诲，并体会到其中的差异，因为你能够看到你平常的心境与此刻所体验到的恩典之间如此巨大的反差。

Grace is not about worship. Grace is relationship. It is shared responsibility, for that is what relationship really means.

恩典并非关乎崇拜。恩典是关系。恩典是共同的责任，因为这才是关系的真谛。

God does not need praise and worship. Heaven is not full of individuals singing hallelujah all day long, for no one wants to do that.

上帝不需要赞美和敬拜。天堂里没有整天唱哈利路亚的人，因为没有人愿意这样做。

Grace here—in this world, living in Separation—for you is shared responsibility. It is a life that is committed and determined according to the direction of Heaven, not merely by adhering to religious beliefs or being a faithful follower of your religion or of your government or culture.

在这里——在这个世界，生活在分离中——的恩典对你来说是一种共同的责任。它是一种根据天国指引而做出的奉献和决心的生命，而非仅仅通过坚持宗教信仰，或成为你的宗教、你的政府或文化的忠实追随者。

We are speaking of something very different here. We are speaking of something that does not create destruction in the world, that does not oppress people or destroy people or oppress those who disagree with you.

我们在这里谈论的是截然不同的事情。我们谈论的是不会给世界带来毁灭的内识，不会压迫他人、毁灭他人、也不会压迫那些与你意见相左的人的内识。

Religion governed by your worldly mind becomes a tool of the state. It becomes a force of division, conquest, destroying or overcoming its enemies. This is what generates the notion of a Judgment Day and Heaven and Hell and all these things that have no bearing on reality.

受世俗心灵支配的宗教会成为国家的工具。它成为一种分裂、征服、摧毁或战胜敌人的力量。这催生了“审判日”、“天堂”、“地狱”以及所有这些与现实毫不相关的概念。

This gives rise to the angry God, the judgmental God, the vengeful God. You could never experience Grace with such an idea and belief about God. You could never love an angry God. You could never love a God you have to fear. You could never unite with a God who would punish you for eternity should you fail some expectation.

这导致了愤怒的上帝、审判的上帝、复仇的上帝。怀着这样的想法和信念，你永远无法体验到上帝的恩典。你永远无法爱一个愤怒的上帝。你永远无法爱一个让你感到恐惧的上帝。你永远无法与一个只要你未能达到某种期望就会永远惩罚你的上帝联合。

You must outgrow these things to understand Grace, to experience Grace, to know the graciousness of God, to know the meaning of Grace as a powerful force that moves your life and that holds you back.

你必须超越这些事情才能理解恩典，体验恩典，知道上帝的仁慈，知道恩典的意义，知道恩典作为一种强大的力量，推动你的生命，让你退缩。

For Grace is not merely a moment of extreme and exquisite peacefulness and inclusion. It is the force that is here to move your life if you can respond to it, if you recognize your need for it, if you yield to it and seek its guidance and counsel in all things you trust, that you choose and that you see around you.

因为恩典不仅仅是一个极致精致的平和与包容的时刻。它是推动你生命的力量，只要你能回应它，只要你认识到你对它的需求，只要你屈服于它，并在你信任的、你选择的以及你周围看到的一切事物中寻求它的指引和忠告。

Grace will give you the eyes to see and the ears to hear. But first it must change your life and free you from the circumstances that will never allow you to move in a new direction.

恩典会赐予你眼睛去看，耳朵去听。但首先，它必须改变你的生命，让你摆脱那些阻碍你迈向新方向的境况。

This is not merely the gift to unique and prescient individuals. This is not merely a gift for the rare and select individual. This is for you today. Now.

这不仅仅是一份给独特而有远见卓识之人的礼物。这不仅仅是一份给稀有而精选之人的礼物。这是今天送给你的。现在。

God has provided the Steps to Knowledge to bring you to Grace and all that Grace is and all that Grace is here to do with you and through you.

上帝提供了内识进阶，带你走向恩典，走向恩典的一切，以及恩典在此与你一起并通过你所做的一切。

Here you cannot be a witless follower. You cannot be passive. You cannot sit on the sidelines of life. You cannot withdraw into some secure place beyond the reach of the world permanently. You must return, and you must be strong. And you must know how to govern your mind and hold yourself back from anger and revenge and condemnation.

在这里，你不能做一个愚昧的追随者。你不能被动。你不能置身于生命的边缘。你不能永远退缩到某个远离世界的安全之地。你必须回归，你必须坚强。你必须懂得如何掌控你的心灵，阻止你自己陷入愤怒、复仇和谴责。

Grace must teach you to do these things—to use your mind productively, to be its wise guide, to be the captain of your ship under the direction of Heaven.

恩典必须教会你做这些事情——有效地运用你的心灵，成为它的明智向导，在天国的指引下成为你的船的船长。

You do not simply wait around for messages to come to you. You do not wait around for God to tell you what to do. That is irresponsible. You are not doing your part when you behave like this or think like this.

你不会只是等待着讯息传来。你不会等着上帝告诉你该做什么。这是不负责任的。当你这样做或这样想的时候，你就没有尽到你的责任。

Grace will teach you how to be in the world, how to direct your mind, how to see clearly, how to listen for the truth in others, no matter how false they may seem to be.

恩典会教你如何在这个世界生存，如何引导你的心灵，如何看清事物，如何倾听他人的真理，无论这些真理看起来有多么虚假。

Heaven will teach you to be in the world in a new way so that Grace can move through you naturally, so that you become a conduit for spiritual power and that the force of redemption may move through you to touch others who so desperately need this and who are waiting to receive.

天国将教导你以一种新的方式生活在这个世界上，这样恩典就可以自然地流经你，这样你就可以成为精神力量的管道，救赎的力量可以通过你去触及那些迫切需要它并等待接收它的人。

The tragedies of the world are growing. The upheaval of the Great Waves of change is growing. The oceans are rising. The skies are changing. Nations are contracting. You will have to be able to have the Power of Heaven to withstand the anguish, the anger, the condemnation and the violence this will produce.

世界的悲剧正在增长。变革巨浪的的动荡正在加剧。海洋正在上升。天空正在变化。国家正在萎缩。你必须拥有天国的力量，才能抵御由此产生的痛苦、愤怒、谴责和暴力。

This will not be easy. You will need Grace to guide you through the perilous journey ahead, navigating the Great Waves of change and all that it will produce in the world, which is beyond human recognition at this moment.

这并非易事。你需要恩典来指引你走过前方危险的旅程，航行于变革的巨浪及其将给这个世界带来的一切，而这一切此刻还超越人类的认知。

You must become committed to this journey and not simply seek pleasure or reprieve from your engagement with the Divine. It is a calling. It is a path of strengthening and greater determination.

你必须全身心投入这段旅程，而不是仅仅为了从与神性的接触​​中寻求快乐或喘息。它是一种召唤。它是一条通往力量与更伟大决心的道路。

If you are weak and indulgent, seeking only pleasure and high experiences, you will not be called. And your experience of Grace will not yet be real.

如果你软弱放纵，只追求享乐和崇高体验，你就不会被召唤。你体验到的恩典也还不是真实的。

If you are aggressive or ambitious, seeking to acquire wealth and recognition, you will not be called, for your approach is incorrect. You are not yet honest with yourself sufficiently to see how inappropriate that is, how self-serving it is, how manipulative it is and how harmful to others it really is.

如果你咄咄逼人、雄心勃勃，渴望获得财富和认可，你不会被召唤，因为你的方法不正确。你对你自己还不够诚实，无法看清这种做法有多么不合适，有多么自私自利，有多么操纵人心，以及它对他人有多么有害。

Heaven must prepare you. This is Grace at work. Heaven must build a strong foundation underneath you. You will build this. You will do the work. You will do the labor. You will have to apply yourself.

天国必须为你做好准备。这是恩典在工作。天国必须为你筑起坚实的基础。你将构建它。你将付出努力。你将付出劳动。你必须全力以赴。

Heaven gives you the directive. You have to figure out how to do it, how to make it happen. And in so doing, you become strong. You become engaged in the world that you had avoided before. You become engaged in a world you had condemned before.

天国给了你指示。你必须弄清楚如何去做，如何让它发生。如此一来，你就会变得强大。你开始投入到你曾经回避的世界。你开始投入到你曾经谴责的世界。

You have to build the foundation, or you will not be strong enough. You will not be reliable enough. That is the first thing, to build the foundation internally and externally, a strong platform in life—your relationships, your work, your health and your spiritual development all being built together. This is the work of Grace. For without this, you have no foundation and the Great Waves can easily take you away.

你必须构建基础，否则你将不够强大。你将不够可靠。这是首要的事情，构建内在和外在的基础，一个强大的生命平台——你的人际关系、你的工作、你的健康和你的精神发展，所有这些都将共同构建。这是恩典的工作。因为没有这些，你就没有基础，巨浪很容易将你卷走。

You build the internal foundation, building your connection to Knowledge so that Grace may appear to you and move through you in the world.

你构建了内在的基础，构建了你与内识的联接，这样恩典就可以出现在你面前，并通过你在世界上流动。

This is a long preparation, for the outcome is so very important. It is the most important thing for you to do with your time beyond meeting the bare essentials of life. This is what time is for. Otherwise, you will waste it away on momentary pleasures and meaningless pursuits, hopeless romances and ever-growing aggravation with yourself.

这是一段漫长的准备，因为结果至关重要。除了满足生命的基本需求之外，这是你使用时间最重要的事情。这就是时间的意义所在。否则，你会把它浪费在短暂的快乐、毫无意义的追求、无望的恋情以及不断增长的自我怨恨上。

You are either rising or sinking in life. You are either getting up this mountain, or you are falling away. You may stop and rest, certainly, and you will have to do this often. But you must keep going up this mountain. This is where Grace wants to take you. This is where your heart wants to go. This is where your life was destined to go. This is why you were sent.

人生在世，你要么奋起，要么沉沦。你要么攀登这座山峰，要么坠落。你当然可以停下来休息，而且你得经常这样做。但你必须继续攀登这座山峰。这是恩典想要带你去的地方。这是你内心渴望去的地方。这是你的生命注定要去的地方。这就是你被派遣的原因。

Grace knows all this, of course, but you, and all others living in Separation, have forgotten and have given yourself to other things—other pursuits and obsessions, fears and concerns.

当然，恩典知道这一切，但是你，以及所有其他生活在分离中的人，已经忘记，并把你自己交给了其他事物——其他追求和痴迷、恐惧和担忧。

Grace comes with Our Voice. Grace comes with God’s New Revelation for the world. Grace comes from taking the Steps to Knowledge, which is returning you to your ancient association while you are living here in this chaotic world.

恩典伴随着我们的声音而来。恩典伴随着上帝赋予世界的新启示而来。恩典来自于内识进阶，当你生活在这个混乱的世界中时，它将你回归到你古老的关联。

Grace is choosing to continue. Grace is trusting yourself enough to continue. Grace is holding yourself back from your weak tendencies and the persuasions of others. This is Grace at work.

恩典是选择继续前行。恩典是足够信任你自己能够继续前行。恩典是克制你自己，不让你被自己的弱点和他人的劝说所左右。这就是恩典的工作。
