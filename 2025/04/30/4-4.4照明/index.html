第四章：照明

发表于2025-04-30|更新于2025-05-01|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷灵魂在地球上的旅程

The Illumination

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on July 29, 2013 in Boulder, Colorado
2013 年 7 月 29 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

When your eyes are finally clear and your mind can truly be quiet for any length of time, you will begin to have moments of illumination, moments where you can see beyond what the ordinary senses report and hear things far more beautiful than anything you can hear on Earth.

当你的眼睛终于变得清澈，你的心灵能够真正安静一段时间时，你就会开始拥有顿悟的时刻，在这些时刻，你可以看到超越普通感官所报告的事情，听到比你在地球上所能听到的任何事物都更美丽的事情。

These are the signs of illumination. They perhaps will only be momentary at first, but they will stand in great contrast to all of your other experiences. Even your happiest moments or most contented experiences cannot in any way compare with these.

这些是照明的迹象。起初，它们或许只是昙花一现，但它们将与你所有其他的经历形成鲜明对比。即使是你最快乐的时刻，或最满足的经历，也无法与这些相比。

It is as if you have gained the eyes to see something that has already been there, that has always been there, that surrounds you continuously. But your eyes are fixated on a different kind of reality. And you are in relationship with that reality so completely, fixated upon it so completely, well, that you cannot see everything else.

仿佛你获得了一双眼睛，能够看到某个早已存在、始终存在、持续围绕着你的事情。但你的眼睛却注视着另一种现实。你与那个现实如此紧密地联系在一起，如此彻底地注视着它，以至于你无法看到其他的一切。

This Greater Reality is like the stars shining above you. They do not only shine at night. They shine every moment of every day, but perhaps you only care to look up at them for brief moments, very infrequently. But they are always there.

这更伟大的现实就像闪耀在你头顶的星辰。它们不仅在夜晚闪耀，它们每时每刻都在闪耀，但或许你只会在极少数的短暂时刻抬头仰望它们。但它们始终存在。

The eyes of Heaven are always watching, every moment of every day, but perhaps only once or twice in a lifetime will you experience this more directly. Until you begin to take the Steps to Knowledge, this will be the case for nearly everyone, for their senses were designed to react and respond to the world. Their ideas are fixated here. Their problems exist here. Their irresolution exists here. Their lack of forgiveness, lack of self-respect, exist here.

天国的眼睛始终注视着你，每时每刻，但或许一生中你只有一两次能更直接地体验到这一点。在你开始踏上内识进阶之前，几乎每个人都会经历这种情况，因为他们的感官被设计用来对世界作出反应和回应。他们的想法固着于此。他们的问题存在于此。他们的优柔寡断存在于此。他们的缺乏宽恕，缺乏自尊，也存在于此。

It is like your mind is a slum, living in a part of a greater city, far away from what is beautiful and elegant. Perhaps your life is like a factory, where people are oppressed to work too much. These are merely examples of different states of mind.

你的心灵仿佛是贫民窟，身处大都市的某个角落，远离美丽与优雅。或许你的生活就像一座工厂，人们被压迫着过度劳作。这些只是不同心灵状态的例子。

Purchasing your way into a beautiful life does not change these states very much. You carry with you your own inner darkness, no matter how splendid your circumstances can become. And the pleasures you purchase, you will soon tire of, for they do not give much satisfaction.

通过花钱买来过上美好的生活，并不能真正改变这些状态。无论你的境遇多么辉煌，你内心的黑暗依然存在。你买来的快乐，很快就会厌倦，因为它们并不能给你带来多少满足感。

At every moment of every day, you are living in Creation—not the physical landscape you see, or the urban landscape you see, but in Creation itself. This is why, in taking the Steps to Knowledge, you are taught that you will see a whole new world once your eyes are clear and your mind is still and connected to the deeper Knowledge that God has placed within you.

每一天的每一刻，你都生活在创造之中——不是你看到的物质景观，也不是你看到的城市景观，而是创造本身。正因如此，在学习内识进阶的过程中，你被教导，一旦你的眼睛清晰，你的心灵宁静，并与上帝放置在你内在的更深层次内识相联接，你将看到一个全新的世界。

Yes, you are still in the world. You are still here doing what you are supposed to be doing, or trying to find what you are supposed to be doing, which is most often the case. But you will have glimpses, you see. And these glimpses are important, for they speak of a Greater Reality in which you live at this moment but to which you are largely unaware and unavailable. Preoccupied you are so much, distracted so much, taken up so much with your fears and desires and obligations, it is like Heaven cannot get your attention. But Heaven is here.

是的，你依然身处尘世。你依然在这里，做着你本该做的事，或者试图寻找你本该做的事，这往往是常态。但你会看到，你会瞥见一些事情。这些瞥见很重要，因为它们诉说着你此刻生活其中的一个更伟大现实，但你对它大多浑然不觉，难以接近。你如此心事重重，如此心烦意乱，如此被你的恐惧、渴望和义务所占据，仿佛天国无法引起你的注意。但天国就在这里。

Amongst the Angelic Assembly, someone is assigned to watch you. It will not be one of the great figures, but one of their assistants instead. They will be watching you and others. So in a sense, you are never alone, and all the things you do alone that are degrading or disgraceful to you are observed by someone else.

在天使圣团中，有天使被指派守护你。它不会是某个伟大人物，而是他们的助手之一。他们会守望你和其他人。因此，从某种意义上说，你永远不会孤单，所有你独自做的、有辱你身份或让你蒙羞的事，都会被他人观察到。

You cannot escape this fact, for there is no real privacy in the universe, and in the universe of Creation, well, everyone knows everything about everyone. So the idea of hiding and pretending is unnecessary. It does not even come up.

你无法逃避这个事实，因为宇宙中没有真正的隐私，而在创造的宇宙中，嗯，每个人都知道每个人的一切。所以隐藏和伪装的想法是不必要的。它甚至根本不存在。

But that is not where you are now. That is where you have come from originally, and that is what you will return to eventually. But here you are a human being, an individual, trying to survive and accomplish something valuable in the world, dealing with a world of great difficulty—a world that requires constant adaptation and decision making; a world that is unpredictable, despite all your assurances of security; a world that can change in a moment, a life that can change in a moment.

但那并非你现在所在之处。那是你最初来自的地方，也是你最终将回归的地方。但在此，你是一个人，一个个体，努力在世界上生存并成就某种有价值的事情，应对着一个充满巨大艰难的世界——一个需要不断适应和决策的世界；一个尽管你保证了所有安全却依然难以预测的世界；一个可能瞬间改变的世界，一个可以瞬间改变的生命。

Your only real security is the power of Knowledge that God has placed within you to guide you, to protect you and to lead you to a greater life if you are willing to be prepared and to take the Steps to Knowledge.

你唯一真正的安全是上帝放置在你内在的内识的力量，它指引你、保护你并引领你走向更伟大生命，如果你愿意做好准备并开展内识进阶的话。

When We speak of illumination, do not think this is the goal of your life. Many people have made this assumption and will dedicate themselves to arduous spiritual practice to try to attain this state, which seems so elusive and which may only be gained after great investment of time. But even if you were to attain such a state, which so very few people ever do, you would still have to come back to serve in the world, as the great Teachers have always had to do.

当我们谈到照明时，不要认为这就是你人生的目标。许多人都抱有这种假设，并会致力于艰苦的精神修行，试图达到这种境界。这种境界看似难以捉摸，只有投入大量时间才能获得。但即使你达到了这种境界——虽然很少有人能做到——你仍然必须回到世间服务，就像伟大的导师们一直以来所做的那样。

So it is more correct to say that you attain moments of illumination by following the course that Knowledge is laying out for you: coming into relationship with Heaven, aligning your personal mind in service to Knowledge.

因此更正确的说法是，通过遵循内识为你规划的路线，你将获得照明的时刻：与天国建立联系，调整你个人的心灵以服务内识。

This is a far greater task than you might think. In fact, it is different from anything you think, for your ideas are always geared towards self-advancement, self-enrichment, self-protection, but We are speaking of something very different here.

这是一个远比你想象的更艰巨的任务。事实上，它与你所想的任何事情都不同，因为你的想法总是指向自我提升、自我充实、自我保护，但我们在此谈论的是一些非常不同的事情。

If you are to follow a life of greater purpose and meaning, you will have moments of illumination. They will just occur because your vision is becoming refined. Your mind is in greater alignment with the greater purpose that has brought you here. You have freed yourself from divisive and destructive relationships and have gained new alliances that can support you truly and meaningfully here.

如果你要追寻一种更伟大目的和意义的生命，你将会拥有顿悟的时刻。它们会自然而然地出现，因为你的视野正在变得精炼。你的心灵与引领你来到此地的更伟大目的更加契合。你已经将你自己从分裂和破坏的关系中解放出来，并获得了能够在此真正且有意义地支持你的新联盟。

This takes a long time to accomplish in general. There is no magic pill. There is no magic button. There is no elevator that takes you to Heaven. You have to forge a new union within yourself, and this represents the core purpose of most spiritual practice, [which] is a reorientation of the mind from trying to be its own master and lord to serving that which is there to guide it, which God has put within you to guide it. This will change everything—your values, your priorities, your incentives, your problems, everything.

这通常需要很长时间才能实现。没有神奇的药丸。没有神奇的按钮。没有电梯能带你去天堂。你必须在你内在打造一种新的结合，这代表了大多数精神实践的核心目的，即重新定位心灵，不再试图成为它自身的主人和领主，而是服务于引导它的内识，上帝放置在你内在来引导它的内识。这将改变一切——你的价值观，你的优先事项，你的动机，你的问题，一切。

When you have finally gotten on the true path of your life, then moments of illumination can occur here and there. They will show you something that you have always known but have long forgotten.

当你最终踏上生命的正确道路时，顿悟的时刻就会不时出现。它们会让你看到一些你一直知道却早已遗忘的事情。

In this moment, you will see a world of revelation, a world of light, a world of marvelous creation, and the physical world will seem like it is being superimposed upon this Greater Reality. Here you may even see a world of darkness superimposed on a world of great light and expansion.

此刻，你将看到一个启示的世界，一个光明的世界，一个奇妙造物的世界，物质世界仿佛被叠加在这个更伟大的现实之上。在这里，你甚至可能看到一个黑暗的世界叠加在一个充满光明和扩张的世界之上。

You will recognize that both exist, but the Greater Reality is permanent, whereas the changing reality is temporary. And you will realize you have to serve this temporary reality because this is your task, and this represents the culmination of your own return, your own redemption.

你将认识到两者都存在，但更伟大的现实是永恒的，而变化的现实是暂时的。你将意识到你必须服务于这个暂时的现实，因为这是你的任务，这代表着你自身回归、你自身救赎的顶点。

There will be no argument here. It is simply a natural thing. But in this moment, which will seem so timeless, you will see Creation itself with the physical reality superimposed upon it. You might even have a moment of purely seeing the Greater Reality, but this is only a moment, for you must return. You cannot escape into this. That is not the purpose of this experience.

这里不会有任何争论。它只是一件自然之事。但在这个仿佛超越时间的瞬间，你将看到创造本身，以及叠加于其上的物质现实。你甚至可能有片刻纯粹地看到更伟大的现实，但这仅仅是片刻，因为你必须回归。你无法逃避其中。那并非此体验的目的。

The purpose of this experience is to free you from fear and to give you greater strength in your true alignment with Knowledge, for here you come into alignment with all life in Creation itself. Knowledge here is not your personal advisor. It is your connection to everything. It is your connection to those who sent you into the world. It is your connection to your Spiritual Family. It is your connection to God.

这个体验的目的是让你摆脱恐惧，并在你与内识的真正契合中赋予你更伟大的力量，因为在此你与创造本身的所有生命达成了契合。在这里，内识并非你的个人顾问。它是你与一切事情的联接。它是你与那些派遣你来到这个世界的人们的联接。它是你与你的精神家庭的联接。它是你与上帝的联接。

If your eyes were truly clear, if your mind was truly still and aligned properly, you would feel the presence of your Spiritual Family with you, increasingly, and in an increasing number of situations because they are there, you see. You cannot escape entirely the reality from which you have come and to which you will return. It is there with you now.

如果你的目光真正清澈，如果你的心灵真正宁静且正确调整，你就会感受到你的精神家庭与你同在，这种感觉会越来越强烈，在越来越多的场合中出现，因为他们就在那里，你瞧。你无法完全逃避你来自哪里、也将回归到哪里的现实。它现在就在那里，与你同在。

This is the force of redemption that is so very powerful, but it exists beyond the boundaries of Separation—boundaries of a separated reality, a separated identity and everything that is involved with that. It is like waking up during a dream and not only after the dream is over.

这是无比强大的救赎之力，但它超越了分离的界限——分离的现实、分离的身份以及与之相关的一切的界限。它就像在梦中醒来，而不仅仅是在梦结束之后。

Knowledge is your connection to this Greater Reality, for it is the part of you that has never left God. It is still connected to Heaven. That is why it is fearless. That is why it is powerful. That is why it is unconflicted. That is why it is certain of its mission and purpose here on Earth.

内识是你与这个更伟大现实的联接，因为它是你从未离开上帝的部分。它依然与天堂相连。正因如此，它无所畏惧。正因如此，它强大无比。正因如此，它无所冲突。正因如此，它确信它在地球上的使命和目的。

While your mind vacillates—seeking pleasure, running away from fear, engaging in fantasy and tragedy, giving your life away to meaningless things—the greater part of you is certain, calling to you, waiting for you to respond, trying to get your attention so that it can correct your errors and give you the certainty you have fruitlessly sought in so many other things.

当你的心灵摇摆不定——寻求快乐，逃避恐惧，陷入幻想和悲剧，把你的生命交给毫无意义的事物——你更伟大的部分是确定的，它正召唤着你，正等待着你的回应，试图引起你的注意，这样它就能纠正你的错误，并赋予你你在许多其他事物中徒劳寻求的确定性。

Illumination then is the sign of what is really true in your life, what calls to you and what strengthens you on your return.

那么，照明就是你生命中真正生命的标志，是什么东西在召唤你，是什么在你回归时给你力量。

Heaven watches you. That is why alone you can do nothing because you are never really totally alone, you see. Perhaps at first this will seem like an intrusion. It will seem uninvited. It will seem harsh. You will resist it and resent it, but once you come to your senses, you will realize what a great strengthening this is, for the eyes that watch you are totally compassionate.

天堂注视着你。正因如此，你独自一人却无能为力，因为你从来都不是完全孤独的，你瞧。或许一开始，这看起来像是一种侵扰。它似乎不请自来。它似乎很刺耳。你会抗拒它，怨恨它，但一旦你恢复理智，你就会意识到这是多么强大的力量，因为注视着你的那双眼睛充满同情。

This is the evidence that you have never really left Creation, and the experiences it will give you will reinforce this understanding, which is only just beginning to emerge at this time.

这是你从未真正离开过创造的证据，它给予你的体验将强化这种理解，而这种理解此时才刚刚开始浮现。

God’s New Revelation provides the pathway to illumination, but illumination is merely the result of assuming a greater service in the world and a greater pathway of preparation. It is not a goal in and of itself. You did not come into the world simply to try to escape the world, for you have a purpose in being here, a mission to serve while you are here. And your ability to engage with this and to fulfill this will strengthen your ability to return, for your heart seeks to return to that which is its Source. Beneath all your other desires and goals and aspirations, this is the deepest and most powerful goal.

上帝的新启示提供了通往照明的道路，但照明仅仅是承担起在世界上一个更伟大服务和一条更伟大准备道路的结果。它本身并非一个目标。你来到这个世界并非只是为了试图逃离世界，因为你身处此地有一个目的，一个当你在此时要服务的使命。你参与其中并实现它的能力将增强你回归的能力，因为你的心寻求回归它的本源。在你所有其他的渴望、目标和渴望之下，这是最深刻、最强大的目标。

This will take you even beyond your notions of love and happiness and all the things associated with that, not because these things do not have value, for many of them have great value, but it is simply there is a greater realm awaiting you. To the extent that you can experience this, even momentarily, you will be able to yield its presence to others, which will give reassurance and strength and encouragement to them in a way that your mind could never do.

这将带你超越你对爱、幸福以及所有与之相关事物的理解，并非因为这些事物没有价值，因为它们中很多都具有伟大的价值，而仅仅是因为有一个更伟大的领域正在等待着你。只要你能够体验到这一点，哪怕只是片刻，你就能将它的存在交付给他人，这将以一种你的心灵永远无法做到的方式，给予他们安心、力量和鼓励。

All others here seek this too. Regardless of the destructive nature of their involvements, and their hopeless pursuits they try to fulfill, and the tragedies they have created, and the errors they have created, and the wasted time they have produced, this is still their deepest inclination.

在这里的其他人也都在寻求这一点。无论他们的参与多么具有破坏性，无论他们试图实现多么无望的追求，无论他们制造了多少悲剧，犯下多少错误，无论他们浪费了多少时间，这仍然是他们最深层次的倾向。

A greater purpose in life will restore to you your connection with the Greater Reality. At first, this will only reside at the level of your ideas, creating images and ideas of that which really is beyond your mind’s capacity to comprehend. That is all right as long as you know that that is not really it.

生命中的更伟大目的将重建你与更伟大现实的联结。起初，它仅仅停留在你的想法层面，创造出那些超出你心灵理解能力的意象和想法。只要你知道那并非真正的意义，那就没问题。

For the mind that you think with, your worldly mind, can only conceive of things in this world. It cannot imagine Creation. It cannot imagine your Heavenly state. It cannot imagine the Angelic Assembly. It cannot imagine everything that exists beyond your realm, in whatever dimension. It is best to leave these open to the Mystery and not tax your mind needlessly, trying to create an understanding.

因为你用来思考的心灵，你世俗的心灵，只能构想此世间的事物。它无法想象创造。它无法想象你的天堂状态。它无法想象天使圣团。它无法想象存在于你领域之外的一切，无论在哪个维度。最好将这些留给神秘去探索，而不是不必要地消耗你的心灵，试图去创造一种理解。

It is your approach that is important. It is taking the Steps to Knowledge that is important and all that that will teach you, and all that that will require, and all that that will show you regarding what to seek and what to avoid in your daily life.

重要的是你的方法。重要的是开展内识进阶，以及所有那些将教导你、所有那些将要求你、以及所有那些将向你展示关于在你的日常生命中该寻求什么、该避免什么的一切。

Therefore, at the outset, the illumination is very practical because that is your most immediate need: to build the Four Pillars of your life—the Pillar of Relationships; the Pillar of Work and Providership; the Pillar of Health, mental and physical health; the Pillar of your Spiritual Development. These are very practical things for the most part, and this is what the illumination will guide you to attend to because if this is not attended to, you are not going anywhere. You are broken down by the side of the road.

因此，从一开始，照明就非常实用，因为那是你最迫切的需求：构建你生命的四大支柱——关系支柱；工作和供养支柱；健康支柱，即身心健康；以及你的精神发展支柱。这些大部分都是非常实用的事情，而这正是光照将引导你去关注的，因为如果不关注这些，你就哪儿也去不了。你会被抛在路边。

Heaven will call, but you cannot move yet because you do not have the foundation. The Four Pillars are like the four tires on your vehicle. Well, without them, you are not going anywhere. So there is your foundation, and that is what the illumination will seek to bring to you first and foremost before you can seek greater and more majestic things.

天堂会召唤，但你还无法行动，因为你还没有根基。四大支柱就像你车上的四个轮胎。没有它们，你哪儿也去不了。所以，这就是你的根基，而这正是在你能够寻求更伟大、更宏伟的事物之前，光照会首先寻求带给你的。

Even if you seek majestic things, it is all the product of your service to others, which is humble and honest and practical. It is this that will bring you the moments of illumination.

即使你追求宏伟的事业，那也都是你服务他人的成果，谦逊、诚实、务实。正是这些，才会给你带来光照的时刻。

This is breaking through the veil of Separation. It is not a product of taking your mind into higher and higher realms, for your personal mind really is grounded in the world. You must go beyond it. It is meant to serve a greater power within you, the power of Knowledge. That is its true role, and here it will function magnificently, whereas now it is functioning more destructively because it does not have the proper context in which to function.

这是突破分离的帷幕。它并非将你的心灵带入越来越高境界的产物，因为你的个体心灵实际扎根于世界。你必须超越它。它注定服务于你内在的一个更伟大力量，内识的力量。这是它真正的角色，在此它将伟大地运作，而现在它正在更具破坏性地运作，因为它没有合适的运作环境。

If you will listen to what We are telling you and allow your heart to respond, then perhaps you will begin to comprehend what We are saying here. For the mind will create things of the mind. We are speaking of something greater than your mind, things which your mind must come to serve, but which it cannot really duplicate itself.

如果你聆听我们所说，并让你的心回应，那么或许你就能开始理解我们在此所说的。因为心灵将创造心灵的事物。我们谈论的是某种超越你心灵的伟大事物，你的心灵必须服务于这些事物，但心灵无法真正复制它自身。

When We say that Creation is with you at this moment, well, We are talking about something that exceeds your normal range of experience. But should you experience this, it can alter the course of your life and give you a real commitment to taking the steps you must take to discern your greater purpose, which will come into view once other things have been cleared away and set aside. It is the product of true self-honesty.

当我们说创造此刻与你同在时，嗯，我们谈论的是某种超越你正常体验范围的事情。但如果你体验到这一点，它就能改变你的生命轨迹，并赋予你真正的承诺，去迈出你必须迈出的步伐，去辨识你更伟大的目的。一旦其他事物被清理并搁置，它就会显现。它是真正自我坦诚的产物。

Here you do not give up everything in the world. You simply set aside those things that are standing in your way and holding you back. You already know what they are, but you have not really been willing to face some of these things yet.

在这里，你无需放弃世间的一切。你只需将那些阻碍你、阻碍你前进的事物抛在一边。你已经知道它们是什么，但你尚未真正愿意去面对其中的一些。

So Heaven waits—waits for you to do this primary work of clearing the pathway. Then a pathway out of the jungle can be given to you, but you cannot be ensnared within it.

所以天堂在等待——等待你完成这清理道路的首要工作。然后，一条走出丛林的道路就会赐予你，但你不会被困在丛林里。

All the great religions of the world can bring you to experiences of illumination if they are understood correctly and if your heart is not filled with grievance and malice, but with humility and reverence and the desire to serve others. But this is hard to find in traditions that have been overlaid with other emphases, with other priorities and other requirements.

世界上所有伟大的宗教都能带给你照明，只要它们被正确理解，只要你的内心不被怨恨和恶意所填满，而是充满谦逊、敬畏和服务他人的渴望。但这在那些被其他重点、其他优先次序和其他要求所覆盖的传统中很难找到。

So God has spoken again to provide the Steps to Knowledge, like a ladder sent down into the world. You need only climb out, and as you do, you will learn things you never could have learned before. You will find freedom from things that you thought would be with you forever. Your mind will be cleansed and your vision will be clear. And you will be able to see the Light that exists beyond the fog of confusion.

因此，上帝再次讲话，提供了内识进阶，如同一架被送入世界的梯子。你只需爬上去，当你这样做时，你将学到你以前从未学到的东西。你将从你认为会永远伴随你的事物中找到自由。你的心灵将得到净化，你的视野将会清晰。你将能够看到存在于困惑迷雾之外的光。
