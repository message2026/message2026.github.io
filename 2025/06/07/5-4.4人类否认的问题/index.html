第四章：人类否认的问题

发表于2025-06-07|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第五卷更大的黑暗
|总字数:18.5 k|阅读时长:78 分钟

The Problem of Human Denial

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on April 9, 2000 in Boulder, Colorado
2000 年 4 月 9 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

Many people, when reading The Allies of Humanity Briefings, will become afraid. They will read it as if they think it is real and genuine, which it is, and they will become frightened and want to withdraw. They will want to deny it in some way.

许多人在阅读人类盟友简报时会感到害怕。他们会把它读得仿佛它是真实的，事实也的确如此，他们会感到恐惧，想要退缩。他们会想以某种方式否认它。

In a way, The Allies of Humanity material reveals your weaknesses. It calls upon your strength. It calls upon your inherent wisdom. It calls upon Knowledge, the spiritual Mind within you, but it also reveals your weaknesses. It reveals your vulnerability. It reveals your lack of control over your own consciousness. It reveals your undefended borders to space. It reveals your assumptions, your preoccupations and overall your ignorance of life in the universe.

在某种程度上，人类的盟友材料揭示了你们的弱点。它召唤你们的实力。它召唤你们固有的智慧。它召唤内识，你们内在的精神心灵，但它也揭示了你们的软弱。它揭示了你们的脆弱性。它揭示了你对你自身意识的缺乏掌控。它揭示了你们对太空不设防的边界。它揭示了你们的假设、你们的执念以及总体而言你们对宇宙生命的无知。

Here it is important to ask yourself if humanity will ever encounter intelligent life from beyond its world. And if it does, when that happens, how will humanity respond? How will humanity know if its new visitors are friendly or not? How will humanity be able to discern the visitors’ motives and their consciousness? How will humanity be able to discern their organization, their methods and so forth.

在这里，重要的是要问你自己：人类是否会遭遇来自其世界之外的智能生命？如果真的遭遇，人类将如何应对？人类将如何辨别这些新的来访者是否友善？人类将如何辨别来访者的动机和意识？人类将如何辨别他们的组织、手段等等。

If you consider these questions seriously, you will realize that you have very few answers. And without answers, perhaps you will become afraid. You will feel your own vulnerability, and you will feel how unprepared people really are for an encounter such as this.

如果你认真思考这些问题，你就会意识到，你几乎没有什么答案。而如果没有答案，你或许会感到害怕。你会感受到你自身的脆弱性，你会感受到人们面对这样的遭遇是多么的毫无准备。

Because most people still think that they are alone in the universe, that they have always been alone and will always be alone, well, this important set of questions really does not get considered by very many people. And even those who do consider it often think very romantically about life in the universe and what it will bring humanity and how much humanity will gain from this encounter. They think how interested the visitors will be in humanity’s art and culture, history and temperament.

因为大多数人仍然认为他们在宇宙中是孤独的，认为他们一直以来都是孤独的，并且永远都是孤独的，所以，这一系列重要的问题实际上并没有被很多人认真思考过。即使是那些认真思考过这些问题的人，也常常对宇宙中的生命、它将给人类带来什么以及人类将从这次相遇中获益多少抱有非常浪漫的幻想。他们想象着这些来访者会对人类的艺术、文化、历史和气质产生多么浓厚的兴趣。

So even amongst the very few people who really consider these questions and think they are important, there is often a great deal of romantic speculation. People are afraid to be realistic regarding life in the universe because it reveals their weaknesses. When we speak of weaknesses, we are not talking about your lack of technology. Really, what we are talking about is your lack of awareness, your lack of focus in life, your lack of social cohesion in your nations and cultures.

因此，即使在极少数真正思考这些问题并认为它们很重要的人中，也常常存在着大量的浪漫主义臆测。人们害怕以现实的态度看待宇宙中的生命，因为这会暴露他们的弱点。我们所说的弱点，并非指你们缺乏科技。我们真正谈论的是你们缺乏觉知，缺乏生命的焦点，以及你们国家和文化中缺乏社会凝聚力。

Humanity is divided and contentious within itself. This makes you vulnerable to outside forces. But really your vulnerability goes even beyond this because it has to do with your state of mind. It has to do with your view of yourself and your world. It has to do with your assumptions, your illusions and your preoccupations.

人类内部分裂纷争，这让你们容易受到外部势力的冲击。但实际上，你们的脆弱性远不止于此，因为它与你们的心灵状态有关。它与你们对自己和世界的看法有关。它与你们的假设、你们的幻想和你们的执念有关。

It is as if a great storm were building and had been building for some time, but people paid no attention. And then when the storm hits, well, it hits with such fury. It hits with such an impact. People are completely taken by surprise and are outraged and terrified. And yet the signs were there.

它就好像一场巨大的风暴正在酝酿，而且酝酿了一段时间，但人们却毫不在意。然而，当风暴来袭时，它来势汹汹，冲击力巨大。人们完全措手不及，既愤怒又恐惧。然而，迹象已经显现。

Even human science is beginning now to acknowledge the preponderance of life in the universe as a theoretical probability. Yet who is concerned with what your first great encounters might mean?

就连人类科学现在也开始承认宇宙中存在生命，这在理论上是可能的。然而，谁又会关心你们第一次的伟大邂逅究竟意味着什么呢？

As The Allies Briefings are presented, there will be all kinds of denial, criticism and repudiation. Why? Why so much rejection over an encounter that everyone really considers to be very possible? Yet when it is actually accounted for, you will see a great deal of denial. “Outrageous! Ridiculous! Cannot happen!” You will hear scientists say, “Well, it is not possible for another race to get here given the limitations of travel and speed and so forth.”

随着盟友简报的发布，各种否认、批评和驳斥将接踵而至。为什么？为什么一场人人都认为很有可能发生的相遇，却遭到如此多的拒绝？然而，当它真正被提及时，你会看到大量的否认。“太离谱了！太荒谬了！不可能发生！”你会听到科学家们说：“嗯，考虑到旅行、速度等等的限制，其他种族不可能到达这里。”

How presumptuous! Do human beings dare to presume what other nations and cultures have been able to develop over a long period of time? Is the universe limited by human understanding? Is it not possible that other races could have far surpassed human accomplishment in technology?

真是妄自尊大！人类竟敢妄加揣测其他民族和文化在漫长岁月中取得的成就？宇宙难道受限于人类的理解吗？其他种族的科技成就难道不可能远远超越人类吗？

You may say, “Well, of course!” But when faced with the prospect of a real encounter, people sing a very different tune. Their idealism leaves them. Their romanticism is thrown into doubt. Their glorious anticipation is overshadowed with concern and anxiety.

你可能会说：“好吧，当然！”但当真正面临相遇的可能时，人们却唱起了截然不同的调子。他们的理想主义离他们而去。他们的浪漫主义陷入怀疑。他们美好的期盼被担忧和焦虑所笼罩。

So when the Allies of Humanity message is presented, people begin to feel the real core of their fear, their lack of preparation, the weakness of their position. The Allies present a very realistic view of life in the universe. They are not here to answer every question that you may have, but instead to give you an awareness of what is really occurring in the world today and also to dispel much of the fanciful speculation that surrounds the prospect of humanity encountering other forms of intelligent life.

因此，当人类盟友的讯息被呈现时，人们开始感受到他们恐惧的真正核心，他们的准备不足，以及他们立场的薄弱。盟友呈现了一种对宇宙生命非常现实的看法。他们并非来回答你们所有的问题，而是让你们觉知当今世界正在发生什么，并驱散围绕着人类可能遭遇其他智能生命形式的前景的许多不切实际的猜测。

Even fanciful expectation, hopeful expectation, really has fear at its core because you are uncertain, because you do not know, because you are unprepared, because you realize in a moment of honest recognition how vulnerable you really are, living on the surface of your world, exposed to the universe and undefended.

即使是幻想中的期望、充满希望的期待，其核心也确实充满恐惧，因为你不确定，因为你不知道，因为你没有准备好，因为你在诚实认知的时刻意识到你是多么脆弱，生活在你的世界的表面，暴露在宇宙中，毫无防备。

Think for a moment if you were another race visiting your world and you wanted to just simply observe human behavior. Just observe it, without interfering. Well, you could look down on everything. It is all there. Human activity, human involvements, human conflict, human relationships, human technology, human communication—it is all available to the discreet observer.

试想一下，如果你是另一个种族，造访你们的星球，你只想观察人类的行为。只是观察，不加干涉。那么，你就可以俯视一切。一切都在那里。人类的活动、人类的参与、人类的冲突、人类的关系、人类的科技、人类的交流——所有这些都对谨慎的观察者来说触手可及。

Therefore, it is very important to understand why people are afraid. The fear of the reality of contact is deep seated. Many people have this very glorified view that they are at the pinnacle of God’s Creation, that their religions are built upon the pre-eminence of human spirituality and human identity. What will happen when they find out that humanity is one little race evolving within a Greater Community of intelligent life? And that this race is actually very weak, divided and insignificant in the vastness of the Creation!

因此，理解人们为何感到恐惧至关重要。对接触现实的恐惧根深蒂固。许多人抱有这种极其荣耀的观点，认为他们处于上帝创造的巅峰，他们的宗教建立在人类精神性和人类身份的卓越之上。当他们发现人类只是在智能生命大社区中进化的渺小种族时，会发生什么？而这个种族在浩瀚的创造中实际上非常弱小、分裂且微不足道！

People’s religious views cannot tolerate this kind of realization. Many of these views are already being eroded by the discoveries of science that show that the universe does not revolve around this world, that this world is just a little planet revolving around an insignificant star in a vast galaxy amongst many. Where is human pre-eminence then? Who are you in the universe? Are you really important to anyone or anything?

人们的宗教观念无法容忍这种认知。许多这样的观念已经被科学发现所侵蚀，这些发现表明宇宙并​​非围绕着这个世界旋转，这个世界只不过是一颗小行星，围绕着浩瀚星系中一颗微不足道的恒星旋转。那么，人类的卓越之处又在哪里呢？你们在宇宙中究竟是谁？你们对任何种族或任何事物真的重要吗？

We are asking these questions to bring you into the core of your anxiety because you must confront this within yourself. The inability or unwillingness to do this is really the source of all human ignorance and presumption regarding humanity’s place in the universe and regarding the reality of the extraterrestrial Intervention that is occurring in the world today.

我们提出这些问题，是为了带你进入你焦虑的核心，因为你必须直面你内心的焦虑。无力或不愿这样做，才是人类对人类在宇宙中的地位，以及对当今世界正在发生的外星干预的现实，所有无知和臆断的根源。

People say, “Well, there is no Intervention happening here. How ridiculous! It is all the fancy of certain people and their need for attention or their boredom or something.” What are you really hearing here but an excuse? This is the way that one pacifies oneself from experiencing real anxiety.

人们说：“好吧，这里根本就没有什么干预。真是荒谬！这都是某些人的幻想，他们想得到关注，或者无聊之类的。”你在这里听到的其实只是借口？这只是人们为了安抚自己，避免经历真正的焦虑而采取的方式。

If you could consider this rationally and objectively, you would say, “Well, of course we would be visited at some point! I mean, if there is intelligent life in the universe, someone out there must know that we exist here.” And if you are not limited by the limitations of humanity’s science, that opens the door to many more possibilities.

如果你能理性客观地思考这个问题，你会说：“嗯，我们当然会在某个时刻被造访！我的意思是，如果宇宙中存在智能生命，那一定有种族知道我们在这里。” 如果你不受人类科学局限性的限制，那么这将为更多可能性打开大门。

If you can ask these questions, you might think to yourself, “Yes, of course humanity will be encountered. Yes, our resources will be scrutinized. Yes, our world will be evaluated. Yes, there are other powers in the universe that would perhaps want our world to be part of their organization. And yes, they would want to take advantage of this beautiful place in some practical way.”

如果你能问这些问题，你可能会想：“是的，人类当然会遭遇。是的，我们的资源将受到审视。是的，我们的世界将受到评估。是的，宇宙中或许还有其他大国希望我们的世界成为他们组织的一部分。是的，他们也想以某种实际的方式利用这片美丽的土地。”

You see, these observations are so obvious. These speculations, if you could call them that, are so reasonable, but people will not consider them. They will not face them because of their anxiety, because of their fear.

你看，这些观察是如此明显。这些推测（如果你可以称之为推测的话）是如此合理，但人们却不会去考虑它们。他们因为焦虑、因为恐惧而不愿面对它们。

In fact, the reality of life in the universe, the encounter with life in the universe and the Intervention itself together represent the most denied reality in the world today. People will think, “It is not important. I have my job. I have my family. I have, you know, my own daily problems. I mean, why should it matter to me?”

事实上，宇宙生命的现实、与宇宙生命的相遇以及干预本身，共同代表着当今世界最被否认的现实。人们会想：“这不重要。我有我的工作。我有我的家庭。你知道，我有我自身的日常问题。我的意思是，这对我来说有什么关系？”

What are you talking about? If there is an Intervention going on in the world, do you think that it is not relevant to you and to your life and to what will happen to you?

你正在说什么？如果世界上真的存在干预，你认为这与你、你的生命以及你将要发生的事情无关吗？

Do you see here? This is “isolationist thinking.” The Allies Briefings bring home the reality that humanity’s isolation is over. But the isolationist thinking continues, unabated. Unless you have a direct encounter with forces from the Greater Community where your life is disturbed or upset in some way, well, you will just continue on thinking the way you have always thought, living upon the assumptions that you have always lived upon, oblivious to the greater realities that are shaping your life and destiny.

在这里，你看到了吗？这就是“孤立主义思维”。盟友简报带你认识到人类的孤立时代已经结束的现实。但孤立主义思维仍在继续，丝毫没有减弱。除非你直接遭遇来自大社区的势力，你的生活在某种程度上受到干扰或扰乱，否则，你只会继续以你一直以来的方式思考，活在你一直以来赖以生存的假设之上，对正在塑造你生命和天命的更伟大现实视而不见。

How can God reach you to tell you to become alert and aware, to make you responsive to these greater realities that are changing your life and destiny?

上帝如何才能告诉你要保持警惕和察觉，让你对这些正在改变你的生命和天命的更大现实做出回应？

People love the idea of a God out there that will throw them a life preserver when they are drowning, but the idea that God will interfere in their life and show them something they really do not want to see, well, that is really a test of faith, isn’t it? That is exactly what is happening today.

人们热衷于相信上帝会在他们溺水时抛出救生圈，但上帝竟然会干预他们的生命，让他们看到一些他们不愿看到的事情，这真是对信仰的考验，不是吗？这正是今天正在发生的事情。

That is why the Teaching in Greater Community Spirituality is in the world because this is God’s Message to alert humanity to this greater reality and to prepare humanity. The awareness is not enough. If the awareness engenders primary anxiety and fear, people will not know what to do. They will say, “Oh, my God! What do we do?” And they do not know what to do because they have never had to respond to this directly before.

正因如此，大社区精神性教导才会来到这个世界，因为这是上帝的讯息，旨在警醒人类关注这个更大的现实，并帮助人类做好准备。仅有觉察是不够的。如果觉察引发了最初的焦虑和恐惧，人们将不知所措。他们会说：“哦，我的上帝！我们该怎么办？”他们不知道该怎么做，因为他们从未直接回应过这个问题。

That is why God’s Message brings with it the preparation so people can begin to think like they live in a Greater Community. Then they can begin to become aware that there are Greater Community realities out there that are going to have a direct impact on them, their lives and their world.

正因如此，上帝的讯息才带来准备，让人们能够开始像生活在大社区中一样去思考。如此一来，他们就能开始觉察到，外面存在着大社区现实，它们将直接影响他们、他们的生命以及他们的世界。

The preparation must give you this greater awareness and consciousness, this sensitivity. Otherwise, you are like an ant colony in a field that is about to be plowed under. And the poor ant colony has not a clue of what is about to happen. Up until the moment of their destruction, well, life will be as it has always been.

准备工作必须赋予你这种更伟大的觉知和意识，以及这种敏锐度。否则，你就像田里即将被犁翻的蚁群。可怜的蚁群对即将发生的事情一无所知。直到它们毁灭的那一刻，生命依然如故。

But you are not ants, and you have a consciousness. You can consider the future, and you can think about things that are beyond your visible range. And you can consider yourself living within a larger arena of life, both within the world and beyond the world within a Greater Community.

但你并非蝼蚁，你拥有一种意识。你能够思考未来，你能够思考超越你可见范围的事物。你能够思考你自己生活在一个更广阔的生命场景里，既在世界之内，也在世界之外，在一个大社区之中。

As you develop this Greater Community awareness, you begin to see that your world is a very special place, with wonderful attributes. And you may think, “Well, of course others would be interested in our planet. And interested in us, not because we are magnificent but because we are the stewards of this place. We control this world.” And you would begin to think very, very objectively about your situation here.

随着你发展出这种大社区觉知，你开始看到你们的世界是一个非常特殊的地方，拥有着奇妙的特质。你可能会想：“好吧，当然会有其他生命也会对我们的星球感兴趣。对我们感兴趣，不是因为我们多么伟大，而是因为我们是这个地方的管家。我们掌控着这个世界。”你会开始非常非常客观地思考你在此的处境。

However, even people who claim to be objective and scientific in their perspective are really still governed by this fundamental fear and anxiety and are still living under the general denial of the fact that the world is being visited and that the Intervention is occurring. They are in such a state of denial that they will not even consider it. They will not come near it. The information, the evidence, is all over the place, but they will not even come near it. They will just say, “Oh, no, no. That’s all foolishness. It’s just insecure people trying to get attention.”

然而，即使是那些自称客观、科学的人，实际上也仍然被这种根本性的恐惧和焦虑所支配，仍然普遍否认世界正在被探访、干预正在发生的事实。他们处于如此否认的状态，以至于他们甚至不会去考虑它。他们不会去接近它。信息、证据，到处都是，但他们甚至不会去接近它。他们只会说：“哦，不，不。那都是愚蠢的。那只是缺乏安全感的人试图引起注意而已。”

They are in denial. They think they are being reasonable, but really they are being so unreasonable. They are supporting and fortifying their own ignorance and the ignorance of others. And even if they cannot accept that the Intervention is occurring, well, the prospect of life in the universe is still a very wondrous, romantic journey to them. It is like something that you dream about in the most glorious ways. “Oh, we will meet these advanced races who will give us so much technology and enlighten us on how to live in peace and so forth.” This is all foolishness. They have not a clue what is happening out there in the universe.

他们在否认。他们自以为是通情达理的，但实际上他们如此不讲道理。他们在支持和强化他们自身以及他人的无知。即便他们无法接受干预正在发生，嗯，宇宙生命的前景对他们来说依然是一段非常奇妙、浪漫的旅程。它就像你以最辉煌的方式梦想的东西。“哦，我们将遇见这些先进的种族，他们将赋予我们如此多的科技，并启发我们如何和平地生活等等。”这完全是愚蠢的。他们根本不知道宇宙里正在发生什么。

If these reasonable people are given a Greater Community perspective, which the Teaching in Greater Community Spirituality provides, oh, my God. It touches that core fear and anxiety. And they feel in that moment how utterly vulnerable they are, how undefended they are, how unprepared they are. And you look at people, and they do not have a clue. And they do not want to have a clue. And you look at your world, and you say, “Oh, my God! We could be overtaken without our even knowing it!”

如果这些理性的人拥有大社区精神性教导所提供的大社区视角，哦，我的天哪。它触及了他们内心深处的恐惧和焦虑。他们会在那一刻感受到他们是多么的脆弱，多么的不设防，多么的毫无准备。你看着人们，他们毫无头绪。他们也不想知道。你看着你的世界，你会说：“哦，我的天哪！我们可能在不知不觉中就被超越了！”

If you did not have the preparation in Greater Community Spirituality, if you did not have anywhere to go with this awareness, it could be overwhelming because you would think that you had no recourse. It is as if humanity, like an unsuspecting native tribe, were just waiting to be taken over by someone else.

如果你没有在大社区精神性中做好准备，如果你没有带着这份觉知去任何地方，它可能会令你感到不知所措，因为你认为你无路可走。人类就像一个毫无戒心的土著部落，等待着被其他种族接管。

Then, of course, there is the problem with the assumption that technology equals salvation. This is becoming a modern religion in many cultures in the world.
