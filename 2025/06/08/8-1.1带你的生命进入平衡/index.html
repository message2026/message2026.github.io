第一章：带你的生命进入平衡

发表于2025-06-08|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息其他教导
|总字数:13.9 k|阅读时长:58 分钟

Bringing Your Life into Balance

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on October 6, 2008 in Boulder, Colorado
2008 年 10 月 6 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

Bringing your life into balance means that you are addressing all of the key areas of your life, and you are doing this in such a way that your life can establish a stable continuity in the face of changing circumstances.

带你的生命进入平衡意味着你正在解决生命中的所有关键领域，并且你正在以这样的方式做到这一点，即你的生命可以在不断变化的情况下建立稳定的连续性。

When most people think of balancing their life, they are really thinking of catching up, of trying to deal with something that has been neglected. Therefore, they have to put a great deal of energy into one area of their life just to correct the problems there, to plug up the leaks, to deal with an emerging difficulty. But that does not mean that they are achieving a balance.

当大多数人想平衡他们的生命时，其实是在想着弥补，想着努力处理那些被忽视的事情。因此，他们不得不在他们生命的某个领域投入大量精力，只是为了纠正那里的问题，堵住漏洞，应对新出现的困难。但这并不意味着他们已经达到了平衡。

First, it is necessary to realize that a balance is really just a range of experience. It is not one fine point that you achieve and then you are able to maintain from there on.

首先，有必要认识到，平衡实际上只是一种经验的范围。它并不是你达到了一个微妙的点，然后你就可以从此保持下去。

A balance is something that requires constant adjustments. Like sailing a ship out on the open ocean, you are constantly having to deal with changing circumstances. You are constantly dealing with going off course in your life, having to readjust your navigation to bring yourself back on course and to re-establish balance.

平衡需要不断调整。就像在浩瀚大海上航行的船，你必须不断应对不断变化的环境。你不断应对人生中偏离航向的危险，需要重新调整航向，带你自己回到正轨，重新建立平衡。

Life is always moving, and so balance can never be a static state or one kind of experience only. If the fulcrum point upon which you are trying to balance never moved or never changed, then you could over time establish a very stable equilibrium. But that fulcrum point is constantly moving, and so balance is important.

生命总是在运动，因此平衡绝非静止状态，也绝非仅仅一种体验。如果你试图保持平衡的支点从未移动或改变，那么随着时间的推移，你就能建立一个非常稳定的平衡。但这个支点总是在移动，因此平衡至关重要。

To be in a position to experience balance on an ongoing basis, you must be building the Four Pillars of your life—the Pillar of Relationships, the Pillar of Work and Providership, the Pillar of Health and the Pillar of Spiritual Development. Like the four legs of a table, the Four Pillars of your life uphold your life. And if these Pillars are built sufficiently, giving you stability, then you can assume greater tasks. You can experience a greater purpose. You can achieve greater things.

为了能够持续体验平衡，你必须构建你生命的四大支柱——关系支柱、工作与供养支柱、健康支柱以及精神发展支柱。如同桌子的四条腿，你生命的四大支柱支撑着你的生命。如果这些支柱得到充分构建，赋予你稳定性，那么你就能承担更伟大的任务。你就能体验到更伟大的目的。你就能成就更伟大的事。

A table is a solid object. It is standing on solid ground. It is not moving, in most cases. But life is not just a stationary object, and it is always moving. The balance is elusive and even out of reach for people because they have not built the foundation that is necessary in order to achieve and experience a greater balance in life.

桌子是固体，立在坚实的地面上，大多数情况下静止不动。但生命并非静止不动，它始终在运动。平衡难以捉摸，甚至对人们来说遥不可及，因为他们尚未建立实现和体验更伟大生命平衡所必需的基础。

Here the Pillars are very important. Most people put a lot of energy into one Pillar alone, to the neglect of the others. As a result, the Pillars that have not been built are always undermining their progress and their well-being and their stability, making balance an elusive and impossible achievement.

在这里，支柱非常重要。大多数人只把大量精力投入到某个支柱上，而忽略了其他支柱。结果，那些尚未建成的支柱总是在损害他们的进步、福祉和稳定，使平衡成为难以捉摸、不可能实现的成就。

Some people give everything to their relationships: the pursuit of relationships, negotiation in relationships, the management of relationships. Meanwhile, the Pillar of Work is undeveloped, they are financially unstable, their personal health can be neglected, even completely, and perhaps they have no inner life and are not developing their awareness of the deeper spiritual power within themselves.

有些人为了人际关系倾尽所有：追求人际关系、在人际关系中协商、管理人际关系。与此同时，他们的工作支柱尚未开发，经济不稳定，个人健康可能被忽视，甚至完全被忽视，或许他们缺乏内在生命，也从未培养对他们内在更深层次精神力量的觉知。

Then there are others who give everything to their career and to the accumulation of wealth, or who are fascinated by the economy through their investments and so forth. Likewise, they can sacrifice the quality of their relationships, allowing their marriage to deteriorate, being estranged even from their own children. They may have no inner life at all, and their health may be in great jeopardy.

还有一些人，他们把一切都交给了事业和财富的积累，或者通过投资等方式沉迷于经济。同样，他们也可能牺牲人际关系的质量，导致婚姻破裂，甚至与他们自己的孩子疏远。他们可能完全没有内心世界，健康也可能面临巨大的危​​险。

Over time, those Pillars that have not been built will undermine your progress. Even if you are able to keep going, they will erode away your experience of well-being. For if you do not have healthy, meaningful relationships, then that will erode away your sense of well-being. If you do not have financial stability, that will erode away your well-being and your stability. If you do not have good health that can be sustained into the future, this will undermine your well-being and can even undermine your entire life. If your Pillar of Spiritual Development is neglected, this will rob you of the power of Knowledge within yourself—the ability to see, to know and to act with certainty. It will make you a stranger unto yourself, and as a result a stranger unto others.

随着时间的推移，那些尚未建立的支柱将损害你的进步。即使你能够继续前进，它们也会侵蚀你的福祉体验。因为如果你没有健康、有意义的关系，那么这将侵蚀你的幸福。如果你没有经济的稳定，那么这将侵蚀你的福祉和你的稳定。如果你没有能够持续到未来的良好健康，那么这将损害你的福祉，甚至可能损害你的整个人生。如果你的精神发展支柱被忽视，这将夺走你内在内识的力量——去看见、去知道并带着确定性行动的能力。这将使你对你自己成为一个陌生人，并因此对他人成为一个陌生人。

You must be operating in all four of these arenas. And when you look at your life objectively, you will see that everything you do falls into one or more of these Pillars. So if you are really engaged with your life to this degree, you cannot afford to be eccentric. You cannot afford to be self-obsessed. You cannot be addicted to anything because you are giving your attention to so many things at once.

你必须在这四个领域都运作。当你客观地审视你的生命时，你会发现你所做的一切都落入了这些支柱中的一个或多个。所以，如果你真的如此投入你的生命，你就承受不起古怪。你承受不起自我痴迷。你不会对任何事上瘾，因为你同时关注着太多的事情。

It is like, again, sailing a great ship at sea. It has complex rigging, complex sails. You have to manage this instrument, this boat that is taking you across very unpredictable circumstances, called life.

它就像驾驶一艘巨轮在海上航行。它有复杂的索具，复杂的船帆。你必须驾驭这艘工具，这艘载你穿越难以预测的境遇的船，它被称为生命。

Here if you are paying attention to your environment, if you are paying attention to your own condition, if you are paying attention to the Four Pillars of your life, you do not have time to be self-obsessed or obsessive about anything.

在这里，如果你关注你的环境，如果你关注你自身的状况，如果你关注你生命的四大支柱，你就没有时间自我痴迷或对任何事情着迷。

Problems will arise that will require extra effort, of course. Unforeseen difficulties will occur, problems in relationships, even personal loss and tragedy. But you can weather these storms if you have the strength of these Pillars. You will have sufficient support. You will have sufficient mental and physical health. You will have sufficient financial stability, not to be overwhelmed by the events of life.

当然，会出现一些需要额外努力的问题。不可预见的困难将会发生，人际关系出现问题，甚至个人的损失和悲剧。但如果你拥有这些支柱的力量，你就能渡过这些风暴。你将获得足够的支持。你将拥有足够的身心健康。你将拥有足够的经济稳定，不会被生命中的事件压垮。

This has now become ever more important because Great Waves of change are coming over the horizon—converging, creating a set of currents and cross-currents that will be very difficult to navigate, more difficult than even the normal parameters of life. This will require a greater stability, a greater self-awareness, a greater responsibility, a greater caution and care.

这现在变得比以往任何时候都更加重要，因为变革的巨浪正在地平线上涌来——它们汇聚在一起，创造出一系列非常难以驾驭的潮流和逆流，甚至比生命的正常参数还要困难。这将需要一种更伟大的稳定性，一种更伟大的自我觉知，一种更伟大的责任感，一种更伟大的谨慎和关怀。

You cannot be fooling around in the face of these Great Waves, for they are powerful. And you cannot run away from them, for they will affect everyone.

面对这些巨浪，你不能敷衍，因为它们威力巨大。你也不能逃避，因为它们会影响到每个人。

Great Waves of change are largely the result of humanity’s misuse and overuse of the world—depleting resources, degrading environment, violent weather, growing economic and political instability, and the risk of conflict and war.

变革的巨浪很大程度上是人类对世界的错误使用和过度使用的结果——资源枯竭、环境恶化、恶劣天气、经济和政治不稳定性增加、冲突和战争的风险。

The New Message from God that has been sent into the world reveals the real nature and the potential of these Great Waves of change, and why they cannot be resolved with simple solutions. For they will change the landscape of humanity, and they will change the conditions of your life.

来自上帝发送给世界的新讯息揭示了这些变革巨浪的实际自然和潜力，以及为什么它们无法用简单的解决方案来解决。因为它们将改变人类的面貌，它们将改变你们生命的状况。

Humanity is heading into stormy waters and times of great upheaval and uncertainty. Many people do not have the stability to be able to maintain themselves in any kind of healthy manner in the face of these Great Waves of change. For they have not built the Pillars of their life, and they have not looked carefully and objectively and courageously to see what is coming over the horizon, to see what they will have to contend with, and what they must change and focus upon in their life.

人类正在走向风暴的水域、巨大的动荡与不确定性的时代。面对这些巨大的变革浪潮，许多人无法以任何健康的方式保持他们自身的稳定。因为他们没有构建他们生命的支柱，他们没有仔细、客观、勇敢地看向地平线上即将发生的事情，没有看到他们将不得不面对的一切，看向他们在生命中必须改变和聚焦的内识。

Here the importance of looking ahead is very significant. You do not want to be caught off guard by great events. And great events always have signs preceding them, giving warning, giving an alert.

在这里，展望未来至关重要。你不想被重大事件打个措手不及。重大事件总是有预兆，发出警告，发出警报。

Here life is telling you what is coming. It is warning you of danger. It is alerting you to the presence of conflicting forces and changing circumstances. It is giving you time to reconsider your position and to change your position if that is necessary.

在这里，生命正在告诉你即将发生的事情。它正在警告你危险。它正在提醒你注意冲突力量和变化境况的存在。它正在给你时间重新考虑你的位置，并在必要时改变你的位置。

But people are not looking. They are in avoidance. Perhaps they feel overwhelmed already and think they cannot take on any more concern. But really this is just an excuse to not pay attention to your life and to your environment.

但人们并没有关注。他们在逃避。也许他们已经感到力不从心，认为他们无法承担更多的责任。但实际上，这只是一个借口，让你不去关注你的生命和周围的环境。

Your environment includes not only the natural world and all of its forces, but also your social and economic environment as well. These will undergo profound and at times wrenching change.

你们的环境不仅包括自然界及其所有的驱动力，还包括你们的社会和经济环境。这些都将经历深刻的、有时甚至是痛苦的变革。

So balance here becomes something that comes after one has built a strong enough foundation within the Four Pillars of their lives. Otherwise, balance is kind of an elusive dream. It is something that will take your eyes off of the horizon. You will become only concerned with what you have now, trying to balance that, and not become mindful of the great change that is approaching.

因此，平衡是人们在生命的四大支柱中建立足够坚实基础之后才能实现的。否则，平衡就像一个难以捉摸的梦。它会让你的目光远离地平线。你会只关心你现在拥有的一切，努力去平衡它们，而忽略了即将到来的变革巨浪。

Look at the animals in nature. They are always watching. They have to do this in order to maintain safety in their environments and to be alert to the presence of danger or changing circumstances. No matter what they are doing, they are always watching. They are always alert.

看看大自然中的动物。它们总是在观察。它们必须这样做才能维护周围环境的安全，并对危险的存在或不断变化的环境保持警惕。无论它们在做什么，它们总是在观察。它们总是保持警惕。

But, strangely, people—who have the greatest intelligence—are not watching and are not alert. They are trying to establish balance within their own current circumstances without realizing they must build a stronger foundation, and without realizing that their environment is changing.

但奇怪的是，那些拥有最强智能的人却没有观察，也没有警觉。他们试图在他们自身的现状中建立平衡，却没有意识到必须建立更坚实的基础，也没有意识到他们的环境正在发生变化。

This neglect of one’s environment is very tragic because people will fail for unforeseen reasons. They will lose their employment. They will lose, perhaps, their homes—to forces they did not foresee and did not anticipate. They were trying to establish balance, but they were not paying attention to the larger environment around them. And when you are not paying attention, you are not seeing the signs. You are not allowing life to alert you and educate you about what is coming and what is taking place around you.

这种对自身环境的忽视是非常悲惨的，因为人们会因为无法预见的原因而失败。他们会失去工作。他们或许会失去家园——因为他们没有预见到、没有预料到的力量。他们试图建立平衡，但却没有关注周围更广阔的环境。而当你不关注时，你就看不到迹象。你没有让生命警示你，教育你即将发生的事情以及你周围正在发生的事情。

Real balance here means that no matter what happens, you are able to stay afloat. You are able to maintain your inner stability. You are able to maintain objectivity and clarity about what you are doing and what you may have to do next.

真正的平衡意味着无论发生什么，你都能保持平衡。你能够保持内在的稳定。你能够客观、清晰地看待你正在做的事情以及接下来可能要做的事情。

In light of this, most people are very ill prepared for the Great Waves of change. Even in the wealthy nations you see this, especially. People are obsessed in one part of their life, or trying to remediate another part of their life, or trying to deal with emerging problems all around them. But they do not have a strong foundation, and often they are not really paying attention to their environment.

鉴于此，大多数人对变革巨浪的准备非常不足。即使在富裕国家，你也尤其会看到这种情况。人们沉迷于他们生命的某个部分，或试图补救生命的另一部分，或试图应对周围不断涌现的问题。但他们缺乏坚实的基础，而且往往没有真正关注他们的环境。

Here if you are trying to repair a relationship that is in jeopardy, you may ignore the fact that your economic reality is falling apart. Here while you are trying to either become beautiful and magnificent in the area of health, or trying to deal with serious illness or a health problem, you may fail to see that your career or your employment will not survive the great economic changes that are coming. And all of a sudden you find yourself in financial difficulty.

如果你正试图修复一段岌岌可危的关系，你可能会忽略你的经济状况正在崩溃的事实。如果你正努力让你的健康变得光彩照人，或者努力应对严重的疾病或健康问题，你可能会忽略你的事业或工作将无法承受即将到来的巨大经济改变。突然之间，你就会发现你自己陷入了财务困境。

It takes a lot of work to build and maintain the Four Pillars of your life. But this work is healthy and redemptive. It keeps you out of trouble with yourself. It builds real stability and solidity in one’s life. And it will enable you to withstand great change and difficulty. It will enable you to withstand personal loss and confusion.

建立并维持生命的四大支柱需要付出很多努力。但这些努力是健康的，具有救赎作用。它能让你远离自我困扰，在生命中建立真正的稳定与坚固。它能帮助你经受住巨大的变化和困难，也能让你经受住个人的损失和困惑。

It is really unfortunate that this teaching on personal awareness and responsibility is not emphasized throughout children’s education. People are encouraged to pursue their dreams, to follow whatever they want that fascinates them or that they think will make them wealthy or successful.

令人遗憾的是，这种关于个人觉知和责任的教育并没有在儿童教育中得到强调。人们被鼓励去追求他们的梦想，去追寻任何令他们着迷或认为能让他们致富或成功的事情。

They are not taught a wise approach to life. They are not taught about the meaning of their social and financial environment. They are not connected to the natural forces of the world.

他们没有学会如何明智地对待生命。他们没有学会如何理解他们的社会和经济环境。他们没有与世界的自然力量建立联系。

So they launch themselves obsessively on their pursuit of love, or their pursuit of wealth, and they do not know how to balance their lives. And they are not encouraged to restrain themselves from overinvesting in any area of their lives.

因此，他们痴迷地追求爱情或财富，却不知道如何平衡生命。他们也不被鼓励克制他们自己，不要在生命中的任何领域过度投资。

People have spent too much. They have overextended themselves. And now the Great Waves are coming, affecting the economic stability of nations. People suddenly find themselves in jeopardy, overwhelmed by their circumstances—circumstances that could have been foreseen, for life has been giving you the signs.

人们挥霍过度。他们过度扩张。如今，巨浪正在袭来，影响着各国的经济稳定。人们突然发现他们自己身处险境，被他们的处境压垮——这些处境原本是可以预见的，因为生命一直在给予你们迹象。

It takes a lot of work to be aware of all these things. But this awareness gives you strength. And if you are building your Pillar of Spiritual Development, you are gaining a greater connection to a deeper and wiser Mind within yourself—a Mind that is here to guide you, to protect you and to lead you to your fulfillment in service to the world.

要觉察所有这些迹象需要付出很多努力。但这种觉知会赋予你实力。如果你正在构建你的精神发展支柱，你就会与你内在一种更深层次、更明智的心灵建立一种更伟大的连接——这个心灵在此指引你、保护你，并引领你通过服务世界获得成就。

When you are maintaining a Four Pillars approach to life, you are allowing this Knowledge to give you the signs that you need and the indicators that you need in every aspect of your life, for you are looking at all of them at once. Here your natural inner guidance has the optimum opportunity to reveal itself to you and to be of service to you in your life.

当你秉持四大支柱的人生观时，你就是在让这内识赋予你生命中各个面向所需的迹象和指示，因为你正在同时审视它们。在这里，你自然的内在指引拥有最佳的机会向你展现，并在你的生命中为你服务。

Life in the world at this time is complex and can be very confusing. But this is your life, and this is the world that you have created with everyone else. But within you there is something very fundamental and very simple.

在这个时间，世界的生命是复杂的，可能非常令人困惑。但这就是你的生命，这是你和其他人共同创造的世界。但在你的内心深处，有一种非常根本且非常简单的东西。

It is the power and the presence of Knowledge. It is not confused or perplexed by the world around you. It is not seduced or intrigued by the world around you. It is not impressed by beauty, wealth or charm. It is not seduced by the desire for power, or by the fear of loss. It remains pure and potent within you, but it exists beyond the realm and the reach of your intellect.

它是内识的力量和存在。它不会被你周遭的世界所困惑或迷惑。它不会被你周遭的世界所诱惑或迷惑。它不会被美貌、财富或魅力所打动。它不会被对权力的渴望，或对失去的恐惧所诱惑。它依然纯粹而强大地存在于你的内在，但它存在于你的智力的领域和范围之外。

For in truth, you have two minds. You have the social mind that has been conditioned by the world, where you live every day. And then you have a deeper Mind that was created by God, that represents your true existence and the greater wisdom that God has given you to be able to navigate life in this world and all of the change that is coming to this world.

因为真理是，你有两个心灵。你有一个被你每天生活的世界所熏陶的社会心灵。然后你还有一个由上帝创造的更深层次的心灵，它代表着你真正的存在，以及上帝赋予你的更伟大智慧，使你能够驾驭这个世界上的生命，以及即将降临这个世界的所有变化。

You must take the steps to this Knowledge. You must connect your thinking mind with the deeper Mind within you if you are to benefit from its guidance, its wisdom and its power. It will bring you back into the world and require you to deal with the Four Pillars of your life, even if you have been avoiding any of them for a long time.

It does this not simply to meet a great inner need, but also to enable you to not only survive the Great Waves of change but to be of service to others. For it is within these Great Waves of change that your greater gifts to others will emerge and become known to you.

新讯息将你的视野、你的实力、你的能力以及有意义关系的力量和潜能回归你的生命。它这样做，不仅是为了满足你一种伟大的内在需求，也是为了让你不仅能够在变革巨浪中生存下来，还能服务他人。因为正是在这些变革巨浪中，你给予他人的更伟大礼物将会浮现并被你知道。

Everything here can work to assist you, to strengthen you and to empower you, but you must approach them correctly. You must build the Four Pillars of your life, and you must build them under the guidance of Knowledge within yourself for your actions to be truly effective and to have a permanent benefit.

这里的一切都能够帮助你、强化你、授权你，但你必须正确地对待它们。你必须建立你生命的四大支柱，你必须在你内在内识的指引下构建它们，这样你的行动才能真正有效，并拥有持久的益处。

The New Message from God reveals how this can be done, how to prepare, what you must see, know, and do. It builds a base of wisdom and competence within yourself, wisdom and confidence that you need today and that you will need increasingly in the times to come.

来自上帝的新讯息揭示了如何做到这一点，如何进行准备，以及你必须看见、知道和做的事情。它构建了你自己内在的智慧和能力的基础，这些智慧和信心是你今天需要的，并且你将在未来的时代越来越需要它。

Do not withdraw from the face of life, for you were sent here to serve this world under the very circumstances that are emerging now. Your social mind will want to hide and protect itself, but your deeper Mind is here for a greater purpose. But for this greater purpose to be realized, experienced and fulfilled, you must build a strong foundation within yourself and around yourself.

不要逃避生命，因为你被派遣来此，就是为了在当下正在呈现的境况下服务这个世界。你的社会心灵将想要隐藏和保护它自己，但你更深层次的心灵在此是为了一个更伟大目的。但为了让这个更伟大目的得以实现、体验和成就，你必须在你内在以及在你周围构建一个坚实的基础。

Do not simply trust change because change can destroy you. Do not simply have confidence that whatever happens is appropriate, for circumstances can conspire against you. Do not live on false assumptions. Do not be complacent or falsely self-assured, or you will not be alert and prepared and observant enough to recognize what life is telling you and to prepare accordingly.

不要轻易相信改变，因为改变会摧毁你。不要轻易相信无论发生什么都是适当的，因为情况可能会对你不利。不要生活在错误的假设之中。不要沾沾自喜或虚假地自我肯定，否则你将无法保持足够的警觉、准备和观察力，从而无法识别生命正在告诉你的事情，并做出相应的准备。

This is all a gift of love for you and for all of humanity. People are confused about love and fear. They think love is passivity and acceptance. They do not see the power of love to challenge people, to confront people, to demand things of people, to require courage and integrity from people, to require higher standards from people, to require selfless behavior from people. This is love.

这一切都是爱的礼物，赐予你，也赐予全人类。人们混淆了爱与恐惧。他们认为爱是被动和接受。他们看不到爱的力量：挑战他人，对抗他人，向他人索取，要求他人拥有勇气和完整性，要求他人拥有更高的标准，要求他人无私奉献。这是爱。

So much of what people think is love is really fear—the fear of not having, the fear of being inadequate or insufficient, the fear of being unacceptable, the fear of not surviving. So much behavior is put on top of this fear, and much of this behavior looks loving and caring and beatific. But underneath, what motivates it is fear itself.

人们认为的爱，很多其实是恐惧——害怕一无所有，害怕自己不够好或不足，害怕不被接受，害怕无法生存。很多行为都建立在这种恐惧之上，很多行为看起来充满爱意、关怀备至、充满幸福。但其深层含义，驱动这些行为的，其实是恐惧本身。

That is why, do not think you can use love and fear as the criteria for determining the reality of life around yourself. You do not even know what love and fear is, and their realities have become so intertwined and so twisted that it is very difficult for people to make clear distinctions here.

正因如此，不要认为你可以用爱与恐惧来判断你自己周围生命的现实。你甚至不知道爱与恐惧是什么，它们的现实已经如此交织、如此扭曲，以至于人们很难在这里做出清晰的区分。

That is why you need the power and the presence of Knowledge within yourself, for Knowledge is not confused about these things. God has given you Knowledge to guide you, to prepare you and to redeem you. And this Knowledge is prepared for the Great Waves of change. It is not threatened by them. This represents your greater strength and the great endowment from God.

正因为如此，你需要你内在内识的力量和存在，因为内识对这些事情并不困惑。上帝赋予你内识来指引你、准备你、救赎你。并且这个内识是为变革巨浪而准备的。它不会受到它们的威胁。这代表着你的更伟大实力和来自上帝的伟大恩赐。

That is why you must be a student of Knowledge if you are to fully benefit from the power and the potency of this greater Intelligence within yourself, and to encourage people’s awareness of this power and potency within themselves.

这就是为什么你必须成为一名内识的学生，如果你想充分受益于你自己内在的这种更伟大智能的力量和潜能，并鼓励人们觉知到他们自己内在的这种力量和潜能。

It will require great strength and fortitude, great compassion and great determination for humanity to face and to weather the Great Waves of change. For the demands of life will grow, exceeding what you experience now.

人类需要伟大的实力和毅力、伟大的同情心和伟大的决心来面对和抵御变革的巨浪。因为生命的要求将会增长，超越你们现在所经历的。

You must be prepared for this and to build your foundation in Knowledge while you have time, and to learn The Way of Knowledge so you may bring a greater strength and a greater clarity to your decisions.

你必须为此做好准备，并在有时间的时候在内识中建立你的基础，并学习内识之路，这样你就可以为你的决策带来一种更伟大的力量和一种更清晰的觉知。

You must find the source of your inner strength if you are to have the courage and the determination to overcome the limitations you have placed in your life already. This strength and this power lives within you now, waiting to be discovered.

你必须找到内在力量的源泉，才能有勇气和决心克服你已经在生命中设置的限制。这种力量现在就生活在你的内心，正等待着你去发现。
