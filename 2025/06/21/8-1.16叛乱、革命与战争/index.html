第十六章：叛乱、革命与战争

发表于2025-06-21|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息其他教导

Rebellion, Revolution and War

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on February 22, 2011 in Boulder, Colorado
2011 年 2 月 22 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

The freedom movements in the world are gaining momentum. Peoples long oppressed by crushing and unethical regimes are now rising up. They are rising up not only to gain freedom, but to gain more essential things—employment, food, water, security—the very basic things of life, the very basic things that people in all nations need.

世界的自由运动正在蓬勃发展。长期饱受压迫和不道德政权压迫的人民如今正在奋起反抗。他们奋起反抗不仅是为了获得自由，也是为了获得更重要的东西——就业、食物、水、安全——这些是生命的基本必需品，是所有国家人民都需要的基本必需品。

This is not simply a political phenomenon or a social phenomenon. It is in keeping with the Great Waves of change that are coming to the world. For as humanity grows and the world’s resources are expended and diminished, more and more people will be pressed to meet the basic requirements of life.

这不仅仅是一个政治现象或社会现象。它与正在降临世界的变革变革相符。因为随着人类的发展和世界资源的消耗和减少，越来越多的人将被迫满足基本的生命需求。

This will tip the scale, you see. Their long aggravation or political oppression, their long disenfranchisement of their rights will now be driven to extreme revolution for which people are willing to die and to sacrifice themselves—ordinary people, average people, people in many, many places.

你看，这将扭转乾坤。他们长期遭受的政治压迫，以及长期被剥夺的权利，如今将被推向极端革命，人们——普通人、普通民众，许许多多地方的人们——愿意为此献出生命，牺牲他们自己。

The revolutions that are happening in many countries, the struggle for freedom and justice and rule by law—all of these things now will be propelled forward by the impacts of the Great Waves of change. If a government cannot feed and provide for its people, and if it is too corrupt at its center, the Great Waves will forge an upheaval, an upheaval that will overcome even the most powerful and resistant governments and the most powerful and resistant overlords.

许多国家正在发生的革命，争取自由、正义和法治的斗争——所有这些现在都将在变革巨浪的影响下被推进。如果一个政府无法养活和供养它的人民，如果它的核心过于腐败，那么巨浪将锻造一场剧变，这场剧变甚至将战胜最强大、最顽强的政府和最强大、最顽强的霸主。

You are seeing this today, and you will see it tomorrow. But you must see it in context of the entire world and the condition of the world and not simply as a matter of politics or religion. If people do not have what they basically require for life, then they have no alternative but to rise up. If their needs are not met, if their expression of dissent is crushed, they will rise up.

你今天正在看到这一点，明天也会看到它。但你必须放在整个世界和世界现状的背景下看待它，而不能仅仅把它看作政治或宗教问题。如果人们得不到基本生命所需，那么他们别无选择，只能奋起反抗。如果他们的需求得不到满足，如果他们的异议表达被压制，他们就会奋起反抗。

But their problems are not over, even if they are successful. For they still have the dilemma, you see, of how will they feed their peoples, how will they employ their peoples, how will they provide a stable and just society? Even the greatest platform of political expression, even the most perfect system, will not be able to function if the basic resources are not there. Even if all the corrupted ones left or were removed, you still have this fundamental dilemma, you see.

但即使他们成功了，他们的问题也远未结束。因为他们仍然面临着困境：如何养活他们的人民，如何让他们就业，如何建立一个稳定公正的社会？即使是最伟大的政治表达平台，即使是最完善的制度，如果没有基本的资源，也无法运作。即使所有腐败分子都离开或被清除，你们仍然面临着这个根本性的困境。

So all of the resident problems and dissent and aggravation will be pushed to the boiling point by the power of the Great Waves of change. If the price of food continues to rise, revolutions will occur. If governments cannot provide for their peoples, revolutions will occur. Revolutions will be the only option that people have under these oppressive conditions.

因此，所有居民的问题、异议和愤怒都将被变革巨浪的力量推向沸点。如果食品价格持续上涨，革命就会发生。如果政府无法养活其人民，革命就会发生。在这些压迫性条件下，革命将是人们唯一的选择。

You are seeing this in the world today. It is part of the Great Waves of change. And though it looks encouraging from the standpoint of people finally, after such long oppression, gaining fundamental freedoms and rights, you must see that the great dilemma that they are facing is still before them. It is not just the challenge of establishing a just and balanced governance; it is the challenge of the Great Waves itself.

你在当今世界看到了这一点。这是变革巨浪的一部分。尽管从人们在如此漫长的压迫之后终于获得基本自由和权利的角度来看，这看起来令人鼓舞，但你必须看到，他们面临的巨大困境依然摆在他们面前。它不仅仅是建立公正和平衡治理的挑战；它是巨浪本身的挑战。

There are many poor countries in the world with very large populations. As food production declines in the future, how will these nations and governments provide for the basic needs of their people? How will they keep one part of their population or one tribe or one political persuasion from attacking another? How will they be able to maintain stability and control, even if they have good intentions and a just system?

世界上有许多人口众多、贫穷的国家。随着未来粮食产量下降，这些国家和政府将如何满足其人民的基本需求？他们将如何阻止一部分人口、一个部落或一个政治派别互相攻击？即使他们怀有良好的意愿并拥有公正的制度，他们又如何能够维持稳定和控制？

You can see that people who are immersed in political interests or political actions fail to see the bigger picture here. They think everything is a political process or a social evolutionary process, but they have forgotten nature. They have forgotten the thing that provides the most essential elements that they require to be able to survive in this world. They have forgotten where their food comes from.

你可以看到，那些沉迷于政治利益或政治行动的人，却看不到更广阔的视野。他们认为一切都是政治过程或社会进化过程，却忘记了自然。他们忘记了提供他们在这个世界上生存所需的最基本要素的东西。他们忘记了食物从何而来。

Politics is the means to achieve greater or lesser ends, but the greater and lesser ends are beyond the realm of politics itself. The intent to oppress a people and to become enriched by it is initiated before the political process is set in place over time. If the provision of the world is diminished, then the political process must try to adapt to this.

政治是实现或大或小目标的手段，但这些或大或小的目标都超出了政治本身的范畴。压迫一个民族并以此谋取利益的意图早在政治进程逐渐建立之前就已萌芽。如果世界供给减少，那么政治进程就必须努力适应这种情况。

This is why there is a New Message from God in the world, you see, for humanity is facing the risk of collapse—the collapse of human civilization, the collapse of a stable world and certainly a just world, the gradual and incremental collapse of everything that is established for the good, the means by which individuals and groups and nations carry on essential and valuable and beneficial activities, as well as those that tend to be destructive and foolish in nature.

正因为如此，世界上有一条来自上帝的新讯息，你看，因为人类正面临崩溃的风险——人类文明的崩溃，一个稳定的世界，当然还有一个公正世界的崩溃，一切为了善而建立的事物的逐渐和增量的崩溃，个体、群体和国家开展重要、有价值和有益活动的方式，以及那些本质上往往具有破坏性和愚蠢性的活动的方式。

People have lost their connection to nature. They do not see that where the food is grown and how it is transported and shared is the most essential network that there is, in any society or civilization. They have forgotten that the quality and the availability of water is more important than any other resource could possibly be. They fail to see that the degradation of the environment is setting in motion the forces that could undermine their world and their lives.

人们失去了与自然的联系。他们没有看见，食物的产地、运输和分享方式，是任何社会或文明中最重要的网络。他们忘记了水的质量和可用性比任何其他资源都更为重要。他们没有看见，环境的恶化正在引发可能破坏他们的世界和生活的力量。

This failure to respond to the Great Waves of change is a fundamental one, and that is why people do not see the coming storms. They do not recognize the great risk. They do not see that things are in motion, greater forces now, that will alter the landscape of the world. And their actions will become more desperate, more foolish and more shortsighted. And officials elected or who rise to power through force will be more ignorant and more blind.

这种未能回应变革巨浪的失败是根本性的，正因如此，人们看不到即将到来的风暴。他们没有认出巨大的风险。他们没有看到事态正在发展，现在正有更强大的势力，将改变世界的格局。他们的行动将变得更加绝望、更加愚蠢、更加短视。民选官员或通过武力上台的官员将更加无知、更加盲目。

If a nation cannot provide the basic elements for its peoples, even if it becomes a democracy, even if it achieves a just system, it will not be able to stand. It will never have stability. And the people will be in constant distress, leading to further upheavals, leading to war between factions and groups vying for power, not only for their own political or religious ideologies, but to survive.

如果一个国家无法为其人民提供基本生活保障，即使它成为一个民主国家，即使它建立了公正的体制，它也无法立足。它永远不会拥有稳定。人民将持续处于苦难之中，导致进一步的动乱，导致派系和群体之间争夺权力的战争，这不仅是为了他们自己的政治或宗教意识形态，也是为了生存。

This produces war at a level that is rarely seen in the modern world and has rarely been seen even historically. Yet it is a reality that has plagued the human family for the vast majority of its experience and existence here on Earth.

这导致了现代世界罕见、甚至历史上也罕见的战争。然而，这却是困扰人类大家庭在地球上绝大多数经历和存在的现实。

As nations become more desperate in providing for their people, they will not sell food abroad. They will not export the very things that they need now more than ever. And it will not only be individuals who will go hungry; it will be entire nations. This will lead to war, and a war of a very desperate kind, a war not for political achievement or not for revenge, but a war of desperation, a war of one people trying to eliminate another people. It would be the cruelest and the most savage and devastating kind of conflict.

随着各国越来越急于养活本国人民，他们不会将粮食出口到国外。他们不会出口他们现在比以往任何时候都更需要的资源。挨饿的将不仅仅是个人，而是整个国家。这将导致战争，一场极其绝望的战争，一场不是为了政治成就或复仇的战争，而是一场绝望的战争，一场一个民族试图消灭另一个民族的战争。这将是最残酷、最野蛮、最具破坏性的冲突。

This is a great risk now, you see, in the face of the Great Waves of change. If a nation cannot feed its peoples, it must look to the nations around it for help. If these nations are in the same desperate condition, then war will arise, on a small scale and on a great scale. People will take whatever they can and try to destroy those who compete against them.

你看，在变革巨浪面前，这如今是一个巨大风险。如果一个国家无法养活其人民，它就必须向周边国家寻求帮助。如果这些国家也处于同样绝望的境地，那么战争就会爆发，规模或大或小。人们会攫取一切他们能够攫取的东西，并试图摧毁那些与他们竞争的人。

This is very hard to imagine for the educated peoples of the world, who have lived in relative degrees of affluence. They cannot imagine brutality at this level, for it has not been their personal experience.

对于世界上受过教育、生活相对富裕的人来说，这很难想象。他们无法想象这种程度的残酷，因为他们从未亲身经历过。

And though wars, the great wars of the century, were immensely devastating and tragic, only in rare situations were entire populations destroyed. It was not the intent, in most cases, for this to occur.

尽管战争，尤其是本世纪的重大战争，都极具破坏性和悲剧性，但只有在极少数情况下才会造成全体人民的毁灭。在大多数情况下，这种情况并非有意为之。

Therefore, you must see revolution and war now within the context of the greater movement of the world. You must face the reality that the production of food will decline and will become more expensive to grow, to transport and to distribute.

因此，你现在必须把革命和战争放在世界更广阔运动的背景下看待。你必须面对这样一个现实：粮食产量将会下降，种植、运输和分配的成本也将变得更加昂贵。

With so much of the world living in poverty and in destitution, how can any nation provide for them—those who cannot afford to pay more for the essential products and resources of life; those who cannot withstand to go hungry for very long, for they are hungry already; those who cannot be patient for the food to arrive, for their needs are far greater and more pressing than for those who are wealthier and more comfortable?

世界上有这么多人生活在贫困和赤贫之中，任何一个国家如何能为他们提供食物呢？这些人无力支付更多钱来购买生活必需品和资源；他们无法忍受长期挨饿，因为他们已经饿了；他们无法耐心等待食物的到来，因为他们的需求比那些更富裕、更舒适的人更大、更迫切。

As the resources continue to slowly decline, the wealthy individuals and even wealthy nations will begin to hoard whatever they can. Seeing the possible risk of deprivation, seeing the possible risk of being denied what they need in the future, they will begin to hoard. And that will begin a desperate pursuit to claim the remaining resources of the world and the remaining wealth of the world.

随着资源持续缓慢减少，富人乃至富裕国家都会开始囤积一切可能的东西。他们意识到可能面临被剥夺的风险，意识到未来可能得不到所需，于是开始囤积。这会导致他们不顾一切地攫取世界上剩余的资源和财富。

God knows this. And that is why there is a New Message in the world—to warn people, to prepare people and to call upon the greater spiritual Intelligence within people of all faiths and nations and economic status. For there is a far better alternative, you see.

上帝知道这一点。正因如此，世界上才有一条新讯息——警示人们，准备人们，并召唤所有信仰、国家和经济地位的人们内在的更伟大精神智能。因为，你看，还有一个更好的选择。

The Great Waves of change, though immensely dangerous—so dangerous they could crush human civilization—provide another alternative. And that is for nations to cooperate, to assure for their own populations and for their neighbors as well, the availability of the essential resources of life.

变革的巨浪，尽管极其危险——危险到足以摧毁人类文明——却提供了另一种选择。那就是各国合作，确保本国民众以及邻国获得必要的生命资源。

Instead of spending fortunes on building huge military empires, they will have to spend fortunes on building food production and the purification of the world’s rivers and the transportation of food wherever it is needed and the relocation of vast populations who are now driven from their homes and their lands because they cannot live there anymore.

他们不必花费巨资建立庞大的军事帝国，而是要花费巨资进行粮食生产、净化世界河流、将粮食运输到任何需要的地方，以及重新安置那些因无法再居住而被迫离开家园和土地的大量人口。

The priorities of government will change dramatically. The priorities of financial investment will change dramatically. The people of the wealthy nations will have to accept the prospect of having to take care of someone who must escape a desperate situation, to take an individual or even a family, if possible, to provide shelter and a home for them.

政府的优先事项将发生巨大变化。金融投资的优先事项也将发生巨大变化。富裕国家的人民将不得不接受这样的前景：他们必须照顾那些必须逃离绝境的人，收留一个人，甚至一个家庭，如果可能的话，为他们提供住所和家。

People in the wealthy nations would have to give up the idea of growth and expansion and consider they are facing a reality and a new world—a world in decline where stability and security become essential, and the welfare of people of all nations and faiths and colors is the top priority.

富裕国家的人民将不得不放弃增长和扩张的想法，并考虑他们所面临的现实和新世界——一个衰落的世界，稳定和安全变得至关重要，所有国家、信仰和肤色的人民的福祉都是重中之重。

This would be a 180-degree change from what nations are doing today. Nations are not preparing for the future. They are not preparing for the Great Waves of change. They are preparing for the reality of the past, which they think will continue indefinitely into the future.

这与各国当前的做法将发生180度大转变。各国并未为未来做准备。他们并未为变革的巨浪做准备。他们正在为过去的现实做准备，而他们认为这种现实将无限延续到未来。

They think they are going to vie for power in a more difficult world perhaps, but they do not see that it will be a new world, requiring the kind of change that humanity will need to build a new foundation for human civilization and to survive the Great Waves of change.

他们或许认为他们将在一个更加艰难的世界中争夺权力，但他们没有看到这将是一个新世界，需要人类需要的那种改变，以便为人类文明构建一个新的基础，并在变革的巨浪中生存下来。

This is a difficult message to provide, but an essential one. At this moment, there are many people who cannot hear these things. It is too terrifying. It is too upsetting to their plans and goals. It brings forth their insecurities, their hostilities and their anxiety about the future.

这是一个难以传达的讯息，但却至关重要。此刻，许多人无法听到这些话。它太令人恐惧了。它太扰乱他们的计划和目标了。它引发了他们的不安全感、敌意和对未来的焦虑。

But the New Revelation must be given, and the time is critical. The time when it must be received and the time when it must be accepted is critical. It must go around the world. It must be in every country. This does not mean that everyone will learn of it or accept it, of course, but it does mean that it is active and alive, for it points to a different kind of future, a different kind of preparation.

但新启示必须被赋予，时机至关重要。它必须被接收的时间，它必须被接受的时间，都至关重要。它必须传遍世界。它必须存在于每个国家。当然，这并不意味着每个人都会学习它或接受它，但它确实意味着它是活跃的、鲜活的，因为它指向一种不同的未来，一种不同的准备。

And even more fundamentally, it points to a change of heart in people around the world. If they value what civilization provides, which is wealth, security, stability and opportunity—things which primitive peoples rarely ever experienced—then they will have to join together to build this for the future. It will not be a one-world government. That is silly. But it will be a world of cooperation because the alternative is too terrible to consider.

更为根本的是，它表明世界各地人民的内心正在转变。如果他们珍视文明所提供的财富、安全、稳定和机遇——这些是原始民族鲜少体验到的——那么他们就必须团结起来，共同建设未来。它不会是一个世界政府。这是愚蠢的。但它将是一个合作的世界，因为另一种选择太可怕了，令人难以想象。

The choices here are very limited because the world has changed. It is not that the world will change in ten years or twenty years or fifty or a hundred years. It has changed. You are not living in the world you were living in twenty years ago.

这里的选择非常有限，因为世界已经改变了。世界不会在十年、二十年、五十年或一百年后改变。它已经改变了。你现在生活的世界已经和你二十年前不一样了。

Nature will now determine the terms of engagement. People’s belief and hubris about technology will be overcome by the realities of nature. If you cannot create or gain access to food and essential materials for life, your wondrous little technological gadgets are not going to help you. They are not going to get you through the day. And their unimportance will become ever more significant.

大自然现在将决定参与的条件。人们对科技的信仰和傲慢将被自然的现实所克服。如果你无法创造或获取食物和生命必需品，你那些奇妙的小科技产品就无法帮助你。它们无法帮助你度过每一天。而且，它们的不重要性将变得越来越明显。

This is the world you are moving into. The revolutions that are occurring in the world now are but a preamble for what is to come. Though they are promising, and you will wish the peoples of these countries success, and your heart will go out to them, and you will wonder how you can help them in some way, you must see this within a bigger picture. You must recognize that these are the opening acts to a far greater drama that humanity will have to face.

这就是你正在迈向的世界。世界上正在发生的革命，仅仅是即将到来的序曲。尽管它们充满希望，你将祝愿这些国家的人民取得成功，你将同情他们，你将思考如何才能以某种方式帮助他们，但你必须以更广阔的视野看待这一切。你必须认识到，这些是人类将要面对的一场更伟大戏剧的开场。

This drama will not only be a tragedy. It will also be an opportunity, but one that is so demanding that it can provoke a change of heart in people. The wealthy now will want to help people increasingly because they realize that everything that they value and everything that they own are at stake. People will want to support the poorer nations because they cannot afford to have them collapse and descend into chaos. Nations will have to work together and reinvest in a stable economy not based on growth and endless expansion, but upon stability and security and humanity.

这场戏剧不仅是一场悲剧。它也将是一个机遇，但这个机遇的要求很高，它可以促使人们改变内心。现在的富人会越来越想帮助人们，因为他们意识到他们所珍视的一切和他们所拥有的一切都岌岌可危。人们会想要支持较贫穷的国家，因为他们无法承受这些国家崩溃、陷入混乱的后果。各国必须携手合作，重新投资于一个稳定的经济，它并非建立在增长和无休止扩张的基础上，而是建立在稳定、安全和人道的基础上。

The very rich will have to give up their very silly little games, their indulgences and addictions, to really get out and help people. Otherwise, they too will be overthrown. The anger towards the rich will become so severe that the rich will have nowhere to run in the future if the world goes out of control.

富豪们必须放弃他们那些愚蠢的小把戏、放纵和瘾癖，才能真正走出去帮助他人。否则，他们也将被推翻。对富人的愤怒将变得如此强烈，以至于如果世界失控，富人将来将无处可逃。

For the world to go out of control would be a very easy thing to create. It would not take much for this to happen. It is based upon essential things.

世界失控是很容易发生的。这不需要太多努力就能实现。它基于一些基本的事物。

If food production were severely damaged all at one time, it could set in motion forces that would be very difficult or impossible to control. The wealthy nations could not send food to the poor nations because they could not gain access to it. It would not be available, for everyone would be hoarding now, protecting their own populations. You can give away your surplus, but if you have no surplus, you have nothing to give in this regard.

如果粮食生产一下子遭受重创，可能会引发难以控制甚至无法控制的局面。富裕国家无法向贫穷国家运送粮食，因为他们无法获得粮食。由于现在每个国家都在囤积粮食，保护他们自己的人口，所以粮食供应不足。你可以捐出你的剩余粮食，但如果没有剩余粮食，你就没有什么可以捐献的了。

This is a real wake-up call for the peoples of the world. But they must see beyond their grievances and their political agendas and their tribal struggles and their hostilities towards other groups and other nations and begin to see that humanity will have to unite or it faces inevitable failure.

这对世界各国人民来说是一个真正的警钟。但他们必须超越他们的怨恨、政治议程、部族斗争以及对其他群体和国家的敌视，开始认识到人类必须团结起来，否则将面临不可避免的失败。

Even the resources of the world could not be squandered on a war. Every city is important. You cannot destroy a city now. You cannot destroy the infrastructure because you may not be able to rebuild it. You cannot destroy a nation through invasion because you may not have the resources to rebuild it because the world has changed.

即使是世界资源也不能浪费在战争上。每座城市都很重要。你现在不能摧毁一座城市。你不能摧毁它的基础设施，因为你可能无法重建它。你也不能通过入侵摧毁一个国家，因为你可能没有资源重建它，因为世界已经发生了改变。

The wealth to do those sorts of things is no longer there. The needs of the peoples of each nation will escalate so much that whatever wealth the nation possesses will be tapped to the absolute limit.

做这些事情所需的财富已经不复存在。各国人民的需求将急剧增加，以至于该国所拥有的财富将被消耗到极限。

The world has changed. You are facing the Great Waves. You must prepare. But you cannot prepare yourself adequately without a greater Revelation. For it is not a matter of physical effort or political will alone. You must have a greater strength and a greater compassion.

世界已然改变。你们正面临巨浪。你们必须进行准备。但如果没有更伟大的启示，你就无法充分地准备你自己。因为这不仅仅是物质努力或政治意愿的问题。你必须拥有一种更伟大的力量和一种更伟大的同情心。

These are something that reside within the hearts of people everywhere—buried beneath oppression, buried beneath tragedy, buried beneath political dissent and political conflict, buried beneath hostility and unforgiveness.

这些都存在于世界各地人们的心中——被压迫所埋葬，被悲剧所埋葬，被政治异议和政治冲突所埋葬，被敌意和不宽恕所埋葬。

The power of the Revelation, the power of God, who has now spoken to the world again, can make the critical difference. It is not the answer to everything, but it is a vital and essential element in the resolution that humanity will have to forge to avoid total calamity in the future.

启示的力量，上帝的力量，如今再次向世界讲话的力量，能够带来至关重要的改变。它并非万能的答案，但它是人类为避免未来彻底灾难而必须做出的决心中至关重要且不可或缺的要素。

It is an essential element in your preparation for the Great Waves of change. It is an essential element in your ability to bring your life into balance, harmony and a greater order so that you will have the luxury to be able to assist others and will not be a casualty yourself.

它是你为变革巨浪做准备的一个关键要素。它是你使生命回归平衡、和谐和更伟大秩序的一个关键要素，这样你才能有幸去帮助他人，而你自己不会成为牺牲品。

It will call from you the greater purpose that has brought you into the world, for God has sent you into the world to be in the world at the time of Revelation and at the time of the Great Waves of change.

它将召唤你来到这个世界的更伟大目的，因为上帝派你来到这个世界，是为了在启示的时代、在变革巨浪的时代身处这个世界。

It is also the time when humanity will gain contact with other races in the universe, who are here to take advantage of a weak and divided humanity. This complicates the picture tremendously. But just like the Great Waves of change, this Intervention into the world can forge a greater human unity and collective strength and determination.

这也是人类与宇宙中其他种族建立联系的时间，这些种族来此是为了利用人类的软弱与分裂。这极大地复杂化了局面。但正如变革的巨浪一样，这次对世界的干预能够铸就更伟大的人类团结、集体力量和决心。

You face competition from the outside for the value of this world by small non-military groups who can only succeed through persuasion and deception, and you face risk of collapse from within the world itself.

你们面临着来自外部的、只有通过说服和欺骗才能成功的小型非军事团体对这个世界的价值的竞争，而且你们还面临着来自世界内部崩溃的风险。

As people begin to exceed what the world can provide, they have to face the consequences of damaging the world’s climate and the world’s environments. Having abused and squandered your natural inheritance, you will now have to face the consequences of this.

当人类开始超越世界所能提供的资源时，他们必须面对破坏全球气候和环境的后果。你们滥用和浪费了你们的自然遗产，现在必须承担由此造成的后果。

Any really honest and visionary person can see this. It is not a mystery that is clouded or so obscure that people cannot see it at all. It is elemental to your nature, but people have lost contact with their deeper and more natural nature. People have lost contact with the foundation of their existence. People have lost contact with nature itself.

任何真正诚实且富有远见的人都能看到这一点。它并非一个云雾缭绕、晦涩难懂、人们根本无法察觉的奥秘。它是你自然的根本，但人们已经失去了与他们更深层次、更自然的本性的联系。人们已经失去了与他们存在根基的联系。人们已经失去了与自然本身的联系。

People are living in a world of human politics, human economics, human speculation and human fantasy—being heedless and foolish, squandering the wealth of the world as quickly as possible with nothing saved for the future, with no thought for the future, with no thought for the well-being of children and their children and the kind of world they will have to face and live in.

人们生活在一个由人类政治、人类经济、人类投机和人类幻想组成的世界里——粗心大意、愚蠢至极，尽可能快地挥霍世界财富，没有为未来储蓄任何钱，没有为未来考虑，没有为孩子及其孩子的福祉考虑，也没有为他们将要面对和生活的世界考虑。

What kind of ignorance, what kind of irresponsibility, what kind of self-serving and self-gratifying approach is this? You are not living in a world that has wildernesses waiting to be discovered, continents waiting to be discovered or vast resources waiting to be utilized.

这是何等的无知，何等的不负责任，何等的自私自利和自我满足？你生活的世界，并没有等待被探索的荒野，等待被发现的大陆，等待被利用的丰富资源。

With a few small exceptions, you have come to the final limit, and the world has been damaged and changed. It is a new world now. And this is the world you have come to serve. This is what your greater purpose and your greater nature are really about, you see, so very different from what you think or believe or how you occupy yourself or utilize your time.

除了少数例外，你们已经到达了最终的极限，世界已经遭到破坏和改变。现在，这是一个新的世界。而这正是你来此服务的世界。这才是你更伟大目的和你更伟大自然的真正意义所在，你看，这与你所想、所信，或者你如何度过你自己或善用你的时间，如此截然不同。

This is why the shock of deprivation, the shock of revolution and the shock of war must awaken people now. It is not simply that humanity has a few problems. It is not simply that humanity has big problems. Humanity is facing that which can undermine its foundation. This is happening now.

正因如此，贫困的冲击、革命的冲击和战争的冲击现在必须唤醒人们。人类面临的问题不仅仅是一些，也不仅仅是大问题。人类正面临着可能破坏其根基的威胁。而这一切正在发生。

Therefore, you must see that God’s Revelation will reveal what is coming over the horizon. It will speak of what is to come and what humanity must do to avoid catastrophe. It will reveal your deeper nature to you and bring harmony and balance to your life. It will call you to become of service. It will call you to be of service, to the people you know and to people you do not know. It will call out of you your essential gifts, which never would arise under a life of indulgence and mindless affluence.

因此，你必须看到，上帝的启示将揭示地平线上正在发生的事情。它将讲述即将发生的事情以及人类必须做些什么来避免灾难。它将向你揭示你更深层次的自然，并为你的生命带来和谐与平衡。它将召唤你成为服务者。它将召唤你去服务，去服务你认识的人和你不认识的人。它将从你身上唤起你至关重要的礼物，这些礼物在放纵和盲目富裕的生命中永远不会出现。

People become wealthy and spend all their resources on their hobbies, or their appearance, or how they decorate their bodies, upon acquiring land and buildings and automobiles. What is this? This is the individual functioning without being connected to their deeper nature.

人们变得富有，却把所有资源都花在他们的爱好、外表或装扮上，买下土地、房屋和汽车。这是什么？这说明个体在运作，却没有与他们更深层次的自然相连接。

It is not evil. It is only blind. But its tragedy exists beyond the emptiness that that person will feel as they proceed in life. It now extends to the condition of the world, for overconsumption is the engine of war. Fundamentally, this is the case.

它并非邪恶，只是盲目。但它的悲剧性远不止于此人日后人生中感受到的空虚。如今，它已蔓延至世界，因为过度消费是战争的引擎。从根本上来说，情况确实如此。

Therefore, listen with your heart. This is not about ideas. This is not about possibilities. This is not about what you want. This is not about your theory—your political or social theory. This is not about your opinions. This is not about your grievances.

因此，请用心聆听。这无关乎想法，无关乎可能性，无关乎你想要什么，无关乎你的理论——你的政治或社会理论，无关乎你的观点，无关乎你的不满。

This is about the reality of the world and the reality you are going to have to live in and the choice before you as to how you will live in this world—whether you can face it or not, whether you can prepare for it or not, whether you can serve it or not.

这是关于世界的现实，你已经生活的现实，以及你面前关于你将如何生活在这个世界上的选择——你是否能够面对它，你是否能够为它做准备，你是否能够服务它。

Receive then God’s New Revelation. Do not condemn it at the outset, for it is here to save humanity and to provide humanity what it cannot provide itself. It is a gift from the Creator of all life, for God has spoken again at a time of greatest peril and greatest change and challenge to the human family.

那么，接受上帝的新启示吧。不要一开始就谴责它，因为它在此是为了拯救人类，并为人类提供其自身无法提供的一切。它是来自所有生命创造者的一份礼物，因为在人类大家庭面临最大危险、最大变革和挑战的时间，上帝再次讲话了。

Let this be your understanding.

让这成为你的理解。
