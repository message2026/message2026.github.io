第二十章：伟大的协调

The Great Coordination

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on October 25, 2016 in Boulder, Colorado
2016 年 10 月 25 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

Today We shall speak on The Great Coordination.

今天我们来讲伟大的协调。

Beyond what your senses can report, what your eyes can see and your ears can hear, there is a Greater Coordination in the world and in the universe at large—a Great Coordination, the Work of God in the universe, working through layers of servants and representatives.

超越你的感官所能报道的，你的眼睛所能看见的，你的耳朵所能听见的，在世界上以及在广阔的宇宙中存在着一个更伟大的协调——一个伟大的协调，上帝在宇宙中的工作，通过层层的服务者和代表进行着工作。

It is something you must feel, deeply—this Presence, and this abiding sense that your life is really for something greater than what you have become accustomed to and what your culture promotes within you and you have come to believe. A Greater Coordination that has brought you into the world for a higher purpose, a greater coordination that you cannot define and you can never fully understand, for it extends far beyond this world into countless worlds in the universe. So great it is that no theology could contain it, no human understanding could grasp its expanse, its complexity and its perfect functionality.

它是你必须深刻感受到的——这种存在感，以及这种持久的感觉，即你的生命其实是为了某种比你已经习惯的、你的文化在你内心提倡的、你已经开始相信的更伟大的生命。一种更伟大的协调，它为了一个更高宗旨将你带到这个世界上，一种你无法定义、你永远无法完全理解的更伟大的协调，因为它远远超越这个世界，延伸到宇宙中的无数世界。它是如此伟大，以至于任何神学都无法容纳它，没有任何人类理解能够把握它的广袤、它的复杂性和它的完美功能性。

For you live two lives. You live a life in the mundane world that preoccupies you 99 percent of the time, and you live as part of this Greater Coordination, which you may glimpse for moments here and there—moments of clarity, perhaps even moments of great sobriety, where you reconsider your life from a more objective perspective.

因为你过着两种生命。一种是生活在世俗世界的生命，它占据了你99%的时间；另一种是作为这种更伟大协调的一部分的生命，你或许偶尔会瞥见它——清晰的时刻，或许甚至是极其清醒的时刻，你会从一个更客观的视角重新审视你的生命。

But the Coordination lives within you at a deeper level, beneath the surface of your mind, beneath your social mind, your worldly mind that has been so conditioned and shaped by your experiences in the world and by the decisions and conclusions you have made for yourself.

但是协调存在于你内在的一个更深层次的层面，存在于你心灵的表面之下，存在于你的社会心灵之下，存在于你的世俗心灵之下，世俗心灵被你在世界上的经历以及你为你自己做出的决定和结论所熏陶和塑造。

Deep within you there is a sacred Mind—a Mind created by God, a Mind We call Knowledge, a Mind that is connected already to the Great Coordination. For Knowledge represents your connection to God, but it also represents your purpose for being here, a purpose you have not invented for yourself, a purpose that you do not yet understand. Even if you have a very spiritually oriented understanding, it is something greater than this.

在你的内心深处，有一个神圣的心灵——一个由上帝创造的心灵，一个我们称之为内识的心灵，一个早已与伟大协调联接的心灵。因为内识代表着你与上帝的联接，但它也代表着你身处此地的目的，一个你尚未为你自己发明的目的，一个你尚未理解的目的。即使你拥有一种非常精神的理解，它也比这更伟大。

For Knowledge represents the part of you that is eternal, but it is here on a mission, you see. It has a greater purpose for being in the world, which represents your greater purpose. Beyond all your goals and plans, or what you think life requires of you, this greater purpose, this Greater Coordination, lives within you.

因为内识代表着你永恒的那部分，但它在此肩负着一个使命，你看。它身处世界有着一个更伟大目的，它代表着你的更伟大目的。超越你所有的目标和计划，或者你认为生命对你的要求，这个更伟大目的，这个更伟大协调，活在你的内在。

While you seek union in relationships, so hopelessly; while you seek to fulfill yourself in the world, so hopelessly; while you seek to meet the deeper need of your soul by acquiring things, people, places, possessions, everything, or keeping yourself stimulated at all times, what will really satisfy you and fulfill you and return you to your true relationships in the universe is the Greater Coordination itself.

当你如此绝望地寻求关系中的结合时；当你如此绝望地寻求在世界上实现你自己时；当你通过获取事物、人物、地方、财产、一切，或时刻保持自我激励来满足你灵魂更深层次的需求时，真正让你满意、让你充实并让你回归宇宙中真正关系的是更伟大的协调本身。

It will always be mysterious, for you can only grasp your small part of it and how it might relate to certain important people you may be fortunate enough to meet in your journey here on Earth.

它永远是神秘的，因为你只能掌握其中的一小部分，以及它可能与你在地球旅程中幸运遇到的某些重要之人有何关联。

God is working throughout the universe to reclaim the separated, for all sentient beings living in the physical reality are living in Separation from their eternal home and union with God. Everyone has come into this reality seeking to have a different experience, seeking to individualize themselves completely and give themselves form, and live in a reality where there is very little evidence of their true nature, their true home, their true origin or their true destiny. An expanding universe of ever-changing events—chaotic it is, unpredictable, difficult, challenging, dangerous, yet beautiful and promising and important if you can grasp it from a greater perspective, from the Greater Coordination.

上帝正遍及整个宇宙，努力唤回分离者，因为所有生活在物质现实中的有情众生，都生活在与他们永恒家园以及与上帝的结合的分离之中。每个人来到这个现实，都是为了寻求一种不同的体验，寻求彻底地个体化他们自己，赋予他们自己形态，并生活在一个几乎看不到他们真正自然、真正家园、真正起源或真正天命迹象的现实中。一个不断扩张、事件不断变化着的宇宙——它混乱、难以预测、艰难、富有挑战、危险，然而如果你能从一个更伟大的视角，从更伟大的协调中去把握它，它也是美丽的、充满承诺的、重要的。

God works through the Higher Powers in the universe, which represent the Angelic Assemblies that attend all worlds where sentient life has either originated or has colonized…the Higher Powers, existing beyond the visible range but ever watchful, ever attentive to those who remain below.

上帝通过宇宙中的更高力量来工作，这些更高力量代表着天使群体，他们出席所有有知觉生命起源或殖民的世界……更高力量存在于可见范围之外，但始终警惕，始终关注着那些留在下面的生命。

You cannot comprehend what this means yet, for you are dominated by your senses and by this temporary reality that you live in. But the Greater Coordination is in you and all around you.

你还无法理解这意味着什么，因为你被你的感官和你所生活的这个暂时的现实所支配。但是更伟大的协调就在你之内，就在你身边。

The Higher Powers are directing those individuals who at the highest levels represent the Networks of the Wise: unique individuals in small groups, operating usually in secrecy in the Greater Community, for there are very few free worlds that would accept them and support them—the Networks of the Wise, who respond to a greater directive from above, directing their activities, their skills, superseding their human nature or the nature of whoever they are in the universe.

更高力量正在指引那些在最高层面代表智者网络的个体：小团体中的独特个体，通常在大社区中秘密运作，因为很少有自由世界会接受他们并支持他们——智者网络，他们响应来自上层的更伟大指令，指引他们的活动、他们的技能，超越他们的人性或他们在宇宙中任何生命的自然。

They have their supporters and their assistants, who protect them, who guard them, who carry out their directives in the world, who support their activities. There are many of these, particularly in your world where there is still sufficient freedom for the Network of the Wise to function in greater openness, greater freedom, in many places here.

他们有支持者和助手，保护他们，守护他们，执行他们在世界上的指令，支持他们的活动。有许多这样的人，特别是在你们的世界，在这里的许多地方，智者网络仍然有足够的自由，可以更开放、更自由地运作。

It is a perfect Plan, but it must function in a changing and unpredictable reality, ever adapting itself to changing circumstances, unexpected events, for the Wise in any world cannot foresee everything nor understand the meaning of all things. The Higher Powers need this Network of the Wise as their representatives down here on the ground.

它是一个完美的计划，但它必须在不断变化、难以预测的现实中运作，不断适应不断变化的环境和意料之外的事件，因为任何世界的智者都无法预见一切，也无法理解所有事物的意义。更高力量需要这个智者网络作为他们在地上的代表。

The Higher Powers must oversee many greater things and cannot be involved in the details of daily life. Therefore, do not think they attend to you personally as if they are your personal assistants or attendants, for this is hardly the case. You may be visited periodically to restrain you from harming yourself or giving your life away to something that has no purpose, meaning or success. But do not think the Angelic Presence stands by you at all times, for this is hardly the case. For God has put Knowledge within you to guide you. It is part of the Greater Coordination.

更高力量必须监管许多更伟大的事情，无法介入日常生命的细节。因此，不要认为他们会像你的私人助理或侍从一样亲自照顾你，因为这几乎不可能。你或许会定期受到探访，阻止你伤害你自己，或将你的生命交给某些没有目的、意义或成功的事情。但不要认为天使存在会一直陪伴着你，因为这几乎不可能。因为上帝已经将内识置于你的内在来指引你。它是更伟大协调的组成部分。

God does not have to run your life. God does not have to run the world. For all of the biological and geological forces were set in motion at the beginning of time, everywhere. It is all running itself now, so do not think that God is creating the calamities or the events of your day. That is a foolish, ignorant error and will only lead you into greater confusion and misunderstanding.

上帝不必掌控你的生命。上帝不必掌控世界。因为所有生物和地质力量在时间之初就已启动，无处不在。如今一切都在自行运转，所以不要认为上帝创造了你今日的灾难或事件。这是一个愚蠢、无知的错误，只会让你陷入更大的困惑和错误理解。

It is God’s Plan to keep Knowledge alive in this world in enough individuals to guide humanity through the dangerous events to come—through the great decline of your world, through your contact and dangerous encounter with forces from the universe who are here to take advantage of humanity.

上帝的计划是让内识在这个世界上存活，存在于足够多的个体中，从而引导人类渡过即将到来的危险事件——渡过你们世界的大衰落，渡过你们与来自宇宙势力的接触和危险遭遇，这些势力在这里是为了利用人类。

God’s Plan is to ignite Knowledge in enough people here in the world through God’s New Revelation for the world, to steer humanity in a positive direction and to keep Knowledge alive here—a light that has gone out in so many places in the universe, so many technological races that have lost the gift of Revelation. Their only hope now is that the Network of the Wise may exist within them, deep in secrecy, functioning under very difficult and dangerous circumstances. For such great power and freedom is feared by governments even within your own world, and especially in the Greater Community, where there is very little personal freedom and where the Power of Heaven is either forgotten or is greatly feared.

上帝的计划是通过上帝致世界的新启示，点燃世界上足够多的人的内识，引导人类走向积极的方向，并让内识在此保持活力——这道光在宇宙中的许多地方已经熄灭，许多技术种族已经失去了启示的礼物。他们现在唯一的希望是，智者网络或许能够存在于他们之内，深藏秘密，在非常艰难和危险的境况下运作。因为如此伟大的力量和自由，即使在你们自己的世界里，尤其是在大社区里，也遭到各国政府的畏惧，在那里，个人自由非常匮乏，天国的力量要么被遗忘，要么被极度恐惧。

It is an education you are only now beginning to have because God is throwing open the doors of the universe to teach you about life in the universe and the Work of God in the universe. Never before has this been revealed to humanity, for it was not needed. But it is needed now greatly as you stand at the threshold of this Greater Community of life, and as your world declines and you are facing the greatest threshold that humanity has ever faced, or might ever face, at this great turning point in your evolution.

这是你现在才开始拥有的一种教育，因为上帝正在敞开宇宙的大门，教导你关于宇宙中的生命以及上帝在宇宙中的工作。这从未被揭示给人类，因为它过去并不需要。但现在，当你们站在这个生命大社区的门槛上，当你们的世界正在衰落，当你们正面临人类在进化的这个伟大转折点上曾经面临过，或者可能面临过的最伟大门槛时，它被极大地需要。

It is a time of great promise for a promising race, but there are many hazards and many dangers here, and many errors that people are making and will make that could destroy the great opportunity for humanity, which is still alive in your world.

对于一个拥有伟大承诺的种族来说，这是一个充满伟大承诺的时期，但是这里存在着许多危害和许多危险，人们正在犯和将要犯的许多错误可能会毁掉人类的伟大机会，而这个机会在你们的世界上依然生机勃勃。

You are preoccupied with yourselves, with your needs, your issues, your fears, your desires, your predicaments. And this keeps you from experiencing the power of the Greater Coordination within your life. And indeed you cannot simply believe it is going to take care of everything for you as if you are a witless person who must be directed in all things, for this will hardly be the case.

你们专注于你们自身，专注于你们的需求、你们的问题、你们的恐惧、你们的渴望、你们的困境。这阻碍了你体验你生命中更伟大协调的力量。事实上，你不能简单地相信它会为你照顾好一切，仿佛你是一个愚昧的人，需要在所有事情上都得到指引，因为这几乎不可能。

You must become strong and determined, competent and responsible in all things, or the Greater Coordination will remain in the background, and your haphazard life will continue regardless of your beliefs and assumptions.

你必须变得坚强、坚定、有能力、对所有事情都负责，否则伟大的协调仍将处于次要的位置，无论你的信仰和假设如何，你杂乱无章的生命仍将继续。

What We are presenting here today requires a great preparation to comprehend and to experience authentically and consistently. And that is why through God’s Revelation for the world, the Steps to Knowledge have been given, the ancient preparation in the restoration of individuals and in the hope for races everywhere. It is a miracle of untold significance that you have such a preparation now.

我们今天在此呈现的一切，需要一种伟大的准备，才能真正且持续地领会和体验。正因如此，通过上帝给世界的启示，内识进阶被赋予了，这是在个体复兴中以及在世界各地种族的希望中所做的古老准备。你现在拥有这样的准备，是一个意义非凡的奇迹。

Do not think you can prepare yourself. Do not think you are ready. Oh, my goodness. Do not think that your ideas and beliefs are sufficient for your greater needs and for your ability to navigate the great change that is coming to the world. Do not think your faith in the Divine is sufficient if you will not prepare, if you will not take these Steps to Knowledge, if you will not reform your life and allow it to be shaped by the Great Coordination that lives within you.

不要认为你能够准备你自己。不要认为你已经准备好了。哦，我的天哪。不要认为你的想法和信仰足以满足你的更伟大需求，也不要认为足以满足你驾驭即将降临世界的剧变能力。如果你不去准备，如果你不去开展这些内识步骤，如果你不去改造你的生命并让它被活在你内在的伟大协调所塑造，就不要认为你对神圣的信仰就足够了。

This is the Revelation that you have been waiting for, but you are uncertain if you are willing to take it on, to accept its challenge and its gift and its promise. This is your ambivalence, which weakens you and keeps you from clearly seeing the path you must follow and the work you must do and the decisions you must make. This is the challenge for all who can receive something of this magnitude.

这是你一直在等待的启示，但你不确定你是否愿意接受它，接受它的挑战、它的礼物和它的承诺。这是你的矛盾心理，它削弱了你，阻止你清晰地看到你必须遵循的道路，你必须做的工作，你必须做出的决定。这是对所有能够接收这种宏大事物的人的挑战。

Heaven knows what this means. Heaven knows how important this is. Heaven knows the difficulties that stand in people’s way. It is the ambivalence about rejoining reality, the ambivalence about returning to the Great Coordination, of which you are intrinsically a part, regardless of your religious ideas and beliefs.

天国知道这意味着什么。天国知道这有多么重要。天国知道人们前进道路上的种种困难。这是对重新融入现实的矛盾心理，是对回归伟大协调的矛盾心理，无论你的宗教观念和信仰如何，你本质上都是伟大协调的一部分。

Even if you are opposed to religion, even if you are committed to a secular life, you have not changed anything regarding your true nature, circumstances or calling. Your position only either strengthens you or weakens you in your ability to respond. You cannot change what God has set forth for you.

即使你反对宗教，即使你致力于世俗生命，你也丝毫没有改变你的真正自然、情况或召唤。你的立场只会增强或削弱你回应的能力。你无法改变上帝为你安排的一切。

The gift before you now is the Gift, but it confronts you with your own ambivalence, your fear, your uncertainty, your grievances, your unforgiveness, your contempt for others and possibly your contempt for yourself. All these things must be cleared away as you proceed. And God has given you the Power of Revelation to enable you to do this, for on your own you could not do this.

现在摆在你面前的礼物是礼物，但它让你面对的是你自己内心的矛盾、你的恐惧、你的不确定、你的怨恨、你的不宽恕、你对他人的蔑视，或许还有你对你自己的蔑视。当你前行时，所有这些必须被清除。上帝赋予了你启示的力量，使你能够做到这一点，因为靠你自己你无法做到这一点。

The Great Coordination, which extends far beyond this world, far beyond this galaxy and to other galaxies, reaches all the way down inside of you to the most sacred and essential part of you. You are already wired into this great assistance, you see.

伟大的协调，远远超越了这个世界，远远超越了这个星系，延伸到其他星系，它深入到你的内心，直达你最神圣、最本质的部分。你看，你已经融入了这个伟大的协助之中。

It was created by God. It is overseen by the Higher Powers. It is carried out through the Networks of the Wise and all of their many assistants and supporters, in ways that are so great, so varied and so manifest, you could not account for it all. Even within your local environment, even within your town or city, you could not account for all of the work of the Great Coordination. You cannot yet even account for it within yourself, for you are just beginning that journey.

它由上帝创造。它由更高力量监管。它通过智者网络及其众多助手和支持者得以实现，其方式如此伟大、如此多样、如此明显，以至于你无法全部解释。即使在你的本地环境中，即使在你的城镇或城市中，你也无法解释伟大协调的所有工作。你甚至无法在你自己的内部解释它，因为你才刚刚开始这段旅程。

Do not worry about Hell and damnation, for that is a human invention. God will rescue you in time, but time is your problem, you see, for you do not have a lot of it. And you are beset with many things that are inappropriate for you, that are harming you and holding you back.

不要担心地狱和诅咒，因为那是人类的发明。上帝终将拯救你，但你看，时间才是你的问题，因为你没有多少时间。你被许多不适合你、伤害你、阻碍你前进的事情所困扰。

Perhaps you have given your life to people and circumstances that do not represent your true destiny here. And now you must face the fact that this must be unraveled to a certain extent so that you may be free enough to follow that which you must follow within yourself—the deeper Knowledge that is here to guide you in all things.

或许你把你的生命交给了那些并不代表你在此的真正天命的人和情况。现在你必须面对这个事实：这必须在一定程度上被解开，这样你才能足够自由地去遵循你必须遵循你内在的内识——在这里，指引你一切事物的更深层次的内识。

But you must do the work to open the way, to take the Steps, to engage with this greater process of reclamation within yourself. You do this for yourself most certainly, but you do it for others and for those you are meant to serve in this life, which accounts for more relationships than you can understand at this moment.

但你必须工作去打开道路，去迈出步骤，去参与你内在这个更伟大的唤回过程。你这样做当然是为了你自己，但你这样做也是为了他人，为了那些你注定在此生服务的人，这构成了比你此刻所能理解的更多的关系。

You are not called to be a great person here but only to carry out your simple and essential service. Do not assign roles to yourself in this way. Allow your life to open. Take the Steps to Knowledge, patiently, for the things you must learn along the way are the essential wisdom that you must have, the essential strength you must have, the essential discernment and discretion you must have, which can only be created by taking a journey of this magnitude and importance.

你并非被召唤来此成为一位伟人，而只是来履行你简单而必要的服务。不要以这种方式给你自己分配角色。让你的生命敞开。耐心地开展内识进阶，因为你沿途必须学习的事情，是你必须拥有的必要智慧，你必须拥有的必要实力，你必须拥有的必要辨识力和判断力，而这些只有通过踏上如此宏大而重要的旅程才能创造。

Do not think the Greater Coordination is running everything, for there is much chaos in the world. There is much danger and misery in the world. There is much destruction being carried out in the world because so many people live without this Knowledge. And so many people follow them. This is the great network of the foolish, the ignorant and the destructive.

不要认为更伟大的协调在掌控一切，因为世界上有很多混乱。世界上有很多危险和苦难。世界上有许多破坏正在进行，因为有许多人生活在没有这种内识的环境中。还有那么多人追随他们。这就是愚昧、无知和破坏者的巨大网络。

Do not think the world is as it is because the Great Coordination has fashioned it in this way, for it is truly in a dangerous and self-destructive pattern of events—destroying its climate; destroying its resources; contaminating its food, its water, its soils; engaged in constant conflict, competition and war.

不要认为世界之所以是现在这个样子是因为伟大协调这样塑造了它，因为它确实处于一种危险和自我毁灭的事件模式中——破坏它的气候；破坏它的资源；污染它的食物、水和土壤；陷入持续的冲突、竞争和战争。

The Greater Coordination must work in spite of all of these things, you see. Do not be lazy and just think that everything is being done for you or being managed by greater powers. You must become far more responsible and engaged than this. You cannot merely be a passenger on this ship. You must be part of the crew that is engaged in rescuing human civilization from self-destruction, a path it is most surely on at this moment.

你看，尽管存在所有这些情况，更伟大的协调仍必须运作。不要懒惰，不要只是认为一切都在为你完成，或被更伟大的力量掌控。你必须变得更加负责，更加投入。你不能仅仅是这艘船上的乘客。你必须成为拯救人类文明免于自我毁灭的船员的一员，而这正是它此刻无疑正在走的一条道路。

We, therefore, bring you great truth, great encouragement, great promise. But with this We bring great sobriety, great correction, great clarification. For without Knowledge, people will be in error and will continue to be in error and live a haphazard and destructive life—unfulfilling at least and destructive at the worst.

因此，我们为你带来伟大的真理、伟大的鼓励、伟大的承诺。与此同时，我们也带来伟大的清醒、伟大的修正、伟大的澄清。因为没有内识，人们将会陷入错误，并将继续陷入错误，过着杂乱无章、充满破坏性的生命——至少是没有成就感的，在最坏的情况下是毁灭性的。

That is why God is not going to condemn anyone because God knows without Knowledge to guide you, you will live a haphazard life full of error and misfortune. You will be subject to all the forces in the world. You will be shaped by your culture, your family and your religions in such a way that your chance of ever discovering your real nature will be extremely limited.

正因为如此，上帝不会谴责任何人，因为上帝知道，如果没有内识的指引，你将过着充满错误和不幸的杂乱无章的生命。你将受制于世界上所有的力量。你将受到你的文化、你的家庭和你的宗教的塑造，以至于你发现你真正自然的机会将极其有限。

The Great Coordination is something you must prepare for. Do not think by having grand ideas or egalitarian views that you can understand this or appreciate it or experience it with any potency. This is not the realm of ideas and belief that We speak of here today. This is a realm of true relationship of higher purpose working through your life, working throughout the universe.

伟大的协调是你必须为之做好准备的事情。不要认为凭借宏大的想法或平等主义的观点，你就能理解它、欣赏它或体验它，并拥有任何力量。这不是我们今天在此讲述的想法和信念的领域。这是一个更高宗旨的真正关系的领域，它贯穿你的生命，贯穿整个宇宙。

Here you might even have to give up your spiritual ideas because they are merely clouding your vision, or obstructing your view, or giving you fanciful notions that have nothing to do with reality—used to comfort yourself, used to give you a sense of security because you really are insecure.

在这里，你甚至可能不得不放弃你的精神想法，因为它们只是模糊了你的视野，或者阻碍了你的观点，或者给了你一些与现实无关的幻想——用来安慰你自己，用来给你一种安全感，因为你真的没有安全感。

No, you must face your condition very squarely, very objectively, very honestly to see the need for Knowledge within your life. It is a great reckoning. Living with layers of fanciful assumptions will only hold you back and give you a false sense of meaning and security here.

不，你必须非常坦率、非常客观、非常诚实地面对你的处境，才能看到你生命中对内识的需求。它是一个伟大的清算。生活在层层奇思妙想的假设中只会阻碍你，并给你一种虚假的意义和安全感。

Here your suffering can serve you if you can face it honestly, without self-condemnation. Here it brings you to your need for God, your need for Knowledge, your need for purpose and real meaning in life, your need for a Greater Coordination.

在这里，你的痛苦能够服务于你，如果你能坦诚地面对它，不带自我谴责。在这里，它带你去寻求你对上帝的需求，你对内识的需求，你对生命目的和真正意义的需求，你对一种更伟大协调的需求。

Heaven is waiting for you to reach out—honestly, deeply. You must send the sign to the Higher Powers that you are ready to have your true life restored, to begin to return to your fundamental relationship with your Source and with the Power of Heaven as it is working within people and through people and throughout the universe.

天堂正在等待你伸出援手——真诚地、深刻地。你必须向更高力量发出信号，表明你已经准备好恢复你真正的生命，开始回归你与你本源以及与天国力量的根本关系，因为它正在人们内在、通过人们、在整个宇宙中运作。

You may pray for little things: for needs, for comforts, for resolution to problems. But We are speaking of something much more important here.

你可以为一些琐事祈祷：为所需，为安慰，为问题的解决。但我们在这里谈论的是更重要的事情。

Heaven will allow you to live your haphazard life regardless of your opinions, your views, or your assumptions. For you are free to do this.

无论你的观点、看法或假设如何，天堂都会允许你随意地生活。因为你有自由这样做。

But Heaven is waiting for you to reach the point of great sobriety where you realize that you are not really living the life you were meant to live, that all you have built around you really is not that important, that there is something greater within you that must emerge—a great turning point in your life where you can begin the return to your true relationships and true purpose for being here. Heaven is waiting for you to reach this point, and your suffering will help you find it if you can really be honest about this.

可是天堂正在等待你达到一个极其清醒的节点，在那里你意识到你并没有真正过着你本该过的生命，你周围构建的一切实际上并不那么重要，你内在更伟大的内识必须浮现——你生命中的一个伟大转折点，在那里你能够开始回归你真正的关系以及身处此地的真正目的。天堂正在等待你达到这一点，如果你能真正坦诚地面对这一点，你的苦难将帮助你找到它。

The Great Coordination is at work everywhere—in some places to a greater extent, in others to a lesser extent, depending on the circumstances of those worlds and situations, the degree of freedom or lack of it that exists there, and how many can be prepared for a greater level of service: both amongst those chosen to be the Wise and all those who can assist them in specific and particular ways.

伟大的协调无处不在——在某些地方作用较大，在其他地方作用较小，这取决于那些世界和局势的境况，那里存在的自由程度，以及有多少人能够为更高水平的服务做好准备：既包括那些被选中成为智者的人，也包括所有那些能够以特定和特殊的方式帮助他们的人。

The prayer for you is: Let me be of service so that I may give. For in the end, it is all about simple service. No matter how difficult the journey, no matter how great the mountain is you must walk up, in the end it is all to assume a role that is yours to assume and to play your small but essential part in this Greater Coordination.

为你祈祷：让我服务，以便我能够给予。因为归根结底，这一切都是为了简单的服务。无论旅途多么艰辛，无论你必须攀登多么巍峨的高山，最终都是为了承担起你应该承担的角色，并在这场更伟大的协调中扮演你微小却至关重要的角色。

Here there are no superstars. Forget all that. Here there are no saviors and avatars and masters. Forget all that. In the Great Coordination, this does not matter. There are no heroes to worship. There is only the great Calling going out into the world, especially at this time in planet Earth because God’s New Revelation is here. It is so rare in the universe that a great Revelation can be given to a whole world, for most worlds do not have the freedom to allow anything like that to happen.

这里没有超级巨星。忘掉这一切吧。这里没有救世主、化身和大师。忘掉这一切吧。在伟大协调中，这些都不重要。没有英雄可以崇拜。只有伟大的召唤正在走向世界，尤其是在地球的这个时刻，因为上帝的新启示就在这里。在宇宙中，一个伟大的启示能够赐予整个世界是如此罕见，因为大多数世界都没有自由允许这样的事情发生。

Likewise, give up the notion that there are enlightened races in the universe. That is really not true. There are groups of the Wise in the network, but these are not whole races. Within Separation, that goal really cannot be achieved, except under very rare circumstances.

同样，放弃宇宙中存在开悟种族的想法。这根本不是事实。网络中确实存在智者群体，但这些群体并非整个种族。在分离的框架下，除非在极少数情况下，否则这个目标实际上无法实现。

Never think that an enlightened race is going to come to Earth to help humanity, for that is a total deception and confusion. God’s New Revelation for the world will prepare you for the reality of life in the universe and for the spirituality of life in the universe, which represents your true spirituality, free of human assumptions, ideas and beliefs.

千万不要认为一个开明的种族会来到地球帮助人类，因为那完全是欺骗和混淆。上帝赐予世界的新启示，将帮助你准备面对宇宙生命的现实，以及宇宙生命的精神性，这代表着你真正的精神性，摆脱了人类的假设、想法和信念。

For God’s Work is not for this one world alone, but in concert with God’s Work everywhere. This one world…a tiny grain of sand on a beach as far as you can see in the physical reality. But God is aware of your world. The greater Network of the Wise and the Higher Powers are at work everywhere in ways far beyond human understanding or the understanding of any race in the universe.

因为上帝的工作并非只针对这一个世界，而是与上帝在各地的工作相辅相成。这一个世界……在物质现实中，它不过是海滩上你所能看到的极致的一粒细沙。但上帝知晓你们的世界。更伟大的智者网络和更高力量正在无处不在工作，其方式远超人类的理解，也远超宇宙中任何种族的理解。

Hear Our words. Listen to them again and again. Comprehension will come in time with repetition. Think not you understand what We are saying. Do not agree with it or dismiss it based upon your personal viewpoint, for that is being stubborn and foolish.

聆听我们的话语。反复聆听。随着重复，理解自然而然会来。不要自认为理解了我们在说什么。不要基于个人观点而认同或否定它，因为那是固执和愚蠢的表现。

Your heart will know if you can follow this. Deeper within you, there are greater powers and forces at work. This is what you must discover. This is the great challenge and meaning of your life.

你的心会知道你是否能遵循这一点。在你的内心深处，有更伟大的力量和驱动力在运作。这是你必须发现的。这是你生命的伟大挑战和意义。
