第九章：剧变

发表于2025-06-04|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第五卷新世界预言
|总字数:13 k|阅读时长:55 分钟

The Upheaval

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on March 25, 2011 in Boulder, Colorado
2011 年 3 月 25 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

Great Upheaval is underway in the world, an Upheaval that will manifest itself in many ways—in the growing fragility and difficulty in nations’ economies; the declining resources of the world and the impact that that will have on nations and peoples everywhere; the natural disasters that will increase because humanity has changed the climate of the world; and the loss of food production and arable lands as humanity has squandered its natural inheritance.

世界正在经历大动荡，这场动荡将以多种方式表现出来——各国经济日益的脆弱和困难；世界资源的日益减少，并将对世界各国和各国人民产生影响；由于人类改变了世界气候，自然灾害将会增加；由于人类挥霍了自然遗产，粮食生产和可耕地将减少。

You are at the beginning of the Upheaval, and already you see it manifesting here and there and all around you. If you are paying attention, you will wonder what is happening to the world? Is this a world gone mad? You will feel that the stability of even your nation and the things you took for granted now are being questioned, and you will wonder how strong they really are?

你正处于动荡的开端，你已经看到它在你们周围四处显现。如果你留心观察，你会好奇这个世界正在发生什么？这个世界疯了吗？你会感觉到，甚至你国家的稳定，以及你曾经习以为常的一切，现在都受到了质疑，你会怀疑它们究竟有多强大？

Nations are deeply in debt. They cannot afford to repair the damages, or to help other nations in significant ways. Every nation in the world is facing the Upheaval. There is no nation or tribe that is going to be victorious in the face of it. Everyone will have to face the Upheaval, as it unravels, and its many unforeseen consequences.

各国深陷债务泥潭。它们无力修复损失，也无力为其他国家提供重大援助。世界上每个国家都面临着这场剧变。没有哪个国家或部落能够战胜它。每个人都必须面对这场剧变及其带来的诸多难以预料的后果。

If you are honest with yourself, and not simply projecting blame upon others, or trying to hide in your fantasies or distractions, you will begin to feel the changing world. And you will see perhaps—if your eyes are clear and your mind is still—you will see that you are entering a new kind of world, the new world. And in this new world there will be great disorder.

如果你对你自己诚实，而不是简单地把责任推卸给他人，或者试图躲藏在你的幻想或纷扰之中，你就会开始感受到这个世界的变化。你或许会看到——如果你的眼睛清澈，你的心灵宁静——你会看到你正在进入一个全新的世界，一个新的世界。而在这个新的世界里，将会存在巨大的混乱。

Here you cannot simply project the assumptions of the past. Things are not going to work like they did before. You cannot rest assured that everything will turn out, or that you are in some kind of economic cycle alone, or that prosperity and growth will return. In places it will, temporarily.

你不能简单地套用过去的假设。事情不会像以前那样发展。你不能确信一切都会好转，也不能确信你只是处于某种经济周期之中，也不能确信繁荣和增长会回归。在某些地方，繁荣和增长会暂时回归。

No, you are facing the Upheaval, the consequence of humanity’s misuse and overuse of the world for centuries, and the squandering of resources over war and conflict as if there was no thought of tomorrow, and the blind assumption that technology will solve all of these problems for you, giving you a wonderful, marvelous, miraculous future.

不，你们面临的是剧变，这是人类几个世纪以来对世界的滥用和过度使用的后果，是人类在战争和冲突中浪费资源的后果，仿佛没有明天，是盲目地认为技术会为你们解决所有这些问题，给你们一个美好、奇妙、神奇的未来。

But when technology fails, you can see the impact upon people’s lives, the unforeseen consequences. The more you are dependent upon technology, the more fragile your life will be, and the less aware you will be of your environment and the changing circumstances of your life.

但当科技倒下时，你会看到它对人们生命的影响，以及不可预见的后果。你越依赖科技，你的生命就会越脆弱，你对周围环境和生命变化的感知也会越少。

Technology has become a kind of god now, something that will redeem you and restore you in the face of whatever crisis you might be facing. But when technology itself fails you, you see how dependent you really are, and you see how that affects people everywhere in all directions, so that what happens in one nation is now felt in other nations. If one nation hits economic crisis or catastrophe, it is felt in other nations.

如今，科技已然成为一种神明，无论你面临何种危机，它都能救赎你、让你重获新生。但当科技本身令你失望时，你才会发现你是多么依赖科技，才会看到科技如何影响着四面八方的人们，以至于一个国家发生的事情，如今也会波及到其他国家。如果一个国家遭遇经济危机或灾难，其他国家也会感受到。

God knows this is coming, of course, and that is why there is a New Revelation in the world. For God has spoken again—to warn, to bless and to prepare a struggling and unprepared humanity; to save humanity from its own conflicts and folly, its own blind assumptions and its own critical mistakes. This is a Message for the world, not for one nation or group, or one echelon of society. It is to prepare you for a very unstable world, a changing world, a new world.

当然，上帝知道这将到来，正因如此，世界上才会有一个新启示。因为上帝再次讲话了——为了警告、为了祝福、为了准备一个挣扎且毫无准备的人类；为了拯救人类免于它自身的冲突和愚蠢，它自身的盲目假设和它自身的严重错误。这是一条致世界的讯息，而非致于一个国家或群体，或一个社会阶层。它是为了让你们为一个非常不稳定的世界，一个变化的世界，一个全新的世界进行的准备。

You will feel the Upheaval within your own life as things are challenged or fall away; as you are impacted by changes in your economy, changes in your circumstances, changes in your own experience. As people, places and things fall away from you, you will feel the Upheaval.

当一切面临挑战或消逝，当你受到经济、环境和自身经历变化的影响时，你会感受到你自身生命的剧变。当人、地方和事物离你而去时，你也会感受到剧变。

If you are honest with yourself and are not casting blame all around, you will begin to take stock of your own experience and learn over time how to face it objectively and to learn to see what it is telling you.

如果你对你自己诚实并且不到处责怪他人，你就会开始评估你自身的经历，并随着时间的推移学会如何客观地面对它，并学会看见它正告诉你的事情。

The poor are struggling and are facing even graver circumstances. The rich are embedded in their conflicts, their fantasies and their personal pursuits. They do not see what is coming. In either case, there must be a greater understanding and a greater compassion between the peoples.

穷人正在苦苦挣扎，面临着更严峻的环境​​。富人则沉浸在他们的矛盾、幻想和个人的追求之中。他们看不到即将发生的一切。无论是哪种情况，各国人民之间都必须增进一种更伟大的理解和一种更伟大的同情心。

The Upheaval has the power to undermine human civilization. It is more powerful than your technology. It is more powerful than your incentive, if you do not prepare. If you remain blind and preoccupied and do not see the Great Waves of change that are coming, then they have the power to overtake you.

这场剧变拥有摧毁人类文明的力量。它比你们的科技更强大。如果你们不做好准备，它比你们的动机更强大。如果你们继续盲目执着，看不到即将到来的变革巨浪，那么它们就拥有吞噬你们的力量。

The situation is far more dangerous and grave than people will account for. And if they will stop casting blame and stop running away to their distractions, their hobbies and their concerns, they will begin to see and feel the Great Waves of change moving. This will inform them as to what they must see, know and do.

形势远比人们所能想象的更加危险和严峻。如果他们停止责怪，停止逃避他们的纷扰、他们的爱好和他们的担忧，他们就能开始看到并感受到巨浪的推进。这将告知他们必须看见什么、知道什么并采取的行动。

Humanity is gravely unprepared for the Upheaval. It is gravely unprepared for its encounter with intelligent life in the universe, an encounter that is underway in the world by forces who are here to take advantage of a weak and vulnerable humanity.

人类对这场剧变严重缺乏准备。它对与宇宙智能生命的相遇严重缺乏准备，这场相遇正在世界上进行，而那些势力的目的就是利用孱弱易受伤害的人类。

This is why God has sent a New Revelation into the world. This is why there is a New Message. It must speak to many aspects of your life, and the Teaching must be thorough and complete—far greater than any Message that has ever been sent into this world by the Creator of all the universes.

正因如此，上帝向世界发送了一个新启示。正因如此，一条新讯息才会存在。它必须触及你生命的诸多面向，而且这个教导必须透彻而完整——远超所有宇宙的创造者曾经发送给这个世界的任何讯息。

This Message is to prepare you for the Upheaval, and to prepare you for your encounter with intelligent life in the universe, and to prepare you to bring an end to your ceaseless conflicts and to prepare to protect the world and human civilization.

这个讯息是为了让你们为剧变做好准备，为你们与宇宙智能生命的相遇做好准备，为你们结束无休止的冲突做好准备，为你们保护世界和人类文明做好准备。

To not see and to not prepare is not intelligent, and many people are not acting intelligently today. They are not looking and seeing. They are not putting the pieces of the puzzle together. They are not seeing the connection that what happens in nature will determine the fate and the future of humanity. They do not see that the consequences of your encounter with intelligent life in the universe—as that encounter is taking place at this moment—can affect the outcome for everyone in the world.

视而不见、不做准备是不明智的，而今天许多人的行动并不明智。他们没有观察，没有去看。他们没有把拼图的碎片拼凑起来。他们没有看到自然界发生的事情将决定人类命运和未来的联系。他们没有看到你们与宇宙智能生命相遇的后果——正如这场相遇此刻正在发生的那样——会影响世界上每个人的结局。

People are caught up in religion. They are caught up in politics. They are caught up in social movements. They do not see the bigger picture. They cannot see the larger panorama, and, therefore, they do not recognize the Upheaval and the Great Waves of change that it will produce.

人们陷入宗教。他们陷入政治。他们陷入社会运动。他们看不到更广阔的图景。他们无法看到更广阔的全景，因此，他们没有认出剧变以及它将带来的巨浪般的改变。

What humanity is going through is what countless races in the universe have had to face as they have degraded their own environments and despoiled their natural resources to the point where it threatens their survival and their existence. Countless races have become enamored with power and technology, often introduced by other races who seek to take advantage of their world’s wealth and possibilities.

人类正在经历的，正是宇宙中无数种族曾经面对的。他们破坏了他们自身的环境，掠夺了自然资源，以至于威胁到他们的生存和存在。无数种族沉迷于权力和科技，而这些权力和科技往往是由其他种族引入的，其他种族试图利用他们星球的财富和潜能。

You are following an evolutionary pattern that has been traveled countless times before by countless races in the universe. As nations grow and begin to technologically become more sophisticated and advanced, they begin to outstrip their world’s resources. They begin to overwhelm what their planet can provide and sustain. They believe that technology is wondrous and can solve all of these problems and mitigate all the consequences of the damage that they have done.

你们正在遵循一种进化模式，这种模式此前已被宇宙中无数种族无数次地演绎过。随着国家发展壮大，科技也开始变得更加精进和先进，它们开始超越其世界资源的承载能力。它们开始超越其星球所能提供和维持的极限。他们相信科技是神奇的，能够解决所有这些问题，并减轻它们所造成的所有破坏后果。

So it is not that humanity is exceptionally foolish in this regard. It is simply following a pattern. Not all nations in the universe have followed this pattern. Some have been wiser at the outset and were able to avoid the calamities that so many others had to face.

所以，人类在这方面并非异常愚蠢。它只是遵循一种模式。宇宙中并非所有国家都遵循这种模式。有些国家从一开始就更加明智，从而避免了许多其他国家不得不面对的灾难。

There is Intervention in the world today from races who are here to take advantage of the situation. They are cold and calculating. They see their opportunity. They know that humanity is following this pattern, and that it is entering a period of grave vulnerability and instability. This is a perfect environment to cast persuasion and to establish influence.

当今世界存在着来自某些种族的干预，他们来此是为了利用这一局势。他们冷酷而精于算计。他们看到了他们的机会。他们知道人类正在遵循这种模式，并且正在进入一个极其脆弱和不稳定的时期。这是一个进行说服和建立影响力的完美环境。

As people in the world lose faith in their governments and institutions, they will turn to other powers in the universe to guide them and to save them. It is the ideal environment for Intervention. It is part of the great Upheaval that people do not see.

当世界上的人们对他们的政府和机构失去信心时，他们将转向宇宙中的其他大国来指引他们、拯救他们。这是干预的理想环境。这是人们看不见剧变的一部分。

Even the wisest of the leaders, who see many aspects of the Upheaval, cannot really communicate what they see to their populations. They have to sustain the stability of their country today and tomorrow and meet all the immediate problems that are arising. They do not have the time or the consensus of support to really prepare for the big things in the future, the big changes that will come to their countries.

即使是最明智的领导人，即使他们洞察了剧变的方方面面，也无法真正将他们所看到的传达给民众。他们必须维护国家当前和未来的稳定，并应对所有正在出现的紧迫问题。他们没有时间，也没有共识，无法真正为未来的重大事件、即将降临国家的巨大变革做好准备。

What happens when they run out of fuel? What happens when water resources diminish significantly? What happens when other nations begin to abandon them, pulling away all of their investments?

如果燃料耗尽，会发生什么？如果水资源大幅减少，会发生什么？如果其他国家开始放弃这些项目，撤走所有投资，会发生什么？

These are the things you prepare for, for the future, for the Upheaval itself. But what leader in the world can do this and still meet the problems of the day, and the complicated networks of support and administration?

这些都是你们为未来、为剧变本身所准备的。但世界上哪位领导人能够做到这一点，同时还能应对当下的种种问题，以及错综复杂的支持和行政网络？

God knows this, and there is no blame. It is humanity acting out its long-established beliefs and assumptions, and following the weakness of its emphasis on living for the moment and not preparing adequately for the future.

上帝知道这一点，对此无可指责。它是人类在践行其根深蒂固的信念和假设，并遵循其只顾眼前而未充分为未来做准备的弱点。

At this time, humanity is sacrificing the future of its children. The world that they will have to face will be so much more arduous and difficult. Even if they have more advanced technology, where will be the energy to support it and to generate it? Where will their food come from? Will they have clean waters to drink as the nations become more arid and as populations become unstable?

此时此刻，人类正在牺牲子孙后代的未来。他们将要面对的世界将更加艰辛和艰难。即使他们拥有更先进的技术，又从哪里获得能源来支持和创造这些技术呢？他们的食物从何而来？随着各国日益干旱，人口日益不稳定，他们还能喝上干净的水吗？

To seek more growth at this time is to spend the future of humanity. But growth will be sought to meet the demands of today and the needs of the people in this moment and in the days to come.

此时寻求更多增长，就等于牺牲人类的未来。但增长的追求，不仅要满足当今的需求，还要满足人们此刻及未来的需求。

War and conflict will only squander the precious resources. Do not think that cities can be rebuilt and nations restored if they are devastated, for no one will have the resources to really do this. War and conflict, for whatever purpose, will only serve to hasten your demise.

战争和冲突只会浪费宝贵的资源。不要认为城市被毁后还能重建，国家被毁后还能复兴，因为没有人拥有真正做到这一点的资源。战争和冲突，无论出于何种目的，都只会加速你们的灭亡。

Even if conflicts are generated to support freedom, there will be a great cost, not only in this moment but in the future of that nation. This is not to say that freedom movements should not occur, but there must be a reckoning of the greater costs by all who are involved.

即使为了维护自由而引发冲突，也必然会付出巨大的代价，不仅在当下，而且在国家的未来。这并不是说自由运动不应该发生，而是所有参与者都必须考虑到更大的代价。

If leaders realized this, and their populations were dissatisfied, they would step aside. They would relinquish the reins of control. For the future of their nation is at stake—not just for today and tomorrow but for the years and decades to come.

如果领导人意识到这一点，而民众又不满意，他们就会下台。他们会放弃控制权。因为他们国家的未来岌岌可危——不仅是今天和明天，更是未来数年乃至数十年。

A hundred years from now, many nations in the world will not even exist. People will not even be able to live in them. There will not be enough food production, not enough water, not enough fuel to sustain a large population.

一百年后，世界上许多国家将不复存在。人们甚至无法在这些国家生存。粮食产量、水资源和燃料都不足以养活如此庞大的人口。

Between now and then, the great Upheaval will occur and is beginning to occur now. You cannot stop it. You can only mitigate it and prepare for it. You can prevent some of its graver consequences, but you cannot stop it entirely.

从现在到那时，大动荡将会发生，而且现在已经开始发生。你无法阻止它。你只能减轻它的影响并为之做好准备。你可以预防一些更严重的后果，但你无法完全阻止它。

Now is not [the time] to say, “What do I want out of life?” It is more appropriate to say, “What must I do?” That is the question. And that is the question that you must keep asking as every new situation emerges, as every unforeseen change occurs, as every new opportunity seems to arise—whether that opportunity be genuine or not.

现在不是问“我想要从生命中得到什么”的时候。更合适的问是“我必须做什么？”。这才是问题所在。当每一个新情况出现，当每一个不可预见的变化发生，当每一个新机会似乎出现——无论这个机会是否真实——时，你必须不断问这个问题。

You cannot be satisfied and rest on your assumptions. You cannot think your nation will survive anything and everything. You cannot think your economy is immutable. You cannot think that your leaders will know what to do in the face of growing crises occurring in many places all at once.

你不能满足于你的假设。你不能认为你的国家能够安然度过一切。你不能认为你的经济是不可改变的。你不能认为你的领导人能够知道在多地同时发生的日益严重的危机中该如何应对。

You can either panic and be afraid and try to go run and hide somewhere—which would be a grave and probably fatal mistake—or you can begin to gain access to the deeper Intelligence within you that God has put there to guide you, to protect you and to bless you in the face of the Upheaval.

你可以惊慌失措、害怕不已，试图逃跑、躲藏在某个地方——这将是一个严重的、甚至可能是致命的错误——或者你可以开始接触上帝放置在你内心深处的智能，上帝把它放置在你的内心深处，在动荡中指引你、保护你、祝福你。

You were sent into the world to live in the world at these great and momentous times. God has given you the clarity and the strength and the wisdom, but this exists beyond the realm and the reach of your intellect and stands apart from your social conditioning and your consensus beliefs, attitudes and assumptions.

你被派到这个世界，生活在这些伟大而重要的时间。上帝赋予你清晰性、实力和智慧，但这超越了你的智力所能及的范围，并且与你的社会熏陶以及你的共识信仰、态度和假设截然不同。

God has given you the power, but you must take the Steps to Knowledge to find it, to understand it and to discern what it means and how it is to be used appropriately in life. You cannot seize Knowledge and use it to increase your wealth, or to overtake other people or to establish your priorities. Knowledge is not a tool of the intellect. It is more powerful than the intellect.

上帝赋予了你力量，但你必须开展内识进阶去发现它，理解它，辨识它的意义以及如何在生命中恰当地运用它。你无法抓住内识并利用它来增加你的财富，或超越他人，或确立你的优先次序。内识并非智力的工具。它比智力更强大。

Wishing and praying and hiding will not help you, for the world is giving you signs as to what is coming, and to what you must see know and do, and the degree to which you must change your life and circumstances to find higher ground, to secure your own position so that you will have the ability and opportunity to help others.

希望、祈祷和躲藏都无法帮助你，因为世界正在向你发出迹象，告诉你即将发生的事情，你必须看见、知道并采取行动，你必须在多大程度上改变你的生命和境遇，以找到更高的境界，确保你自身的位置，这样你就有能力和机会去帮助他人。

For the human need will grow beyond what you have ever seen before, and the degree of service that must be rendered will be far greater than anything the human family has ever had to deal with on a large scale. The time of giving is just beginning.

因为人类的需求将远超以往任何时候，而需要提供的服务规模也将远远超过人类家庭以往所经历的任何大规模服务。给予的时间才刚刚开始。

The need will be so great that you will feel it is impossible to meet. But that does not mean that you must not do what you were sent here to do. And only Knowledge within you knows what that is—where you must go, whom you must meet, what you must undertake in your life and what you must release and set aside.

For this, you need a Revelation from the Creator of all life. You need God to help you. You need a greater wisdom. You need a greater strength. You need a greater determination that must come from a much more powerful part of yourself, which you are barely aware of.

你没有答案。没有人真正拥有这一切的答案，没有关于剧变和巨浪的答案。为此，你需要内识的力量和存在来指引你。为此，你需要来自所有生命创造者的一个启示。你需要上帝来帮助你。你需要一种更伟大的智慧。你需要一种更伟大的力量。你需要一种更伟大的决心，这必须来自你自己的一个更强大部分，而你几乎没有察觉到它。

It is not about what you want. It is what you must do. This will give you strength, courage and confidence if you can respond to this consistently and repeatedly.

它不是你想要什么的问题，而是你必须做什么的问题。如果你能持续不断地回应，这将赋予你力量、勇气和信心。

This is the warning and the blessing, but it is also a preparation. God has provided the Steps to Knowledge. God has provided the wisdom from the universe that can instruct you on how to face, comprehend and navigate a world undergoing Upheaval.

这是警告和祝福，但这也是一种准备。上帝提供了内识进阶。上帝提供了来自宇宙的智慧，能够指导你如何面对、理解和驾驭一个正在经历剧变的世界。

People have native skills that have become dormant from lack of use that can alert them to the presence of danger and to prepare them for the future with a greater wisdom and certainty. All these native skills are connected to Knowledge, you see.

人们拥有一些与生俱来的技能，由于缺乏运用而处于休眠状态，这些技能能够警示他们危险的存在，并以更伟大的智慧和确定性为未来做好准备。所有这些与生俱来的技能都与内识相连，你看。

For Knowledge to guide you, it must keep you alive. For Knowledge to fulfill itself through you so that you may give your true gifts to the world, it must protect you from destruction.

为了让内识指引你，它必须让你活下去。为了让内识通过你实现它自身，以便你能够将你真正的礼物给予世界，它必须保护你免遭毁灭。

That is why it is the great protector and sustainer. It is the redeemer. But it resides beyond the realm and the reach of the intellect, so you must go beyond your mind to hear it, to discern it and to respond to it. This is what it means to take the Steps to Knowledge.

正因如此，它是伟大的保护者和维系者。它是救赎者。但它超越智力的领域和范围，所以你必须超越你的心灵才能聆听它、辨识它并回应它。这就是开展内识进阶的意义。

Do not think or pretend that you can do this sufficiently today, for you are only a beginner. No matter how intuitive you think you are, you are still a beginner, for you do not yet understand what is coming to the world and what is here already. Your skills and your strengths are not yet sufficient to face these things and are barely adequate even to deal with the normal circumstances of your life.

不要认为或假装你今天就能充分做到这一点，因为你只是一名初学者。无论你认为你多么有直觉，你依然是一名初学者，因为你还不理解什么即将降临世界，什么已经存在。你的技能和你的力量还不足以面对这些，甚至勉强足以应对你生命中的日常处境。

To save humanity, you must prepare humanity. To assure the future and the stability of human civilization, you must prepare for the Upheaval and the Great Waves of change that it will produce.

要拯救人类，你必须让人类做好准备。为了确保人类文明的未来和稳定，你必须为剧变和它将产生的变革巨浪做好准备。

Do this, and you will serve the world as you were intended to serve the world. Do this, and your life will make sense, and you will no longer be in serious conflict with yourself. Do this, and your mind will change, and your heart will change, and your priorities will change—as they should.

这样做，你就能以你本该服务世界的方式服务世界。这样做，你的生命就会有意义，你就不会再与你自己产生严重的冲突。这样做，你的心灵就会改变，你的内心就会改变，你的优先事项也会改变——正如它们理所当然的那样。

Let this be your understanding.

让这成为你的理解。
