第二十二章：回归

发表于2025-06-27|更新于2025-06-27|来自上帝的新讯息其他教导

The Return

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on August 5, 2013 in Boulder, Colorado
2013 年 8 月 5 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

At a certain point in your life, if you are really honest with yourself and with your circumstances and agreements with others, you will come to a point where your life will begin to change fundamentally. This change will happen on the inside first because it is born of a deeper recognition, the recognition that you are not living the life you were meant to live and that you are not fulfilling the greater purpose that has brought you into the world.

在你生命的一个特定点，如果你真正坦诚地面对你自己，坦诚你的境遇以及与他人的约定，你将会到达一个点，你的生命将开始发生根本性的改变。这种改变将首先发生在你的内在，因为它源于一种更深层次的认知，即你没有过着你本该过的生命，你没有履行你来到这个世界的更伟大目的。

This stands in great contrast to your former emphasis, which is one of acquisition and advancement according to your ideas and the ideas of your culture and nation. Now something really different has happened. An awakening has begun—an awakening to a deeper realm within yourself, a deeper experience. And though it may seem momentary and fragmentary at this point, it is enough to initiate a great shift in your life.

这与你以前强调的获取和进步形成了巨大反差，你以前强调的是按照你的想法以及你文化和民族的想法获取和进步。现在，一些真正不同的事情发生了。一种觉醒已经开始——觉醒到你自己内心一种更深层次的领域，一种更深层次的体验。尽管此时此刻它可能看起来很短暂、很零碎，但它足以让你的生命发生伟大的转变。

This is the beginning of the return—the return to relationship and communication, the return to your connection with those who sent you into the world and with the greater purpose that has brought you here, a purpose that cannot be realized through intellectual inquiry alone, for it lives within you at this moment, waiting to be discovered.

这是回归的开始——回归关系和沟通，回归你与那些派你来到世界存在的联接，回归与带你来到这里的更伟大目的的联接，这个目的无法仅通过智力探索来实现，因为它此刻活在你的内在，正等待着被发现。

This has to do with your relationship with God and your Spiritual Family, who sent you here. It has to do with a deeper responsibility and accountability that exceeds that which you assume with others around you—even with your family, even with your nation. For your greater commitment is to Creation and to God, for this is what has brought you into the world, fundamentally.

这关乎你与上帝以及你精神家庭的关系，是他们派你来到这个世界。这关乎一种更深层次的责任和义务，超越你对周围其他人——甚至对你的家庭，甚至对你的国家——所承担的责任和义务。因为你更伟大的承诺是对创造和对上帝的，因为从根本上来说，正是这把你带到了这个世界。

The return is a key turning point. And though it might seem inconsequential and slight at the outset, a very fragile beginning, if you will stay with this, if you will honor this, if you will follow what the deeper Knowledge within you is guiding you to do, if you will stop pretending about your life and indulging yourself in things that have no promise or destiny, then the return will begin to build momentum and become stronger with each decision that you make in alignment with it.

回归是一个关键的转折点。尽管它一开始可能看起来无关紧要、微不足道，一个非常脆弱的开端，但如果你坚持这一点，如果你尊重这一点，如果你遵循你内在更深层次的内识指引你去做的事情，如果你停止假装你的生命，不再沉溺于没有希望或天命的事情，那么回归将开始积聚势头，并随着你做出的每一个与之一致的决定而变得更加强大。

It is like a revolution on the inside, where something greater is going to take over, greater in you—more real, more powerful, more essential. And all that is false and fragmentary and temporary is overshadowed by this greater force and power that God has put within you, awaiting the time when you would come to your senses, waiting for a time of a deeper recognition, when a greater power and a greater destiny could emerge within you.

它就像一场内在的革命，有一种更伟大的东西将接管你，在你内在更伟大——更真实，更强大，更本质。所有虚假的、碎片化和暂时的东西，都被上帝置于你内在的这更伟大动力和力量所掩盖，正等待着你恢复知觉的那一刻，正等待着一种更深层次认知的那一刻，一种更伟大力量与一个更伟大天命能够在你内在呈现的那一刻。

This is what Heaven waits for, you see. All that happened previous to this is really inconsequential except that it could have led you so far astray that the turning point might never happen. To prevent this, counsel has been given to you previously, but your mind was so obsessed and taken up that it is like you did not hear it or feel it sufficiently to heed its warnings, to follow its recommendations.

你看，这就是天堂所等待的。在此之前发生的一切其实无关紧要，只是它们可能将你引向歧途，以至于转折点可能永远不会到来。为了防止这种情况发生，你之前已经得到了忠告，但你的心灵如此痴迷和沉迷，以至于你似乎没有充分听到或感受到它，以至于没有注意到它的警告，没有遵循它的建议。

So Heaven waits for the beginning of the return and the meaning of the return for you. You will not understand it at the outset. And you will not understand it for a long time to come. But you follow this because it is natural and essential and comes from the most reliable part of you, beyond the realm and the reach of the intellect.

因此，天堂等待着回归的开始，以及回归的意义。你一开始不会理解它。你在未来很长一段时间内也不会理解它。但你遵循它，因为它是自然的、本质的，它来自你最可靠的部分，超越智力的领域和范围。

It is the most important event of your life. But at the outset, it will be very confusing because now you seem to have competing agendas. Part of you wants to go one way; part of you wants to go another. You have conflicting goals. It is very confusing, you see.

这是你生命中最重要的一件事。但一开始，你会感到困惑，因为现在你似乎有互相冲突的计划。一部分的你希望走这条路，一部分的你希望走另一条路。你的目标相互冲突。你看，它很令人困惑。

But it is merely the result of the greater voice within you emerging and competing with what you have been following all along, which is the voice of your culture, the voice of your fear, the voice of your desire and the voice of others telling you who you should be, what you should be and what you should do and so forth, which has been there since the day you were born.

但它仅仅是你内在更伟大声音浮现并与你始终遵循的事物竞争的结果，那是你文化的声音，你恐惧的声音，你渴望的声音以及其他人告诉你你应该是谁、你应该是什么、你应该做什么等等的声音，它自从你出生那天起就一直在那里。

Now something else is taking place. Now your life is becoming really important. It has the potential for being this. And though you have a long way to go and your beginning is fragile and uncertain, Heaven will begin to pay attention to you. It will pay attention to you also because it is so easy to fail and to fall back into the shadows at the outset. Your deeper incentive, though real and consistent, has to compete with greater forces in your mind and circumstances that want to lead you back into your former life.

现在，别的事情正在发生。现在你的生命正在变得真正重要。它有潜力成为这样。尽管你还有很长的路要走，你的开端脆弱而不确定，但天国将开始关注你。它也将关注你，因为一开始就很容易失败，很容易重新陷入阴影。你更深层次的动机，尽管真实而始终如一，却必须与你的心灵和境遇中那些想要引领你回到你过去生命的更伟大力量竞争。

But the journey begins with disappointment, when you begin to see the goals you have followed have failed you and will fail you. Even if you attempt them again and again, they will not lead to what you are really looking for. Success, romance, falling in love, pleasure, excitement—you will see none of these will ever give you what you are really looking for, which is a deeper connection in life and a fulfillment of a greater destiny that has brought you here in the first place.

但旅程始于失望，当你开始看到你追随的目标已经让你失望，并且将会让你失望。即使你一次又一次地尝试，它们也不会带来你真正追寻的东西。成功、浪漫、坠入爱河、快乐、兴奋——你会发现，这些都无法给你真正追寻的东西，那是一种生命中更深层次的联结，以及对最初将你带到这里的一个更伟大天命的履行。

Now Heaven begins to pay attention to you because you need its strength. There are things you must overcome. There are things you must set aside. There are decisions that are consequential that you must make. Some of them are very big and significant. Some of them are small, but still important.

现在，天堂开始关注你，因为你需要它的力量。有些事你必须克服。有些事你必须放下。有些决定至关重要，你必须做出。有些决定重大而意义重大。有些决定虽小，但仍然重要。

From this point on, you are either strengthening yourself or weakening yourself. You are moving forward or moving backwards. You are swimming or you are sinking. So everything you do, every commitment you make, every decision now becomes more significant because the outcome is so very important.

从这个点开始，你要么增强你自己，要么削弱你自己。你要么前进，要么后退。你要么游泳，要么沉沦。所以，你做的每一件事，你做的每一个承诺，你做的每一个决定，现在都变得更加重要，因为结果至关重要。

You have begun a greater journey—the most important journey of your life, the most important journey of anyone’s life, a journey back to your Source, a journey back to redemption and integration with your deeper nature, which is represented by Knowledge.

你已经开始了一段更伟大的旅程——你生命中最重要的旅程，任何人生命中最重要的旅程，一段回归你本源的旅程，一段回归救赎和与你更深层次自然融合的旅程，这由内识所代表。

Now the part of you that has wandered in Separation begins to reconnect with the part of you that has never left God. This is the great Homecoming, but it is not happening in a moment, or in a great realization, or in a flash of lightning. It is a journey you must take. For you are here to give something to the world, and it is in the process of doing this that you begin to truly reunite with your true Self.

现在，你在分离中徘徊的那部分，开始与你从未离开上帝的那部分重新连接。这是伟大的归家，但它并非一瞬间发生，并非在一次伟大的领悟中，亦非在一道闪电中。它是一段你必须踏上的旅程。因为你在此是为了给予世界，而正是在这个过程中，你开始真正地与你真正的自我重新结合。

But your desires are still there. Your regrets are still there. Your hostilities are still there. You have not escaped them yet. Your weakness is still there. Your vulnerability to manipulation and persuasion and admonition from others is still there. You have the shadows and the overcast of your past—staying with you, pulling you back, pulling you down. It is still there.

但你的欲望依然存在。你的悔恨依然存在。你的敌意依然存在。你尚未摆脱它们。你的软弱依然存在。你容易被他人操纵、说服和规劝的弱点依然存在。你身上依然有过去的阴影和阴云——它一直萦绕在你的心头，拖累着你，让你跌入深渊。它依然存在。

God does not wipe all this away. God gives you something important to do, and that is the journey out of your hopeless dilemma, your hopeless pursuit for happiness and fulfillment, your hopeless pursuit for true security and meaning. God gives you the way out by giving you something important to do in the world because the world needs the contribution from you, and contribution is the only reason to be here anyway, the only thing that will satisfy the deeper yearning of your heart. True relationship and true contribution—this is what you are really looking for.

上帝不会抹去这一切。上帝会给你重要的事去做，那就是走出你那无望的困境，走出你那无望的对幸福和满足的追求，走出你那无望的对真正安全感和意义的追求。上帝通过给你在世上重要的事去做，为你指明了出路，因为这个世界需要你的贡献，而贡献是你存在的唯一理由，唯一能满足你内心深处渴望的东西。真正的关系和真正的贡献——这才是你真正追寻的。

Until the return was initiated, you were looking for other things. Though inside yourself you were looking for these, but you were looking everywhere else. You were lost in the world. In a way, your former position was completely hopeless. There was no possibility of success.

在回归开始之前，你一直在寻找其他的东西。尽管你自己内心也在寻找内识，但你却四处寻觅。你迷失在世间。在某种程度上，你之前的处境完全没有希望。没有任何成功的可能。

That is why God initiated the return. And that is why Heaven looks upon you now to support that return, to encourage it, to strengthen you and to counteract your weak tendencies, fears and self-doubt. You need now great assistance. This will set you on a search for new relationships that represent your destiny and that can support the emerging power and awareness within you.

正因如此，上帝启动了回归。正因如此，天堂现在注视着你，支持这个回归，鼓励它，强化你，并抵消你的软弱倾向、恐惧和自我怀疑。你现在需要伟大的援助。这将使你开始寻找新的关系，它们代表着你的天命，并能够支持你内在正在浮现的力量和觉知。

The return will occupy the remaining years of your life because it is far greater than you realize at this moment. It is not enough to seek healing, for you were not sent into the world to seek healing. There is greater work for you to do. There is a world to serve. There are specific situations that will be yours to attend to. There will be very important relationships to be established. There is great strengthening that must occur and the development of important skills and abilities.

回归将占据你余生的岁月，因为它远比你此刻意识到的要伟大。仅仅寻求疗愈是不够的，因为你并非被派到世界上寻求疗愈。还有更伟大的工作等着你去做。有一个世界需要你去服务。有一些特定的情况需要你去关注。将会有一些非常重要的关系需要你去建立。你必须进行伟大的强化，并发展重要的技能和能力。

Never think that you just awaken to yourself one day and you are complete, for this is a big mountain to climb, and climb it you must. And gain the strength and the wisdom and the freedom to climb it you must. And to learn of its ways, its pathways, its hazards and its great benefits, you must.

永远不要认为有一天你醒来，你就完整了，因为这是一座需要攀登的高山，你必须攀登。你必须获得攀登它的力量、智慧和自由。你必须学习它的方式、它的路径、它的危险以及它的伟大益处。

Now your life begins to take a great turn, substantiated by many different steps and stages and situations. Everything around you will begin to change because everything within you is beginning to change. And with it your values, your priorities, what you look for in relationships and communication, what you seek—all will change, as they must.

现在，你的生命开始发生伟大的转变，这由许多不同的步骤、阶段和境遇所证实。你周围的一切都将开始改变，因为你内在的一切都开始改变。随之而来的是，你的价值观、你的优先顺序、你在关系和沟通中所追求的东西、你所追求的事物——一切都将改变，因为它们必然会改变。

Here your destiny begins to pull you, for destiny is everything. When you leave this life and return to your Spiritual Family, they will ask you, “Did you accomplish your tasks?” And in that moment, it will be absolutely clear whether you did or did not. There is no condemnation here, but the truth will be entirely evident, and you will see completely, without deception, what must be done. For when you leave this life, you will not have beliefs. You will not have the concerns you have today. You will return to how you really are and to your true condition.

在这里，你的天命开始牵引你，因为天命就是一切。当你离开这个生命，回归你的精神家庭时，他们会问你：“你完成了你的任务吗？”在那一刻，你完成了还是没有完成，将绝对清晰。这里没有谴责，但真理将完全显明，你将完全地、不带欺骗地看到必须做什么。因为当你离开这个生命时，你将不再拥有信仰。你将不再拥有你今天的担忧。你将回归你真正的状态，回归你真正的情况。

Your Spiritual Family will not be interested in all the things that you did and the places you went and the people you were with and the thrilling things and the disappointing things and all the details of your life, for that is behind you at that point. Their concern is, did you fulfill what you set out to do?

你的精神家庭不会对你做过的所有事、去过的所有地方、和你在一起的人、激动人心的事情、失望的事情以及你生命中的所有细节感兴趣，因为到那时，这些都已经过去了。他们关心的是，你是否履行了你设定的目标？

This, then, gives you a sense of what life is really for, and what it is really about, and what is really important, and what will speak to your heart and to your soul. It is not the same things that feed the anxiety or the wandering desires of your mind—your personal mind, your worldly mind.

因此，这赋予你一种感知，去理解生命真正为了什么，它真正关于什么，什么真正重要，什么将与你的心和你的灵魂对话。这些与滋养你心灵——你的个人心灵，你的世俗心灵——的焦虑或游移不定的欲望，并非同一回事。

But you must be very patient, for this is a big mountain and a long journey. And there is more for you to learn and to unlearn, to see and to recognize, more for you to forgive. A different way of being in the world must be established, a different way of being with yourself, a different way of viewing your mind and your body and your circumstances.

但你必须非常有耐心，因为这是一座高山，一段漫长的旅程。你还有更多东西需要学习和忘却，需要观察和认知，需要原谅。你必须建立一种不同的世界存在方式，一种不同的自我存在方式，一种不同的审视你的心灵、你的身体和你的处境的方式。

Now your greater relationships have a foundation. Now the people you are destined to meet can finally begin to come into your life. But before this could happen, your life must be open and free. If you are bound by your former commitments entirely, you may feel the pull of Heaven, but you cannot move. You will be chained to the ground.

现在，你的更伟大关系有了基础。现在，你命中注定要遇见的人终于可以开始进入你的生命了。但在这一切发生之前，你的生命必须开放而自由。如果你完全被你过去的承诺所束缚，你或许会感受到天堂的牵引，但你无法动弹。你将被束缚在地上。

Heaven will bless that which is real in your life from the past, but you have to be open to a whole new reality. This will liberate your mind. This will free your emotions. This will give you the eyes to see and the ears to hear, in time.

天国会祝福你过去生命中那些真实的事物，但你必须敞开心扉，迎接一个全新的现实。这将解放你的心灵，释放你的情感，并最终赋予你一双可以观察的眼睛和一双可以倾听的耳朵。

You are living now not for yourself alone. You are living for your Spiritual Family. You are living because you are in relationship with Creation beyond the physical realm. Your range of relationship now is expanding. Instead of a lonely, forlorn individual trying to seek happiness and avoid pain and disaster in the world, you are now associating with something magnificent and great.

你现在不是在为你自己而活。你是为了你的精神家庭而活。你活着，是因为你与超越物质领域的创造建立了关系。你的关系范围正在拓展。你不再是一个孤独、绝望的个体，试图在世间寻求幸福，逃避痛苦和灾难，你现在正与宏伟而伟大的关系联系在一起。

But it is purposeful. For the journey is not simply to be happy and to experience bliss. For true happiness will come through the fulfillment of your purpose and destiny here. There is no running away into happiness and bliss, as so many people think. For that is not the return.

但它是有目的的。因为这段旅程并非仅仅为了快乐和体验极乐。真正的幸福将来自于你在这个世界的目的和天命的履行。你不可能像许多人认为的那样，可以逃到幸福和快乐之中。因为那并非回归。

Only God knows the way. You cannot create it yourself. Nor can anyone else in life. Something greater must be happening. You cannot redeem yourself.

只有上帝知道道路。你无法独自创造，生命中其他任何人也无法创造。一定有更伟大的事情正在发生。你无法救赎你自己。

God has sent a New Revelation into the world to speak of these things, to bring great clarity into the world regarding that which is most essential, to clarify the intention and the purpose of your greater nature and the deep relationship you have with those who sent you here and with those who watch over your life once the return has begun. You will not view their presence as an intrusion. You will view it as a great strength and reinforcement and a great blessing.

上帝向世界发送了一个新的启示，来讲述这些事情，来给世界带来关于最本质的伟大明晰，来阐明你更伟大自然的意图和目的，以及你与那些派遣你至此的存在，以及与那些在回归开始后守护你生命的存在所拥有的深厚关系。你不会将他们的存在视为一种侵扰。你将把它视为一种伟大的力量、强化和一种伟大的祝福。

For now your life has greater importance and a greater possibility. And though you do not understand what this means, or what it will require, or what the journey ahead will bring to you, you follow this because it is so very natural. You follow this because you have been sent here to receive and to follow this.

因为现在你的生命拥有一种更伟大的意义和一种更伟大的可能性。尽管你不理解这意味着什么，它需要什么，或者前方的旅程将带给你什么，但你遵循它，因为它是如此自然。你遵循它，因为你被派来接收和遵循它。

Here you are not a great savior or a saint. Here you are not given a glorious and profound role. Forget these things. They are attempts to elevate yourself above others, to make yourself significant, to draw others’ attention to you. This is not what We are speaking of at all.

在这里，你并非一位伟大的救世主或圣人。在这里，你并未被赋予一个光荣而深远的角色。忘掉这些吧。它们不过是试图抬高你自己，让你自己变得重要，吸引他人的注意力。这根本不是我们正在谈论的。

Your role will be simple. It will be humble. It will likely go unnoticed in the world, but it will have a great impact on those people you are destined to serve and to meet. There is no glory or fame here. Do not seek these things, for they will lead you astray.

你的角色将会是简单的。它将会是谦逊的。它或许不会被世人所注意，但它将对你注定要服务、要遇见的人们产生伟大的影响。这里没有荣耀或名望。不要追求这些东西，因为它们会把你引入歧途。

Now you are coming back into relationship with Creation itself, though you are still in the world as a person carrying out the duties of being a person in the world and dealing with all the difficulties and problems of being a person in the world—your internal problems, the problems with others, the difficulty of communicating with people who do not know how to communicate, the difficulty of being in relationship with people who do not know how to be in a relationship, being with people who have not begun the return, who are still drifting aimlessly and suffering terribly.

现在你正在回归和创造本身的关系，尽管你依然作为一个人身处世界上，履行着作为一个人在世界上的职责，并应对着作为一个人在世界上的所有困难和问题——你的内在问题，与他人的问题，与那些不知道如何沟通的人沟通的困难，与那些不知道如何建立关系的人建立关系的困难，与那些尚未开始回归、依然漫无目的地漂泊并遭受着可怕苦难的人建立关系的困难。

Now you are beginning to know things, to see things, to feel things that were not available to you before, for your mind was too taken up and too captivated by the world.

现在你开始知道了一些事情，看到了一些事情，感受到了一些你以前无法感受到的事情，因为你的心灵被世界占据和迷惑了。

It is not an easy situation. You will feel the suffering of the world. You will feel the suffering of others. You will see how difficult it is to establish a meaningful relationship with people—even the people you have known for a long time, even the people who are close to you. They are not yet responding to what you are responding to. You will feel alone and isolated perhaps, estranged from your former circumstances to a certain degree. This is entirely natural, but you must be reminded of this constantly.

它并非易事。你会感受到世界的苦难。你会感受到他人的苦难。你会看到与人们建立有意义的关系是多么困难——即使是你认识已久的人，即使是你亲近的人。他们尚未回应你所回应的。你或许会感到孤独和孤立，在某种程度上与你之前的境遇疏远。这完全是自然的，但你必须不断地被提醒这一点。

That is why God has provided the Steps to Knowledge, to occupy your mind and to give you strength and reinforcement and a deeper education while you undergo this fundamental and essential transition within yourself.

这就是为什么上帝提供了内识进阶，来占据你的心灵，并在你经历你自己内在这个根本和必要的转变时给你力量、强化和更深刻的教育。

You are now living at a time of Revelation. The Messenger is in the world. It is the greatest time to be in the world. God has given a New Revelation for humanity. It is of the greatest importance for you. These things We speak of will be difficult to establish in the former traditions because they have been so overlaid with human interpretation, human adaptation and human corruption. You would need a wise teacher within any of the traditions to learn of the things We speak of here today.

你现在生活在一个启示的时代。信使就在这个世界上。这是世界上最伟大的时间。上帝已经赋予人类一个新的启示。它对你至关重要。我们所说的这些事情很难在以前的传统中确立，因为它们已经被人类的解释、人类的改编和人类的腐败所覆盖。你需要在任何一个传统中找到一位明智的老师，才能学习我们今天在这里所讲述的事情。

Humanity needs a great Revelation now, for the world is entering a period of great travail. And you have come into the world to serve the world in this difficult and challenging time. It is not merely for your own restoration that We speak of these things. It is for the restoration of the world, where you will play your small but important part.

人类现在需要一个伟大的启示，因为世界正在进入一个巨大阵痛的时期。你来到这个世界，是为了在这个艰难而充满挑战的时间服务世界。我们讲述这些，不仅仅是为了你自身的复兴。而是为了世界的复兴，你将在其中扮演你微小却重要的角色。

For your liberation will be in part because you are freed from being obsessed with yourself—obsessed with your needs, your problems, your fears, your dilemmas, your conflicts, your difficulties with others, your circumstances, your needs. Your mind will be free now to attend to greater things, which represent the real importance of your life. And you will have to attend to other matters, the practical matters, with great efficiency and clarity.

因为你的解放部分在于你摆脱了对你自己的执着——摆脱了对你的需求、你的问题、你的恐惧、你的困境、你的冲突、你与他人的矛盾、你的境遇、你的需求的执着。你的心灵现在将自由地去关注更伟大的事情，它们代表着你生命真正的重要性。而你将必须以极大的效率和清晰度去关注其他事务，那些实际的事务。

The return will require a different way of being with everything. But it will not be imposed upon you. It will simply be the natural emergence of Knowledge within you as you take the Steps to Knowledge.

回归将需要一种与万物共存的不同方式。但它不会强加于你。它只是你内在内识在你开展内识进阶时自然呈现的过程。

Do not try to convince others. Do not try to teach others. For if the return has not begun in them, they will not understand. And though you may love them and hope for the best for them, it is you who must take this journey now. You are not strong enough or wise enough or clear enough to be a real teacher. And you do not know who to teach or how to teach or what to teach.

不要试图说服别人。不要试图教导别人。因为如果回归尚未在他们心中开始，他们就不会理解。尽管你可能爱他们，并希望他们一切安好，但现在必须踏上这段旅程的是你。你不够强大，不够明智，也不够清晰，无法成为一名真正的老师。你不知道该教谁，如何教，或者教什么。

You must receive before you can give. You must take the Steps to Knowledge. You must learn what Knowledge will reveal, what must be strengthened, what must be relieved, what must be avoided, how to be with people, who to be with, how to be with them, how to be with yourself, how to see the world without condemnation, how to navigate changing circumstances.

你必须先接受，然后才能给予。你必须开展内识进阶。你必须学习内识将揭示什么，什么必须得到强化，什么必须得到缓解，什么必须避免，如何与人相处，与谁相处，如何与他们相处，如何与你自己相处，如何不带谴责地看待世界，如何应对不断变化的情况。

You are standing at the beginning of a great education. You are blessed for this, though you may not feel blessed. You are saved, though you may not feel saved. It is building that which is strong and true and relieving yourself of that which is false and weak that represents the essential pathway that you are on. For this is the return—the return to wholeness, greatness, wisdom, power, contribution.

你正站在伟大教育的开端。你因此而受到祝福，尽管你或许并不感到被祝福。你得救了，尽管你或许并不感到得救。构建强大与真实，并摆脱虚假与软弱，这代表着你所走的必经之路。因为这就是回归——回归完整、伟大、智慧、力量与贡献。

Finally, you can begin to live the life you were meant to live. Finally, you can begin to take the journey that you must take, that is so different from what you did before. Finally, you have the chance of coming back into true relationship and communication with those in the world and beyond the world who represent your core and fundamental relationships. Finally, you begin to comprehend the real meaning of God’s Power and Presence and how God works within the world, within a world of Separation. Finally, you begin to recognize your value and the value of others and to look on the world with compassion. Finally, you will begin to see what could not be seen before, what could not be heard before and what could not be felt before.

最终，你可以开始过上你本应过的生命。最终，你可以开始踏上你必须踏上的旅程，这与你之前所经历的截然不同。最终，你有机会重新与那些代表你核心和根本关系的人——无论是世界中的人还是超越世界的存在——建立真正的关系和沟通。最终，你开始领会上帝的力量和存在的真正意义，以及上帝如何在这个分离的世界中运作。最终，你开始认知你的价值和他人的价值，并带着同情心看待这个​​世界。最终，你将开始看到过去无法看到、无法听到和无法感受到的事情。

Finally, you have begun the return.

最后，你开始了回归。
