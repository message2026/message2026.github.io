第十五章：关于宇宙生命的新讯息

New Message on Life in the Universe

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on September 3, 2007 in Boulder, Colorado
2007 年 9 月 3 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

For centuries, people of all cultures and faiths have wondered about life in the universe, speculating and pondering on the possibilities there. What would advanced civilizations be like? What would they have achieved with their technology? How would they be able to travel within the vast distances between stars and solar systems? And have they themselves, as advanced races in the universe, overcome competition, conflict and war?

几个世纪以来，不同文化和信仰的人们一直在探索宇宙中的生命，推测和思考那里存在的各种可能性。先进的文明会是什么样子？他们运用他们的科技取得了哪些成就？他们如何在恒星和太阳系之间遥远的距离上旅行？作为宇宙中的先进种族，他们自身是否已经克服了竞争、冲突和战争？

Even today, many people believe that advanced technology is the same as advanced ethics and morality. They assume that if a race has survived over a long period of time that they have by necessity overcome the predisposition to competition, conflict and war. In addition, people project their own values upon what life might be like in the universe.

即使在今天，许多人仍然认为先进的技术就等同于先进的伦理道德。他们认为，如果一个种族能够长期生存，他们必然会克服竞争、冲突和战争的倾向。此外，人们还会将他们自己的价值观投射到宇宙中的生命形态上。

As a result of this, people project human values, human aspirations, human concerns and human ethics on their speculation about intelligent life in the universe. For many people, this results in projection of the belief in a superhuman race in the universe, for it is very difficult for people to consider there are races very different from their own, whose values and ethics are very different from their own.

因此，人们将人类的价值观、人类的渴望、人类的关切和人类的伦理道德投射到他们对宇宙智能生命的推测上。对许多人来说，这导致他们相信宇宙中存在一个超人类种族，因为人们很难想象存在着与他们自己截然不同、价值观和伦理道德也截然不同的种族。

But the projection of human values generates both hope and fear. People hope there is a more intelligent, more benevolent race of beings like us out there in the universe, who could teach us and help us and shepherd us through our difficult transition into becoming a race that is capable of functioning within a Greater Community of intelligent life.

但人类价值观的投射既带来了希望，也带来了恐惧。人们希望宇宙中存在一个像我们一样更聪慧、更仁慈的种族，他们能够教导我们、帮助我们，并引领我们度过艰难的过渡期，成为一个能够在智能生命大社区中运作的种族。

So around this, there is a great deal of hope and speculation. However, there is also fear. For if there were a humanlike race with our current values and attitudes and predispositions that is technologically advanced, they could be incredibly dangerous and destructive, even predatory in their affairs and connections with other races.

因此，围绕这一点，人们充满了希望和猜测。然而，恐惧也随之而来。因为如果存在一个拥有我们当前价值观、态度和倾向的类人种族，并且拥有先进的技术，那么他们在与其他种族的事务和联系中，可能会具有令人难以置信的危险性和破坏性，甚至是掠夺性的。

So the projection of human values can generate either hope or fear, depending on what part of the human family’s psychology and behavior you want to emphasize—the highest potential or the worst tendencies.

因此，人类价值观的投射可以产生希望或恐惧，这取决于你想要强调人类大家庭心理和行为的哪一部分——最高的潜力还是最坏的倾向。

Humanity is unprepared for the realities of life within the universe, within this Greater Community of intelligent life. For the part of the universe that your world exists in is greatly inhabited. There are many parts of even your galaxy that are uncharted, for there are very few worlds that support the evolution of intelligent life. For most worlds are barren, where life cannot take hold.

人类尚未准备好面对宇宙的现实，宇宙智能生命大社区的生命现实。因为你们世界所在的宇宙部分已经大量居住。甚至你们的银河系中也有许多地方尚未探索，因为很少有世界能够支持智能生命的进化。因为大多数世界都是贫瘠的，生命无法在那里扎根。

This is important for you to understand because you must see that your world is incredibly rich and valuable—not only rich and valuable for you, but rich and valuable for other races, who see your world as a great prize, something they want to have for themselves, a world that you must value and that you must learn to defend.

理解这一点对你来说很重要，因为你必须看到，你们的世界极其丰富和宝贵——不仅对你们来说是丰富和宝贵的，而且对其他种族来说也是丰富和宝贵的，他们把你们的世界视为一个伟大的奖赏，一种他们想要为他们自己拥有的东西，一个你们必须珍视并且你们必须学习捍卫的世界。

Of course, in the projection of human ideas, people assume that advanced technology is the equivalent of advanced ethics and morality, so it is hard for them to consider that any advanced race would want to take advantage of such a wonderful species as humanity. And over the centuries, theologians and philosophers and the general values of human culture assume that humanity is the centerpiece of Creation—God’s primary emphasis, focus and obsession.

当然，在人类观念的投射中，人们认为先进的科技就等同于先进的伦理道德，因此他们很难想象任何先进的种族会想要利用人类这样一个奇妙的物种。几个世纪以来，神学家、哲学家以及人类文化的普遍价值观都认为，人类是创世的核心——是上帝首要的关注点、焦点和执念。

All these predispositions, all these projections of values indicate that you are not yet prepared for the reality of contact. You are not yet prepared to face a Greater Community of intelligent life, which in itself represents a competitive environment on a scale you cannot even imagine.

所有这些先入之见，所有这些价值观投射，都表明你们尚未准备好面对接触的现实。你们尚未准备好面对一个智能生命大社区，它本身就代表着一个你们甚至无法想象规模的竞争环境。

You are very primitive in your thinking still, but this is understandable because you have been living in relative isolation for a very long time. When We say relative isolation, it means that your world has been visited by different races for different purposes at different times in your history. So your isolation is not complete.

你们的思维方式仍然非常原始，但这可以理解，因为你们长期以来一直生活在相对孤立的状态中。我们所说的相对孤立，是指在你们历史上的不同时期，不同的种族曾为了不同的目的造访过你们的世界。所以，你们的孤立并非完全的。

But for you, in essence, it is complete, for you know nothing of the affairs of other nations and worlds. You know nothing of the realities of living within a Greater Community of intelligent life. You do not know the rules of conduct that are established between trading nations. You do not understand the ethics of other races. You do not understand their needs or even the problems that advanced technology within itself creates for any society that can establish it.

但对你们来说，本质上它是完整的，因为你们对其他国家和世界的事务一无所知。你们对生活在一个智能生命大社区里的现实一无所知。你们不知道贸易国家之间建立的行为准则。你们不理解其他种族的伦理。你们不理解他们的需求，甚至不理解先进科技本身给任何能够建立它的社会带来的问题。

Therefore, there must be a new education here, for the projection of human wishes and fears, hopes and values, cannot prepare you for the Greater Community. Even your scientific community, which seeks to be objective in its approach, is still highly governed by these predispositions. As a result, there are many fanciful notions about what life in the universe will really be like.

因此，这里必须有一种新的教育，因为人类的愿望和恐惧、希望和价值观的投射，无法帮助你为大社区进行准备。即使是你们力求客观的科学界，也仍然高度受制于这些先入之见。结果，存在着许多关于宇宙生命究竟会是什么样子的奇思妙想。

Of course, it is hard for the scientists to imagine a technological capability so far beyond what humanity has established thus far. And so many people within the scientific community believe that certain things are just impossible, for instance, the assertion that you cannot travel faster than the speed of light.

当然，科学家很难想象一种超越人类现有水平的科技能力。科学界有许多人认为某些事情根本不可能实现，例如，有人断言“你的旅行速度不可能超过光速”。

But if you could not travel faster than the speed of light, then space travel would not be possible. Yet within the Greater Community of intelligent life in which you live, there is a great deal of space travel. There is a great deal of trade, both legal and illegal, both ethical and unethical, by your standards.

但如果你们无法以超光速旅行，那么太空旅行就不可能实现。然而，在你们所生活的智能生命大社区里，却存在着大量的太空旅行。存在着大量的贸易，以你们的标准来看，既有合法的，也有非法的；既有道德的，也有不道德的。

So clearly there must be a new education, a new revelation, a new paradigm of thinking and comprehension. For you are the natives of this new world, and you have no idea what is going on beyond your shores. And yet visitors, intervening visitors from beyond your shores, are already in the world and have been here for some time.

所以显然，必须有一种新的教育，一种新的启示，一种新的思维和理解范式。因为你们是这个新世界的原住民，你们对你们的海岸之外正在发生什么一无所知。然而，来自你们海岸之外的来访者，介入的来访者，已经来到这个世界，并且已经在这里待了一段时间。

They are not governed by human values or human ethics or human morality. They are not subject to these things, for they are here for their own reasons. They are not interested in you particularly. They do not need your culture and your art and your wonderfully confusing expressions. They are here on a very serious purpose of their own. They are ready for you. They have prepared for you for some time. But you are not ready for them. You have not prepared for them, and your lack of preparation makes you incredibly vulnerable to manipulation and to deception.

他们不受人类价值观、人类伦理或人类道德的支配。他们不受这些事情的支配，因为他们在此是为了他们自己的原因。他们对你们并非特别感兴趣。他们不需要你们的文化、你们的艺术以及你们奇妙而令人困惑的表达。他们在此是为了他们自己一个非常严肃的目的。他们已经为你们做好了准备。他们已经为你们准备了一段时间。但你们还没有为他们做好准备。你们没有为他们做好准备，你们的缺乏准备让你们极度易受操控和欺骗。

Given the reality of your isolation, given the anthropocentric foundation to your thinking, given the projection of your own ideals and hopes upon the Greater Community, and especially given the condition of human societies today and their relationship with one another, you are in a very poor position as the native peoples of this one world. You are extremely vulnerable in the universe now.

鉴于你们孤立的现实，鉴于你们思维的人类中心主义基础，鉴于你们将自身的理想和希望投射到大社区，尤其鉴于当今人类社会的状况及其相互关系，你作为这个世界的原住民处于一个非常不利的位置。你们现在在宇宙中极其脆弱。

You have great defenses against one another. You have great armament and weapons of destruction against one another, but you have no defenses against the Greater Community, nor do you understand what will be necessary for you and for all people in the world, and especially for nations and religious leaders, to protect and preserve human freedom and sovereignty within this world.

你们彼此拥有强大的防御。你们彼此拥有强大的军备和毁灭性武器，但你们却没有针对大社区的防御，你们也不理解对于你们以及对于世界上所有民众，特别是对于国家和宗教领袖来说，为了保护和维护这个世界上的人类自由和主权，什么是必不可少的。

Obsessed with their own affairs, the human family is not looking to the skies, is not paying attention to the Intervention that is occurring and does not give significance to those sightings that people around the world are making on a regular basis. It is not their concern. It is amazing and incredible and unbelievable perhaps, but easily dismissed, given their preoccupations.

人类大家庭沉迷于他们自身的事务，不仰望天空，不关注正在发生的干预，也不重视世界各地人们定期目击的那些景象。这并非他们的关切。这或许令人惊叹、不可思议、难以置信，但考虑到他们的执念，很容易被忽视。

There are two great dangers facing the human family, greater than you yet realize. There is the Great Waves of change coming to the world in terms of environmental degradation, in terms of diminishing resources and the great risk of competition, conflict and war that these will generate. The second great threat to humanity is Intervention from beyond by races who are here to seek the world for its resources, who are here to use humanity and to establish this world as part of their network for its strategic and biological value.

人类大家庭面临两大危险，远超你们目前所意识到的。一是变革的巨浪即将降临世界，表现为环境恶化，表现为资源缩减，以及由此将引发的竞争、冲突和战争的巨大风险。人类面临的第二大威胁是来自外来种族的干预，他们来此是为了掠夺这个世界的资源，他们来此是为了利用人类，并将这个世界确立为他们网络的一部分，以获取其战略和生物价值。

These are the greatest events in human history. These are the most perilous threats to human freedom and sovereignty. These are the most consequential events in human history, and yet how few people ever think of these things? How few people concern themselves with these things? It is not part of the public agenda of any government. It is not the concern of world religious leaders or institutions, at least not publicly.

这些是人类历史上最伟大的事件。这些是对人类自由和主权最危险的威胁。这些是人类历史上最具影响力的事件，然而，却又有多少人思考过这些事情？又有多少人关心过这些事情？这不是任何政府的公共议程的一部分。这也不是全球宗教领袖或机构关注的事情，至少在公开场合并非如此。

The problems that humanity is concerned with, though real in and of themselves, are insignificant compared to these two great challenges. For what could you hope to accomplish or to protect or gain if human freedom and sovereignty is lost through the clever Intervention of other races? And what can you hope to achieve in terms of averting poverty and building human wealth and human security if you cannot face the Great Waves of change that are occurring here in the world, which are largely the result of humanity’s abuse of the world itself?

人类所关注的问题，尽管本身实际存在，但与这两大挑战相比却微不足道。因为，如果人类的自由和主权因其他种族的巧妙干预而丧失，你还能指望成就、保护或获得什么呢？如果你无法面对正在这个世界上发生的巨浪——这很大程度上是人类滥用世界本身的结果——你还能指望在避免贫困、构建人类财富和人类安全方面取得什么成就呢？

Your challenges now are greater, and you must prepare for them. And these two great challenges are not unrelated. For those intervening in the world today, who represent commercial forces, will support humanity’s decline, for that will make their proposition of governance far easier for them to establish, either surreptitiously or publicly or both. A weakened humanity will have difficulty declining the offers of an advanced race, and the more that humanity has fallen prey to conflict and war and destitution, the greater will be the attraction of the Intervention’s offers and proposals.

你现在的挑战更加巨大，你必须为它们进行准备。这两大挑战并非毫无关联。因为那些代表商业势力、正在干预当今世界的种族，将支持人类的衰落，因为这将使他们的治理主张更容易建立，无论是秘密的、公开的，还是两者兼而有之。一个被弱化的人类将难以拒绝一个先进种族的提议，人类越是沦为冲突、战争和贫困的牺牲品，干预的提议和建议的吸引力就越大。

Already today the Intervention is grooming its advocates and apologists to promote an extraterrestrial guidance for the world. Already today the Intervention is establishing its bases in the world and influencing certain select individuals in positions of power and influence—in government, in commerce and in religion.

如今，干预已经在培养它的拥护者和辩护者，以推广一种针对世界的外星指导。如今，干预已经在世界上建立其基地，并影响着某些身居权力和影响力位置的精选个体——在政府、商业和宗教领域。

Certain people are obsessed with conspiracy theories, but their focus is misguided. The malevolent behavior of human governments or human commercial institutions is nothing compared to what the Intervention can do here. And if its agenda succeeds, which remains yet uncertain, there will be no winners amongst the human family. There will be no vaulted societies. There will be no humans in positions of power and authority. Everyone will be used, and everyone will share the same fate. For the Intervention is intelligent. It does not seek to destroy humanity but to employ it for its own purposes.

某些人痴迷于阴谋论，但他们的焦点被误导了。人类政府或人类商业机构的恶意行为与干预在此所能造成的后果相比根本不值一提。如果干预者的议程成功实现（尽管这仍不确定），人类大家庭中将没有赢家。没有高高在上的社会，也没有处于权力和权威位置的人类。每个人都会被利用，每个人都会共享相同的命运。因为干预是智能的。它不是要摧毁人类，而是利用人类来达到它自身的目的。

The Intervention’s view of humanity is cold and calculating and unsympathetic. It views you as chaotic and destructive. It does not know of individual freedom. Individual freedom is unknown to this Intervention. It sees you are destroying this magnificent world—a world it seeks to have for itself, a world it firmly believes it can manage far better than you.

干预对人类的看法是冷酷、算计且无情的。它视你们为混乱且破坏性的。它不知道个人自由。个人自由对这个干预而言是未知的。它看到你们正在毁灭这个宏伟的世界——一个它试图为它自己拥有的世界，一个它坚信自己能够比你们管理得更好的世界。

Yet do not ever think that their governance of the world would lead to advantage for humanity. You have no idea of what harsh conditions it would create. Everything that humanity has ever created that has been beneficial—in its philosophy, in its sociology, in its political arena, in its art—would be destroyed. You would be slaves to a foreign power, a foreign power that is cruel and exacting—quite unlike what you believe an advanced civilization would do or be.

然而，千万不要认为他们对世界的治理会给人类带来益处。你们根本不知道这会造成多么严酷的境况。人类曾经创造的一切有益之物——无论是在哲学、社会学、政治舞台还是艺术领域——都将被毁灭。你们将沦为一个外来势力的奴隶，一个残酷苛刻的外国势力——这与你们所认为的先进文明的所作所为截然不同。

And this Intervention is not being generated from your neighbors in the Greater Community, for the rules of conduct in this part of the universe do not allow for conquest of other worlds, other inhabited worlds where there is a native race. Those intervening in the world today are mercenary. They are resource explorers and economic collectives, groups that are not highly regarded, even amongst your neighboring worlds in the Greater Community. They are scavengers.

这种干预并非源自你们大社区的邻居，因为宇宙这一部分的行为准则不允许征服其他世界，其他有原住民族居住的世界。当今干预世界的种族是雇佣兵。他们是资源探索者和经济集体，即使在你们大社区的邻近世界，这些群体也不受重视。他们是拾荒者。

They will present themselves as noble, and they will use your spirituality and your spiritual idealism to paint themselves as liberators, as emancipators, as almost angelic. But they are nothing more than opportunists. And this world and humanity is the opportunity. And there is more than one group that is intervening in the world today.

他们会把他们自己标榜为高尚之人，利用你们的精神性和你们的精神理想主义，把他们自己描绘成解放者、拯救者，甚至近乎天使。但他们不过是机会主义者。而这个世界和人类本身就是机会。而且，当今世界，干预的团体不止一个。

As long as humanity appears to not be rejecting the Intervention or fighting it visibly, the rules of conduct in your part of the Galaxy will not prevent their participation here.

只要人类表现出不拒绝干预或明显地反抗干预，你们银河系的行为准则就不会阻止他们参与其中。

Some people say, “Well, if another race wants to take over, they will just invade, take over!” But you see, this is not how things work in the Greater Community. If you were living in a very remote area and there were no rules of engagement regarding trade, commerce and interaction between worlds, yes, that is possible. But this more reflects your values and the limits of your awareness.

有些人说：“好吧，如果另一个种族想要接管，他们就会入侵，接管！”但你看，大社区里的事情并非如此。如果你生活在一个非常偏远的地区，那里没有关于贸易、商业和世界间互动的规则，是的，这是可能的。但这更多地反映了你们的价值观和你们觉知的局限。

For worlds such as yours could be taken over without firing a shot. Take advantage of the natives’ conflicts and their superstitions, paint yourselves as gods and emancipators, assist them in their own decline and their own conflicts, and you can step into the corridors of power very easily. It just takes time. It has to be done gradually.

因为像你们这样的世界，无需费一枪一弹就能被占领。利用当地人的冲突和迷信，把他们自己描绘成神明和解放者，在他们自身的衰落和冲突中助纣为虐，你就能轻而易举地步入权力的殿堂。它只需要时间。它必须循序渐进。

The Intervention does not seek to destroy the world’s resources. They even view humanity as one of the world’s resources. Though they would seek to limit your population, they do not want to destroy you. You are a part of the resources of the world to them.

干预并非寻求摧毁世界资源。他们甚至将人类视为世界资源之一。尽管他们会寻求限制你们的人口，但他们并不想毁灭你们。对他们来说，你们是世界资源的一部分。

Do you see? As above, so below. Do you think the universe is full of enlightened beings? How can you be so foolish to think these things? Do you think other races are fascinated with humanity, as if your idiosyncracies are just a delight to other races? Do you really believe that advanced technology liberates you from having to survive in a competitive environment and having to gain access to resources?

你看到了吗？上行如是，下行亦然。你认为宇宙中充满了觉悟者吗？你怎么会这么愚蠢地想这些事情？你认为其他种族会对人类着迷吗？仿佛你们的特质只是其他种族的乐趣？你真的相信先进科技能把你们从竞争激烈的环境中解放出来，让你们不必为了获取资源而挣扎求生吗？

它不是人类想要的。它是人类必须做的。而这“必须”将基于必要性——而非基于幻想、渴望、愿望、宗教法令或意识形态——来组织人类活动、人类觉知和人类合作。人类必须做某些事，否则它就会失败，而如果它失败了，它就会被超越。这是肯定的。

Therefore, you must learn about the Greater Community, and you must learn about the Great Waves of change. Receive, then, God’s Revelation, for only it can prepare you and alert you and encourage you and ignite the deeper spiritual Knowledge that God has placed within each person to guide and to protect them—a Knowledge that has yet been discovered by so few within your world, a Knowledge that will be necessary now, to see and to know and to take the steps of preparation that are necessary.

因此，你必须学习大社区，你必须学习变革的巨浪。因此，接收上帝的启示吧，因为唯有它能够准备你们、警示你们、鼓励你们，并点燃上帝置于每个人内在以指引和保护他们的更深层次的精神内识——这个内识在你们的世界中尚未被极少数人发现，这个内识现在将是必要的，以便去看见、去知道，并采取必要的准备步骤。

For the hour is late. And humanity does not have much time. Time now is of the essence. What will make the difference is inspired individuals and small groups of individuals. Not everyone is going to awaken to this. Not everyone is going to learn to prepare. So it is up to those individuals and small groups of people to establish this emphasis upon human unity and cooperation and the recognition of necessity.

因为时间已晚。人类没有多少时间了。现在时间至关重要。能够带来改变的是那些受到启发的个人和小群体。并非每个人都会意识到这一点。并非每个人都会学会做好准备。因此，建立对人类团结与合作以及对必要性的认知的重视，取决于这些个人和小群体。

You must see that those who are flying around in your skies are not tourists. They are not here on a science project. They are not part of some university project from some other world. They are resource explorers.

你必须看见，那些在你们天空中飞行的生物并非游客。他们来这里不是为了某个科学项目。他们也不是来自其他星球的某个大学项目的成员。他们是资源勘探者。

Read about this. Learn about this. And do not be deceived by the Intervention and its claims of spirituality and nobility. Do not believe it is here to save humanity. Its intentions are far more prosaic.

阅读这个。学习这个。不要被干预及其宣称的精神性和高尚所欺骗。不要相信它来此是为了拯救人类。它的意图远比这更平淡无奇。

Nature is competitive. The competition for environment is part of nature. Nature exists throughout the universe. Do not think that the laws of nature do not hold true for other races, regardless of their technology. Do not think that technology will save you, or you will give yourself to a race who is technologically superior to you.

自然界充满竞争。对环境的竞争是自然的一部分。自然存在于整个宇宙。不要认为自然法则不适用于其他种族，无论他们的科技如何。不要认为科技会拯救你们，或者你把你自己交给一个科技比你们更优越的种族。

All these things that are being shared with you here are part of gaining a Greater Community awareness and understanding, which is so greatly needed now. When you begin to see this, it will be clear as day to you. But you must begin to see it, and you must stay with seeing it.

这里与你分享的所有这些，都是获得大社区觉知和理解的一部分，而这在当下是极其必要的。当你开始看到这一点时，它对你来说将清晰如白昼。但你必须开始看到它，并且必须坚持看到它。

You must look and look and look. And then you can gain an understanding. And the New Message will help you gain this understanding. And you will see that this understanding is not some fantastic or distorted view of reality, but it is reality itself and that it coincides with everything that you know about nature and everything that history has taught you about the nature of interventions, which are always carried out for self-interest.

你必须观察、观察、再观察。然后你才能获得理解。新讯息将帮助你获得这种理解。你将看到，这种理解并非某种奇异或扭曲的现实观，而是现实本身，它与你所知的关于自然的一切，以及历史教给你的关于干预本质的一切相吻合，而干预总是为了自身利益而进行。

The New Message wants to amplify your worldly wisdom and give it greater scope and application within a Greater Community context. It wants to amplify what Knowledge within you is already telling you and has always been telling you.

新讯息想要放大你的世俗智慧，并赋予它在大社区背景下更广阔的视野和应用。它想要放大你内在内识已经告诉你的、以及一直在告诉你的事情。

It wants you to outgrow your fantasies, and your preferences, and your self-obsessions so that you can see the movement of life—both within your world and in the Greater Community itself. It wants your religions to be based on Knowledge, not upon the belief in heroes and heroines, not upon the absurd assertion of absolute ideas.

它希望你超越你的空想、你的偏好和你的自我痴迷，这样你才能看到生命的运动——既在你们的世界中，也在大社区之中。它希望你的宗教基于内识，而非基于对英雄的信仰，而非基于对绝对想法的荒谬断言。

For no one in the world knows the Mind of God or what God is doing now. Never think that God’s Revelations to humanity are complete, for they must be ongoing. And these Revelations are given at critical times in history to prepare humanity to meet a great challenge and to use a great opportunity.

因为世上无人知晓上帝的心意，也无人知晓上帝此刻正在做什么。切勿认为上帝给予人类的启示是完整的，因为它们必定是持续不断的。这些启示是在历史的关键时刻赐予人类，为的是帮助人类做好准备，迎接伟大挑战，并使用一种伟大的机会。

The great challenge before you now is facing the Great Waves of change in the world with all of your energy and commitment, and to face the reality of the Intervention and the Greater Community with all of your energy and commitment.

你现在面临的伟大挑战是用你所有的能量和承诺去面对世界的变革巨浪，用你所有的精力和承诺去面对干预和大社区的现实。

The opportunity for you is to escape from the Hell of your own self-obsession, to end human conflict, to generate human unity based upon necessity, upon a Greater Community awareness, to meet the Great Waves of change and to offset their great hazards; and to turn your defenses against the Greater Community, for you must protect the world from Intervention. For there is Intervention now and there will be Intervention in the future.

你们的机会是逃离你们自我痴迷的地狱，结束人类冲突，基于必要性、基于大社区觉知来构建人类团结，应对变革的巨浪并抵消它们的巨大危害；并将你们的防御转向大社区，因为你们必须保护世界免遭干预。因为现在有干预，未来也将有干预。

If you are to be free, if you are to be respected, if you are to be a sovereign race in the Greater Community, you must learn to establish your boundaries in space. You may claim authority over this solar system, but no more. That is the limit to God’s endowment for humanity. If you seek control beyond this, then you will become an aggressive and predatory nation in the universe, and others will oppose you, and eventually you will fall.

如果你们想要自由，如果你们想要受人尊敬，如果你们想要成为大社区中的一个主权种族，你们就必须学会在太空中确立你们的界限。你们可以宣称对这个太阳系拥有权威，但仅此而已。这是上帝赋予人类的极限。如果你们寻求超越此界限的控制，那么你们将成为宇宙中一个侵略性和掠夺性的国家，其他种族将反对你们，最终你们将衰亡。

You have certain rights. But you have certain responsibilities too. Do not claim these rights without claiming these responsibilities, or you will not realize what you are doing.

你们拥有某些权利，但你们也承担某些责任。切勿只主张这些权利而不承担这些责任，否则你将无法意识到你正在做什么。

In the history of the universe, emerging races such as your own have so often fallen prey to foreign domination, have been absorbed by other races, cultures and political establishments. And some in many cases have simply been enslaved, like herds of animals. In the history of the universe, every possible error you can imagine has been committed countless times. Do not then become another statistic in this.

在宇宙的历史中，像你们这样的新兴种族常常沦为外来统治的牺牲品，被其他种族、文化和政治体制同化。在很多情况下，有些种族甚至像兽群一样被奴役。在宇宙的历史中，你们所能想到的所有错误都曾被无数次地犯过。因此，不要成为其中的又一个统计数字。

God wills that humanity become a free and united race in the Greater Community. But to achieve this, you must become united. You must become self-sufficient. And you must become extremely discerning and discreet regarding your relations and interactions with intelligent life in the universe. You must gain the wisdom and experience to tell friend from foe and to discern those who would use you for their own purposes.

上帝的意志是人类成为大社区中一个自由团结的种族。但要实现这一点，你们必须团结起来。你们必须自给自足。你们必须变得极其明辨和审慎，以应对你们与宇宙智能生命的关系和互动。你们必须获得智慧和经验，以区分敌友，并辨别那些为了他们自身目的而利用你们的种族。

God gives humanity what it needs, not what it expects or wants. You pray to God for little things, and God gives you the great thing. You do not know your predicament or your vulnerability. But God speaks to that. You do not know your true priorities in life. But God speaks to your true priorities.

上帝赐予人类所需的，而非其期望或欲望。你向上帝祈求小事，上帝却赐予你大事。你不知道你的困境或脆弱，但上帝会对此讲话。你不知道你在生命中真正优先考虑的内识，但上帝会对你真正优先考虑的内识进行讲话。

What people want and expect from God at this point do not represent the great needs of humanity. And it is to these great needs that God has given the New Message for the world—to prepare the world so that the world may have a future; to prepare humanity so that it may have a future; to nurture the growing evolution of human freedom and cooperation, which is still so fledgling here; to nurture this and to prepare it for the great challenges to come.

人们此刻对上帝的渴望和期待并不代表人类的伟大需求。正是针对这些伟大需求，上帝赋予了世界的新讯息——让世界进行准备，这样世界才能拥有一个未来；让人类进行准备，这样它才能拥有一个未来；滋养人类自由与合作的不断演进，它在这里依然如此羽翼未丰；滋养它，并为即将到来的伟大挑战进行准备。
