第六章：国家的命运

发表于2025-05-21|更新于2025-05-21|来自上帝的新讯息第五卷新世界预言
|总字数:6.2 k|阅读时长:26 分钟

The Fate of Nations

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on April 17, 2007 in Istanbul, Turkey
2007 年 4 月 17 日，在土耳其伊斯坦布尔(Istanbul, Turkey)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

This is part of the Message for the World regarding the fate of nations and peoples and the greater Calling that is now sounding for humanity.

这是关于国家和人民的命运以及现在正在向人类发出的更伟大召唤的世界讯息的一部分。

The peoples and the nations of the world are entering a grave time of difficulty and change. The world’s resources are declining. Populations are growing. And as this occurs, there will be continued competition and the grave danger of conflict and war.

世界各国人民和国家正步入一个充满困难和变革的严峻时期。世界资源正在减少，人口却在增长。随着这种情况的发生，竞争将持续下去，冲突和战争的危险也将加剧。

Here ancient animosities will be aroused. Prejudices will be played upon and used by those who are ambitious within both governments and religious institutions. Human weakness will be preyed upon, and fear will be pervasive.

古老的仇恨将被唤醒。偏见将被政府和宗教机构中那些野心勃勃的人利用。人类的弱点将被利用，恐惧将弥漫全球。

It is because of this great threshold that a New Message from God has been sent into the world for the protection and advancement of humanity. In order to understand the greatness of this Message and its relevance to your time, you must come to understand and recognize those great changes that are now threatening the human family—threatening to send it into decline, threatening to ruin the great accomplishments of civilization, threatening to undermine the well-being and the survival of people everywhere.

正是因为这个伟大的门槛，一个来自上帝的新讯息被发送到世界上，以保护和促进人类。为了理解这个讯息的伟大及其与你们时代的关联，你必须理解并认知那些正在威胁人类家庭的重大变化——威胁着将其推向衰落，威胁着摧毁文明的伟大成就，威胁着破坏世界各地民众的福祉和生存。

Here politics and religion itself will be used as weapons of war, as a leverage to separate people and to pit them against one another in a great conflict, not over ideology, but over the resources of the world.

在这里，政治和宗教本身将被用作战争武器，成为分裂人民、使人民在巨大冲突中相互对抗的筹码，冲突的起因不是意识形态，而是世界资源。

Here the debate and the conflict will be masked. It will be clothed in the disguise of politics and religion, but in essence it is a competition for resources. It is a conflict over access and the distribution of resources.

在这里，争论和冲突将被掩盖，披着政治和宗教的外衣，但本质上是一场资源竞争，一场争夺资源获取和分配的冲突。

It is a great danger that humanity now faces as it begins to encounter a world in decline—a world whose environments have been degraded, a world whose climate has been affected, a world whose life-sustaining resources are being depleted and ruined by human ignorance and human greed. It is for this reason that a New Message from God has been sent into the world.

人类现在面临的巨大危险是，它开始面对一个衰落的世界——一个环境恶化的世界，一个气候受到影响的世界，一个维持生命的资源正因人类的无知和贪婪而枯竭和毁灭的世界。正因如此，一条来自上帝的新讯息被发送到了这个世界。

To recognize this New Message and to understand its Source and its meaning for this time, this time of Revelation, you must come to recognize the predicament that humanity is now entering and that it is already facing in so many places.

为了认知这个新讯息，并理解它的源泉以及它对这个时代、这个启示时代的意义，你必须认识到人类现在正在进入的、并且它已经在很多地方面对的困境。

You will not be able to appreciate the New Message from God unless you can face these realities, unless you can recognize these conditions, unless you can see that without a greater Message from the Source of all life, humanity’s possibility to navigate these difficult times is very small. The possibility for humanity to avoid ongoing war, conflict and degradation is very small. The promise for humanity to begin a greater future, a future that will be unlike the past, will be very small.

除非你能够面对这些现实，除非你能够认知这些状况，除非你能够看见，如果没有一个来自所有生命源头的更伟大讯息，人类渡过这些艰难时期的可能性将非常渺茫。人类避免持续战争、冲突和退化的可能性非常渺茫。人类开启一个更伟大未来、一个不同于过去的未来的希望也将非常渺茫。

The odds against humanity’s success now are so significant that there must be a New Message from God. You must see it within this context, or you will not understand the time of Revelation in which you live. You will not understand.

现在人类成功的几率如此之大，以至于必须有一个来自上帝的新讯息。你必须在这个背景中看待它，否则你将无法理解你所生活的这个启示时代。你将无法理解。

To those who will be tempted to take up the sword in the name of religion or political advantage: you must recognize that your thoughts, your intent and your actions go against God’s Will for humanity. They are a violation of what God intends for humanity.

对于那些为了宗教或政治利益而试图拿起刀剑的人：你必须认识到，你的思想、意图和行动违背了上帝对人类的意志。它们违背了上帝对人类的旨意。

For God intends for humanity to become united and self-sufficient within a universe full of intelligent life. Even at this moment, there are other powers beyond this world who are awaiting humanity’s failure, awaiting the opportunity to intervene to take advantage of a weak and conflicted humanity.

因为上帝的旨意是让人类在一个充满智能生命的宇宙中团结一致、自给自足。即使在此刻，这个世界之外也存在着其他大国，它们正在等待人类的失败，等待着介入的机会，利用人类的软弱和冲突。

If you cannot see this or cannot accept this, then you do not understand humanity’s great predicament at this time. You do not understand the travail that is now upon the human family. And you will not see the great danger, the Greater Darkness that now lurks in the world, a Darkness that is born of intervention from beyond and a Darkness that is born of human ignorance, greed, competition, conflict and war.

如果你看不到这一点，或者无法接受这一点，那么你就无法理解人类此刻所面临的巨大困境。你无法理解人类家庭如今所承受的苦难。你也无法看到那巨大的危险，那潜伏在世间的更大黑暗，那源于外来干预的黑暗，那源于人类的无知、贪婪、竞争、冲突和战争的黑暗。

To you who would take up the sword in the name of religion or political advantage: You must realize that you are violating God’s Will for humanity. And though the Creator will not punish you, you will within yourself step away from the Knowledge of God within yourself. You will cast yourself into darkness and confusion. And you will lead your peoples and your nation into conflict, desolation and ruin. For you are beyond the time in which one nation can simply profit from the decline of another, for the whole world is now at stake.

致那些为了宗教或政治利益而拿起剑的人：你们必须意识到，你们正在违背上帝对人类的旨意。尽管造物主不会惩罚你们，但你们的内心却远离了对内在上帝的认知。你们将把你们自己投入到黑暗与混乱。你们将带领你们的人民和国家陷入冲突、荒凉与毁灭。因为你们已经超越了一个国家仅仅从另一个国家的衰落中获利的时代，因为整个世界如今都岌岌可危。

If you would take up the sword in the name of religion or political advantage, you will violate God’s Plan for humanity. Though God will not punish you directly, you will separate yourself and turn away from the Knowledge of God that is within you, that God has placed within you for your own protection and redemption. And you will cast yourself into chaos and conflict, into rage and misery, and bring ruin and desolation to your people.

如果你以宗教或政治利益的名义拿起刀剑，你将违背上帝对人类的计划。尽管上帝不会直接惩罚你，但你会分离你自己，背离上帝的内识，而上帝的内识就在你的内在，是上帝为了保护和救赎你而放在你内心的。你将把你自己投入到混乱和冲突之中，陷入愤怒和痛苦之中，并给你们的人民带来毁灭和荒凉。

This is a great risk, and this will be the great temptation of humanity—to exercise its grievance against one another, to exercise its ancient animosity, to begin to compete and enter into conflict with other nations, believing it is for the well-being of your nation or for the Will of God.

这是一个巨大的风险，这将是人类的巨大诱惑——对彼此表达它的不满，表达它的古老敌意，开始与其他国家竞争和冲突，相信这是为了你们国家的福祉或是为了上帝的意志。

But in both cases you are deceived. You are in error. You are violating God’s Will and Plan for humanity. Though God will not punish you directly, you will separate yourself from God’s Wisdom and God’s Will. You will enter into confusion and darkness, into grievance and ultimately into despair.

但在这两种情况下，你都被欺骗了。你犯了错误。你违背了上帝对人类的意志和计划。虽然上帝不会直接惩罚你，但你会让你自己远离上帝的智慧和上帝的意志。你会陷入困惑与黑暗，陷入怨恨，最终陷入绝望。

Take heed, then, the nations of the world, the leaders of the world and the citizens of the world. Heed these words, for they come from the Divine Will and Presence in your life. They represent the Knowledge that has been placed within you to guide and protect you. It is the same Knowledge that is here to guide and protect all of humanity.

那么，世界各国、世界各国的领导人以及世界各国的公民们，请注意。留意这些话语，因为它们来自你生命中的神圣意志和存在。它们代表着被置于你内在以指引和保护你的内识。正是这同样的内识在此指引和保护着全人类。

This is why the Messenger has been sent into the world—to bring this Message of hope, this Message of restitution and resolution, to the human family at this time of great travail.

正因为如此，信使被派到这个世界——在人类大家庭遭受巨大苦难的时刻，将这个希望的讯息、恢复和解决的讯息带给他们。

For you are entering a time of great travail. You are just beginning the time in which the great wars and the great conflicts can emerge. The embers for these wars are already glowing. The intent for these wars is already alive in the minds and hearts of many people. And the conditions for these conflicts and wars are upon you.

因为你们正在进入一个极其艰难的时期。你们才刚刚开始一个可能爆发重大战争和重大冲突的时期。这些战争的余烬已然熊熊燃烧。这些战争的意图已然在许多人的心灵和内心中活跃起来。而这些冲突和战争的条件已经降临到你们身上。

Here humanity has its one great chance to unite in its own defense and for its own preservation—both within the world and in the face of a Greater Community of life in the universe, where freedom is unknown and humanity’s value is not recognized, save but by a few.

在这里，人类有了一次团结起来捍卫和保护它自身的伟大机会——无论是在世界内部，还是在面对宇宙生命的大社区，在那里，自由是未知的，人类的价值不被认可，只有少数种族除外。

Those who would take up the sword in the name of God or for political advantage will have failed their mission and calling in life, and they will lead others to failure as well. Within this failure, there will be no peace and no resolution. For you have denied the Knowledge of God within yourself. You have denied the Plan of the Creator for humanity. You have turned yourself against the greater Wisdom that God has placed within you and within all hearts. You will have, through ignorance and arrogance and vanity, destroyed your great opportunity to make a real contribution to your people, to your nation and to the world.

那些以上帝的名义或为了政治利益拿起刀剑的人，将辜负他们生命中的使命和召唤，他们也将导致他人的失败。在这个失败中，将不会有和平，不会有解决。因为你们否认了你们自己内在的上帝内识。你们否认了造物主对人类的计划。你们背叛了上帝放置在你们内在以及所有内心内的更伟大智慧。你们将通过无知、傲慢和虚荣，毁掉你们为你们的人民、你们的国家和世界做出真正贡献的伟大机会。

Take heed, then, of this warning. It carries with it the gravest of consequences and the greatest meaning. Its meaning and its message is beyond your comprehension, and yet what it has to say to you will be clear and simple and easy for you to understand.

那么，请留意这个警告。它蕴含着极其严重的后果和极其深远的意义。它的意义和讯息超出了你的理解范围，然而它要对你说的话却清晰、简单，你很容易理解。

Beware, the leaders of nations and the leaders of religion. Beware if you seek to hurt or conquer another nation for your own advantage or to express your grievance or your ancient animosities. Be aware that you yourself are casting yourself into the darkness, into confusion and misery. And you will bring desolation, destitution and ruin upon your peoples as a result.

各国领袖和宗教领袖们，要当心。要警觉，如果你们为了自身的利益，或为了表达你们的怨恨或你们古老的仇恨，而试图伤害或征服另一个国家。要察觉到，你们自己正在把自己投入黑暗、混乱和苦难之中。结果，你们将给你们的人民带来荒凉、贫困和毁灭。

And be aware, the average citizen, that you too have a responsibility to follow the Wisdom and Knowledge that God has placed within you, to discover this Wisdom and Knowledge and not to violate it because of anger or revenge, rage or greed. You too have a responsibility, for the outcome is not simply up to the leaders of the nations and the religions. It is up to every citizen ultimately .

作为普通公民，请意识到，你也有责任遵循上帝赋予你的智慧和内识，去发现这个智慧和内识，而不是因为愤怒、报复、暴怒或贪婪而违背它。你也有责任，因为结果不仅仅取决于国家和宗教的领导人。最终，它取决于每一位公民。

Take heed then of this great warning. This warning is born of the Love of God, but it is a warning nonetheless. It is warning you against the ever-increasing possibility for error. It is advising you to turn to the Knowledge of God that lives within you—beyond and beneath your thinking mind; beyond the ideas and concepts and beliefs that are historically established within humanity.

那么，留意这伟大的警告吧。这警告源于上帝的爱，但它终究是一个警告。它警告你警惕日益增长的错误可能性。它建议你转向活在你内在的上帝的内识——超越和深入到你心灵表面层次之下的心灵；超越人类历史上确立的想法、概念和信仰。

For there is a moral and ethical foundation to your existence. Should you violate this, you will suffer the consequences—not because God is punishing you, but because you are punishing yourself, because you have turned away from what God has placed within you and because, out of arrogance, ignorance and vanity, you have given yourself authority to override what God has placed within you. Be aware of the great danger for you individually, for your people, your nation and the world. For the great time of trial and tribulation is upon you.

因为你的存在有着道德和伦理的基础。如果你违背了这一点，你将承受后果——并非因为上帝在惩罚你，而是因为你在惩罚你自己，因为你背离了上帝赋予你内在的内识，因为你出于傲慢、无知和虚荣，赋予你自己凌驾于上帝赋予你内在的内识之上的权威。要察觉到这对你个人、对你的人民、你的国家以及世界都构成了巨大的危险。因为伟大的考验和磨难时代已经降临到你身上。

How humanity will respond, both its leaders and its citizens, will make all the difference in the outcome. The power of decision here is with you. God’s Will is clear. Where God is taking humanity in preparation for its encounter with life in the universe is clear, and is presented in the New Message.

人类，无论是其领导者还是公民，将如何回应，将对结果产生至关重要的影响。决定权在于你们。上帝的意志是明确的。上帝将带领人类走向何方，为与宇宙生命的相遇做准备，这一点是明确的，并已在新讯息中呈现。

But in order for you to reap these great rewards, in order for you to prepare yourself for a future that will be unlike the past, in order for you to avoid calamity and disaster, you must take heed. You must stop. You must restrain your anger and your passions. You must withhold your judgments of others. You must consider the wisdom of your thoughts and actions and their consequences for the future.

但为了你收获这些伟大的回报，为了你为一个不同于过去的未来做好准备，为了你避免灾难和灾祸，你必须谨慎。你必须停下来。你必须抑制你的愤怒和激情。你必须克制你对他人的评判。你必须思考你思想和行动的智慧，以及它们对未来的后果。

Be prepared, for great and difficult times are upon you. They are a product of human ignorance and human abuse of the world. They are the product of humanity’s lack of vision and its lack of preparation for the future.

做好准备吧，因为伟大而艰难的时代即将来临。他们是人类的无知和对世界滥用的产物。他们是人类缺乏远见和对未来缺乏准备的产物。

It will be the responsibility of all nations and [their] leaders to provide for their people adequately in the times to come, but this will become ever more difficult and challenging. The rich will have to take care of the poor. And the poor will have to take care of one another. There will have to be greater service to humanity, greater selflessness under these difficult conditions.

在未来的日子里，所有国家及其领导人都有责任为其人民提供充足的供给，但这将变得越来越困难和富有挑战性。富人必须照顾穷人。穷人必须互相照顾。在这些艰难的条件下，必须为人类提供更伟大的服务，更伟大的无私。

For humanity has pillaged the Earth to such an extent that it can barely sustain the human family. The conditions and the circumstances of your life, whether you be rich or poor, no matter what nation you live in, will now press these needs upon you.

人类对地球的掠夺已经到了难以维持人类大家庭生存的地步。你们的生活条件和境遇，无论贫富，无论身处哪个国家，都将把这些需求强加于你们。

Be clear and aware that this great difficulty has called the New Message from God into the world and God’s Messenger has been sent into the world to give this Message, to elucidate it and to be its primary focus and vehicle of expression.

要清楚并察觉到，这个巨大困难召唤了来自上帝的新讯息来到这个世界，上帝的信使被派到这个世界来传递这条讯息，阐明它，并成为它的主要焦点和表达载体。

Do not judge and condemn the Messenger lest you judge and condemn what God has placed within you. For though the Messenger is human and fallible, he brings with him a Message that is beyond humanity’s invention and that is without error and conflict and contradiction.

不要评判和谴责信使，否则你也会评判和谴责上帝赋予你的内识。尽管信使是凡人，会犯错，但他带来的讯息超越人类的发明，没有错误、冲突和矛盾。

Receive this Message then. Consider it in light of the world you see and the world you know. Think of the history of humanity and its great battles, its great conquests and how utterly destructive this has been—that so many people should sacrifice so that a few may enrich themselves.

那么，接收这个讯息吧。结合你所见的世界和你所认知的世界来思考它。想想人类的历史，想想它伟大的战役，它伟大的征服，想想这有多么彻底的破坏性——如此多的人牺牲，只为让少数人致富。

Consider now that this cannot proceed into the future lest the entire human family enter a state of permanent decline, lest you make yourself vulnerable to intervention and invasion from forces from beyond the world, who even now are planning to take advantage of humanity’s conflicts and dissolution.

现在考虑一下，这种情况不能继续下去，否则整个人类大家庭将进入永久衰落的状态，否则你们将容易受到来自世界以外势力的干预和入侵，这些势力现在正计划利用人类的冲突和解体。

To know yourself is to know God’s Presence in your life, to know that God has placed a deeper Knowledge within you to guide and to protect you and to override your self-destructive tendencies and your selfish pursuits. To know yourself is to know what God has placed within you—not to understand it intellectually, but to know of its reality, to experience its presence and to follow its direction.

知道你自己，就是知道上帝在你生命中的存在，就是知道上帝已经将一个更深层次的内识置于你的内在，来指引、保护你，并克服你的自我毁灭倾向和你的自私追求。知道你自己，就是知道上帝已将内识置于你的内在——并非在理智上理解它，而是知道它的现实，体验它的存在，并遵循它的指引。

As uncertainty and anxiety and competition increase in the world, you will need this guidance now more than ever before. For the institutions, the comforts and the consolations that perhaps you have benefited from may not be there for you in the future. The assurances and the security that you believe were provided for you, to whatever extent they were, may not be there for you in the future.

随着世界不确定性、焦虑和竞争的加剧，你现在比以往任何时候都更需要这种指引。因为那些机构，你曾经受益的舒适和慰藉，未来未必能为你提供。你曾经认为曾经得到过的保障和安全，无论程度如何，未来也未必能为你提供。

So how will you know how to be secure? How will you know what to do in the face of uncertainty and change? God’s answer lives within you, and to remind you of this [God] has brought a New Message from God into the world and has sent Its Messenger into the world. You do not know enough yet to question this or to dispute it, for that is merely an expression of your ignorance and arrogance and vanity.

那么你如何知道如何获得安全？你如何知道在面对不确定性和变化时该做什么？上帝的答案生活在你的内在，为了提醒你这一点，上帝把一条来自上帝的新讯息带到了世界上，并派遣上帝的信使来到了这个世界。你还没有足够的知道去质疑它或争论它，因为那仅仅是你无知、傲慢和虚荣的一种表达。

You must consider the New Message. You must hear the Messenger to know, to see and to understand. For on your own, without this great endowment, you will not be able to see, to know and to understand. Your behavior will be determined by others. And you will follow fear and not wisdom in your approach.

你必须思考新讯息。你必须聆听信使才能知道、看见和理解。因为单凭你自己，如果没有这个伟大的禀赋，你将无法看见、知道和理解。你的行为将被他人决定。你将追随恐惧而非智慧。

Given this great danger and the great risks ahead, perhaps you can begin to see the Light that has been brought into the world in the face of a Greater Darkness that is now growing over the horizon.

这是你的责任。这是你们时代的召唤。它是一个启示的时代，而启示就在你身上。
