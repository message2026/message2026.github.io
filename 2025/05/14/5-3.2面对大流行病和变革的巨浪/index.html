第二章：面对大流行病和变革的巨浪

发表于2025-05-14|更新于2025-05-14|来自上帝的新讯息第五卷新世界预言
|总字数:8.8 k|阅读时长:36 分钟

Facing the Pandemic and the Great Waves of Change

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on November 7, 2021 in Boulder, Colorado
2021 年 11 月 7 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

At this moment in history, humanity is facing a great pandemic, a worldwide pandemic, a pandemic that came from nature, a pandemic that is highly dangerous, transmissible and highly effective at moving around the world with all the travel and interaction that human nations and populations have and engagements with one another.

在历史的这一刻，人类正面临着一场大流行病，一场世界性的流行病，一场来自自然的流行病，一场高度危险、传染性极强、能够通过人类国家和人口之间的旅行和互动在世界范围内迅速传播的流行病。

It is a great test for the human family. Can it respond? Will it become educated about it? Or will it simply hope that it is just a passing phase, a cloud passing over their life, to which they seek to return as if nothing serious has really happened?

它对人类大家庭来说是一次巨大的考验。它能应对吗？它会接受教训吗？还是它只是希望这只是一个短暂的阶段，是他们生命中飘过的一片云，然后试图回到过去，仿佛什么重大的事情都没发生过一样？

But alas, the pandemic is a great changing element for the human family, for it will last for years, and the destruction it will bring about will be so great and such a life-changing experience that it will impact people literally everywhere: their economies, their social structures, how they engage with other people, relations between nations and so forth.

但遗憾的是，这场大流行对人类大家庭来说是一个巨大的改变因素，因为它将持续数年，它所带来的破坏将是如此巨大，它将是一次改变人生的经历，它将影响到世界各地的人们：他们的经济、他们的社会结构、他们与他人的交往方式、国家之间的关系等等。

It is a great equalizer. It does not discriminate over who it wants to affect for it will go wherever the opportunity exists, be you rich or poor.

它是一个伟大的均衡器。它不会歧视任何想要影响的人，因为无论你富有还是贫穷，只要有机会，它就会去。

Even with modern medicine, it poses a great difficulty, for many people will not receive this medicine, thinking it is an invasion in their lives, or that it is questionable, or suspicious, or that it comes from a government they do not trust. But it will be helpful and effective. But it will require much more than this.

即使现代医学也难以做到这一点，因为许多人不愿接受这种药物，认为它是对他们生命的侵犯，或者认为它值得怀疑，或者认为它来自一个他们不信任的政府。但它确实有益且有效。但它需要的远不止这些。

Even at this moment, even after this has already done immense damage to the human family, to nations all over the world, even now people are skeptical and ambivalent. They are not really responding appropriately. They think this is just a great dilemma, a great nuisance, that it will pass soon, and everyone will go back to sleep, or try to survive, or whatever they were doing before, whether it be meaningful or not.

即使在此时此刻，即使在这已经对人类大家庭、对世界各国造成巨大损害之后，人们仍然心存疑虑，矛盾重重。他们并没有真正做出恰当的回应。他们认为这只是一个巨大的困境，一个巨大的麻烦，它很快就会过去，每个人都会回去睡觉，或者努力生存，或者继续他们之前所做的一切，无论它是否有意义。

But this is a Great Wave of change, a Great Wave upon the world. For humanity has disrupted nature so much that it has revealed elements in nature with which it should never have direct contact, entering into and exploiting the hidden resources of the world, the underground caverns of the world, and all of the diseases that are now embedded in the great Arctic regions which could be released in this century and the centuries to come to a humanity that has no resistance to them.

但这是一场变革的巨浪，一场席卷世界的巨浪。因为人类如此严重地扰乱了自然，以至于它揭示了自然中它本不该直接接触的元素，进入并开发了世界隐藏的资源，世界地下洞穴，以及现在深藏在北极大地的所有疾病，这些疾病可能在本世纪以及未来的几个世纪里被释放给一个对它们毫无抵抗力的人类。

Therefore, you must face these things. Stop pretending. Stop telling yourself stories about where it came from, and how it will be solved, and how it will soon be over, and you don’t need to worry about it because you are healthy, or you are young, or something like this. Stop deceiving yourself. You are facing a great adversary, an adversary that is now literally everywhere. Everywhere where people function and interact, it will be there. It will find every avenue of transmission.

因此，你必须面对这些。别再假装了。别再给你自己编故事，说什么病毒从何而来，会如何解决，很快就会结束，还说你不必担心，因为你健康，或者你年轻，诸如此类。别再自欺欺人了。你们正面临着一个强大的对手，一个如今几乎无处不在的对手。凡是人们活动和互动的地方，它都会在那里。它会找到每一条传播途径。

You cannot blame God. This has to do with your relationship with nature—a relationship that you have despoiled, that you have not taken seriously; a relationship that you have forgotten or did not feel connected to; a relationship that carries with it great benefits but also great responsibilities; a relationship that requires great restraint, caution and wisdom, which have largely been lacking in humanity’s exploitation of the world.

你不能责怪上帝。这关乎你们与自然的关系——一种你们已掠夺、未曾认真对待的关系；一种你们已遗忘或感觉与之无关联的关系；一种既带来巨大利益也承担巨大责任的关系；一种需要极大克制、谨慎和智慧的关系，而这些在人类对世界的开发中大多缺失。

There are consequences for this. It is not God’s Will. It is simply a result of living in your natural environment. There are forces there that you do not know how to contend with, invisible perhaps, something you would never think of, something the ancient peoples could never think of. It is not mysterious. It is not coming from some other planet or some other dimension of reality. It is part of the physical fabric of life in which you live, living in Separation in this one world.

这会带来一些后果。这并非上帝的意志。这仅仅是生活在你们自然环境中的结果。那里存在着你们不知道如何应对的力量，或许是看不见的，某种你们从未想过的事情，某种古代人们从未想过的事情。它并不神秘。它并非来自某个其他星球或某个其他现实维度。它是你们生活于其中的生命物质结构的一部分，生活在这个唯一世界的分离之中。

Look about and you will see who is responding, and who is not, who is being responsible and who is not, who is taking precautions and who is not, how few people today are really being educated about what they are facing.

环顾四周，你会看到谁在做出反应，谁没有；谁在负责任，谁没有；谁在采取预防措施，谁没有；如今，真正了解他们所面临的问题的人们是多么的少。

This has happened in the world in great ways in the history of humanity. More devastating than world wars it is, more consequential than anything that has ever happened to the planet before in the time of humanity. And yet people remain ignorant about it. They do not study the past, so they are not prepared for the future or even for the present moment.

在人类历史上，这种情况曾以各种重大的方式在世界上发生过。它比世界大战更具破坏性，比人类历史上地球上发生过的任何事件都更具后果性。然而，人们对此仍然一无所知。他们不研究过去，所以他们没有为未来，甚至没有为当下做好准备。

You must become strong to face this. It will make you strong facing this. It carries with it great uncertainty. It is not going to end any time soon, so what are you going to do? What kind of change will you bring about in your life and behavior? What kind of ideas will you have to abandon, or beliefs will you have to abandon? How will you change your thinking and your approach to life?

你必须变得坚强才能面对这一切。它会让你更加坚强地面对这一切。它蕴含着巨大的不确定性。它不会很快结束，那么你打算怎么做？你会在你的生命和行为中做出什么样的改变？你必须放弃什么样的想法或信念？你将如何改变你的思维方式和生活方式？

You cannot run away from this. You cannot go hide somewhere until it passes over. You cannot lock yourself in a room and hope it does not reach you.

你无法逃避这一切。你无法躲到别处等它过去。你无法把你自己锁在房间里，祈祷它不会降临到你身上。

We are saying these things to you today because there are greater Waves of Change coming to the world. You live in a world that is becoming hotter. You have changed the climate of the world, the chemistry of the atmosphere.

我们今天之所以对你说这些，是因为更强大的变革浪潮正在席卷世界。你生活在一个日益变热的世界。你们改变了世界的气候，改变了大气的化学成分。

Gradually, moment by moment, you may not notice unless you live in a very polluted place, but you are changing the atmosphere of the world, and the consequences of that will be even greater than the pandemic.

渐渐地，一刻一刻地，除非你生活在污染严重的地方，否则你可能不会注意到，但你们正在改变世界的氛围，其后果将比大流行病更为严重。

For whole regions of the world will become uninhabitable by humans. And where will they go? Who will take them into their countries? Who will respond? It carries even greater consequences, greater challenges than what you are facing here today.

因为世界将有整片地区变得不再适合人类居住。他们将去往何方？谁将把他们带回他们的国家？谁将做出回应？这将带来比你们今天在此所面临的更为严重的后果和挑战。

A pandemic, as great as it is—and it is great—is but one of the Great Waves of change. It can prepare you for what is to come beyond it, but if you cannot prepare, if you will not prepare, if you will not become educated and adapt yourself to changing circumstances, then you will not fare well in the future.

一场大流行病，尽管它很严重——而且确实很严重——但只是变革巨浪之一。它可以让你为之后的命运做好准备，但如果你不能做好准备，如果你不愿做好准备，如果你不愿接受教育并适应不断变化的环境，那么你在未来将不会有好的结果。

God has given you a greater strength of Knowledge, a deeper Intelligence within you, to teach you how to respond, to guide you where to go and not to go, what to do and not to do, what to think and what not to think. That is how God is going to save you, you see.

上帝赋予了你内识的更伟大力量，你内在的一种更深层次的智能，来教导你如何回应，指引你该去哪里，不该去哪里，该做什么，不该做什么，该思考什么，不该思考什么。你看，这就是上帝将要拯救你的方式。

God does not intervene in the world at the last moment to save everyone. That is foolishness. The miracle of God’s Grace and assistance lives within you at this moment, waiting to be discovered. But how few people in the world today know of this. It is really not emphasized in the world’s religions, though it does exist there as part of the teaching.

上帝不会在最后一刻介入世界，拯救所有人。那是愚蠢的。上帝的恩典和援助的奇迹此刻就存在于你的内心，等待着你去发现。但如今世界上很少有人知道这一点。虽然它确实存在于世界宗教的教导中，但实际上并没有被强调。

People follow each other around like sheep. People do what is easiest, only. People do not want to deal with things—their health, their relationships, their work, the quality of their life, what they are doing for themselves and other people. So much complacency, so much denial.

人们像绵羊一样互相追逐。人们只做最容易的事。人们不愿面对现实——他们的健康、他们的人际关系、他们的工作、他们的生命质量，以及他们为他们自己和他人所做的一切。如此多的自满，如此多的否认。

And then you have the poorest people of the world who literally cannot do anything of a greater magnitude given their circumstances—striving each day just to survive through great hardship and deprivation.

然后，你会看到世界上最贫穷的人们，他们由于自身境遇而无法做出任何更伟大的事情——他们每天挣扎求生，忍受着巨大的艰辛和贫困。

To know the truth, first you must face the truth, the truth of your circumstances, personally—your life, your health, your well-being, your stability and all the factors that support it or undermine it, the quality of your relationships, for no one in your life is neutral. They are all casting an influence upon you for better or for worse. You cannot wait for things to happen, for when they do, you will suffer. And you will be powerless to respond.

要知道真理，首先你必须面对真理，面对你个人境遇的真相——你的生命、你的健康、你的福祉、你的稳定以及所有支持或破坏它的因素，你关系的质量，因为在你的生命中没有人是中立的。它们都在对你产生影响，无论好坏。你不能坐等事情发生，因为一旦发生，你就会受苦。而你将无力应对。

There is so much for humanity to learn. It has become—with its wealth, the wealth that relatively few people in the world really have—it has become lazy. It has become unresponsive to the world, to nature—to nature around them and even their deeper nature within themselves, which is the reality of the Knowledge that God has placed there.

人类有太多事情需要学习。它已经变得——凭借它的财富，世界上相对少数人真正拥有的财富——它已经变得懒惰。它已经对世界、对自然——对他们周围的自然，甚至对他们自身内在的更深层次的自然——变得麻木不仁，而这正是上帝放置在内在的内识的现实。

Let there be no fantasies about this. Let there be no denial about this. Do not think that you will do well in these circumstances without preparing for them, consciously and in a determined way—not just for the moment here and there, but in a sustainable way.

不要对此抱有幻想。不要否认这一点。不要认为在这种情况下，如果没有做好准备，你就会做得很好，要有意识地、坚定地做好准备——不仅仅是为了此时此刻，而是要持续地做好准备。

You are going to be living in a more difficult and hazardous world. You cannot be fooling around in the face of this if you wish to survive and have well-being of any real meaning and value.

你将生活在一个更加艰难、更加危险的世界。如果你希望生存下去，并拥有真正意义和价值的幸福，就不能在这样的境况下胡闹。

This is the reality in which you live. You cannot make it go away. It is the circumstances of what has been created before, for humanity has destroyed so much of the wealth and the stability of nature around them. Now you must face the consequences of the past, as your children and you will face the consequences of the present as you go forward in life.

这就是你所处的现实。你无法让它消失。这是过去所创造的境况，因为人类已经摧毁了太多的财富和周围自然的稳定。现在你们必须面对过去的后果，正如你的孩子和你在生命前进的过程中都将面对现在的后果一样。

This is a gift, what We are telling you here today. It is not what you want to hear, but it is what will save you. It is not what you prefer, but what you prefer will only make you weak and unresponsive and cast in doubt your well-being and welfare for the future.

这是我们今天在此告诉你的一份礼物。它并非你想听的，但它将拯救你。它并非你想要的，但你想要的，只会让你变得软弱无力，反应迟钝，并质疑你未来的福祉。

People can be very determined about things they want, but you have to face things you do not want. That is reality. You have to re-engage with reality—not as you want it to be or hope it to be or insist that it is, but as it really is.

人们可以对他们想要的东西非常执着，但你必须面对你不想要的东西。这就是现实。你必须重新面对现实——不是你希望它变成什么样，希望它变成什么样，或者坚持它变成什么样，而是它本来的样子。

There are no conspiracies here. There is only the real interface with life, what is truly happening. That is the reality that you must engage with. Some of it will be hopeful. Some of it will not be hopeful. Some of it will be reassuring. Some of it will be quite unnerving. You have to face it all.

这里没有阴谋。只有与生命的真实交汇，真正正在发生的事情。这才是你必须面对的现实。有些会充满希望，有些则令人绝望，有些会令人安心，有些则会令人不安。你必须面对这一切。

Don’t demand that other people face it. Don’t expect that other people will face it. You have to face it first. If you do not, then life will be harsh for you, and you will feel helpless in its wake and its manifestations.

不要要求别人去面对它。不要指望别人会去面对它。你必须先面对它。如果你不这样做，生命对你来说将会很残酷，你会在它的影响和表现中感到无助。

This is a great wakeup call for people. You cannot be dreaming now of happiness and all the wonderful things you are going to be, do and have. You have to respond to the changing landscape.

这对人们来说是一个伟大的警钟。你现在不能梦想着幸福，以及你将要成为、做和拥有的一切美好事物。你必须应对不断变化的形势。

When your environment changes, everything changes. When your climate changes, everything changes. When your resources are depleted, everything changes.

当你们的环境改变时，一切都会改变。当你们的气候改变时，一切都会改变。当你们的资源枯竭时，一切都会改变。

Those who can see this and face this will find ways to prepare in a constructive way, not simply to fortify themselves, but to put themselves in a position where they can be stable enough to be of service to others. That is the goal here. You cannot hide somewhere and build a fortress around yourself, for there is no protecting that in the future.

那些能够看到并面对这一点的人，将会找到建设性地做好准备的方法，不仅仅是为了强化他们自己，更是为了让他们自己处于一个足够稳定的位置，从而能够服务他人。这就是我们的目标。你不能躲在某个地方，在你自己的周围筑起一座堡垒，因为未来没有任何保护措施。

I am speaking not to your personal wishes or your personality. I am speaking to a deeper part of you that can respond and will respond, if you allow it. It is why you have come.

我并非针对你的个人愿望或人格说话。我针对的是你内心更深层次的部分，它能够回应，而且只要你允许，它就会回应。这就是你来这里的原因。

You will never find your true purpose in life by chasing happiness and fantasies. It is by meeting real challenges that you were designed and meant to face that will really restore you and give you a life of purpose and meaning and greater direction.

追逐幸福和幻想永远无法找到生命的真正目的。只有直面你被设计、注定要面对的真正挑战，才能真正让你重获新生，让你拥有一个充满目的、意义和更伟大方向的生命。

You can argue. You can debate. You can believe anything. But the only thing that matters is that you can face and engage with reality and become a force of service and contribution there.

你可以争论，你可以辩论，你可以相信任何事。但唯一重要的是，你能直面现实，参与其中，成为一股服务和贡献的力量。

The needs in the future will be so predominating you cannot even imagine it. Now you are fighting a battle with nature. It is a battle not that nature intended to have happen. It is a battle that you have established and have set in motion—you and all those who came before you, thinking the world was an endless well, a cornucopia of benefits and wealth.

未来的需求将如此显著，你甚至无法想象。现在，你们正在与自然搏斗。这场搏斗并非自然所愿。这场搏斗是由你们——以及所有先你而来的人——建立并发起的，他们认为世界是一口无尽的井，一个利益和财富的聚宝盆。

But alas you have outstripped the lands, contaminated the air and the waters and depleted the soils. It will take an immense human collaboration and effort sustained over time to be able to rectify these things. And that is not for someone else to do. You will play some part in all of this, you see, based on your skills and your natural inclination.

但遗憾的是，你们已经侵占了土地，污染了空气和水源，耗尽了土壤。要纠正这些现象，需要人类长期不懈的努力和通力合作。而这并非他人所能承担。你看，根据你的技能和天性，你将在这一切中扮演一定的角色。

Already the Earth is becoming more difficult. Already the climate is changing and disasters are re-occurring at an increasing frequency and potency and destructiveness. Already the pandemic is reaching into the far reaches of human society to the remotest places. You will not stop it by running away or hiding out.

地球已然变得愈发艰难。气候已然发生变化，灾害频发，其强度和破坏力也日益增强。大流行病已然蔓延至人类社会的各个角落，直至最偏远的地方。你无法通过逃避或躲藏来阻止它。

It can be confronted, but people will have to really participate and recognize it is a long-term effort, not something you are willing to do for a few weeks or months. It is a great effort. And it can unite people and strengthen people and enable them to overcome their differences and their idealism and their persuasions and their ideologies that clash and produce endless conflict and dissonance in the world.

它可以应对，但人们必须真正参与其中，并认识到这是一项长期努力，而不是你愿意在几周或几个月内完成的事情。这是一项伟大的努力。它能够团结人们，增强人们的力量，使他们能够克服彼此之间的分歧、理想主义、信仰和意识形态，这些正是世界上造成无尽冲突与不和谐的原因。

The pandemic is your training now. First, protect yourself, either through vaccination or through some other means if it is effective, but you must protect yourself, for it can either kill you or it can maim you for life. And it can re-occur because even with vaccination, if that is not sustained, it can re-occur. That is how big this problem is, you see.

大流行病现在就是你的训练。首先，保护好你自己，可以通过接种疫苗或其他有效途径，但你必须保护好你自己，因为它可能夺走你的生命，也可能让你终身残疾。而且疫情可能会复发，因为即使接种了疫苗，如果不坚持下去，它也可能复发。这就是这个问题的严重性。

You do not simply have to agree. Just walk around this. Consider it. Ponder it. See where you are weak and where you are strong. See where you are likely to give up and how you have given up before. And then recall times you did things that were strong and demanding and difficult. You are going to be living in a more difficult world now. You cannot be weak and fragile and foolish in the wake of this.

你不必简单地同意。只需围绕这一点走一走，认真思考，深思熟虑。看看你的弱点在哪里，你的强项在哪里。看看你可能在哪些方面放弃，以及你以前是如何放弃的。然后回想一下你曾经做过的那些困难、艰巨和艰难的事情。你现在将生活在一个更加艰难的世界里。在经历了这一切之后，你不能再软弱、脆弱和愚蠢了。

God’s Will is that humanity will prepare, and this pandemic will be part of the preparation. You are facing great hazards here on Earth and great hazards from beyond the Earth from races who will seek to use this world for their own purposes as humanity declines and struggles with its own difficulties.

上帝的意志是人类将做好准备，而这场疫情将是这个准备的一部分。你们正面临着地球上的巨大危险，以及来自地球之外的巨大危险，这些危险来自那些试图利用这个世界来达到他们自身目的的种族，因为人类正在衰落，并在它自身的困境中挣扎。

This has been foreseen by certain people. It is part of the great times for which you have come. And you have come here with a purpose sent from Heaven to make a difference in this world that you would rather avoid or deny.

某些人早已预见到了这一点。这是你为之而来的伟大时代的一部分。你来到这里，带着天国赋予的目的，为这个世界带来改变，而你宁愿逃避或否认这种改变。

This is your greater life, not simply being weak and shallow and incompetent. Some people will choose that path, and have chosen it already. Let that not be your decision, for the outcome for them will be certainly not favorable.

这是你更伟大的生命，而不仅仅是软弱、肤浅和无能。有些人会选择这条路，而且已经选择了。但愿你不要选择这条路，因为他们的结果肯定不会好。

Nature is unmerciful to the unprepared. And you are dealing with nature now. God is watching to see what you will do, for God has already put Knowledge within each person, waiting to be discovered. If they do not utilize it, if they do not become aware of it, if they do not follow it, then they will have to face the consequences—the difficulty of living in physical life, the great tragedy of living in Separation from your Source in a temporary physical reality that is full of hazard, change and uncertainty.

大自然对毫无准备的人是无情的。而你现在正在与大自然打交道。上帝正在注视着你将做什么，因为上帝已经将内识放置在每个人的内在，等待着被发现。如果他们不运用它，如果他们不察觉到它，如果他们不遵循它，那么他们将不得不面对后果——生活在物质生命中的艰难，生活在与你本源分离、在一个充满危险、变化和不确定性的暂时物质现实中的巨大悲剧。

Before you can help others, you must stabilize your life, to prepare your life for living in a financially unstable world, a practically unstable world. You must strengthen your health, your determination and only associate with people who can support this within you. Serve those who are weaker, yes, but they should not be your companions.

在你能够帮助他人之前，你必须先稳定你的生命，为在一个经济不稳定、实际上也不稳定的世界里生活做好准备。你必须增强你的健康，增强你的决心，并且只与那些能够支持你内心这些的人交往。服务那些弱者，没错，但他们不应该成为你的同伴。

This is great Wisdom that We are presenting for you here today. If you neglect it, or disagree, or avoid it, or resist it, well, you are only closing the door on your greater possibilities in life. For greatness is not simply having big ideas and fanciful notions, it is the product of meeting real events, providing great service under duress. This is what forges great men and women in all fields of endeavor.

这就是我们今天在此呈现给你的伟大智慧。如果你忽视它，或不认同它，或回避它，或抵制它，那么，你只是在关闭你生命中更伟大可能性的大门。因为伟大并非仅仅拥有宏大的想法和天马行空的想法，而是应对真实事件、在压力下提供伟大服务的产物。正是这，在各行各业中锻造出伟大的男人和女人。

If you want to be safe and protected, you will hide out somewhere, and the world will become darker. And you will become ever more terrified of it with very little relief.

如果你想要安全和保护，你就会躲在某个地方，世界就会变得更加黑暗。你会越来越害怕，却几乎得不到任何安慰。

You were born with Knowledge within yourself. It is your connection to God. Build your connection to God. But to do that, you need something from God to help you, for you do not know what Knowledge is. You think it is your ideas or your emotions or your feelings. You may think it is your firm and fixed beliefs. But that is not Knowledge that We speak of here today.

你生来就拥有内在的内识。它是你与上帝的连接。构建你与上帝的连接。但要做到这一点，你需要来自上帝的讯息来帮助你，因为你不知道内识是什么。你认为它是你的想法、你的情绪或你的感受。你或许认为它是你坚定不移的信念。但那并非我们今天在此讲述的内识。

You will need to take the Steps to Knowledge, which God has now provided to the world in the face of the great pandemic and the Great Waves of change that are now building on the horizon, some of which you can see and some of which you cannot yet see. They are all building there.

你将需要开展内识进阶，这是上帝如今在面对巨大疫情和正在地平线上构建的变革巨浪时提供给世界的，其中一些你能看见，一些你还看不见。它们都正在构建。

Do not seek to be comfortable and happy in all situations, for you have great work to do, great strengthening to accomplish, and great change to bring about in your thinking, your attitudes and your behavior.

不要追求在所有情况下都感到舒适和快乐，因为你有伟大的工作要做，有伟大的强化要完成，有伟大的改变要在你的思维、态度和行为上带来。

Not everyone will do this, of course, but not everyone will survive the Great Waves of change. Not everyone will engage with Knowledge, but not everyone will have the strength and courage to navigate the difficult times ahead.

当然，并非每个人都能做到这一点，但并非每个人都能在变革巨浪中幸存。并非每个人都能参与内识，但并非每个人都拥有力量和勇气来渡过未来的艰难时期。

You do not remember, but you made an agreement before you came into this world that there were certain things that you would try to accomplish under very difficult circumstances. That is your road ahead. That is what will give you strength, courage, self-respect, and self-acceptance. Anything else will take these things away from you, most assuredly.

你或许已经不记得，但在你来到这个世界之前，你曾约定，有些事，即使身处困境，你也要努力去完成。这就是你未来的路。这将赋予你力量、勇气、自尊和自我接纳。任何其他事，都必定会夺走你这一切。

As you can see all around you people striving after fantasies or pleasures or comforts, and how unhappy and lost and distracted they really are.

正如你所看见的，你周围的人们都在追求幻想、快乐或舒适，而他们实际上是多么的不快乐、迷失和心烦意乱。

God wants you to succeed, but to succeed you have to become courageous and determined. You cannot create that yourself. You need great assistance, which the Source of all life has now provided for you through a New Revelation for the world, for nothing else will really save you.

上帝希望你成功，但要成功，你必须变得勇敢和坚定。你无法独自创造这一切。你需要伟大的协助，而所有生命的源泉如今已通过一个致世界的新启示为你提供了这种协助，因为除此之外，没有任何其他事物能够真正拯救你。

Believing in Jesus will not save you. Trying to purify yourself and have no attachments will not save you, for that is not why you have come into the world. Following the dictates of religion alone will not save you, for they were given in antiquity and cannot really respond to your life now and your life going forward.

信仰耶稣并不能拯救你。试图净化你自己、不执着于任何事物也不能拯救你，因为那并非你来到世界的目的。仅仅遵循宗教的教诲并不能拯救你，因为它们是古代留下的，无法真正回应你现在和未来的生命。

God knows this, of course, but the people are confused. It is through this confusion you must now pass and not become a victim of it or an adherent to it. It is like clouds and fog keeping you from seeing where you must go and what you must do—clouding up your mind, filling up your life, dominating your associations with others perhaps.

上帝当然知道这一点，但人们感到困惑。你现在必须跨越这困惑，而不是成为它的受害者或追随者。它就像云雾，阻碍你看清你必须去往何方，你必须做什么——它笼罩着你的心灵，填满你的生命，或许还主宰着你与他人的交往。

This is the great pathway forward. It does not lead to a pleasant life but a challenging life. It does not lead to endless happiness but great determination, great service and greater rewards. It is not what you want necessarily, but it is what you know because God has put Knowledge within you, and that part of you knows what We speak of here today and will resonate with it completely if you are able to experience this Knowledge within yourself.

这是伟大的前进之路。它并非通向愉悦的人生，而是充满挑战的生命。它并非通向无尽的幸福，而是伟大的决心、伟大的服务和更伟大的回报。它未必是你想要的，但它是你所知道的，因为上帝将内识置于你的内在，而你的那部分知道我们今天在此讲述的内容，并且如果你能够在你自己内在体验这个内识，你就会与它产生完全的共鸣。

Regardless of how difficult and dangerous and uncertain the world may appear, the Blessing is within you. God has already given you the gift. If you will learn to discover it, align yourself with it and follow it, you will see exactly what this means. Before that, you will have to believe. You will have to have faith in it. You will have to allow it to prove itself to you through real circumstances and real challenges.

无论世界看起来多么艰难、危险和不确定，祝福就在你的内在。上帝已经将这份礼物赐予你。如果你学会发现它，与它协调一致，并追随它，你就会看见这究竟意味着什么。在此之前，你必须相信。你必须对它充满信仰。你必须让它通过真实的境遇和真实的挑战向你证明它。

The Blessing goes with you every day, for part of you is still connected to your Source. That is not the part of you that has a nametag and is identified as a member of society here, but the deeper element of you that is permanent and that is in the world for a greater purpose, which must be discovered and undertaken today.

祝福每天都伴随着你，因为你的一部分依然与你的本源相连。它并非你戴着名牌、被认定为社会成员的部分，而是你永恒的、为了更伟大目的而存在于世的更深层次的元素，而这个目的必须在今天被发现和践行。
