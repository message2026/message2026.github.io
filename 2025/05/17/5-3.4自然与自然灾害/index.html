第四章：自然与自然灾害

发表于2025-05-17|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第五卷新世界预言
|总字数:13.1 k|阅读时长:55 分钟

Nature and Natural Disasters

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on September 1, 2008 in Boulder, Colorado
2008 年 9 月 1 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

The world is driven by powerful geologic and biologic forces. They were set in motion at the beginning of the physical universe. God has set them in motion to operate on their own, moving in a certain direction. This has produced immense turbulence, even after the creation of this planet. These turbulent forces work throughout the universe, depending on a world’s particular environment.

世界是由强大的地质和生物力量驱动的。这些力量在物质宇宙诞生之初就已启动。上帝使它们自行运转，朝着特定的方向运动。即使在地球被创造之后，这仍然产生了巨大的湍流。这些湍流力量在整个宇宙中发挥作用，取决于每个世界特定的环境。

Humanity has had to endure violent weather, droughts, floods, pestilence—all manner of things because this is all part of living within a changing environment governed by physical laws and natural laws.

人类必须忍受恶劣的天气、干旱、洪水、瘟疫等各种各样的事情，因为这都是在受物理定律和自然定律支配的不断变化的环境中生活的一部分。

Entirely different this is from your Ancient Home, from which you have come and to which you will return.

这与你来自并将回归的古老家园完全不同。

To be in an environment like this requires constant adaptation and constant problem solving. It is not easy. And while the world is beautiful and magnificent, it is also hazardous and difficult, placing great requirements on all intelligent creatures who live here, on all biological entities, but particularly upon those who are self-aware and who are aware of the future and the past.

在这样的环境中生存需要不断适应，不断解决问题。这并不容易。这个世界虽然美丽壮丽，但也充满危险和艰辛，对所有生活于此的智能生物，所有生物实体，尤其是那些拥有自我意识、洞悉未来和过去的人，都提出了极高的要求。

The fact that your life here is temporary and that your physical vehicle is temporary, as you experience being in the world, this places a further burden on you—a psychological burden now, and a great anxiety about the possibility of future difficulty and even the loss of your life.

事实上，你在此的生命是暂时的，你的物质载体也是暂时的，正如你体验身处世界时那样，这给你带来了进一步的负担——现在是一种心理负担，以及对未来困难甚至失去生命的可能性的一种巨大焦虑。

This is not a punishment from God, but it does represent what life means if you are living apart from God, if you are living in Separation. Life will be changing. Life will be difficult. It will be a life unlike your Ancient Home, unlike your heavenly state, and you will have to face many adversities.

这并非来自上帝的惩罚，但它确实代表了如果你远离上帝生活，如果你生活在分离之中，生命意味着什么。生命将会改变。生命将会艰难。它将是一种不同于你的古老家园、不同于你的天堂状态的生命，你将不得不面对许多逆境。

Now humanity is facing Great Waves of change—changing climate, violent weather, diminishing resources, environmental degradation, mounting economic and political instability and a greater risk of conflict and war over the remaining resources.

现在，人类正面临着巨大的变革浪潮——气候变化、恶劣的天气、资源减少、环境恶化、经济和政治不稳定加剧以及因剩余资源而引发冲突和战争的风险增大。

You have come into the world to live in the face of these Great Waves of change, and these Great Waves of change are already occurring in the world today. Perhaps you have not faced them yet. Perhaps you are yet unaware of the magnitude of the Great Waves of change. This is now having a great impact, altering the balance of the natural environment, setting in train forces of change in your climate, creating greater disasters—hurricanes, tornadoes, floods and droughts, extreme and violent weather conditions in many parts of the world.

你来到这个世界，是为了生活在这些变革的巨浪面前，而这些变革的巨浪如今已然在世界上发生。或许你尚未面对它们。或许你尚未察觉到变革巨浪的规模。这正在产生巨大的影响，改变着自然环境的平衡，引发着你们气候的改变力量，制造着更严重的灾难——飓风、龙卷风、洪水和干旱，以及世界许多地方的极端和猛烈的天气状况。

Humanity’s overuse and abuse of the world is now changing the natural balance, and this change is not in your favor. Even at this moment, nations and peoples do not seem to comprehend the magnitude of what is happening. People do not want to give up their privileges. Networks of trade and the exploitation of resources are now well established. People do not want to restrain themselves. In certain wealthy nations, limits will be placed, but in other nations, there are no such limits and no such restraints.

人类对世界的过度使用和滥用正在改变自然平衡，而这种改变对你们不利。即使在此刻，各个国家和民族似乎仍未理解正在发生的事情的严重性。人们不愿放弃他们的特权。贸易网络和资源剥削如今已根深蒂固。人们不愿自我约束。在某些富裕国家，人们会受到限制，但在其他国家，既没有这样的限制，也没有这样的约束。

This means that humanity will further impact the natural environment, and the natural environment will further impact humanity. In the end, of course, nature will be victorious. But this creates a dilemma, you see, a fundamental dilemma because greater than your national laws, greater than your social customs, greater than your religious beliefs, greater than your individual or national attitudes or perspective is your fundamental relationship with nature—your relationship with your environment.

这意味着人类将进一步影响自然环境，而自然环境也将进一步影响人类。最终，自然当然会取得胜利。但这也造成了一个困境，一个根本性的困境，因为比你们的国家法律、社会习俗、宗教信仰、个人或国家态度或观点更重要的，是你们与自然的根本关系——你们与环境的关系。

Humanity as a whole has lost touch with this, has lost the recognition of the power and the importance of your relationship with your environment. You feel that with technology you can overcome the forces of nature, and to a certain degree, you can harness these forces, but you cannot overcome them. Throughout the universe, powerful nations have tried but have only found themselves to be at the mercy of the greater powers and forces of nature.

人类作为一个整体已经与此失去联系，失去了对你们与环境关系的力量及其重要性的认知。你们觉得凭借科技你们可以克服自然的力量，并且在某种程度上你们可以驾驭这些力量，但你们无法战胜它们。在整个宇宙中，强大的国家都曾尝试过，但最终却发现他们自己只能任由更强大的自然力量摆布。

At this moment, humanity, like an adolescent, is giddy with its own power, thinking that it has the ability to transcend the natural world, to create an environment not subject to the forces of nature. But this is not true. You are experiencing, even at this moment, the powerful response, the powerful reaction of natural law to human abuse and human irresponsibility, and the results are tragic.

此时此刻，人类如同青少年，陶醉于它自身的力量，认为他能够超越自然界，创造一个不受自然力量支配的环境。但这并非事实。即使在此时此刻，你们也正在经历着自然法则对人类滥用和不负责任行为的强烈反应，其后果是悲剧性的。

Your planet is getting warmer. Hotter. Drier. It is driving change. The world will change to a new kind of balance, but it will not be a balance that will be favorable to the human family. And the only way to alter this and to prevent this outcome would be a radical change in human behavior and activity and governance, which are unlikely to happen any time soon.

你们的星球正在变暖。更热。更干燥。它正在推动变革。世界将转向一种新的平衡，但这并非一种有利于人类大家庭的平衡。改变这一现状并阻止这一结果的唯一途径，是彻底改变人类的行为、活动和治理，而这不太可能在短期内发生。

Even in your separated state, you have to submit to the forces that govern your life. You can control them up to a point. You can mitigate the hazards. You can develop the opportunities. Perhaps even for a brief period, you can create great affluence for a large portion of the human family. But in the end nature will be dominant.

即使在你分离的状态下，你也必须顺服那些掌控你生命的力量。你可以在一定程度上控制它们。你可以减轻危险。你可以开发机遇。或许，哪怕只是短暂的一段时间，你也能为人类家庭的大部分成员创造巨大的富足。但最终，自然将占据主导地位。

If humanity exhausts the resources of this world, it will fall under the persuasion of foreign powers in the universe, who will seek to exploit a weak and divided humanity. They will seek to gain control of the world’s natural beauty and resources, a world rich with biological diversity, a world highly valued by others.

如果人类耗尽了这个世界的资源，就会落入宇宙外来势力的圈套，这些势力会试图剥削弱小而分裂的人类。他们会试图控制这个世界的自然美景和资源，一个生物多样性丰富的世界，一个受其他种族高度重视的世界。

They will not come now because they will not use force. Force is not the strength they will use. It will be the force of persuasion. And the more destitute humanity becomes as a result of its own behavior, the more powerful this persuasion will be and the less humanity will be able to resist the temptation of the offerings of those who come under the pretension of being here for peaceful purposes, but who in fact are here to take advantage of you and your situation.

他们现在不会来，因为他们不会使用武力。武力并非他们将使用的力量。它将是说服的力量。人类因其自身行为而变得越贫困，这种说服力就越强大，人类就越无法抵挡那些假装为和平目的而来、但实际上却在此利用你们和你们处境之人的供奉的诱惑。

These forces will be small, but clever. They will see that you have robbed yourself, and now you are poor and you will not be in a position to resist the temptation of what they have to offer—stability, security, resources—things that you have given away, things that have been sacrificed here on Earth.

这些势力规模虽小，却非常狡猾。他们会发现你们掠夺了自己，如今你们一贫如洗，无法抵挡他们提供的诱惑——稳定、安全、资源——这些都是你们已经放弃的东西，是你们在地球上牺牲的东西。

These forces will not come in great masses or great numbers. It will only take small groups of individuals to gain pre-eminence in this world, under the conditions of human degradation.

这些势力不会以巨大的规模或数量出现。在人类堕落的条件下，只需要一小群个体就能在这个世界上取得主导地位。

This does not have to be your fate. This does not have to be your destiny. This is not meant to be. It is a result of the decisions—countless decisions made previously, decisions being made today and the decisions being made tomorrow.

这不一定是你们的命运。这不一定是你们的宿命。这并非注定。它是你们做出的决定的结果——无数你们之前做出的决定，今天正在做出的决定，以及明天正在做出的决定。

Already, forces of change have been unleashed on the world, which will alter, in some cases radically, the circumstances of large parts of the human family. Even if you were to alter course, even if you were to restrain yourself and your activities, you have already set in motion forces you cannot change.

变革的力量已然在世界上释放，这将改变人类大家庭中许多成员的境况，在某些情况下甚至是彻底的改变。即使你们改变方向，即使你们克制自己和你们的行为，你们也已经启动了你们无法改变的力量。

You may call these forces “acts of God.” You may think that God is bringing violent weather and drought conditions as a kind of punishment, but it is not true. These represent your impact upon the environment.

你可能会把这些力量称为“天灾”。你或许认为上帝带来恶劣天气和干旱是一种惩罚，但事实并非如此。这些代表了你们对环境的影响。

If you empty the well, the well is dry. If you do not farm correctly, the soil blows away. If you burn too many things into the environment, the weather changes. If you overexploit the world for resources, the resources disappear.

如果你们把井水抽干，井水就会干涸。如果你们耕作不当，土壤就会被风吹走。如果你们在环境中燃烧过多的物质，天气就会发生变化。如果你们过度开发世界资源，资源就会消失。

It does not take a genius to figure this out. It takes common sense, but common sense is not common, it seems.

解决这个问题并不需要天才，只需要常识，但常识似乎并不常见。

So here you are facing the consequences of humanity’s destructive impact upon the world. And these impacts will continue. God is not creating them though God has created the mechanisms of life and set in motion the geological forces that have shaped the physical universe, and continue to shape it in an evolutionary pattern. You can blame God for the fact that there are hurricanes. “Why did you [God] create hurricanes?” you may ask, but God only created the conditions under which hurricanes emerge. That was before there was any life in this world. But God did not create this [specific] hurricane to punish humanity. This is foolishness. This is ignorant.

所以，你们现在正面临着人类对世界造成破坏性影响的后果。这些影响将会持续下去。上帝并没有创造这些影响，尽管上帝创造了生命的机制，并启动了塑造物质宇宙的地质力量，这些力量以进化的模式继续塑造着它。你可以将飓风的存在归咎于上帝。你可能会问：“你（上帝）为什么创造飓风？”但上帝只是创造了飓风产生的条件。那是在世界上出现任何生命之前。但上帝创造这场（特定的）飓风并非为了惩罚人类。这是愚蠢的。这是无知的。

If humanity continues in its present course, it will generate more events of this kind. Large parts of the world will be heavily damaged, and great droughts will fall upon the arid regions, driving people out. Nations will collapse. There will be millions of refugees with nowhere to go.

如果人类继续按照目前的方式发展，将会引发更多类似的事件。世界大片地区将遭受严重破坏，干旱地区将遭遇严重旱灾，迫使人们逃离家园。国家将崩溃。数百万难民将无处可去。

Humanity will lose its most valuable resources through overexploitation, through ignorant use, through a heedlessness of the future. Not thinking ahead, humanity is going to take everything now, robbing your children of their future, creating a future of unimaginable difficulties. There is no punishment here. It is merely consequence.

人类将因过度开发、无知的利用和对未来的漠视而失去其最宝贵的资源。人类不考虑未来，现在就将夺走一切，剥夺子孙后代的未来，创造一个充满难以想象的困境的未来。这里没有惩罚，它仅仅只是后果。

It is your responsibility to learn of the natural world and to use it effectively in a manner that can be sustained. This has been learned throughout the ages. It is not a new learning. But humanity has forfeited this knowledge out of greed, out of corruption, out of ambition. And now the world has a great many people in it. The boat is full.

你们有责任了解自然界，并以可持续的方式有效使用它。这早已是古往今来人们的共同经验，并非新知。然而，人类却因为贪婪、腐败和野心而丧失了这些知识。如今，这个世界人口众多，船已满。

How will you manage such circumstances? How will you face the Great Waves of change? How can you bring and restore greater balance in the natural world for your benefit? The answer is beyond your intellect. Though you may have very good ideas, and there are many solutions that are being proposed that will be very helpful overall if brought together and if utilized fully, but really the answer to these questions has to go deeper than the intellect, deeper into you.

你将如何应对这样的境况？你将如何面对变革的巨浪？你如何才能为你们的利益带来并重建自然界的更大平衡？答案超越了你的智力。尽管你可能拥有非常好的想法，也有很多正在提出的解决方案，如果整合起来并得到充分利用，总体上将非常有益，但真正这些问题的答案必须比智力更深入，更深入你的内心。

It is not a problem for other people to solve for you. It is a problem for everyone. It is not merely a problem for governments, or for science, or for politicians. It is a problem for everyone.

它不是一个别人能帮你解决的问题。它是每个人的问题。它不仅仅是政府、科学或政客的问题。它是每个人的问题。

This represents your relationship with nature. If you violate the relationship, you will have a bad relationship. It will be unhealthy. It will be unstable. There will be friction. There will be difficulty.

这代表着你与自然的关系。如果你破坏了这种关系，你的关系就会变得糟糕。它会不健康，不稳定，会产生摩擦，带来困难。

Just like you having a relationship with another person, if you abuse that person, if you do not respect that person, they will behave in a manner and will respond in a manner that will be unpleasant and discordant. There will be difficulties between you. You will not function in harmony together.

就像你和另一个人的关系一样，如果你辱骂对方，如果你不尊重对方，他们的行为举止和回应方式都会令人不快，不和谐。你们之间就会出现问题，无法和谐相处。

It is like this with your relationship with nature. If you show no respect for nature, if you do not respect how it functions and how you must behave within this relationship, then the relationship becomes discordant—producing unwanted results, unpleasant experiences.

你与自然的关系也是如此。如果你不尊重自然，如果你不尊重它的运作方式以及你在这种关系中必须如何表现，那么这种关系就会变得不和谐——产生不想要的结果，产生不愉快的体验。

If you do not take care of your home, it ceases to be a desirable place to live. If your house is falling apart, if you cannot or will not keep it up, it becomes an undesirable place to live. The world represents the home of humanity. If it is not supported and sustained and managed properly, as far as you can manage it, it ceases to become a desirable place to live.

如果你不好好照顾你的家，它就不再是一个理想的居住之所。如果你的房子摇摇欲坠，如果你无力或不愿维护它，它就不再是一个理想的居住之所。世界是人类的家园。如果它得不到支持、维持和管理，即使你力所能及，它也不再是一个理想的居住之所。

You cannot change the laws of nature. You can limit the impact of nature’s difficulties. You can enhance and develop its opportunities and its benefits. You can utilize natural forces to your benefit. That is what humanity has been doing all along, but it is a balance. And humanity has lost the balance.

你无法改变自然法则。你可以限制自然灾害的影响。你可以增强和发展它的机遇和益处。你们可以善用自然力量为自己谋取益处。这正是人类一直以来所做的，但这是一种平衡。而人类已经失去了这种平衡。

Gaining technology has given you a sense of pre-eminence, and you have taken it too far. If humanity were intelligent, you would be rationing fuel today and not waiting for when rationing will be forced upon you. You would be preserving your finest energy and resources instead of using them up as quickly as possible—believing, assuming, that there will be something there to replace them, without any difficulty in transition.

科技的进步让你们产生了一种优越感，你们已经走得太远了。如果人类真的聪明，你们现在就会实行燃料限量，而不是等到什么时候被强制限量。你们会保存你们最宝贵的能源和资源，而不是尽快耗尽它们——相信、假设会有某种东西来取代它们，而且过渡过程不会有任何困难。

But humanity has created a style of living that far exceeds the carrying capacity of your environment and that places you out of balance with the natural world, and even places you out of harmony with your own nature. Now you are driven to excess—excessive behavior, excessive busyness, excessive use of the world’s resources, excessive indulgences.

但人类创造了一种生活方式，远远超出了你们环境的承载能力，使你们与自然界失去平衡，甚至与你们自身的自然失去和谐。现在，你们被驱使走向了过度——行为过度，过度忙碌，过度消耗世界资源，过度放纵。

Your relationship with time has changed. Your relationship with nature has changed. And your relationship with one another has changed.

你与时间的关系改变了。你与自然的关系改变了。你与他人的关系改变了。

Now you are busy trying to sustain a lifestyle that deprives most of the human family—where the very rich are driving the engine of war, robbing nations and peoples of their own sustaining resources, driving headlong as if you did not have a future, as if you did not care, under a whole array of assumptions that somehow your technology and your cleverness and ingenuity will find a way to keep it all going.

现在，你们正忙于维持一种剥夺大多数人类家庭权利的生活方式——富人驾驶着战争的引擎，掠夺着各个国家和人民赖以生存的资源，一头扎进战争之中，仿佛你们没有未来，仿佛你们毫不关心，你们心中有着一系列的假设，认为你们的技术、你们的聪明才智和独创性将找到一种方法来让这一切继续下去。

You have abandoned your relationship with nature. You have taken on a mistress of technology, forcing a kind of adultery that has set you out of accord with yourself, with one another and with your environment.

你们抛弃了与自然的关系，接受了科技的诱惑，强迫你们自己与自然通奸，这导致你们与自己、与他人、与环境格格不入。

God will not intervene to stop all of this. God will only encourage you to behave correctly, for God has placed a deeper Knowledge within each person through which the Will and the Power of God can flow. But people have lost their native sense. They have forfeited their relationship with this deeper Knowledge to have, and to be and to do what they want—driven by passion and fantasy, driven by a new set of needs now that far exceeds your basic needs of living in the world.

上帝不会干预来阻止这一切。上帝只会鼓励你正确地行事，因为上帝已经将一个更深层次的内识置于每个人内在，上帝的意志和力量由此得以流淌。但人们已丧失了他们原生的感知。他们已经丧失了与这个更深层次内识的关联，去拥有、去成为、去做他们想要的一切——被激情和幻想所驱动，被如今远超你们在世间生存基本需求的一系列新需求所驱动。

So God speaks, but you cannot hear. You cannot feel, except perhaps superficially or periodically, the restraints that God is trying to place upon your actions and the motivation that God is giving you for other kinds of action. It is as if you are out of control and cannot stop yourself, like a person who is eating but cannot stop eating even though they have far exceeded already the needs of the body. Now they keep eating to satiate some kind of emotional insecurity, some kind of sense of deficiency and loss within themselves.

因此，上帝讲话，你却听不见。你无法感受到，或许只是表面上或偶尔地感受到，上帝试图对你的行为施加的限制，以及上帝赋予你其他行动的动力。你仿佛失去了控制，无法控制你自己，就像一个人在吃东西，却停不下来，即使他已经远远超出了身体的需求。现在，他们继续吃，是为了满足内心某种情感上的不安全感，某种匮乏感和失落感。

除了比成瘾更强大的力量，还有什么能让人们摆脱成瘾？还有什么能阻止人类对世界的亵渎？还有什么能阻止人类过度消耗其自然遗产？还有什么能阻止战争？还有什么能阻止人类的残忍？还有什么能减轻并最终阻止所有危及你们未来、并已将自然世界置于你们对立面的行为？

上帝已将一个更深层次的内识置于你的内在。它是你的完整性、你的智慧以及你洞察真理和回应真理的能力的源泉。它是你的勇气的源泉。它是你力量的源泉。

这不仅仅是政府或政策的问题。它关乎你如何看待世界，如何回应世界。它关乎你是否受到内识的指引，还是受到所有其他内识替代品的指引。

那么，你与自然的关系并非仅仅是欣赏它的美丽和复杂性。它真正关乎你如何在自然中生活，如何与自然共事。如果你与自然的关系不仅仅是浪漫——不仅仅是对自然的浪漫情怀，不仅仅是沉迷于它的美——你就必须思考如何与自然共处，如何在自然中生活，如何应对它的逆境，如何在共事中享受它的壮丽。你又将如何与他人相处，如何应对与他人的差异？

上帝已经将一个答案置于你的内在。它并非仅仅是一个想法的答案。它是一种智能。人类的内识越强大，它就越能克制它自己，避免其破坏性行为，就越能培育和创造对其有益的行为、科技和社会正义体系。

这是一件非常伟大的事情。这才是文明进化的真正意义所在。它就像人生的各个阶段。年轻的时候，你想要成长和发展。你想要拥有更多的东西。你想要新的体验。你不断地成长、发展、发展。但如果你想在一个成熟的环境中发挥作用，成为一个成熟的个体，你就必须创造稳定性。如果你想要一个家庭，你必须拥有稳定和安全感。这并不是说要一直拥有新的东西，或者放纵自己、自由自在。现在你必须履行你的基本责任。你寻求稳定和安全感。这是自然而然的。

它并不意味着你不再对生命充满热情、不再享受生命、不再愿意接受改变。它仅仅意味着你必须承担起你的责任，去生活，去维护你所创造的一切。

最终，即使面对一个极其堕落的世界，人类文明也必须接受这一点。然而，青少年行为仍然渗透到人类文化、经济体系、政治议程以及方方面面。你们渴望更多，却与时俱进。你们与自然，以及彼此之间，都缺乏稳定的关系。

It is all about personal fulfillment, personal acquisition, personal wealth, personal happiness—all creating great instability. The real wealth, the real happiness, will be attained by other means.

一切都关乎个人成就、个人收获、个人财富、个人幸福——所有这些都造成了巨大的不稳定。真正的财富，真正的幸福，将通过其他途径获得。

Even facing the Great Waves of change, Knowledge will lead you and guide you and help you navigate the difficult times ahead.

上帝将内识置于你的内在，来指引你、保护你，并教导你如何与你的环境以及彼此和谐相处。它面向真理，面向真实、实际和真诚，面向诚实和同情心。正是你与你更深层次自然的关系，将使你能够重建与自然世界的一种真正运转的关系——去重建一种可持续的生命，一种平衡的生命。即使面对变革的巨浪，内识也将引领你、指引你，并帮助你渡过前方的艰难时期。

There will have to be much human innovation in the future, but if it is guided by Knowledge, it will be beneficial. 未来人类必将迎来许多创新，但如果它受到内识的指引，它将是有益的。你必须改变你的人生境遇，以适应一个资源日益减少的世界，但内识将赋予你力量去做到这一点，你也将因此收获回报。

它是你更深层次的自然。忽视这一点，你将自食其果。逃避这一点，你将面临灾难。你将不知道你自己，你将在世上缺乏智慧。正因如此，回归内识是你所有有益活动的基础。

必须做千万件事来恢复你们与自然的平衡，并防止人类大家庭在未来遭受的灾难，但所有这一切的基础必须是内识，你更深层次的自然。

They think it is inadequate. “Hah, you’re just going to rely upon some kind of intuition?” They think Knowledge is just intuition, a weak and fleeting experience. They do not take it seriously. They do not recognize its power, its immense strength.

当然，会有很多反对的声音：“是的，但是这样”，“是的，但是那样”。人们会抱怨。他们认为这不够充分。“哈，你只是要依赖某种直觉吗？”他们认为内识只是直觉，一种虚弱而短暂的体验。他们不把它当回事。他们没有认识到它的力量，它极大的实力。

People still think it is all about technology and innovation, but they cannot agree. They argue with each other over which political system is better, which social system is better. The arguments go on and on and permeate all of human culture—the disagreements, the conflicts. People fight with each other over innumerable things—with wars of words, wars of action and, in extreme cases, war itself.

人们仍然认为一切都关乎科技和创新，但他们却无法达成共识。他们争论哪种政治制度更好，哪种社会制度更好。这些争论永无休止，渗透到整个人类文化——分歧、冲突。人们为了无数的事情互相争斗——口水战、行动战，在极端情况下，甚至爆发战争。

But within you is a great power that is not in conflict with itself. You cannot be at war with another at the level of Knowledge. You will recognize your mutual needs and will try to satisfy them or address them together. And even your different opinions about what may or may not work will be counteracted by the presence of Knowledge.

但你内在存在着一股伟大的力量，它并不与自身冲突。在内识层面，你无法与他人交战。你将认知你们共同的需求，并努力去满足它们，或共同应对它们。甚至你们对于什么可行或不可行持有的不同观点，也会被内识的存在所抵消。

You will commit yourself to solutions even if those solutions prove to be difficult to recognize and to achieve because you will be guided by Knowledge. You will restrain your behavior. Nations will restrain their behavior, if guided by Knowledge, if guided by people who are strong with Knowledge.

你将致力于解决方案，即使这些解决方案被证明难以认知和实现，因为你将受到内识的指引。你将约束你的行为。如果受到内识的指引，如果受到拥有强大内识的人的指引，国家也将约束他们的行为。

Natural disasters will occur. You will know how to prepare for them. But what must stop, what must be arrested is humanity’s abuse of its natural inheritance. What does this look like? you might ask.

自然灾害将会发生。你们会知道如何应对。但必须制止、必须阻止的是人类对自然遗产的滥用。你可能会问，这会是什么样子？

Half the world’s forests should be preserved. Fuel should be rationed. It should have been rationed twenty years ago. You will have to control human population growth and human consumption if you are ever able to attain stability and security. You cannot devour the Earth without losing the ability to live here. And you cannot migrate to other planets because they are owned by others. Beyond this solar system, it is private property. You must put your house in order, and God has given you the power, the strength and the wisdom to do this.

世界上一半的森林应该得到保护。燃料应该实行定量配给。这早在二十年前就应该实行了。如果你们能够实现稳定与安全，就必须控制人口的增长和人类的消费。你们不能去吞噬地球，否则就会失去在这里生存的能力。你们也无法移民到其他星球，因为那些星球是其他种族的。太阳系之外，一切都是私有财产。你们必须安顿好自己的家园，上帝已经赋予你们力量、实力和智慧来做到这一点。

You have to begin with taking the Steps to Knowledge and with re-evaluating your life. Before you commit yourself to other actions, you must do this, or you will not have a foundation, and you will not be able to experience the source of your wisdom, your power and your strength. You will launch yourself on some kind of campaign of reform, but you will not really have the power and the strength and the wisdom that you will need to be successful.

你必须从开展内识进阶并重新评估你的生命开始。在你承诺其他行动之前，你必须这样做，否则你将没有一个基础，你将无法体验你的智慧、你的力量和你实力的源泉。你将发起某种改革运动，但你将不会真正拥有你成功所需的力量、实力和智慧。

For that, you must come to Knowledge within yourself. That is where you must begin. You begin with what is essential and fundamental. You learn to be still so you can experience Knowledge and feel its presence and hear its guidance, so you can feel its restraint and its motivations. You step out of the world of overstimulation into a deeper, more quiet world within yourself. And you will find that there is a greater strength within you waiting to be discovered.

为此，你必须走向你内在的内识。那是你必须开始的地方。你从根本和根基之处开始。你学习静心，这样你就能体验内识，感受它的存在，聆听它的指引，这样你就能感受到它的克制和它的动机。你走出过度刺激的世界，进入你内在一个更深层次、更宁静的世界。你将会发现，你内在有一种更伟大的力量正等待着被发现。
