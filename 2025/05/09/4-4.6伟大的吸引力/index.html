第六章：伟大的吸引力

发表于2025-05-09|更新于2025-08-22|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷灵魂在地球上的旅程
|总字数:6.4 k|阅读时长:26 分钟

The Great Attraction

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on May 4, 2011 in Boulder, Colorado
2011 年 5 月 4 日，在科罗拉多州博尔德(Boulder, Colorado)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

Living in Separation, you forget, and have forgotten, that you are connected to a Greater Source—a Greater Source that defies definition and description and whose motives and movements in the universe are beyond any race’s comprehension. You are connected to this Source, and there is a deeper part of you that is still connected to this Source, for it has not really entered Separation with you.

生活在分离之中，你忘记了，并且已经忘记了，你与一个更伟大的本源相连——一个无法定义和描述的更伟大的本源，它在宇宙中的动机和运作超越任何种族的理解。你与这个本源相连，而且你内心深处还有一部分仍然与这个本源相连，因为它尚未真正与你一起进入分离。

The Creator of all life in the universe—and in the universes, and in Creation beyond the universes—moves you through this deeper part of you called Knowledge. To engage with Knowledge is to engage with this essential part of your nature—your deeper nature, your permanent nature, your true nature.

宇宙中所有生命的创造者——以及宇宙中所有生命，以及宇宙之外的创造——引导你穿越你被称为内识的这个更深层次的部分。与内识互动，就是与你自然的这个本质部分互动——你更深层次的自然，你永恒的自然，你真正的自然。

Yet people live at the surface of their minds—caught up in the world, caught up in their own thinking, their fears, their desires, their grievances, their obsessions, their distractions—unaware that this greater and deeper part of them is still connected to their Source.

然而人们生活在他们心灵的表面——陷入世界之中，陷在他们自身的思维、他们的恐惧、他们的渴望、他们的不满、他们的痴迷、他们的纷扰中——没有察觉到他们这个更伟大、更深层次的部分依然联接着他们的源头。

People believe in God and believe in the teachings about God. They may even fervently believe to the point where they will become violent with others or give up their life willingly for a principle or an idea. But this is not where the connection is really being made. This is not what it means to be in relationship with the Divine.

人们信仰上帝，也信仰关于上帝的教义。他们甚至可能狂热地信仰到会对他人施暴，或为了某个原则或理念心甘情愿地放弃生命的地步。但这并不是真正建立联系的地方。这并非与神圣建立关系的意义所在。

People are in relationship with their ideas about the Divine, or their beliefs or their customs, or the beliefs of their country or culture. But We are talking about something deeper and more profound—the river that runs beneath the desert, the golden thread that is woven into the coarse fabric of life, the Ancient Corridors of your Mind—beyond the influence of the world, beyond all that you have accumulated so far in this life. It was there before you came into the world. It will be there after you leave. It is connected by the Great Attraction.

人们与他们关于神圣的想法，或他们的信仰或习俗，或他们国家或文化的信仰息息相关。但我们谈论的是一种更深层次、更深刻的事情——沙漠之下流淌的河流，编织进生命粗糙肌理的金线，你心灵的古老回廊——超越世界的影响，超越你这个生命迄今为止所积累的一切。它在你来到世界之前就在那里。在你离开之后，它仍将在那里。它通过伟大的吸引力连接着。

People want many things. They are attracted by many things and repelled by many things. But being repelled is just another form of attraction, you see, for that which repels you engages you, and you enter into relationship with it.

人们渴望许多事物。他们被许多事物所吸引，也被许多事物所排斥。但你看，被排斥只是吸引力的另一种形式，因为那些令你厌恶的事物会吸引你，让你与之建立联系。

People are attracted by power, by beauty, by wealth, and by the idea of spiritual fulfillment—great forces of attraction in the world, so great they overtake the minds of so many and alter the course of human history and lead people to live desperate lives, unfulfilling lives. Shallow, superficial are their emphasis, haunted by the fear of loss, the fear of aging and the fear of death—driven like slaves. Great attractions of the world, the attractions which are both beautiful and ugly, desirable and fearful.

人们被权力、美貌、财富以及精神满足的理念所吸引——这些是世间强大的吸引力，强大到足以掌控无数人的心灵，改变人类历史的进程，引领人们过上绝望、不满足的生命。他们所强调的只是肤浅、困扰，被对失去、对衰老和对死亡的恐惧所困扰——如同奴隶般被驱使。世间最大的诱惑，既美丽又丑陋，既令人向往又令人恐惧。

For even the dark forces of the world hold attraction. People are drawn to them, not only by the allurement of the things the world values, but also by the allurement of the darkness itself. Even those who have fought the darkness or rallied against it will still be drawn in by its powers.

因为即使是世间的黑暗力量也拥有吸引力。人们被它们吸引，不仅是因为世间珍视的事物，也因为黑暗本身的诱惑。即使是那些曾经与黑暗抗争或团结起来反抗黑暗的人，也会被它的力量所吸引。

Evil has its own attraction, and many fall prey to this, even unknowingly. They behave in a civil manner because society demands it, but beneath, they are full of grievance and the desire for revenge—the attractions of darkness.

邪恶自有其吸引力，许多人不知不觉地就沦为这种吸引力的牺牲品。他们表面上彬彬有礼，因为社会要求如此，但内心深处却充满怨恨和复仇的欲望——这正是黑暗的魅力所在。

Yet God reclaims the separated through the power of Knowledge, the deeper Intelligence within you, the part of you that has not been separate from God. God does not lure you with promises of beauty, wealth and power or even spiritual fulfillment. For those are allurements for the surface mind. The deeper part of you does not need these things to be engaged, to experience the engagement.

然而，上帝通过内识的力量，通过你内在更深层次的智能，通过你未曾与上帝分离的这部分，来唤回分离的部分。上帝不会用美貌、财富、权力，甚至精神的满足承诺来引诱你。因为那些只是针对表面心灵的诱惑。你更深层次的部分不需要这些事物来参与，来体验这种参与。

God is the Great Attraction, greater and more powerful than anything in the world—greater than love, greater than fear, greater than fantasy, greater than personal fulfillment. Pervasive, natural, deep, and continuous, the Great Attraction draws upon you. It does not seduce you. It does not promise things for you. Even all the things that religion promises are but a shallow invitation compared to the Great Attraction.

上帝是伟大的吸引力，比世间任何事物都更伟大、更强大——比爱更伟大，比恐惧更伟大，比幻想更伟大，比个人成就更伟大。伟大的吸引力无处不在，自然而然，深邃而持续，它吸引着你。它不会引诱你。它不会为你承诺什么。即使是宗教所承诺的一切，与伟大的吸引力相比，也不过是肤浅的邀请。

You may not be religious. You may have issues regarding religion. You may dispute religion. You may want to have nothing to do with religion. But you are religious, and whatever you value is where you place your faith and your emphasis.

你或许不信教。你或许对宗教有所顾虑。你或许对宗教有所质疑。你或许不想与宗教有任何瓜葛。但你信教，你所珍视的一切，都是你信仰和关注的重点。

You cannot abandon the deeper part of you. Though it may be unknown to you and out of your range of experience, it is still the center of your life and Being. It is still created by the Divine and it is still responding to the Great Attraction.

你无法抛弃你内心深处的那一部分。尽管它可能对你来说未知，也超出你的经验范围，但它依然是你生命和存在的中心。它依然由神圣所创造，依然在回应着那伟大的吸引力。

It is not about belief, for belief is for the worldly mind. Belief is important in either guiding you towards or away from the Great Attraction, but the Great Attraction itself is a thousand times more powerful.

它与信仰无关，因为信仰属于世俗的心灵。信仰在引导你走向或远离伟大的吸引力方面很重要，但伟大的吸引力本身的力量要强大千倍。

God provides great Teachings at critical turning points for the human family—great Messages, delivered by Messengers. Very infrequently this occurs, to guide and orient the human family towards its present demands and its future realities, great Revelations providing the key ingredients to prepare humanity for its next great stage of development and to protect humanity from dangers that it is not even aware of.

上帝在人类大家庭的关键转折点上提供伟大的教导——由信使传递的伟大讯息。这种情况很少发生，是为了指引和引导人类大家庭面向其当前的需求和其未来的现实，伟大的启示提供了关键要素，让人类为其下一个伟大发展阶段进行准备，并保护人类免遭其甚至尚未察觉到的危险。

But the Great Attraction is continuous. You cannot understand it. You cannot conceptualize it. You may give it symbols. You may associate it with wonderful things. You may build edifices to it; create great paintings and works of art; compose music, dance and ritual. The Great Attraction is still more pervasive and mysterious than your mind can comprehend because it is happening at a whole different level.

但伟大的吸引力是持续不断的。你无法理解它。你无法将其概念化。你可以赋予它象征。你可以将它与奇妙的事物联系起来。你可以为它建造大厦；创作伟大的绘画和艺术作品；谱写音乐、舞蹈和仪式。伟大的吸引力仍然比你的心灵所能理解的更加普遍和神秘，因为它发生在一个完全不同的层面上。

Your intellect is a brilliant mechanism, but it only functions within a narrow band of experience and awareness. The rest of life is happening at another level, beyond physical observation. You are only seeing a small part of what is occurring around you. You are only hearing a small part of the communication that is going around you. You can only touch a small part of the reality that you are immersed in, even at this moment.

你的智力是一台精妙的机器，但它只能在狭小的经验和觉知范围内运作。生命的其余部分发生在另一个层面，超越了物理观察。你只能看到周围发生的一切的一小部分。你只能听到周围交流的一小部分。即使在此刻，你也只能触及你所沉浸其中的现实的一小部分。

If your vision can become refined, you will see more. If your listening can become refined through the Great Practices that the New Message is revealing, your listening will become refined, and you will hear more. If your body becomes a more sensitive instrument instead of a blunt object, you will be able to sense and feel the presence of greater things.

如果你的视野能够变得更加精炼，你将看到更多。如果你的聆听能够通过新讯息所揭示的伟大实践变得更加精炼，你的聆听也将变得更加精炼，你将听到更多。如果你的身体成为一个更灵敏的工具而非一个钝器，你将能够感知和感受更伟大内识的存在。

The Great Attraction is like the sun that shines, whether it is night or day. No matter who you are and where you are and what you are thinking or what is preoccupying you at the moment, the sun is shining. That is why people have deified the sun. But the sun is a temporary thing. Though it seems permanent to you, it is a temporary thing in the great scheme. So even it cannot compare to the Great Attraction.

伟大的吸引力就像太阳，无论白天还是黑夜，都闪耀着光芒。无论你是谁，无论你身在何处，无论你此刻在想什么，无论你正在思考什么，太阳都在闪耀。正因如此，人们才将太阳神化。但太阳终究是短暂的。虽然它对你来说似乎是永恒的，但在伟大的体系中，它终究是短暂的。所以，即便如此，它也无法与伟大的吸引力相比。

You can resist it. You can argue against it. You can make a rational argument against it. You may construct your own reality or try to do so, but you cannot undo the Great Attraction. You cannot banish the Creator. You cannot banish that part of you that is permanent, that is still connected to God and Creation.

你可以抗拒它。你可以反驳它。你可以提出理性的论证来反驳它。你可以构建你自身的现实，或者尝试这样做，但你无法消除伟大的吸引力。你无法驱逐创造主。你无法驱逐你身上那永恒的、仍然与上帝和创建相连的部分。

It is pulling on you even at this moment. If you can settle down and become still and observant, you will begin to feel its Presence because it is always there.

即使此刻，它也在牵引着你。如果你能安顿下来，保持平静，并保持观察，你就会开始感受到它的存在，因为它一直都在。

The Presence is with you, but you are looking out somewhere else. Your mind is captivated by the world, captivated by its own beliefs and desires and anxieties—captivated, obsessive. That is why you see so very little and hear so very little and sense so very little through your physical body because you are so obsessed and captivated.

存在与你同在，但你却望向别处。你的心灵被世界俘获，被它自身的信仰、渴望和焦虑俘获——被俘获，被执迷。正因如此，你透过你的身体，看到的如此之少，听到如此之少，感知到如此之少，因为你如此执迷，如此被俘获。

It is like being in a theater watching a movie. While you are watching, you are captivated. The better the movie, the more captivated you are. You are unaware of yourself, the world—everything. Captivated. Except the tragedy of this is that the movie never seems to stop, and you are missing everything, not available, no one at home.

它就像在电影院看电影。观影过程中，你被深深吸引。电影越精彩，你就越着迷。你察觉不到你自己，察觉不到世界——所有的一切。被深深吸引。然而悲剧的是，电影似乎永无休止，你错过了一切，你无法观看，家里空无一人。

So you do not feel the Attraction. And now you are afraid of the Attraction. You do not want to look inside. You do not want to feel those forces that are trying to move your life in a new direction, trying to rescue you from your poor decisions and unwise obligations. You do not want to have to reconsider your life, your priorities, your commitments and your relationships. You do not even know what the Great Attraction is going to offer you, or what it will require of you, or what it will mean to experience it.

所以你感觉不到那股吸引力。而现在你害怕那股吸引力。你不愿向内探寻。你不愿感受那些试图将你的生命引向新方向、试图将你从糟糕的决定和不明智的义务中解救出来的力量。你不愿重新思考你的生命、你的优先顺序、你的承诺和你的人际关系。你甚至不知道那股伟大的吸引力会给你带来什么，它会要求你做什么，或者体验它意味着什么。

People are afraid. They are trying to make their Separation successful. They are trying to fulfill themselves based upon their assumptions and their beliefs and the values of their nation and culture. They are committed to this pursuit they have already invested in so much. They do not want to have to give it up or even question it. They have invested so much.

人们感到恐惧。他们试图让他们的分离成功。他们试图基于他们的假设、他们的信仰以及他们国家和文化的价值观来成就自我。他们致力于这个他们已投入如此之多的追求。他们不想放弃它，甚至不想质疑它。他们已投入如此之多。

It is only when life begins to really disappoint you, to disillusion you to create a greater sobriety and self-honesty that finally you will begin to ask the greater questions: Why are you here? What is your life really for? What are you really connected to? Are you really living the life you were meant to live?

只有当生命开始真正让你失望，让你幻灭，让你更加清醒、更加诚实时，你才会开始问那些更深刻的问题：你为什么在这里？你的生命究竟是为了什么？你真正与什么相连？你真的过着你本该过的生命吗？

Life serves you through its disappointments, its clarifications and its revelations to bring you to this point where you begin to start listening and responding to the Great Attraction. It is not about formulating religious beliefs. It is about responding at a deeper level. It is not about studying the religious texts and becoming a scholarly or ideologically oriented person. It is about the deeper connection.

生命通过它的失望、它的澄清和它的启示来服务你，将你带到这个点，让你开始聆听并回应伟大的吸引力。它并非关乎构建宗教信仰。它关乎在更深层次上的回应。它并非关乎研读宗教经文并成为一个学者或意识形态导向的人。它关乎更深层次的连接。

It is mysterious. It is profound. The engagement is phenomenal. It is confounding to your personal mind. You cannot use it as a resource to get what you want, for it is changing the course of your life. It is reconnecting you to your Source and the center of your power and your integrity and enabling you to begin to learn the Great Practices that will provide you the skills and the wisdom and orientation you will need to fulfill your greater purpose for coming into the world.

它神秘莫测。它深邃深刻。这种参与是非凡的。它令你的个人心灵困惑。你无法将其作为一种资源来获得你想要的东西，因为它正在改变你的生命历程。它正在重新联接你和你的本源，以及你的力量和完整性的中心，并让你能够开始学习伟大的实践，这将为你提供实现你来到这个世界的更伟大目的所需的技能、智慧和导向。

You cannot learn these things at the university. You might learn them through your current religious tradition, but only if you have an exceptionally wise teacher and guide who knows of the Mystery and is not merely a defender of a belief system.

这些东西你无法在大学里学到。你或许可以通过你现有的宗教传统来学习，但前提是你有一位极其睿智的老师和指引者，他深知神秘，而不仅仅是某个信仰体系的捍卫者。

God has spoken again. A New Message for humanity is being sent into the world. The Messenger is here. He is alive at this moment.

上帝再次讲话了。一个致人类的新讯息正在被发送到这个世界。信使就在这里。他此刻活着。

The Revelation will speak of this deeper engagement and give it priority over your ideas and beliefs, bypassing all the controversies that surround humanity’s understanding of the Divine Presence and Will in the world, bringing you to the Great Attraction—so that you can feel it, and begin to respond to it, and begin to give it your confidence and to allow it to clarify your life and purpose; to bring the light of Wisdom into the dark corners of your life; to liberate you from your regrets, your errors and the misfortunes of your past.

启示将讲述这种更深层次的参与，并赋予它高于你的想法和信仰的优先地位，绕过围绕人类对神圣存在和神圣意志在世界的理解的所有争议，带你走向伟大的吸引力——这样你就能够感受到它，开始回应它，开始赋予它你的信心，并允许它厘清你的生命和目的；将智慧之光带入你生命的黑暗角落；将你从你过去的遗憾、你的错误和不幸中解放出来。

God reclaims the separated through Knowledge. Therefore, taking the Steps to Knowledge is critical and important and necessary for you to begin to experience the most profound relationship of your life, and that which will restore to you your true integrity and your greater abilities and purpose in the world.

上帝通过内识唤回分离的个体。因此，开展内识进阶至关重要、重要且必要，这样你才能开始体验你生命中最深刻的关系，这将重建你真正的完整性以及你在世界上的更伟大能力和目的。

Do not worry about other people and what they think. This is about you. This is your destiny. The calling is for you. Do not hide behind others. Do not say, “Well, what about everyone else?” Do not hide behind everyone else. You are not everyone else. God reclaims the separated one by one and two by two and three by three. Not everyone else.

不要担心别人和他们的想法。这是关于你的。这是你的天命。召唤是属于你的。不要躲在别人身后。不要说：“那么，其他人呢？”不要躲在所有人身后。你不是其他人。上帝会把分离的人一个接一个地收回，两个接两个地收回，三个接三个地收回。而不是其他人。

Our Voice is part of the Mystery, part of the Great Attraction. You cannot identify Us by Our Voice, for it is many Voices in one. It is part of the Revelation. It is part of the Mystery. It is part of the Great Attraction. Rahn Maziy—the language of the inner world.

我们的声音是神秘的一部分，是伟大吸引力的一部分。你无法通过我们的声音来识别我们，因为它是众多声音合而为一。它是启示的一部分。它是神秘的一部分。它是伟大吸引力的一部分。Rahn Maziy——内心世界的语言。

What speaks to your heart defies comprehension. Those who base their life on their ideas and understanding will have difficulty coming to terms with the fact that their real existence resides outside the realm of the intellect. Their greater associations, their greater purpose, their true connections are something that they can only estimate and cannot define.

触动你内心的内识难以理解。那些将生命建立在他们的想法和理解之上的人，难以接受这样一个事实：他们真正的存在存在于智力领域之外。他们更伟大的交往，他们更伟大的目的，他们真正的连接，是他们只能评估，却无法定义的内识。

The errors of religion, the excesses of humanity, the cruelty and barbarism of human history represent the inability to experience the true engagement and to comprehend its meaning, its purpose and its expression in the world.

宗教的错误、人性的暴行、人类历史的残酷和野蛮，都代表着无法体验真正的参与，也无法理解它的意义、目的及其在世界上的表现形式。

That is why failure here is so dangerous and why you must bring a very serious mind and a true commitment to the engagement. It is not a plaything. It is not entertainment. It is not a hobby. It is not an escape. For you cannot escape into God, for God will send you back into the world. So much has been done to bring you here. You cannot return, not yet.

正因为如此，在这里失败如此危险，也正因为如此，你必须带着一个非常严肃的心灵和一个真正的承诺去参与。它不是一种玩物。它不是娱乐。它不是一种爱好。它不是一种逃避。因为你无法逃入上帝，因为上帝会送你回到世界。为了把你带到这里，已经做了这么多。你无法返回，现在还不能。

The Attraction is not to take you away. It is to bring you back with greater strength, greater integrity, greater purpose, greater vision, and greater determination. People think that the Great Attraction will annihilate them. They will be like the cup of water that is thrown into the sea, never to be an individual anymore, just melt into everything. This is a misapprehension, a misunderstanding.

吸引力并非要带走你。它是为了带你回归，让你拥有更伟大的力量、更伟大的品格、更伟大的目的、更伟大的视野和更坚定的决心。人们认为伟大的吸引力会毁灭他们。他们会像一杯水被扔进大海，不再独立，融入万物。这是一种误解，一种错误的理解。

God does not destroy. God re-employs. For you to find your greater purpose and the great relationships that are associated with it, you must experience the engagement. You must know of the Great Attraction and learn over time not to fear it, but to welcome it and to be grateful, oh so grateful, for its Presence and Power in your life.

上帝不毁灭。上帝会重新雇佣。为了找到你的更伟大目的以及与之相关的伟大关系，你必须体验这种参与。你必须理解伟大的吸引力，并随着时间的推移学会不去恐惧它，而是去欢迎它，并感激，哦，如此感激，感激它在你生命中的存在和力量。

People want many things. People are attracted to many things. There are many powerful attractions in the world, obviously, or the world would not appear as it does. But what is the greatest attraction of all, but the Attraction between the Creator and Creation? This is what holds all of Creation together. This is the Force of Attraction that unites the universe.

人们渴望许多事物。人们被许多事物所吸引。显然，世界上存在着许多强大的吸引力，否则世界就不会是现在这个样子。但最伟大的吸引力是什么呢？不就是造物主与万物之间的吸引力吗？正是这种吸引力将所有万物维系在一起。正是这种吸引力将宇宙凝聚在一起。

This is the power that gives you strength and wisdom and enables you to use your mistakes and your errors to generate wisdom for yourself and for others. It is what redeems you and reclaims you and reunites you with your Source.

这股力量赋予你力量和智慧，使你能够使用你的错误和失误为你自己和他人创造智慧。它救赎你，唤回你，并让你与你的本源重新联合。
