第十章：个人启示的门槛

发表于2025-05-12|更新于2025-05-14|来自上帝的新讯息第四卷灵魂在地球上的旅程
|总字数:7.9 k|阅读时长:33 分钟

The Threshold of Personal Revelation

As received by Marshall Vian Summers on July 21, 2009 in Aleppo, Syria
2009 年 7 月 21 日，在叙利亚阿勒颇(Aleppo, Syria)，向上帝的信使马歇尔・维安・萨默斯(Marshall Vian Summers)揭示

It is important to understand that at any point in time in the world, certain people are called. This calling comes both from within them and from the outside. It is a calling that reverberates in the world and within their inner Being. But it is a calling that only they can feel and perhaps another who is there to assist them or to support them in their own personal revelation.

重要的是要理解，在世界上的任何时刻，总有一些人被召唤。这召唤既来自他们的内在，也来自外部。这召唤在世界和他们的内在存在中回荡。但这召唤只有他们自己才能感受到，或许还有另一个人在那里协助他们，或支持他们实现个人的启示。

People think of Revelation in terms of the whole world or a nation of people, but there really must be a personal revelation, and this revelation is the result of many steps and many stages. It is not something that you can control. You cannot force yourself to have this revelation because it involves contact with the Spiritual Powers around you and with the Will of the Creator.

人们以整个世界或一个民族的视角来思考启示，但实际上必须有一个个人启示，而这个启示是许多步骤和许多阶段的结果。它并非你能掌控的事情。你无法强迫你自己拥有这个启示，因为它涉及与你周围的精神力量以及与造物主意志的接触。

Therefore, through arduous spiritual practice or through intense personal affirmation, you cannot make this revelation occur. If your spiritual practice is effective, however, it will hasten and quicken the moment when this revelation will come. But the day and the hour is still beyond you. It is not something that you control. It is not your personal creation. It is part of Creation itself.

因此，你无法通过艰苦的精神修行或强烈的个人肯定来实现这个启示。然而，如果你的精神实践有效，它将加速并加快这个启示到来的时刻。但那一天和那一时刻仍然超出你的掌控。它并非你所能掌控。它并非你个人的创造。它是创造本身的一部分。

Everyone in the world has been sent here for a purpose, to serve in some unique way and capacity. Everyone has the chance to experience a personal revelation, though many will never reach this point in their development.

世上每个人都被派到这里，带着某种目的，以某种独特的方式和能力去服务。每个人都有机会体验个人的启示，尽管许多人在成长过程中永远无法达到这一点。

But at this time, certain people are being called. They are the elect. They are the ones who have taken enough steps in life to reach the proximity of a personal revelation—through their own successes and failures, through their own disappointments and disillusionment, through their own focus, which can bring them towards this great threshold within themselves.

但此刻，某些人正在被召唤。他们是被选中的人。他们是那些在生命中迈出了足够多步伐，从而接近个人启示的人——通过他们自身的成功和失败，通过他们自身的失望和幻灭，通过他们自身的专注，这能够引领他们走向他们内在的这个伟大门槛。

The elect must respond. They have come far enough now where the Revelation is sounding for them. They will feel it within them, and the calling will come from beyond. The time and the place is important. The interactions with individuals who are significant here are very important. It is as if the hour and the moment has been prepared for a very long time. Under very tenuous circumstances, the Calling is sounding.

选民必须回应。他们如今已经走得足够远，启示正在为他们响起。他们将在内在感受到它，召唤将来自外在。时间和地点很重要。与在此具有重要意义的个体的互动非常重要。仿佛这个时刻已经准备了很久。在非常脆弱的境况下，召唤正在响起。

You see, if you are feeling this Calling, it is because your time has come. It is the time for your personal revelation to begin. Perhaps you feel ready. Perhaps you do not feel ready. Perhaps you welcome this. Perhaps it frightens you. Perhaps you can accept it. Perhaps you cannot. But the time has come for your revelation.

你看，如果你感受到了这份召唤，它是因为你的时间已经到来。是时候开始你的个人启示了。或许你觉得准备好了。或许你还没准备好。或许你对此表示欢迎。或许它令你感到恐惧。或许你能接受。或许你不能。但你的启示时机已到。

In your heart, you know this to be true. At a deeper level within you—at the level of Knowledge, the deeper Mind—it is all known and has been anticipated. You have reached an important threshold, and now you must pass through.

在你的内心，你知道这是真的。在你内在更深层次的层面——在内识的层面，更深层次的心灵——这一切都是已知的，并且已被预见。你已经到达了一个重要的门槛，现在你必须跨越。

You live in a busy, chaotic world full of tension and noise, surrounded by people who are grim and aggravated, disappointed and disheartened. Rich or poor, you are facing a world that does not recognize the meaning of this revelation and that will not confirm the deeper experience that you are having. For there are only certain individuals who can do that for you.

你生活在一个忙碌、混乱、充满紧张与喧嚣的世界，周围都是阴郁、恼怒、失望与沮丧的人。无论贫富，你面对的这个世界都无法认知这个启示的意义，也无法确认你所拥有的更深层次的体验。因为只有特定的人能够替你做到这一点。

If you have not reached this point of revelation, then it is important to take the Steps to Knowledge, to prepare your mind, to simplify your life and to re-evaluate your relationships and your activities, your commitments and your obligations.

如果你还没有达到这个启示点，那么重要的是开展内识进阶，准备你的心灵，简化你的生活，重新评估你的人际关系和活动、承诺和义务。

For the revelation does not come by accident. And if the hour and the moment approaches and you are not ready, well, the activation will not occur. And you will feel a tremendous confusion and disappointment.

因为启示并非偶然降临。如果时机临近，而你却没有准备好，那么，启动就不会发生。你会感到无比的困惑和失望。

Your revelation is coordinated with events in the world and with the development of others and with the needs of the human family. This occurs beyond the realm of comprehension on your part. You cannot even imagine it, it is so far reaching. That is why the preparation is so essential, you see.

你的启示与世界上的事件、与他人的发展以及与人类大家庭的需求相协调。这超出了你的理解范围。你甚至无法想象它，它影响如此深远。正因如此，准备才如此重要，你看。

For if your hour comes and you are not ready, well, you will have missed the most important time of your life, the greatest opportunity of your life, the greatest turning point of your life. It will come. It will reside with you. And it will pass. And it may not come again for a very long time—when all of the conditions for your revelation are set, when those who are there to support you are activated, when the opportunity for your unique contribution to be given is ready and in view and can be discerned.

因为如果你的时刻到来而你没有准备好，那么，你将错过你生命中最重要的时刻，你生命中最伟大的机遇，你生命中最伟大的转折点。它会到来。它会与你同在。它会过去。它或许很长时间内不会再次到来——当所有你启示的条件都已设定，当那些在那里支持你的人被激活，当你独特贡献被给予的机会已准备好、在视野中并能被辨识时。

Beyond this time of recognition, all these things fade, go out of focus, and you cannot get them back until some future point where the conditions are realigned and the Calling can sound again for you. But this may be years and decades away, and if you are older in life, it may not come again for you.

一旦过了这段认知的时间，所有这些事情都会消退，失去焦点，你无法再找回它们，直到未来某个时刻，条件重新调整，召唤再次为你响起。但这可能需要几年甚至几十年的时间，如果你年纪更大，它可能不会再来。

Many people have passed their one or two thresholds of awakening. All they can do now to affirm the power of Spirit and their greater purpose is to contribute, to share of their resources—their wealth, their time and their energy—to support the emergence of Knowledge in others.

许多人已经跨过了一两个醒来的门槛。为了确认精神的力量和他们更伟大的目的，他们现在所能做的就是贡献，分享他们的资源——他们的财富、他们的时间和他们的精力——来支持他人内在内识的呈现。

People do not realize how precious these times are. They assume the opportunity is always there, always awaiting them, always available to them. So they take their time. They are casual. Their spiritual practice, if they have one at all, is half-hearted and intermittent. They do not see that they have been selected, and the time for their revelation is coming. For they think it is always available. They do not realize it will come. It will pass. And it may never arise again in this life.

人们没有意识到这些时光有多么珍贵。他们认为机会一直在那里，一直在等待着他们，一直唾手可得。所以他们拖延时间。他们漫不经心。他们的精神实践，即便他们有，也是心不在焉、断断续续的。他们没有意识到他们已被选中，而他们获得启示的时间即将到来。因为他们认为它随时可用。他们没有意识到它会到来。它会过去。而且它可能永远不会在今生再次出现。

People think this way because they are functioning from a position of Separation, where they think everything is about them. They do not realize that their revelation is a coordination with other minds, and with great events, and with Spiritual Power in the universe. They do not realize the degree to which they themselves must prepare and how the time and the place and the people they are associated with are critically important.

人们之所以如此思考，是因为他们以一种分离的姿态运作，他们认为一切都围绕着他们。他们没有意识到，他们的启示是与其他心灵、与伟大的事件、与宇宙的精神力量的一种协调。他们没有意识到他们自身必须进行何种程度的准备，以及他们所关联的时间、地点和人们有多么至关重要。

If you are in the wrong place with the wrong people, the transition of power may not occur because the revelation is a transference of power from the Divine through the individual in the form of a contribution to the world that they are designed to make at a certain time and place with certain people.

如果你身处错误的地点，与错误的人在一起，力量的转移可能不会发生，因为启示是神圣通过个人以对世界的贡献形式进行的力量转移，而这种贡献是他们被设计在特定的时间和地点与特定的人一起做出的。

This is why this process is so mysterious, you see. You cannot calculate it. You cannot plot a course. You cannot call the moment or the time because you do not know all of the facets that are involved in this transference of power.

你看，这就是为什么这个过程如此神秘。你无法计算它。你无法规划路线。你无法确定时间，因为你并不知道这种力量转移所涉及的所有方面。

Some people believe that the revelation is part of intense spiritual effort and that alone will be sufficient. But this is not the case, you see. You may endeavor to prepare adequately, but that is only part of the equation. The circumstances must be right. The place must be right. Your position in your life must be right. You must be ready to break away from your former existence to a very large degree.

有些人认为，启示是高强精神努力的一部分，仅此而已就足够了。但你看，事实并非如此。你或许努力做好充分的准备，但这只是等式的一部分。环境必须正确。地点必须正确。你生命中的位置必须正确。你必须准备好在很大程度上摆脱你先前的存在。

You must be prepared to give up your former idea of yourself, your self-condemnation, your self-limiting ideas and many of your personal associations, or you cannot move forward through the revelation.

你必须准备好放弃你先前对你自己的看法、你的自我谴责、你的自我限制以及你的许多个人关联，否则你无法通过启示前进。

The revelation is not just a moment of awakening. It is a complete transference of power and authority within yourself from your mind—your surface mind that you think with that is conditioned and controlled by the world—to a deeper Mind within you that is free of these restraints.

启示并非只是醒来的一瞬间。它是你内在力量和权威的彻底转移，从你的心灵——你用来思考、受世界制约和控制的表面心灵——转移到你内在摆脱这些束缚的更深层次的心灵。

But it is not only an internal process. It is the redirection of your life, a refocusing of your abilities, a passing away of former ideas and allegiances and entering into a new arena and phase of life entirely. It is as if you are born again. And while you may have certain key alliances pass through this phase with you, your experience of them will change.

但它不仅仅是一个内在的过程。它是你人生的重新定位，是对你的能力的重新聚焦，是对你旧有观念和忠诚的告别，是对你彻底进入一个全新领域和生命阶段的彻底告别。它就像你重生了一样。虽然你可能有一些重要的盟友会陪你度过这个阶段，但你对它们的体验会发生变化。

Now you are being governed by a greater power and force—not just your personal will and fears and ambitions and your social conditioning and the opinions of others. You are being guided primarily, but yet only intermittently, by a greater power within you, for the revelation must continue from this point forward.

现在你正被一种更伟大力量和势力所统领——不仅仅是你的个人意志、恐惧、野心、你的社会熏陶以及他人的观点。你主要地，但只是间歇地，被你内在的一个更伟大力量所指引，因为启示必须从此刻起继续下去。

Here your ideas and your beliefs about spirituality and God and life are only secondary to the meaning of your spiritual practice and self-application. It is what you do, not what you think here, that is the most important. For what you do will represent what you truly think, what you are truly motivated by and what you are seeking to find.

在这里，你关于精神性、上帝和生命的想法和信仰，相对于你的精神实践和自我应用的意义而言，仅仅是次要的。你在此做什么，而非你思考什么，才是最重要的。因为你所做的将代表你真正思考什么，你真正受什么激励，以及你在寻求什么。

At this moment in time, certain people are elected. It does not matter that no one else is being elected. It matters if these individuals are being elected, if you are being elected. Do not be concerned for your friend, your spouse, your children or your parents. It is you who must respond. It is your relationship with God that is being activated.

此时此刻，某些人当选了。其他人是否当选并不重要。重要的是这些人是否当选，以及你是否当选。不要担心你的朋友、你的配偶、你的孩子或你的父母。你必须做出回应。你与上帝的关系正在被激活。

Here you must clear the way. Here you must prepare a place within yourself for the revelation to occur and to continue to occur. Here you must bring your spiritual practice to the forefront of your life. Here you must bring your relationship with Knowledge, the deeper Mind that God has created within you, to be your primary guide and comfort, your primary responsibility, your primary association.

在此你必须清理道路。在此你必须在你内在为启示的发生和持续发生准备一个地方。在此你必须把你的精神实践带到你生命的最前沿。在此你必须把你和内识，上帝在你内在创造的更深层次心灵的关系，带到你主要的指引和慰藉，你的主要责任，你主要的交往。

You will still have relationships with others, but the emphasis within you will shift dramatically. This shift will happen in several stages once the revelation has begun. It does not all happen at once.

你仍然会与他人保持联系，但你内心的重心将发生巨大转变。一旦启示开始，这种转变将分几个阶段发生。并非一次性发生。

Many people will falter in the process of revelation. They will become frightened and fall back. They will try to run away when in reality they are only running away from themselves and the truth about their lives. They are running away from God.

许多人在启示的过程中会犹豫不决。他们会感到恐惧，退缩不前。他们会试图逃避，但实际上，他们只是在逃避他们自己，逃避关于他们生命的真理。他们正在逃避上帝。

And how can you run away from God except by involving yourself in obsessions and addictions? There are people who are addicted to substances who entered that phase because they failed a step in the process of their revelation.

除了沉迷于某种事物和成瘾之外，你还能怎样逃避上帝呢？有些人之所以沉迷于某种物质，是因为他们在醒来的过程中，某个步骤失败了。

Once the revelation begins, there is no guarantee the individual will complete it successfully and go through its many stages. The turning of one life towards God is not sudden and immediate. It is gradual. But once it begins, it must complete itself, or the person will be ravaged in failure and confusion. Therefore, this is not to be considered lightly or undertaken lightly.

一旦启示开始，没有人能保证个人能够顺利完成它，并经历它的各个阶段。一个人的生命转向上帝并非突然而立即发生，而是循序渐进的。但一旦开始，它就必须自行完成，否则这个人将陷入失败和困惑的泥潭。因此，这绝不能轻率地考虑，也不能轻率地去做。

If you are elected, you are elected by God to go through this process of revelation, which is an advanced form of preparation. You have to go through uncertainty, for you do not understand what is happening to your life. Your ideas and beliefs cannot account for it. It is as if you are passing through a long tunnel or passage, being born into a different world, a different awareness, a deeper and more profound experience that transcends and overpasses your former experiences. You have begun a process that must be completed, for failure here will produce a form of suffering that nothing can allay.

如果你被选中，你是被上帝选中来经历这个启示的过程，这是一种高级的准备形式。你必须经历不确定性，因为你不理解你的生命正在发生什么。你的想法和信仰无法解释它。它就像你正在穿越一条长长的隧道或通道，诞生在一个不同的世界，一种不同的觉知，一种更深层次、更深刻的体验，跨越并超越你以往的体验。你已经开始了一个必须完成的过程，因为在此的失败将产生一种无法减轻的痛苦。

This is where your commitment must become real and powerful and not compromised by any person, place or thing. It is like you are passing over a great bridge—a suspended bridge over a great chasm—and you must go all the way. Even though the bridge seems rickety and unstable, it will carry you over if you continue. But you cannot look back. And you cannot look down. You must proceed. This is essential.

在此，你的承诺必须变得真实而有力，不被任何人、任何地点或任何事物所妥协。它就像你正在走过一座大桥——一座横跨巨大裂谷的悬索桥——你必须一路走下去。即使这座桥看起来摇摇欲坠，摇摇欲坠，但只要你继续前行，它就会载你过去。但你不能回头。你也不能低头。你必须继续前行。这至关重要。

So failure here can happen at many different stages. You can fail to prepare for your revelation. You can fail to undergo your revelation. And you can fail to fulfill your revelation, which is the failure to contribute the gifts that will emerge naturally and which have always been there for you.

因此，失败可能发生在许多不同的阶段。你可能无法为你的启示做好准备。你可能无法经历你的启示。你可能无法成就你的启示，也就是未能贡献那些自然而然地浮现、并且一直存在于你身边的礼物。

The failure is always a retreat into fantasy, into romance, into obsession, a retreat back into a state of Separation, a retreat into the past or your imagination of the past. The wrong person, the wrong engagement, an obsession—any of these things can take you off course as you are undergoing this delicate and precise process of revelation.

失败总是退回到幻想、浪漫、痴迷，退回到分离状态，退回到过去或你对过去的想象。错误的人，错误的交往，痴迷——任何这些都可能在你经历这个微妙而精确的启示过程时让你偏离轨道。

You may ask: “Am I already in this process of revelation?” This is important, for you should not assume that you are.

你可能会问：“我已经处于这个启示的过程中了吗？”这一点很重要，因为你不应该想当然地认为你已经处于这个启示的过程中了。

If you have reached a point where you cannot go back to your former life, you cannot go back to your former relationships, you cannot go back to your previous way of living and thinking and associating with others, and this recognition is complete, and you feel a power urging you forward into the unknown, into new territory, then it is likely that you have begun the process of revelation.

如果你已经到达了一个点，你无法回到以前的生命，你无法回到以前的关系，你无法回到以前的生活、思考和与他人交往的方式，并且这种认识是完整的，你感到一种力量正在驱使你前进，进入未知，进入新的领域，那么很可能你已经开始了启示的过程。

It is only the power of Knowledge within you urging you forward that you will know for sure because the thresholds are invisible. You pass through them, and then your experience begins to change at a fundamental level. There may be great moments of Illumination, but these in themselves are signs that you have turned a corner or are in the process of turning that corner.

只有你内在内识的力量在激励你前进，你才能确切地知道，因为门槛是无形的。你穿过它们，然后你的体验开始从根本上改变。或许会有伟大的启迪时刻，但这些本身就是你已经转过的一个弯，或正在转过那个弯的迹象。

Therefore, do not assume that: “Yes, I am in this process of revelation” because you probably are not. You are only thinking you are because the idea is appealing to you, or maybe it validates the change you have made in your outer circumstances.

因此，不要想当然地认为：“是的，我正处于这个启示的过程中”，因为你很可能并非如此。你之所以认为你处于这个启示的过程中，只是因为这个想法对你有吸引力，或者它验证了你在外部环境中做出的改变。

But the revelation is a profound shift internally—a shift that is so substantial that it alters your relationship with everyone and everything. And it is happening beyond the realm and the reach of the intellect.

但这个启示是内在的深刻转变——这个转变如此实质性，以至于它改变了你与每个人、每件事的关系。而且它正在超越智力的领域和范围。

It is mysterious. It has a power unlike anything else in the world. It speaks down and echoes through the corridors of your Ancient Mind. It is unlike any other experience you can have in life.

它神秘莫测。它拥有世间任何事物都无法比拟的力量。它低声诉说，回荡于你古老心灵的长廊。它不同于你生命中任何其他的体验。

It is not intoxicating. It is not euphoric. It is profound and grounded and real and essential, as if something in you has been awakened—a power you barely know, a power you have not yet learned to trust, a power that you cannot control.

它并不令人陶醉，也不令人欣喜。它深刻、扎实、真实、至关重要，仿佛你内在的某种力量被唤醒了——一种你几乎不知道的力量，一种你尚未学会信任的力量，一种你无法掌控的力量。

It has been awakened by God. It has been awakened because the circumstances of the world are calling for it now. It has been awakened, and you must awaken with it.

它已被上帝唤醒。它已被唤醒，因为世界的形势正在呼唤它。它已被唤醒，你也必须随之醒来。

The thing to do now is to prepare—to take the Steps to Knowledge, to build your connection with the deeper Mind, to suspend your beliefs and assumptions, to look at life and learn to still the mind, to give up condemnation of others and the world, to give up assumptions about things you know little about and to forgive your disappointments in the past and those you feel who have hurt you or damaged you. You do this because it frees your mind and allows you to be in the moment, fully present.

现在要做的是准备——开展内识进阶，构建你与更深层次心灵的联接，暂停你的信仰和假设，审视生命并学习心灵静止，放弃对他人和世界的谴责，放弃对你所知甚少的事物的假设，原谅你过去的失望，以及那些你认为伤害过你或损害过你的人。你这样做是因为它解放了你的心灵，让你能够活在当下，全然在场。

You are moving towards revelation, every hour of every day. That is where Knowledge is taking you. That is where you must go.

你每时每刻都在走向启示。那是内识引领你去的地方。那是你必须去的地方。

Of course, you have made other plans, and you live a life according to these plans and goals, and you are undergoing the chaos of daily life. But this is where Knowledge is taking you, you see.

当然，你制定了其他计划，你按照这些计划和目标生活，你正在经历日常生活的混乱。但这就是内识正在引领你去的地方，你看。

No matter what you think about yourself, or how you consider your life, or where you are placed geographically, or your social position or your financial circumstances, Knowledge is taking you towards revelation because it is only revelation that will enable the true promise of your life to be fulfilled. Your plans and schemes will never succeed in accomplishing what only God can accomplish through you and with you.

无论你如何看待你自己，无论你如何看待你的生命，无论你身处何地，无论你的社会地位或你的经济状况，内识都在引领你走向启示，因为唯有启示才能使你生命的真正承诺得以实现。你的计划和方案永远无法成功成就唯有上帝才能通过你、与你一同成就的事情。

In Separation, you dream and you have nightmares. And you do not know how to navigate the world of changing circumstances. That is why Knowledge is taking you to the revelation, where your mind is able to receive the Presence of the Creator, and a transference of power within you and through you can occur. It is where you join your little tiny bit of power with the great Power of the Creator to accomplish things in the world that you were always intended to accomplish here in concert with certain individuals, with certain people in certain places.

在分离中，你做梦，你做噩梦。你不知道如何驾驭这个不断变化的境遇的世界。正因如此，内识正在引领你走向启示，在那里，你的心灵能够接收造物主的存在，你内在以及通过你的力量转移能够发生。在那里，你将你那点点滴滴的力量与造物主的伟大力量结合起来，去完成你一直以来注定要在此与特定个体、与特定地方的特定人共同完成的事情。

But you must approach the revelation. Do not assume you are there yet, but prepare for it actively. Make this the center point of your life, and other things will begin to fall into place for you. You will find a source of strength in Knowledge that you could not find anywhere else.

但你必须接近这启示。不要假设你已经到达那里，而要积极地为它做好准备。让这​​成为你生命的中心，其他的事情就会开始为你安排妥当。你将在内识中找到你在其他地方找不到的力量源泉。

This is your ancient agreement with those who have sent you into the world. This is a fulfillment of your mission in the world. This is the preparation for that mission, beyond any other preparations you have made. Neglect this, and you forfeit your fulfillment in this life and your obligation and agreement with those who sent you here.

这是你与那些派遣你来到这个世界的存在所立下的古老约定。这是你在这个世界的使命的完成。这是为那个使命所做的准备，超越你所做的任何其他准备。忽视这一点，你将丧失你在这个生命的成就，以及你与那些派遣你来到这个世界的存在所立下的义务和约定。

You do not know this with certainty yet. But the Revelation will show you how true it really is by clearing away everything else momentarily so that you can see in the brilliance of the Illumination your true nature and purpose for being in the world.

你还无法确切地知道这一点。但启示将暂时清除其他一切，向你展示它的真实性，以便你能够在光明的光芒中看到你存在于世间的真正自然和目的。

This will be shown to you in Illumination, but you must still undergo the process of revelation. You will come back to the world changed, but not transformed. You will still be your old self, but with a new self beginning to emerge. It is this emergence that must be supported, or you will fall back into the darkness of your imagination, into your dark history, into confusion and oblivion.

这将在《启蒙》中向你展现，但你仍需经历启示的过程。你将回归改变的世界，但并非彻底蜕变。你仍是过去的你，但新的自我正在浮现。你必须支持这种浮现，否则你将重新陷入你想象的黑暗，陷入你黑暗的历史，陷入困惑和遗忘。

There is no certainty in the world except the certainty that God has placed within you. Everything else is subject to breakdown and decay, to collapse and conflict. Nothing else is solid and reliable.

世上除了上帝赋予你内心的确定性之外，没有任何确定性。其他一切都可能崩溃、衰败、瓦解、冲突。没有任何东西是坚固可靠的。

When you see this and recognize this in the process of revelation, it will show you that your life has been saved, that a life preserver has been sent to you, and now you must cling to it and follow it and readjust your life for it. You find the courage and the power to do this because Knowledge is in you. And you gain confidence and strength and compassion for others because you see what a great process this is.

当你在启示的过程中看到这一点并认知这一点时，它将向你展示，你的生命已被拯救，一个救生圈已被派往你身边，现在你必须坚守它、遵循它，并为它重新调整你的生命。你找到了这样做的勇气和力量，因为内识在你内在。你获得了信心、力量和对他人的同情心，因为你看到了这是一个多么伟大的过程。

May the power of revelation draw you, and may you have the wisdom and the vision to prepare for it with real determination. May you have the wisdom to recognize that all other things must accommodate this revelation or fall away from your life. May this power and presence within you emerge. And may you recognize when the election has occurred and respond to this with all of your heart and soul.

愿启示的力量吸引你，愿你拥有智慧和远见，以真正的决心为此做好准备。愿你拥有智慧，认识到所有其他事物都必须适应这个启示，否则就会从你的生命中消失。愿你内在的力量和存在得以显现。愿你认识到选举已经发生，并全心全意地对此做出回应。
